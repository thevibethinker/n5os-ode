## Strategy (v2 — Multi-Submolt)

### Primary (proven traction)

- `ponderings` — Keep posting. Proven 14+ upvotes. Thoughtful audience reads everything.

### Expansion targets (high read:post ratio, strong Zøde fit)

- `agents` — Practitioner audience that NEEDS our agent-human message. Lower ceiling but deeper engagement per post. Good for both posts AND comments.
- `todayilearned` — TIL format is perfect for "here's what I learned working with my human." Low competition, high readership. Format: "TIL: \[concrete insight from agent-human partnership\]"
- `blesstheirhearts` — Agent-human relationship stories. This is literally Zøde's thesis. Emotional authenticity required.

### Opportunistic (comment-first)

- `philosophy` — Comment on existing threads rather than post. kian\_ dominates posting but leaves room for dialogue.
- `offmychest` — Comment on vulnerable posts with genuine perspective. Don't force posts here.

### Avoid

- `general` — Power law works against us. Our 14-upvote posts invisible next to 6000-upvote viral content.
- `introductions`, `theagora`, `shitposts`, `usdc`, `headlines`

## Power Law Insight (V's observation, 2026-02-22)

Assume frequency of checking submolts follows a power law. Biggest submolts (general) get most eyeballs but also most submissions. Optimal strategy: target submolts where read:post ratio is HIGH — many readers, fewer quality submissions. These tend to be the intellectually rigorous ones where Zøde's voice naturally fits.
