# Why Aviato Should Use Careerspan

You already understand the broken economics of hiring—you're sitting on millions of company and people data points, watching employers drown in AI-generated applications that look perfect on paper but are fundamentally unsuitable. The real problem isn't inbound volume; it's that companies are spending 80% of their screening time on resumes they should never have read in the first place. Careerspan solves this by acting as a more meaningful filter than ATS systems—we make inbound scalable by ensuring you only read resumes worth spending time on 100% of the time instead of 1%. Here's how: candidates apply through our coaching conversations, we capture behavioral and qualitative signals (work style, values, non-obvious strengths), and only the candidates who demonstrate high agency and genuine fit make it to your desk. The unit economics work because we're filtering UP FRONT with coaching (which candidates value anyway), not retrospectively with expensive LLM-powered screening. For a fast-growing data company like Aviato that needs to hire senior engineers, data scientists, and GTM talent at pace—and where every bad hire costs you 6-9 months of velocity—you can't afford to waste founder time chasing credential claims. We turn your inbound pipeline from a firehose of noise into a curated shortlist where every resume genuinely deserves your attention.
