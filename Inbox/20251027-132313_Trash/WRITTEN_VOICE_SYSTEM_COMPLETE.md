## Centralized Voice Elements (Applies to ALL Content)

### 1. Greetings
- **"Hi {{name}},"** for new/formal contexts
- **"Hey {{name}},"** for warm/established relationships
- **"Hey {{name}}—"** for warm contexts with em-dash rhythm

### 2. Em-Dashes (Signature Style)
- Heavy, intentional use throughout
- In bullets: "Item — detail"
- In prose: "context — additional info"
- After greetings: "Hey Mark—"

### 3. Signature Phrases (40+ Tracked)
**Gratitude:** "Thanks for carving out time on [day]"  
**Enthusiasm:** "I loved your point about...", "You nailed it"  
**Transitions:** "As promised, here's..."  
**Closing:** "Let me know what makes sense", "Best,"

**Full list:** In voice.md "Signature Phrases" section

### 4. Word Count Targets (By Content Type)
- **Email (standard):** 200-300 words
- **Email (complex partnership):** 300-400 words
- **LinkedIn post (standard):** 250-350 words
- **LinkedIn (short):** 150-200 words
- **LinkedIn (long):** 350-450 words

### 5. Structure
- **Bullets + short prose** (not paragraph exposition)
- **Em-dash format** in bullets
- **Short paragraphs** (1-3 sentences)
- **Natural flow** (avoid over-labeling)

### 6. Tone Weights (Consistent)
- Warmth: 0.80-0.85
- Confidence: 0.72-0.80
- Humility: 0.55-0.65 (distinctive)

### 7. Lexicon
- **Preferred:** surface, distill, scaffold, tackle, calibrate, bridge
- **Avoid:** get, make, take, go, have, do
- **Replace:** "go after" → "tackle", "makes sense" → "resonates"
