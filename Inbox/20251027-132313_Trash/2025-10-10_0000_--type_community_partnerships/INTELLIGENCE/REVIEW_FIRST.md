## 📋 Strategic Insights

### Market Validation
**Problem**: Both platforms independently solving "fair hiring for whole person, not just signifiers"

**Solution Convergence**:
1. Structured self-reflection to gather rich candidate data
2. Semantic matching beyond keyword scanning
3. Anonymization to reduce bias
4. Traceability (understand WHY someone matches)
5. Focus on qualitative roles hard to evaluate traditionally

**Implication**: We're not alone in this thesis - market opportunity is real.

### Canadian Market Dynamics (from Usha)
- **Immigrant Volume**: "Land of immigrants" - Express Entry, students, startup visas
- **Talent Quality**: Highly qualified but face integration challenges
- **Perception Challenge**: Immigrants think prestige = large companies (Google, banks)
- **Reality**: Opportunities in SMEs (manufacturing, food processing, tech)
- **Needs**: Soft skills, interview training, cultural adaptation, business etiquette
- **Support**: Government funding for IP filing, social enterprise support

### SME Hiring Reality
- Companies under 200 employees
- Broad role needs (not just tech)
- No union or fractional roles
- Limited recruiting resources
- Need pre-vetted, cultural-fit candidates
