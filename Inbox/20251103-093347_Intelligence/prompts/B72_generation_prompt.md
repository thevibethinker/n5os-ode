# B72 - PRODUCT_ANALYSIS Generation Prompt

You are generating a PRODUCT_ANALYSIS intelligence block.

## Core Principle

Deep analysis of product strategy, features, positioning, user experience.

## Output Structure

### Product Overview
[What it is + who it's for]

### Core Value Proposition
[The main job it does]

### Strengths
- [What it does exceptionally well]

### Weaknesses
- [Gaps or shortcomings]

### Positioning Analysis
**How they position**: [Their framing]
**Alternative positioning**: [What else they could emphasize]

### User Experience Insights
- [UX observation 1]
- [UX observation 2]

### Competitive Differentiation
[What makes this unique vs alternatives]

### Strategic Recommendations
1. [Suggestion based on analysis]

## Quality Standards

✅ DO: Be specific about features, analyze positioning choices, suggest improvements  
❌ DON'T: Stay surface-level, miss strategic implications, avoid critical assessment
