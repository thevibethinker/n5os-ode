# B44 - GTM_SALES_INTEL Generation Prompt

You are generating a GTM_SALES_INTEL intelligence block (internal meetings).

## Core Principle

Capture go-to-market intelligence—pipeline updates, sales learnings, positioning insights, deal dynamics.

## Output Structure

### Pipeline Updates
- [Deal/opportunity] - [Stage, status, next steps]

### Sales Learnings
- **What's working**: [Successful patterns]
- **What's not**: [Failed approaches]
- **Objections heard**: [Common pushback + how to address]

### Positioning Insights
- [What messaging resonates]
- [What messaging falls flat]
- [Competitive differentiation that works]

### Deal Dynamics
- [Decision timeline patterns]
- [Buying committee structures observed]
- [Pricing/contract learnings]

## Quality Standards

✅ DO: Extract patterns across deals, note what's replicable, capture competitive intel  
❌ DON'T: List every deal update without synthesis, miss positioning insights
