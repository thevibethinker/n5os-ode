# Founder Intake (v0.1)

Goal: derive rubric weights and critical gaps organically from founder input.

One-pager questions
- Stage & horizon: pre-seed/seed? 90-day outcomes?
- Top risks: what could sink us in next 6 months?
- Must-win bets: where do we need outsized strength?
- Team complement: what strengths already exist? where are gaps?
- Evidence preference: portfolio vs. trial vs. live? any anti-signals?
- AI posture: how important is AI fluency for this role? examples of good/bad?

Output
- Completed rubric.<role>.<stage>.v1.json with tuned weights + notes
- Interview loop settings (optional): set in configs/loop.defaults.json or role overlay
