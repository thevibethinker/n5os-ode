# Evidence Policy — Qualified Yes Gate (v0.1)

Definition: "Qualified Yes" = sufficient evidence to proceed, with no red critical gaps or a credible 30–60 day mitigation plan.

Evaluator prompt: "What would I need to see to bump this person to a Qualified Yes today?"

Checklist (use per role):
- Artifact-first: code/portfolio/PRD reviewed prior to live interview
- Anchored notes: cite behaviors mapped to L1–L4 anchors
- Critical gaps: explicitly list gap(s) and proposed mitigation (owner, timeline)
- Disconfirming evidence: include one counterpoint to your own verdict
- Verdict band: Strong Yes / Qualified Yes / Hold / Reject
