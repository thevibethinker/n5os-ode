## Strategic Positioning Notes

### What Resonated
1. Marvin Ventures validation (McKinsey alumni fund posting jobs)
2. Automated employer vetting (solves Lisa's manual pain point)
3. Alumni career transition support (2017-2022 cohorts pivoting)
4. Voice message job description feature (novel, practical)
5. Coach access dashboard (empowers advisors without replacing them)
6. Bypass handshake altogether

### Objections/Concerns
1. Universities won't give up handshake despite hating it ("all you can eat buffet, none of it very good but lots of options")
2. Zero probability students get jobs through handshake but creates illusion of options
3. Career center organizational politics and resistance to change
4. Platform fatigue ("another platform to manage")

### Leverage Points
1. Entrepreneurship center pilot (Lisa has autonomy here)
2. Alumni network (immediate market, less organizational friction)
3. Zo system setup (personal relationship building, demonstrates tech sophistication)
4. Hackathon with Davis AI Center (institutional credibility, student engagement)
5. Price point ("you will love paying ours" compared to handshake)
