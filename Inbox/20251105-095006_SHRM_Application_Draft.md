### Question 26: Customer Impact Example
**Final Answer:**
"Bonfire Analytics passed on us initially—they didn't believe conversation-based filtering could beat founder-led resume screening. Two months later, after 400+ resumes and 2-3x more screening calls than previous cycles (chasing AI-generated credential claims), they called us back. Their top candidate had accepted another offer from a company that moved faster.

They distributed Careerspan's magic link to their network and across job boards. Applicants applied through AI coaching that used qualitative insights for matching. Within one month, Bonfire cut screening time 80%, doubled their pass-through rate, and scheduled 14 qualified interviews—more than two months of traditional screening had produced. Notably, nearly 1/4 of candidates were non-traditional fits the founders admitted they would have rejected based on resumes alone. Total founder time saved: 7-10 hours per week.

Their founder, Jaya, later admitted that they were about to give up on inbound recruiting and rely only on referrals because recruiters were entirely unaffordable. That's why giving them inbound-level reach with referral-level signal quality is such a powerful value proposition."

**Status:** Approved 2025-11-03

### Question 27: Competitors & Differentiation
**Final Answer:**
"Competitors fall into three categories: job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed) optimize for volume with no behavioral data; ATS providers (Greenhouse, Lever) manage workflows but ignore candidate experience; AI recruiting tools (HireVue, Paradox) automate screening without improving signal quality. UK startups like Jack & Jill and Dex attempt something similar but focus on employer-side recruiting while paying lip service to candidates—limiting both applicant loyalty and data quality.

We're structurally different: a two-sided marketplace where candidates actually return. 50%+ engage in multiple conversations (20min each), 20% D30 and 10% D60 retention (both 2x industry average), 3% organic reactivation rate. This engagement creates longitudinal behavioral data competitors can't replicate. We distribute through US alumni associations and professional communities (50,000+ reach through signed partnerships)—a low-cost acquisition channel UK competitors and incumbents can't access.

In the long-run, we expect a combination of our pro-candidate brand, advanced tech built atop a uniquely dense dataset, and user love (https://careerspan.short.gy/proofpoints) will win out."

**Status:** Approved 2025-11-04
