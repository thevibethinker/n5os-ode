### Feature/Product Ideas Discussed

**1. Employer Portal for Job Submission**
- **Description:** Interface where partners (like SIEM) can "drop in a job description and Logan and I will mail you candidates the next day"
- **Rationale:** Streamlines partner integration; eliminates manual coordination; makes Careerspan a self-service candidate source for distribution partners
- **Source:** Vrijen (07:00) - "we're hoping to build out... a portal where you can drop in a job description"
- **Confidence:** HIGH - explicitly stated as near-term roadmap item; critical infrastructure for partnership model; Logan already planning "Careerspan Select" which depends on this

**2. Vibe-Based Matching via Voice Memo**
- **Description:** Hiring managers record voice message describing desired personality/vibes (not just skills), Careerspan matches candidates based on conversational style and soft skills demonstrated in AI interviews
- **Rationale:** Increases placement conversion by reducing culture/personality mismatches that skills-only matching misses; differentiates Careerspan from resume-based ATS systems
- **Source:** Vrijen (14:32-16:07) - "if you or the hiring manager records a voice message... of like, I want someone that has this personality or these vibes. We can match across those vibes as well"
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM - product exists (interview-based analysis platform), but voice memo input method and vibe matching specificity may be partially speculative/in-development

**3. "Careerspan Select" Curated Candidate Lists**
- **Description:** Pre-vetted, high-quality candidate lists generated automatically based on matching algorithm; essentially a "best of Careerspan" shortlist for partners
- **Rationale:** Reduces partner administrative burden; increases candidate quality perception; creates premium tier offering for partners
- **Source:** Vrijen (14:13) - "we basically have this idea of career span select where we take the highest matched candidates and were just going to generate a list"
- **Confidence:** HIGH - Logan already organizing this; clear use case for SIEM partnership; operationally feasible given existing matching algorithm

**4. Integration with AI Academy for Candidate Upskilling**
- **Description:** Not a Careerspan feature, but SIEM's AI Academy concept (piloting with Notion AI) that upskills job seekers in AI tools (HubSpot, Notion, Monday AI capabilities)
- **Rationale:** Increases candidate quality and employability; provides non-monetary value to job seekers (avoiding charging them); creates partnership opportunity (Careerspan could feed candidates to SIEM's academy)
- **Source:** Ash (03:09-03:31) - "we launched an AI academy that helps them upskill... we're piloting it with Notion AI and like building a Notion page through Notion AI"
- **Confidence:** HIGH - SIEM is actively piloting this; could become value-add for Careerspan candidates if partnership deepens beyond placement

**5. Tailored Interview Question Generation from Voice Memos**
- **Description:** Hiring managers record voice description of role/candidate; Careerspan auto-generates customized interview questions based on role requirements AND desired personality fit
- **Rationale:** Saves hiring managers time; improves interview quality by tailoring questions to specific role context; leverages Careerspan's conversation analysis AI
- **Source:** Vrijen (14:32-15:32) - "if you just record... a voice message, not just of the [job description]... We can also do is... generate tailored interview questions"
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM - product capability exists (Careerspan already generates interview questions from job descriptions), but voice memo input and personality-based question generation may be in development
