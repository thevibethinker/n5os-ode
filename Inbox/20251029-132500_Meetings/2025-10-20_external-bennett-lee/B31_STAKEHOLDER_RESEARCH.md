### Technical Founder Pattern: Engineering Excellence Can Blind You To Market Realities

**Evidence:** "We like overbuilt because you know, our background is in, mine is in engineering, his is in product. And we sort of think like every solution is an engineering... every problem is an engineering solution. So we like over index on that and then we realize at the end of the tunnel we're like wait, is anybody going to buy this?"

**Why it matters:** Technical founders building AI/LLM products risk over-investing in model accuracy and engineering sophistication before validating GTM - forcing early sales experiments and MVPs prevents this trap.
