**Risk 2: Integration Complexity Underestimation**
ATS integration may be harder than initial research suggests. Over-promising on "Filter Plus" integration could damage credibility with acquirers if technical validation reveals significant complexity.

**Risk 3: Team Dysfunction During Transition**
Three-month notice period creates extended "lame duck" period. Team members may disengage, reducing productivity or actively undermining. Ilse's partial retention creates favoritism perception.

**Risk 4: Acquirer Market Misjudgment**
Three acquirer categories identified but unclear which actually values Careerspan's capabilities. May waste time pitching wrong buyers or positioning incorrectly for right buyers.

**Risk 5: Timeline Pressure → Premature Decisions**
Three-month sprint may force decisions before adequate signal. Field tests with 5-10 hiring managers may not provide sufficient data to validate verification market. May commit to wrong path due to time pressure rather than evidence.
