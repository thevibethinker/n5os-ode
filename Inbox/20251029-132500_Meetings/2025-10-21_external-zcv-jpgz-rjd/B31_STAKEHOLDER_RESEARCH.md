### 2. Offshore contractors lack "co-thinker" product intelligence that matters pre-MVP, even with good documentation

**Evidence:** "Having the Philippines guy is not like a co thinker like Brandon is a co thinker... there's a gap between what I know and what the Filipino guy knows. Like, I don't know if he's gonna be getting me the best results."

**Why it matters:** Documentation captures "what to build" but not "how to make product decisions when specs are ambiguous." Pre-MVP consumer products require user experience intuition that overseas contractors often lack, especially for Western consumer contexts. This suggests tools that give founders more direct building power (Zo vibe-coding) are more valuable than offshore outsourcing for pre-product-market-fit.
