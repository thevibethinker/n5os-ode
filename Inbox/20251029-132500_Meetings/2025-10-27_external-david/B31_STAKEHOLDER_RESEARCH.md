## Insight 3: Strategic buyers prioritize build-vs-buy analysis when you fill a specific gap in their stack

**Evidence:** "You find a company that you know, has a gap that career span fills and if they're looking to invest in that then immediately they would just do you know, build versus buy analysis and decide to buy."

**Why it matters:** Target companies who already know they need what you have - the sale becomes about cost/speed of building vs. buying, not whether they need the capability at all. This focuses outreach on companies with known gaps (e.g., Superposition with recruiting side but no marketplace).
