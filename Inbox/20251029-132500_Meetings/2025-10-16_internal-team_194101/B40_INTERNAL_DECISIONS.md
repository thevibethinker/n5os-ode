## Holistic Pushes

### Initiative: Skeleton Crew Transition

**Strategic Rationale**: [D1] Move from VC-backed growth trajectory to sustainable 2-person operation that preserves exit optionality while reducing burn and team pressure

**Tactical Execution**:
- [T1] Fund team through EOY
- [T2] Reduce product costs (Ilse: inference; Danny/Rochel: self-sustaining features)
- [T3] Plan Ilse part-time extension (paid through Feb)
- [T4] Core team = V + Logan by January 1

**Dependencies**:
- Product must reach sustainable unit economics
- Team transition must be communicated transparently
- Distribution strategy (communities, universities) must work at small scale

**Success Criteria**:
- Reach <$20-25k/month operating costs
- Product can run with 2-person team
- Positive candidate acquisition via community distribution
- Team exits gracefully with relationships intact
