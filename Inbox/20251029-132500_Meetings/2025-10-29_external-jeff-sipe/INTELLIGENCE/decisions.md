## Strategic Decisions

### Decision: Pursue Recruiter-Focused Go-To-Market Strategy
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen (with team validation implied)
- **Rationale**: 
  - Recruiting agencies are "out of ideas for how to get to talent"
  - LinkedIn is saturated, job boards don't work, inbound is terrible
  - Agencies want "highly curated pools of talent, highly refined"
  - Three agencies already showing strong interest (Superposition, LaunchSam, Matchbox)
- **Impact**: Positions Careerspan as infrastructure layer for recruiting industry rather than direct-to-candidate marketplace
- **Category**: Strategic / Go-To-Market
- **Reversibility**: Low - requires significant investment in agency relationships and tooling

### Decision: Magic Linking as Core Product Offering for Recruiters
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen + Logan
- **Rationale**: 
  - Founders' work "reduced to thinking really hard one time about who they want and then just reaping the rewards"
  - Pricing ($200-300/month per role) proving "pretty solid"
  - Allows posting link anywhere without cleanup - candidates get filtered automatically
  - Supports voice messaging for qualitative matching ("unrevealed preferences")
- **Impact**: Product-market fit validation with clear pricing and value prop
- **Category**: Product / Pricing
- **Reversibility**: Medium - pricing can be adjusted, but value prop is core

### Decision: Commission Split Model for Agency Partnerships
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen + Logan
- **Rationale**: 
  - Agencies bring roles, Careerspan sources candidates, split bounty when placed
  - Vrijen emphasized being "very Wheeler and Dealer-y" about "sharing the pie"
  - Need to optimize for partners' constraints (Jeff's "burn rate and priorities")
- **Impact**: Enables rapid marketplace growth by leveraging existing agency relationships without upfront fees
- **Category**: Business Model / Partnerships
- **Reversibility**: High - each partnership negotiated independently

### Decision: Community-Based Sourcing Strategy
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen + Logan
- **Rationale**: 
  - Post magic links to "best communities, not just on Indeed or LinkedIn"
  - Specifically targeting specialized forums (FOE's mentioned)
  - Thesis: Can't distinguish candidates from resumes alone, need Careerspan's "harder to deceive mechanism for storing experiences"
- **Impact**: Differentiated sourcing approach accessing talent pools agencies can't reach
- **Category**: Tactical / Growth
- **Reversibility**: High - experimental, can iterate quickly
