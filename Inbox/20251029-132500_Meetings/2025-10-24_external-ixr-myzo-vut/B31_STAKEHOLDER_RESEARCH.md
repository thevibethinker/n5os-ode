### Insight 2: No-Code Workflow Tools Create "Workflow Integration Explosion" That Defeats Their Simplicity Promise

**Evidence:** "Workflows can exist in a vacuum, right? Like otherwise I just have to make both workflows and workflows that integrate with the first set of workflows, which is just a massive explosion of context and state... versus something like Zo, I don't have to hold that much context in my brain because its agentic processing is reliable enough"

**Why it matters:** Traditional automation tools (Zapier, Make) force users to manually manage workflow interdependencies, creating exponential complexity. Agentic systems with 1-2 hour time horizon reliability eliminate this by handling context management internally. This is the core differentiation for why AI personal computers beat workflow builders.
