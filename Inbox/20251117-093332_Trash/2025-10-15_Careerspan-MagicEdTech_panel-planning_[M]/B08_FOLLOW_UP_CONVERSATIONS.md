# Follow-Up Conversations: Recommended Path Forward

## Immediate Next Step: Scheduled Follow-Up Call (1-2 weeks)

### Purpose
Move from exploratory discovery to concrete pilot evaluation. Test whether conceptual interest converts to operational commitment.

### Recommended Structure

**Duration**: 30-45 minutes

**Attendees**: 
- Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan)
- Whitney Jones (MagicEdTech) 
- Optional: MagicEdTech talent/hiring lead (if Whitney wants internal alignment before committing time)

**Primary Objectives**:
1. Address the "hiring mode tension" Whitney raised (peace time vs. war time)
2. Validate which hiring scenarios at MagicEdTech would benefit most from Careerspan
3. Propose concrete pilot terms (scope, timeline, pricing)
4. Clarify implementation questions (ATS integration, onboarding, data)
5. Set clear next milestone (yes/no decision on pilot by date X)

### Conversation Outline

#### Section A: Validate Hiring Mode Fit (10 min)
**Vrijen to Whitney**: 
- "You mentioned MagicEdTech operates in high-growth mode. Can you walk me through a typical hiring cycle for you? Are there periods where you can be more planful, or is it always urgent?"
- "The weekly batch scan model we discussed—where do you think that fits in your workflow?"
- **Listen for**: Frequency of "peace time" hiring, predictability of hiring needs, realistic resource availability for proactive pipeline cultivation

#### Section B: Prioritize Initial Pilot Role(s) (10 min)
**Vrijen to Whitney**:
- "If we were to pilot for 3 months, which role(s) would give us the best chance to prove value? Analyst positions? Engineering? Product?"
- "What's the typical time-to-first-qualified-interview today? What would success look like—cutting that in half?"
- **Listen for**: Role selection, timeline expectations, success metrics, budget parameters

#### Section C: Proposal & Terms (10 min)
**Vrijen to Whitney**:
- Present pilot proposal:
  - **Duration**: 3-month pilot
  - **Scope**: [1-2 roles identified in Section B]
  - **Pricing**: $500/month/role (single role) or $2k/month unlimited (if multiple roles)
  - **Model**: Weekly batch scans + candidate tracking + direct introductions
  - **Commitment**: MagicEdTech reviews weekly results, provides feedback, decides on continuation at 90 days
- **Confirm**: Terms, timeline, any blockers or concerns

#### Section D: Implementation & Timeline (5 min)
**Vrijen to Whitney**:
- "If we move forward, when could we start? What does onboarding look like from your side?"
- "Are there ATS integrations or data requirements I should know about?"
- "Who would be the primary contact for candidate feedback and metrics?"
- **Listen for**: Availability, technical constraints, internal decision-making timeline

#### Section E: Clear Next Milestone (5 min)
**Vrijen to Whitney**:
- "Based on this conversation, what's your sense of moving forward? If this feels right, when would you want to make a decision?"
- "Is there anyone else at MagicEdTech who needs to be involved in the pilot decision?"
- **Close with**: Explicit date/timeframe for decision (e.g., "Let's plan to decide by [date]")
