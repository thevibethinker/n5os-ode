# Open Questions: MagicEdTech Engagement Path

## Strategic/Business Fit Questions

### 1. **Will MagicEdTech Engage in a Pilot or Trial?**
- **Status**: Unresolved
- **What Whitney said**: She expressed interest in the conceptual value (proactive pipelines, relationship cultivation) but presented operational tension: they operate in high-growth mode where reactive hiring dominates
- **What's unclear**: Whether this tension is surmountable or whether they'll stay trapped in reactive hiring
- **Why it matters**: If MagicEdTech is always in war-time hiring, Careerspan's model may not fit despite product-market conceptual fit

### 2. **What's the Priority Weight of Resume/Candidate Quality Issues at MagicEdTech?**
- **Status**: Not validated
- **Context**: Vrijen positioned Careerspan around broken ATSs, poor resume signal, and candidate fraud. Whitney agreed these are real problems but didn't confirm they're acute pain for MagicEdTech
- **Unanswered**: Is this a pain point for MagicEdTech specifically? Rank relative to other hiring bottlenecks (volume processing? speed? retention?)
- **Why it matters**: Determines product-market fit priority and sales narrative
