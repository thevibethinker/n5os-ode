# Decisions Made

## Strategic Decisions

### 1. **Pursue Partnership with Coral (Explicit)**
**Decision:** Explore multi-faceted collaboration with Coral across content, institutional, and potentially affiliate channels.

**Rationale:** 
- Demonstrated values alignment on authenticity and user-first approach
- Coral's access to 50K+ FIU students (largest HSI in US) represents institutional market opportunity
- Growing TikTok presence (500 followers, viral content validation) suggests scaling trajectory
- Genuine interest from both parties; mutual benefit clear

**Implications:**
- Allocate time from Logan to develop content collaboration pathway
- Prepare institutional sales package for FIU opportunity
- Structure affiliate/revenue-share model with clear terms

---

### 2. **Introduce Logan & Coral for Direct Collaboration (Explicit)**
**Decision:** Create group chat / direct introduction between Logan Currie (Careerspan co-founder, 5K TikTok followers) and Coral to explore content co-creation.

**Rationale:**
- Both are TikTok creators with career content focus
- Logan has proven audience growth; Coral has authentic community credibility
- Complementary strengths: Logan = growth/production skills; Coral = niche community expertise + Fix Your Feed distribution

**Implications:**
- Immediate handoff to coordinate; high touch required
- Potential joint content series on Latina women in tech, career confidence, etc.
- Could result in cross-promotion benefiting both platforms

---

### 3. **Position Careerspan as Replacement for Vmock at FIU (Explicit)**
**Decision:** Pursue institutional contract replacement at FIU through Coral's connection (Ruth, career director) with explicit positioning on cost savings (50% below Vmock) and superior student experience.

**Rationale:**
- Coral identified institutional gap: Vmock has contract but is widely considered poor product by advisors
- Careerspan already has precedent: Columbia using product, Granola in discussions
- FIU scale (50K students) makes this attractive Total Contract Value (TCV) opportunity
- Ruth is decision-maker; Coral has relationship credibility

**Implications:**
- Prepare institutional demo + case study collateral from Columbia
- Clarify SLA/support requirements for university customers (universities noted as "slow customers" but valuable for credibility)
- Price competitively: current positioning is 50% below Vmock; maintain this margin

---

### 4. **Hold Off on Aggressive Monetization Strategy for Coral Relationship (Implicit)**
**Decision:** Keep monetization discussions secondary; focus first on product fit, community value, and trust-building before introducing revenue-share or affiliate models.

**Rationale:**
- Coral is values-driven founder; premature monetization pitch could undermine credibility
- Decision to trial product first is deliberate gate
- Better to demonstrate value, get feedback, then propose win-win revenue model
- Aligns with Careerspan philosophy: build trust first, monetization follows

**Implications:**
- Frame trial period as genuine feedback opportunity, not sales funnel
- Have revenue-share framework ready, but present only after product validation
- Let Coral propose collaboration terms; Careerspan counter-proposes

---

### 5. **Provide Special Promo Code & Resources to Coral (Explicit)**
**Decision:** Give Coral exclusive promo code to share with Fix Your Feed community + prepare positioning language/resources for her to use independently.

**Rationale:**
- Lowers friction for Coral to recommend Careerspan to her audience
- Demonstrates trust; allows her to maintain control of messaging to community
- Creates trackable affiliate pathway without formal agreement yet
- Coral's endorsement carries more weight than direct Careerspan marketing

**Implications:**
- Prepare 1-page positioning language tailored to Coral's audience (Latina women, career confidence, Fix Your Feed integration)
- Create trackable promo code specific to Coral's channel
- Monitor usage to validate demand; inform formal partnership negotiation

---

### 6. **Defer Details on Fix Your Feed Integration (Implicit)**
**Decision:** Acknowledged interest in integrating Careerspan with Fix Your Feed but deferred specifics until after Coral trials Careerspan product.

**Rationale:**
- Premature technical discussion before Coral validates product quality
- Integration scope not defined; need to understand Careerspan product performance first
- Allows Coral time to think through her own roadmap priorities

**Implications:**
- Flag as follow-up conversation after trial period (suggest 2-3 week timeline)
- If integration discussions proceed, Careerspan technical team needs to understand Fix Your Feed architecture
