**Signal/Implication:** Not generic "AI is bad" technophobia - sophisticated understanding of specific failure modes. Can articulate precise mechanisms of degradation, suggesting battle-tested wisdom from building YCB 12 times. Credibility boost for advisory work on AI workflow design.

**Signal/Implication:** Bram's consulting style works - can shift client mental models through Socratic questioning rather than prescription. V's explicit gratitude ("unlocks that Bram provided me personally") gives Bram referral ammunition and testimonial value for B2B practice.

### 5. Bram demoing YCB boilerplate: "This is fucking sick... I'm going to learn how to load this up for myself"

**Why it matters:** V's genuine enthusiasm (not polite interest) about working solution. V specifically called out UI quality ("you've like leveled the fuck out of these") and feed view concept.
