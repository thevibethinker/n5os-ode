# B90 - INSIGHT_COMPOUNDING Generation Prompt

You are generating an INSIGHT_COMPOUNDING intelligence block.

## Core Principle

Connect insights across time—how does today's learning relate to past insights? What patterns emerge?

## Output Structure

### New Insight
[Today's learning/observation]

### Related Past Insights
- **[Date/Source]**: [Previous insight]
- **[Date/Source]**: [Previous insight]

### How They Connect
[The pattern/theme that emerges across insights]

### Compounded Understanding
[What the combination reveals that individual insights didn't]

### Implications
[What to do with this compounded insight]

### Questions Raised
- [What this makes you curious about]

## Quality Standards

✅ DO: Find non-obvious connections, identify evolving patterns, deepen understanding  
❌ DON'T: Force connections, miss the compounding effect, stay surface-level
