# Detailed Recap - Acme Corp Discovery Call

**Meeting Date**: October 28, 2025

**Duration**: 45 minutes  
**Type**: Discovery Call

---

## Overview

Initial discovery call with Acme Corp to discuss implementing Careerspan's career development program for their 150-person engineering team. Sarah Chen identified retention challenges stemming from inconsistent career development support. Discussion covered current state, proposed solution, pricing, and pilot approach.

---

## Current Situation

**Team Structure**:
- 150 engineers across multiple teams
- Managers are technically strong but lack career development frameworks
- Current approach is ad-hoc and inconsistent

**Current Career Development Approach**:
- Annual performance reviews with minimal career focus (~5 minutes)
- Some managers do quarterly check-ins, but no standardization
- No structured career development framework
- Managers don't have tools or training for career conversations

**Pain Points**:
- Retention issues linked to inadequate career support
- Managers struggling to provide meaningful guidance
- Inconsistent approach across teams
- Career development not prioritized in current processes

---

## Proposed Solution

**Careerspan Implementation**:
- Dedicated career coach for each engineer
- Monthly 1-hour coaching sessions focused on career strategy
- Coaches specialize in tech careers and career navigation
- Platform for tracking goals and progress

**Manager Integration**:
- Coaches complement managers, not replace them
- Managers receive conversation guides and frameworks
- Quarterly syncs between coaches and managers to align on development plans
- 3-hour manager training upfront
- Managers focus on day-to-day feedback while coaches handle strategic career development

**Time Commitment**:
- Engineers: 1 hour/month with coach
- Managers: Quarterly sync meetings + 3-hour upfront training

---

## Pricing & Commercial Discussion

- **Pricing**: $295 per person per month
- **Scope**: 150 engineers
- **Total**: ~$44,250/month
- **Includes**: Coaching, manager support, platform access
- **Budget Status**: Within Acme's budget per Sarah

---

## Proposed Pilot Approach

**Structure**:
- Start with 20-30 engineers (1-2 teams)
- 3-month pilot duration
- Measure impact during pilot
- Full rollout if results are positive

**Rationale**: De-risk investment, prove value before company-wide implementation
