## 1. Quick Start Checklist

### Day 1 Setup

- [ ] **Charge everything first**: G2 glasses, R1 ring, and charging case to 100%
- [ ] **Download the Even app** (iOS/Android) and create account
- [ ] **Pair G2 glasses first**, then R1 ring (order matters for stability)
- [ ] **Complete prescription calibration** if using Rx lenses
- [ ] **Run firmware updates** - check app for pending updates on both devices
- [ ] **Test basic gestures** before customizing

### Critical First-Week Settings

| Setting | Recommendation | Why |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| Keep phone unlocked | Yes (especially iOS) | Prevents Bluetooth disconnection |
| Keep Even app in foreground | Yes | iOS background limitations cause drops |
| Auto-brightness | Enable, then calibrate | May need manual override indoors |
| Silent Mode | Learn the gesture | Press & hold both temples 1s |

## 2. R1 Ring Mastery

### Proper Wear

```
CORRECT: Logo facing UP, TouchPad toward THUMB
```

- **Index finger is default** - try both hands to find your preference
- Many users report **left hand with thumb control** is more responsive
- The ring has no wear detection - it activates whether worn or not

### Core Gestures (R1)

| Gesture | Display Off | Display On | Reliability |
|---------|-------------|------------|-------------|
| **Single Tap** | - | Confirm / Toggle view | ⭐⭐⭐ High |
| **Double Tap** | Opens Dashboard | Go Back | ⭐⭐ Medium |
| **Press & Hold 1s** | Opens Menu | Opens Menu | ⭐⭐⭐ High |
| **Scroll Up/Down** | - | Navigate lists | ⭐⭐ Medium |
| **Swipe Left/Right** | - | Switch apps/tabs | ⭐⭐ Medium |

### R1 Pain Points & Solutions

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **R1 won't reconnect** | Place in charging pod - only way to restart |
| **Accidental Conversate activation** | No fix yet; community requesting menu reorder |
| **Wet hands disconnection** | Dry thoroughly; may need pod restart |
| **Input lag / multi-tap needed** | Practice deliberate, firm gestures |

> ⚠️ **Critical**: If your R1 disconnects and won't reconnect, the **only** solution is placing it in the charging pod. Community has requested a 5-tap restart gesture.

## 3. G2 Display Optimization (HAO 2.0)

### HAO 2.0 Specs

- **Display**: Micro LED, 640 x 350 resolution
- **FOV**: 27.5° (wider than G1's 20°)
- **Brightness**: Up to 1,200 nits
- **Passthrough**: 98% (nearly invisible when off)
- **Color**: Green monochrome

### Display Position Settings

| Position | Best For | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Near** | Reading, teleprompter | Easier focus on small text |
| **Mid** | General use, navigation | Balanced experience |
| **Far** | Driving, outdoor | Less intrusive, more peripheral |
| **Height: Bottom** | Desk work | Below natural eye level |
| **Height: Top** | Walking/standing | Higher in field of view |

### Brightness Optimization

```
INDOOR: Set to 40-60% (1200 nits is overkill)
OUTDOOR: Enable auto or max for navigation
NIGHT: Use yellow clip for reduced reflection
DAYTIME: Use photo-fusion clip to hide green line
```

### Auto-Brightness Calibration

Users report auto-brightness can be "too bright or too dark." To fix:
1. Open Even app
2. Find brightness setting (left of "Silent Mode" tile)
3. Switch to manual if auto isn't working well
4. Adjust based on your environment

## 5. Power User Workflows

### Professional Use Cases

1. **Sales Calls (Conversate)**
   - Keep phone unlocked and Even app in foreground
   - Use speaker phone for best voice pickup
   - Conversate provides real-time AI suggestions during discovery calls
   - Expect 30-90 second lag on responses

2. **Technical Presentations**
   - Conversate works well for robotics, SLAM, technical topics
   - AI cues reported as "quite accurate" during complex discussions
   - Use teleprompter mode for scripted content

3. **Meeting Captioning**
   - Live transcription for hard-of-hearing or note-taking
   - Transcripts saved for later review
   - Useful for language learning (review difficult vocabulary)

### Content Creation

4. **Teleprompter for Performers**
   - AI mode auto-scrolls at your speaking/singing pace
   - No manual swiping needed
   - Great for musicians, YouTubers, public speakers

5. **QuickNote for Creators**
   - Jot ideas mid-vlog without breaking flow
   - Voice-to-text for rapid capture

### Daily Life

6. **Walking Navigation**
   - Turn-by-turn directions (walking/cycling only, no driving yet)
   - Requires strong 5G/LTE connection (no offline maps)
   - May miss some trails in rural areas

7. **Discreet Notifications**
   - Calendar, email, text without unlocking phone
   - Avoids the "phone rabbit hole" of endless scrolling

## 6. Known Issues & Workarounds

### Critical Issues

| Issue | Severity | Workaround |
|-------|----------|------------|
| R1 won't reconnect | 🔴 High | Charging pod only solution |
| Navigation needs 5G | 🔴 High | Won't work in rural/offline areas |
| No driving directions | 🔴 High | Walking/cycling only (for now) |
| Conversate lag (up to 90s) | 🟡 Medium | Acceptable in meetings; post-meeting recaps are more accurate |
| Health data stuck at 0 | 🟡 Medium | Wait or contact support; iPhone users affected more |

### Minor Bugs

- **HUD pops up randomly**: Restart glasses to reset
- **Wear detection failure**: Reshape arms or reposition Bluetooth dongle
- **Temperature stuck in Celsius**: No fix yet; requested feature
- **iOS app must be foreground**: Drains phone battery

## 7. Battery Optimization

### Real-World Battery Life

| Device | Rated | Real-World |
|--------|-------|------------|
| G2 Glasses | ~2 days | ~80% after 10 hours moderate use |
| G2 Case | 7 full charges | ~14 total days |
| R1 Ring | 4-7 days | ~2 days with continuous use |

### Battery-Saving Strategies

1. **Use Silent Mode** when not actively using display
2. **Set brightness to 40-60%** indoors
3. **Minimize display timeout** (10s for notifications)
4. **Check health widget intermittently**, not continuously (drains R1)
5. **Keep case charged** - it's your portable power bank
