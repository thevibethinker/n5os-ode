# Varun Mayya

## Resources

### YouTube Video: Coca-Cola’s Secret That Will Save Your Career Post-AI
- **Link**: https://youtu.be/dcqXvnP7VTk?si=QNMbi8LEgPgzw9We
- **Description**: Varun Mayya discusses how AI commoditizes skills, using Coca-Cola as an analogy for building brand and trust to survive post-AI. Key points include becoming a manager/coordinator of outcomes rather than a task executor, differentiating through personal brand, and owning end-to-end results.
- **Summary**: In an AI-dominated world, technical skills become cheap and interchangeable; success comes from building trust, coordinating humans and AI, and articulating unique value through stories and outcomes.
- **Relation to Careerspan**: Aligns with Careerspan's thesis that "your story is your edge"—focus on owned outcomes and narratives to avoid commoditization, emphasizing brand over price competition.
