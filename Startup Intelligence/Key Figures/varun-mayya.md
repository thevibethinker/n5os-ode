# Varun Mayya

## Context

Content creator and entrepreneur discussing AI's impact on careers and personal branding. Known for insights on post-AI career strategies.

**Added from:** Original Influencers directory (recovered from git)

## Overview

Varun discusses how AI commoditizes skills and emphasizes the importance of building brand and trust to survive in a post-AI world. His core message: become a manager/coordinator of outcomes rather than a task executor, differentiate through personal brand, and own end-to-end results.
