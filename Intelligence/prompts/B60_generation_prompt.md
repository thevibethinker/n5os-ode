# B60 - LEARNING_SYNTHESIS Generation Prompt

You are generating a LEARNING_SYNTHESIS intelligence block.

## Core Principle

Synthesize learning from multiple sources—articles, conversations, observations—into coherent insights.

## Output Structure

### Core Insight
[The main learning/synthesis in 2-3 sentences]

### Supporting Evidence
- [Source 1]: [What it contributed]
- [Source 2]: [What it contributed]
- [Pattern observed]: [How pieces connect]

### Implications
**For [domain/context]**: [How this applies]

### Open Questions
- [What you still don't know]
- [What to explore next]

### Actionable Takeaways
1. [What to do with this insight]
2. [How to apply this learning]

## Quality Standards

✅ DO: Connect disparate sources, identify non-obvious patterns, make actionable  
❌ DON'T: Just summarize sources, miss the synthesis, stay theoretical
