# Company Optimization Ideas

This list serves as a repository for ideas related to optimizing operations at CareerSpan. Each idea includes a description, goal, and status for testing and implementation.

## Idea 1: User Research Sharing and Synthesis Platform

**Description:**  
Establish an ongoing session or platform where all user research conducted at CareerSpan is shared with team members. Enable individuals to select, customize, and build prompts to run against the research data, allowing synthesis of insights in their own words and cognitive framework. This addresses the issue where people distrust processed information from others but trust their own AI-generated interpretations more readily. Well-scoped, well-developed prompts can facilitate this, making it a long-term initiative.

**Goal:**  
Create a trusted mechanism for disseminating user research insights, enabling team members to process and synthesize data personally. Outputs can be used to set meeting agendas, gather collective thoughts, and convey ideas effectively, enhancing decision-making and collaboration.

**Status:**  
Proposed - Ready for testing.
