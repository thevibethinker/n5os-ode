## Partnership Overview

### The Vision

Pilot a community-driven virtual recruiter model where Careerspan delivers high-signal jobs to Remotely Good members, warm intros top candidates to employers, and shares placement revenue.

**Strategic Significance:**
- First micro-community partnership for Careerspan
- Validates Logan's thesis about untapped micro-community channel
- Mission-aligned test case (social impact focus on both sides)
- Replicable playbook for future community partnerships

## Scope of Partnership

### Phase 1: Pilot (Current)

**Timeline:** October - December 2025 (tentative)

**Core Services Careerspan Provides:**

1. **Job Distribution**
   - Push high-signal job opportunities to Remotely Good members
   - ~12 employers currently in pipeline
   - Roles curated for social impact / mission-driven fit

2. **Candidate Support**
   - Members access Careerspan platform (REMOTELYGOOD code)
   - AI-mediated coaching to build richer candidate profiles
   - "People and fit, not just keywords" matching

3. **Warm Introductions**
   - Top candidates get warm intros to employers
   - Careerspan vouches for quality based on platform insights

4. **Placement Support**
   - Full recruitment cycle management
   - Success tracking and reporting

**What Remotely Good Provides:**

1. **Community Access**
   - Distribute jobs/opportunities to members
   - Position Careerspan as Premium Perk

2. **Brand Endorsement**
   - Theresa's trust/recommendation carries weight
   - Community members more likely to engage

3. **Feedback Loop**
   - Real-world usage data
   - Product improvement insights
   - Pain point identification

4. **Case Study Material**
   - Success stories for both sides
   - Testimonials if partnership successful

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Activation Rate

**If:** Members don't actually use REMOTELYGOOD code  
**Why It Could Happen:** 
- Not actively job searching
- Don't see value proposition
- Positioning unclear

**Mitigation:**
- Clear communication from Theresa
- Onboarding support
- Quick wins highlighted
- Testimonials from early users

---

### Risk 2: Quality Mismatch

**If:** Jobs don't align with community expectations  
**Why It Could Happen:**
- Employer pipeline doesn't match "social impact" criteria
- Geographic mismatch (not all SF/remote)
- Seniority level mismatch

**Mitigation:**
- Tight curation on job selection
- Clear criteria defined with Theresa
- Feedback loop to adjust
- Under-promise, over-deliver

---

### Risk 3: Bandwidth Constraints

**If:** Partnership requires more effort than either side can sustain  
**Why It Could Happen:**
- Theresa managing many commitments
- Careerspan team small and busy
- Both dealing with founder burnout

**Mitigation:**
- Low-maintenance partnership design
- Clear roles and responsibilities
- Async communication preferred
- Quarterly check-ins sufficient

---

### Risk 4: No Placements During Pilot

**If:** Pilot ends with zero successful hires  
**Why It Could Happen:**
- Market conditions
- Small sample size
- Timing issues
- Product-market fit gaps

**Mitigation:**
- Set realistic expectations upfront
- Success defined beyond just placements
- Learn-first mentality
- Long pilot window (3+ months)
