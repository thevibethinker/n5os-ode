**On technical screening trade-offs:**
_"You could screen for something technical pretty easily, like put on a problem or a prompt. But also, it's kind of a lot to ask of someone with no guarantee of getting on or even talking to an actual person"_
- **Tension**: Want to filter technically but don't want to burden candidates too early
- **Careerspan advantage**: Candidates already invested in profile, so additional screening feels fair

**On passive talent:**
_"It's always worth a conversation, especially if the person is really sharp...cultivating gardens together"_
- **Philosophy**: Build relationships before need is urgent
