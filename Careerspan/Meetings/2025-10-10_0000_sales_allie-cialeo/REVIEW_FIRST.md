## ✅ What Was Agreed

### Careerspan Will Provide:
1. Weekly pre-vetted candidate packets (names, analysis, resumes)
2. Distribution of JDs in micro-communities (100-300 person groups)
3. Semantic matching (not just keyword-based)
4. Iterative refinement based on Greenlite's feedback
5. White-glove follow-up (get candidates to fill profile gaps if interested)

### Greenlite Commits:
1. 15 minutes per week to review candidates
2. Feedback on what's working/not working
3. $300 per candidate they want to meet
4. JDs + subjective context from Ed

### Why This Works:
- **Low effort**: 15 min/week vs hours of sourcing
- **Low risk**: Only pay for intros to candidates they like
- **High quality**: Pre-filtering before candidates reach Greenlite
- **Iterative**: Algorithm improves based on their feedback

## 🎯 Strategic Insights

### Careerspan's Differentiation
- **Coaching-first approach**: Candidates build deep profiles, not just resumes
- **Micro-community focus**: 100-300 person groups vs oversaturated networks
- **Gap identification**: System tells candidates to fill story gaps before matching
- **Passive talent engagement**: Creates ongoing relationship, not just active job seekers

### Market Dynamics
- Post-layoff environment surfacing weak performers
- "Referral is king" — network backlinks critical
- Experienced candidates interested in startups (market shift)
- Companies need better pre-filtering, not just more volume
