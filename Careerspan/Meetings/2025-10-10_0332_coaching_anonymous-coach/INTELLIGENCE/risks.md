# Risks identified

1. Founder burnout → product velocity drop
   - Severity: High
   - Mitigation: Immediate time off; delegate critical tasks; communicate plan to team.

2. Event scope creep without sponsor buy-in
   - Severity: Medium
   - Mitigation: Produce concise sponsor blurb and limit initial pilot scope to 15–20 founders.

3. Over-reliance on single warm intro
   - Severity: Medium
   - Mitigation: Build 2–3 parallel sponsor/venue pathways; use community pilots to show traction.

4. Integration failure post-retreat
   - Severity: Medium
   - Mitigation: Create a clear 7–14 day integration checklist and accountability nudges.

5. Visa constraint causing limited retreat options
   - Severity: Low–Medium
   - Mitigation: Prioritize domestic retreat / nature-based alternatives.
