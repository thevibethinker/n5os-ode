# User research notes (extracted signals)

Observed pain points:
- Burnout markers: reduced output, increased stress, difficulty prioritizing work.
- Time constraints: visas and travel limitations affect retreat choices.
- Community needs: founders want trusted, in-person touchpoints rather than broad webinars.

Behavioral signals:
- High openness to structured interventions (retreats, nature-based resets).
- Willingness to trade a short-term pause for longer-term performance gains.

Implications for product:
- Messaging should emphasize pragmatic ROI (restore productivity) not just wellness rhetoric.
- Community pilots should offer tangible near-term value (access to curated roles, events).
