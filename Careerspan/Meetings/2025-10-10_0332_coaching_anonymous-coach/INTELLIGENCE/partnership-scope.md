# Partnership scope — OtherShip founder meetup (draft)

Objective: Host a small, invite-only founder gathering (15–20) that combines founder wellness, networking, and product feedback.

Core components:
- Venue & logistics: intimate space (co-working or sponsor venue), light catering.
- Format: 90–120 minutes — short talks (10–15m), moderated discussion, structured networking.
- Sponsorship: sponsor covers venue + 20 discounted seats; sponsor mention in materials.
- Outcomes: pilot product access for community members; list of potential partners and sponsors.

Deliverables (for sponsor outreach):
- 1-page sponsor one-pager
- 2-paragraph sponsor blurb
- Attendee prospect list (10–20 founders)

Timeline (fast path):
- Confirm sponsor interest & venue (Due: 2025-10-14)
- Lock date and attendee invitations (Due: 2025-10-17)
