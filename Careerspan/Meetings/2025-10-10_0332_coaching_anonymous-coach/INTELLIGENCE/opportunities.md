# Opportunities (business & community)

1. Community pilot: Offer free Careerspan access to one or two high-quality communities
   - Benefit: Rapid product validation, acquisition of engaged supply-side candidates.

2. Founder "OtherShip" event as a product/brand accelerator
   - Benefit: Builds trust, creates sponsor-ready assets, and surfaces partners and early customers.

3. Sponsor partnerships for founder gatherings
   - Benefit: Subsidize events and provide co-marketing reach; opens B2B conversations.

4. Content & thought leadership (retreat learnings)
   - Benefit: Create content (post-event learnings, founder wellness series) to attract community and PR.

5. Freelance supply-side positioning
   - Benefit: Position Careerspan as infrastructure for high-caliber freelance/contract talent.
