## Info Layering System

| Layer | Encoding | Meaning |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **Bold text** | `**skill**` | Story-verified (highest confidence) |
| Regular text | `skill` | Resume/profile-backed |
| *Italic text* | `*skill*` | Inferred (lowest confidence) |
| Years in parens | `(4y)` | Time spent practicing this skill |
| Gradient bar | `████░░` | Importance × rarity (visual weight) |
| ▲/▼ | Arrow | Strength vs gap |

Bold IS the verification signal — no separate ✓✓ markers needed.

## Temporal Signals

| Signal | Source | Where Shown |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Total years exp | Resume | Header |
| IC vs Mgmt split | Role titles over time | Header: "6y IC · 2.5y Mgmt" |
| Years per skill | Role durations × skill use | Spikes: "**MLOps** (4y)" |
| Trajectory | Title progression | Header: "↗ IC→Lead" |
| Tenure pattern | Avg time at companies | Fit Check: "Watch For" |
| Recency | When skill last used | Dim if >3y ago |

## Section Reference

| Section | Page | Purpose | Content Source |
|---------|------|---------|----------------|
| Header | 1 | Who + temporal context | Resume + computed |
| At a Glance | 1 | Skill snapshot with confidence | Decomposer skills |
| Verdict | 1 | Take meeting Y/N + reasoning | Synthesis |
| **Fit Check** | 1 | Dealbreakers + preferences + risks | Candidate interviews |
| What to Probe | 1 | Gaps expanded with interview angle | Gaps + synthesis |
| Why Him | 1 | Narrative strengths (not skills) | Stories |
| Signal Strength | 1 | % story-verified | Decomposer |
| Behavioral Evidence | 2 | Quotes + patterns from stories | Stories verbatim |
| Your 30 Minutes | 2 | Interview questions linked to risks | Derived from gaps |
| If You Need... | 2 | Trade-off decision table | Synthesis |

---

## FIT CHECK Columns Explained

| Column | What It Contains | Source | Why It Matters |
|--------|------------------|--------|----------------|
| **DEALBREAKERS** | Hard stops—if true, don't take meeting | Candidate interview Q&A | Saves 30 min if known blocker |
| **HE WANTS** | Candidate's stated preferences/requirements | Candidate interview Q&A | Mutual fit (not just employer judging) |
| **WATCH FOR** | High-level risk flags (1-3 words each) | Synthesized from analysis | Quick scan before deep dive |

**Repetition budget:** "Watch For" CAN echo "What to Probe" at headline level. The difference:
- Watch For = 1-3 word flags (scanning)
- What to Probe = expanded context + interview guidance (action)

## Repetition Rules

| Content Type | Lives In | NOT Repeated In |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| Skill names + years | At a Glance spikes | Why Him, Questions |
| Verification signal | Bold text (everywhere) | No ✓✓ markers |
| Risk headlines | Watch For (Fit Check) | — |
| Risk detail + interview angle | What to Probe | — |
| Strengths as narrative | Why Him | At a Glance |
| Interview Qs | Your 30 Minutes | — |
| Trade-offs | If You Need... | — |

## Layering Examples

**Spike with full info density:**
```
▲ **MLOps 0→1** (4y)
  ████████████
```
- `▲` = strength
- `**bold**` = story-verified
- `(4y)` = years practicing
- `████████████` = importance × rarity

**Trade-off row with verification:**
```
A 0→1 platform builder    ✓  **4y doing exactly this**
```
- `✓` = yes verdict
- `**bold**` = story-verified (not just resume claim)

**Fit Check risk flag:**
```
2.5y avg tenure
```
- Headline only—detail lives in "What to Probe"

## Design Principles

1. **Risks first** — Trust-building (Alex feedback)
2. **Every element earns its space** — No decorative content
3. **Info layering over repetition** — Bold/italic/years encode inline
4. **Temporal signals are first-class** — Years matter everywhere
5. **Narrative vs skill separation** — Spikes = skills, columns = stories
6. **Forward references** — Page 2 questions arrow-link to Page 1 risks
7. **Uncertainty is explicit** — Italic + "unknown" label
8. **Mutual fit** — "He Wants" shows candidate preferences, not just employer judging
