## About

I build products that help people work smarter with AI.

Currently at Zo Computer, where I'm working on making powerful AI capabilities accessible to everyone—not just technical users. I care about user research, iterative development, and shipping things that actually solve problems.

Before Zo, I was at Stripe working on developer tools and helping companies integrate payment APIs. Before that, I helped enterprise SaaS companies at Gainsight build better customer success operations.

**What I'm good at:**
- Talking to users and finding patterns in what they actually need
- Working with engineers and designers to ship iteratively
- Balancing customer requests with product vision
- Turning ambiguous problems into concrete solutions

**What I'm learning:**
- Design craft (I'm not a designer, but I'm getting better at design thinking)
- How to build AI products that feel magical without being confusing
- When to slow down and make bigger bets vs. when to ship fast

**What I care about:**
- Products that solve real problems, not just impressive tech demos
- Teams where people challenge each other constructively
- Building things that make complex capabilities simple to use

Always happy to chat about product management, AI tools, or career transitions from engineering → product.
