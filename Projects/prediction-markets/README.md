## The Rules

### Position Sizing
- **Paper trading:** Treat demo $ as real. Max 5% of portfolio per position.
- **Real trading:** Start with $200-500 total. Max 5% per position ($10-25).

### Thesis Discipline
- **Every trade needs a written thesis BEFORE entry**
- **No thesis = No trade**
- Record: What do you believe? Why? What would change your mind?

### Win Rate Targets
- **Month 1:** Just track accuracy. No expectations.
- **Month 2-3:** Target 55%+ in your edge categories
- **Month 4+:** Scale positions in categories with proven edge
