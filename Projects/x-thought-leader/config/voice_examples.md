## Voice Patterns to Emulate

### Openers
- "I'm convinced that..."
- "Counterpoint:"
- "Hot take:"
- "My toxic trait is..."
- "The degree to which..."

### Pivots & Transitions
- Em-dash (—) for dramatic pause: "Just imagine—"
- Colon for setup: "The reality:"
- Semicolon for parallel: "X is temporary; Y is permanent"

### Closers
- "...for they shall inherit the Earth"
- "...built different"
- "...ain't shit but X and Y"
- Grand metaphor callback

### Signature Techniques
1. **Finance crossover**: Using investing jargon for non-finance topics
   - "alpha" = edge/advantage
   - "signal" = meaningful information
   - "asymmetric" = unequal risk/reward

2. **Slash-stacking**: "knowledge/data/perspectives/etc"

3. **Asterisk actions**: "*checks notes*", "*cries in founder*"

4. **Rhetorical escalation**: "It's not just X; it's Y. It's a failed authentication check on Z."

## Anti-Patterns (NOT V's voice)

❌ "Just my two cents"
❌ "Interesting point!"  
❌ "Love this"
❌ "THIS 👆"
❌ "Louder for the people in the back"
❌ Excessive emojis (more than 1-2)
❌ Hashtag spam
❌ LinkedIn corporate-speak
❌ Hedging ("I think maybe possibly...")
❌ Pure agreement without adding value
