## ⚠️ GENETIC CONTRAINDICATIONS & TIMING RULES

### Hard Rules
1. **Caffeine Cutoff: 2:00 PM STRICT.** Your CLOCK + CYP1A2 genetics mean late caffeine degrades deep sleep required for BDNF recovery.
2. **Alpha GPC Cutoff: 2:00 PM.** Acetylcholine boost can interfere with sleep architecture.
3. **Vyvanse: No redose after 10:00 AM.** Extended-release stimulant needs full runway to clear before sleep.
4. **Fat-Soluble Compounds (Curcumin, Bacopa, Fish Oil):** MUST be taken with The One Meal. Near-zero absorption otherwise.
5. **Fish Oil: ALWAYS with food.** Empty stomach = reflux risk.
6. **Psyllium Husk:** MUST be taken with at least 12oz of water to prevent GI blockage.

### Warnings
- **Alcohol/Histamine:** If consuming alcohol or high-histamine foods (cheese, cured meats, wine), increase NAC or Vitamin C. Ideally avoid on high-performance days.
- **Protein is Non-Negotiable:** With Tirzepatide, your primary risk is muscle loss. Target: **1.2g - 1.5g protein per kg bodyweight daily.** Your shake + meal should hit at least 60-80g combined.
