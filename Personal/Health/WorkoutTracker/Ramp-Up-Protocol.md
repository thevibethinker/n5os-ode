# 6-Week Training Ramp-Up Protocol
**Status:** Active
**Target:** Safe transition from "Cold" to "Jair's Phase 1"

## The Philosophy: "System Deployment"
We are treating your body like a production environment. We do not push major updates without testing in staging.
- **Weeks 1-2:** Staging / Smoke Tests (Neural Adaptation)
- **Weeks 3-4:** Load Testing (Tissue Tolerance)
- **Weeks 5-6:** Production Rollout (Full Capacity)

---

## Phase 1: Calibration (Weeks 1 & 2)
**Goal:** Wake up the nervous system. Establish the habit. **NO SORENESS allowed.**
**Rule:** If you are sore the next day, you went too hard.

| Schedule | Workout | Volume Cap | Intensity (RPE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tuesday** | Upper Body (Pick 5 exercises) | **1 Set Only** | 5/10 (Very Easy) |
| **Thursday** | Mobility / Stretching | 15 Mins | Relaxing |
| **Saturday** | Lower Body (Pick 5 exercises) | **1 Set Only** | 5/10 (Very Easy) |
| **Sunday** | Walk (Flat ground) | 30 Mins | Zone 1 |

*Note: It will feel "too easy." That is the point. Do not add more.*

---

## Phase 2: Capacity Building (Weeks 3 & 4)
**Goal:** Strengthen tendons and ligaments. Increase blood flow.
**Rule:** Focus on perfect form. Speed of movement should be slow and controlled.

| Schedule | Workout | Volume Cap | Intensity (RPE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monday** | Day 1: Upper Body Focus | **2 Sets** | 7/10 (Moderate) |
| **Wednesday** | Day 2: Lower Body Focus | **2 Sets** | 7/10 (Moderate) |
| **Friday** | Day 3: Core & Mobility | **2 Rounds** | 6/10 (Moderate) |
| **Sunday** | Hike (Easy terrain) | 60 Mins | Zone 2 |

---

## Phase 3: Production Ready (Weeks 5 & 6)
**Goal:** Hypertrophy (Muscle Growth) and Endurance.
**Rule:** You are now cleared to follow Jair's "Coach Notes" intensity.

| Schedule | Workout | Volume Cap | Intensity (RPE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monday** | Day 1: Upper Body Focus | **3 Sets** | 8/10 (Hard) |
| **Tuesday** | Day 2: Lower Body Focus | **3 Sets** | 8/10 (Hard) |
| **Thursday** | Day 4: Upper Body #2 | **3 Sets** | 8/10 (Hard) |
| **Saturday** | Day 7: Long Hike/Run | Full Duration | Endurance |

---

## Safety Checklist
- [ ] **Hydration:** 1/2 bodyweight in ounces of water daily.
- [ ] **Sleep:** 7+ hours is non-negotiable for recovery.
- [ ] **Pain vs. Discomfort:**
    - *Discomfort (Burning/Fatigue):* Good. Keep going.
    - *Pain (Sharp/Stabbing/Joints):* **STOP IMMEDIATELY.**
