## Archetype Definitions

### 1. The Engine Building (80%)
*   **Purpose:** Aerobic base, fat oxidation, recovery.
*   **Target HR:** 113 – 131 BPM.
*   **Duration:** 30–60 minutes.
*   **Feel:** Easy, conversational, "demeaning" (until it clicks).

### 2. The 20% Interval (20%)
*   **Purpose:** VO2 Max, power, race-pace comfort.
*   **Protocol:** 4x4 (4 mins HARD, 3 mins EASY).
*   **Target HR:** 151+ BPM during work sets.
*   **Feel:** Lungs burning, high focus.
