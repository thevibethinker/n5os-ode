# Supplement Interaction Notes

This file documents known interactions between supplements V takes or may consider. The Nutritionist persona should check this before recommending additions.

## Known Interactions

### Magnesium + Calcium
- **Interaction:** Compete for absorption
- **Guidance:** Take at different times (2+ hours apart)
- **Note:** V takes Magnesium at night; if adding Calcium, take in morning

### Iron + Calcium
- **Interaction:** Calcium significantly reduces iron absorption
- **Guidance:** Never take together; separate by 4+ hours
- **Note:** V doesn't currently supplement iron

### Zinc + Copper
- **Interaction:** High-dose zinc (>40mg) depletes copper over time
- **Guidance:** If supplementing zinc long-term, consider copper balance
- **Note:** Monitor for copper deficiency signs (fatigue, pale skin)

### Vitamin D + Vitamin K2
- **Interaction:** Synergistic — D increases calcium absorption, K2 directs it to bones
- **Guidance:** Consider pairing D3 with K2 (MK-7)
- **Note:** V's genetic profile shows normal Vitamin D metabolism

### B12 + Folate
- **Interaction:** High folate can mask B12 deficiency
- **Guidance:** If supplementing folate, ensure B12 is adequate
- **Note:** V's MTHFR status may affect folate metabolism — check labs

### Caffeine + Magnesium
- **Interaction:** Caffeine increases magnesium excretion
- **Guidance:** If high caffeine intake, may need higher magnesium
- **Note:** V is intermediate caffeine metabolizer — moderate concern

## Timing Guidelines

| Supplement | Best Time | With Food? | Notes |
|------------|-----------|------------|-------|
| Magnesium | Evening | Either | Promotes relaxation |
| Vitamin D | Morning | With fat | Fat-soluble |
| B vitamins | Morning | Either | Can be energizing |
| Fish Oil | With meals | Yes | Better absorption |
| Probiotics | Morning | Empty stomach | Varies by strain |
| Iron | Morning | Empty stomach | If ever needed |

## Red Flags to Monitor

- **Adding multiple new supplements at once** — Can't identify what's working
- **Exceeding safe upper limits** — Check cumulative intake from food + supplements
- **Contradicting lab-verified levels** — Don't supplement what's already optimal

## V-Specific Considerations

Based on genetic profile:
- **COMT GG:** May be sensitive to methyl donors (B12, folate) — start low
- **Lactose intolerant genotype:** Avoid dairy-based supplement delivery
- **Intermediate caffeine metabolizer:** Caffeine timing affects sleep quality
