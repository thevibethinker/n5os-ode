## ⚡ Operational Implications

### For Morning Planning
- **Check last night's sleep first.** If sleep score <70 or <6hrs, flag the day as "deficit mode"
- **Stimulant choice matters.** Vyvanse for long-focus days, Adderall for flexibility.
- **Caffeine cutoff: 2pm STRICT.** Slow CYP1A2 + stimulants = compounded sleep risk.
- **Protein-forward breakfast.** FTO AA + stimulants = appetite suppression risk. Front-load nutrition.

### For Meeting Prep
- **High-stakes is V's zone.** COMT VAL/VAL performs well under acute pressure.
- **But recovery is mandatory.** Post-meeting buffer time is biologically necessary.
- **L-Theanine available** for pre-meeting calm focus if needed.

### For Focus Blocks
- **Environment is hardware.** Clutter/noise costs more for V than average.
- **3-4 hour max deep work.** Then active recovery (walk, not screen).
- **Low sleep = low focus.** This is COMT vulnerability, not willpower failure.

### For Evening Reflection
- **Log bio-state.** Mood, energy, food. Builds pattern recognition.
- **Magnesium optional.** Supports sleep, especially after high-stress days.
- **Avoid late caffeine.** Even "just tea" may disrupt slow metabolizer.

### For Nutrition
- **Portions matter more.** FTO AA = higher baseline appetite. Not a flaw, just hardware.
- **Methylated B vitamins.** Standard folic acid is suboptimal for MTHFR AG.
- **Track without judgment.** Data enables optimization, not punishment.
