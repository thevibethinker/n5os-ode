## Why Not Just Tutorials?

Tutorials teach tools. Tools change every 6 months. The Vibe Pill teaches the meta-layer:

- **Design patterns** that work across any AI platform
- **Architectural thinking** — how systems compose, when to automate, what to keep human
- **Technical primitives** — the mental models that let you understand how AI works, not just use it
- **The meta OS approach** — V teaches heuristics for building AI operating systems, not recipes

**"If you have the technical primitives, you can understand and build no matter what happens next."**

## Session Structure (45 minutes)

**Phase 1: "How I Built This" (10 min)**

- V demonstrates a real system built that week — live, not slides
- Shows before/after, the thinking behind it, the design patterns used

**Phase 2: "Let's Build" (20 min)**

- Group builds together using Zo
- Start with the problem, architect the solution, implement live
- Every participant leaves with a working system

**Phase 3: "Your Turn" (15 min)**

- Apply the pattern to your own context
- Group helps adapt — peer learning
- This is where the real value compounds
