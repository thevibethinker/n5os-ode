# GTM Methodology: Chris Pisarski's YC-Proven Framework

**Source:** Chris Pisarski (YC mentor, GTM expert)  
**Context:** Repeated advice at YC events  
**Authority:** High - founder-tested framework with documented results

## Core GTM Playbook

### Lead Generation & Targeting
1. **Build qualified lead lists** using tools like Crustdata or Apollo
2. **Prioritize warm connections** via LinkedIn Sales Nav (2-sentence personalized outreach)
   - Leverage common touchpoints (university, shared connections, etc.)

### Email Outreach (Outbound)
2. **Minimize AI in email copy** - aim for maximum conciseness and specificity
   - Avoid generic, AI-generated templates
3. **Iterate on deliverability** with daily optimization cycles
   - Make emails progressively "punchier"
   - Measure and adjust based on performance metrics

### Channel Strategy
4. **LinkedIn as content layer** - post daily to build presence and credibility
5. **SEO as long-term foundation** - invest in organic visibility

## Resource Allocation
- **Spend 80% of effort** on the above fundamentals
- **Expected outcome:** Breakthrough in demo generation for those with discipline

## Key Principle
> "Every founder who followed this advice with enough discipline saw results."

**Implication:** Consistency and adherence to fundamentals outweighs fancy tactics.
