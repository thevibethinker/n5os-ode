# Left Lane Capital

## Overview

**Industry:** Venture Capital / Growth Equity
**Founded:** 2019
**Size:** ~44 (Aviato) / ~58 (reported)
**HQ:** New York, NY (Brooklyn)
**Other Offices:** London

Left Lane is a leading global VC and growth equity firm investing in high-growth internet and consumer technology companies. Founded by Harley Miller (Managing Partner & CEO) with a team that previously led early-stage investing at Insight Partners. The firm focuses on internet-first businesses with strong customer retention, writing $3–$30M checks across Seed to Series C.
