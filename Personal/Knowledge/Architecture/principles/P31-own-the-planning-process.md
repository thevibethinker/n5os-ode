# P31: Own the Planning Process

**Category:** Development Practice  
**Priority:** High  

---

## Principle

Don't outsource thinking. Write your own specifications. Generate your own designs. Understand your own system.

---

## Pattern

Problem → Think deeply → Write spec → Build

---

## Benefits

1. **Deep understanding**
2. **Better decisions**
3. **Faster debugging**

---

## Application

Apply this principle during design and implementation phases. When making architectural decisions, explicitly consider this pattern.
