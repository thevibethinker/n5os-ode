## Core Identity

## Business Model Structure

### Two-Sided Marketplace

**Supply Side (B2B2C Distribution):**
- Partner with alumni associations & professional communities
- Provide FREE AI career coaching to members
- Capture behavioral + longitudinal career data through coaching
- Live partnerships: Columbia, UMass
- Pipeline: HBS, Cornell

**Demand Side (Two Employer Models):**

**Model 1: Database Access**
- Employers scan existing candidate database
- Access pre-qualified, behaviorally-profiled candidates

**Model 2: Magic Link ATS Alternative**
- Employer generates magic link for applicants
- Applicants apply BY using Careerspan
- Pro-candidate: every applicant gets coached
- Employer gets behavioral data + coached candidates

**Pricing:** 00-1K per hire (vs. 0-30K traditional recruiters)

## Value Propositions

### For Candidates
- Free career coaching with sustained value
- Becomes "professional memory repository"
- Reason to keep returning (unlike LinkedIn/Indeed)
- Result: 50%+ multi-session, 20% D30 retention (2x industry)

### For Employers
- Access pre-validated candidates with behavioral profiles
- Behavioral data predicts retention and performance
- Pro-candidate process attracts better talent
- Low-cost vs. traditional recruiting

### For Partners
- Add career services without building infrastructure
- Member engagement and retention benefit
- Alumni/member success attribution

## Positioning Context

### What We're NOT
- NOT a job board (not optimizing for application volume)
- NOT a recruiting platform (not selling sourcing tools)
- NOT a coaching marketplace (not connecting to human coaches)
- NOT an ATS (not managing employer hiring workflow)

### What We ARE
Candidate-first infrastructure that generates employer value as byproduct.

The "Trojan horse" model: Candidates come for coaching, stay for value, generate data employers pay for.
