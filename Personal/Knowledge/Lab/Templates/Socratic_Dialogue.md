# Exploration Module: Socratic Dialogue

## Objective
To peel back the layers of an idea through recursive inquiry. Not to "solve" it, but to expose the underlying assumptions and causal structure.

## The Method
1. **The Frame:** What are we actually trying to learn?
2. **The Definitions:** What does each key word mean?
3. **The "Why?" Phase:** Explore the motivations and causality.
4. **The "Counter-Example" Phase:** Where does this break?
5. **The Synthesis:** What did we learn? What changed?
