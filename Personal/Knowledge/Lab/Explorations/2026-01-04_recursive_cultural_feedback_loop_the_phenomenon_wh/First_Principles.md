# Exploration Module: First Principles Decomposition

## Objective
To break the idea down to its atomic, non-negotiable truths and rebuild it from the ground up.

## The Method
1. **The Atoms:** What are the physical or logical constraints that cannot be changed?
2. **The Layers:** What are the layers of abstraction built on top of these atoms?
3. **The Re-Assembly:** If we didn't know the current "best practice," how would we solve this starting from the atoms?
