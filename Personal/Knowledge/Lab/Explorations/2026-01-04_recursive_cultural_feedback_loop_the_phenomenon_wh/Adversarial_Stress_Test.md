# Exploration Module: Adversarial Stress Test

## Objective
To act as the "Devil's Advocate." This module seeks to destroy the idea, identify the "trap doors," and expose the cost of being wrong.

## The Method
1. **The Pre-Mortem:** Assume the idea has failed. Why did it fail?
2. **The Incentive Audit:** Who benefits if this is wrong? Who loses if it's right?
3. **The Complexity Tax:** What is the cognitive or operational cost of maintaining this idea?
4. **The False Consensus Check:** What are the most common valid criticisms of this position?
