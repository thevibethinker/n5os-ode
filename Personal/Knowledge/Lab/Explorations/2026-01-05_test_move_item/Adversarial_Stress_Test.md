# Exploration Module: Adversarial Stress Test

## Objective
To act as the "Devil's Advocate." This module seeks to destroy the idea, identify the "trap doors," and surface non-obvious failure modes.

## The Method
1. **The Steelman:** Restate the idea in its strongest possible form.
2. **The Attack:** What are the 3 most plausible ways this fails?
3. **The Evidence Gap:** What evidence would change our minds?
4. **The Re-Frame:** How might we adjust the claim to survive reality?
