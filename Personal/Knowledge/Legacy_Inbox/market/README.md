# Market Intelligence

**Purpose:** Broader industry trends, market data, competitive landscape, and external context that informs positioning, proposals, and strategic decisions.

**vs. Company Knowledge:** This is EXTERNAL intelligence about the market/industry, not internal Careerspan data.

**vs. Semi-Stable:** Semi-stable = our current state. Market = industry/competitive state.

---

## What Lives Here

### Industry Data
- Market size and growth rates
- TAM/SAM/SOM calculations
- Industry trends and shifts
- Regulatory/policy changes
- Economic factors affecting hiring

### Competitive Intelligence
- Competitor landscape
- Feature comparisons
- Pricing benchmarks
- Competitive positioning
- Recent competitor moves/funding

### Technology Trends
- AI/ML developments in recruiting
- Platform trends (job boards, ATS, etc.)
- Emerging technologies
- Adoption rates and patterns

### Buyer/Customer Intelligence
- Hiring manager pain points
- Recruiting team challenges
- Community leader needs
- Budget/spending patterns
- Decision-making processes

### Talent Market
- Job seeker behaviors
- Career development trends
- Skills gaps and demands
- Remote work impact
- Demographic shifts

## Organization by Topic

Files are organized by major topic areas. Within each file, information is structured with:
- **Data points** (with sources and dates)

## Usage

**For Investor Pitches:**
- Lead with TAM/market size from `recruiting_industry.md`
- Reference trends supporting your thesis from relevant files
- Show competitive differentiation using `job_boards_ats.md`

**For Sales Proposals:**
- Reference buyer pain points from `buyer_personas.md`
- Show ROI using industry benchmarks
- Position against competitors using competitive intel

**For Partnership Discussions:**
- Reference `community_driven_hiring.md` trends
- Show market opportunity they're part of
- Use data to validate partnership thesis

**For Strategic Planning:**
- Identify white space opportunities
- Track competitive threats
- Spot emerging trends to address

## Data Sourcing

**Always include:**
- Source (report name, publication, URL)
- Date (when data was published/collected)
- Confidence (verified, estimated, anecdotal)

**Example:**
```markdown
- Recruiting software market: $3.2B in 2024, projected $5.1B by 2028 (17% CAGR)
  - Source: Gartner HR Tech Report 2024
  - Date: March 2024
  - Confidence: High (analyst report)
```
