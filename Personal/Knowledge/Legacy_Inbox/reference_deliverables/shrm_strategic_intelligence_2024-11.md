**Careerspan is a two-sided AI-powered talent marketplace** building quality-of-hire prediction infrastructure through longitudinal behavioral data captured via high-retention career coaching product.

## Market Problem & Competitive Landscape {#market-problem}

### The Core Market Problem (V's Framing)

**Problem 1: AI Broke ATS Screening**
> "AI-generated resumes broke ATS screening—anyone can now pass keyword filters regardless of fit."

**Source:** Conversation con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW, Q9 problem statement, timestamp 2025-11-03 22:48 ET, V's exact wording

**Problem 2: Resume Was Always Flawed**
> "The resume was always a poor predictor of retention and performance, but now it's actively counterproductive: employers waste resources interviewing candidates who look perfect on paper but are fundamentally unsuitable."

**Source:** Conversation con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW, Q9, V's exact wording

**Problem 3: Incumbent Structural Barriers**
> "The unit economics of LLMs do not allow for the current high-volume, low-signal approach to candidate filtering, ensuring that incumbents are not incentivized to lead the change the market so desperately needs."

**Source:** Conversation con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW, Q9, V's exact wording

**Why This Matters:**
- High-volume AI screening is prohibitively expensive at LLM costs
- Incumbents (LinkedIn, Indeed, Greenhouse, Lever) can't fix this
- Market needs **low-volume, high-signal** approaches
- Careerspan's model (engagement data) is economically viable where theirs isn't

**Source:** Strategic synthesis from Q9 development, Conversation con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW

### Supporting Market Data

**From TA Talent Acquisition Trade Association Research:**

1. **39% of employers can't find candidates with right skills** (vs. only 17% report lack of applicants)
   - Translation: Volume isn't the problem; signal quality is

2. **56% of TA tech purchases require CEO/COO approval**
   - Translation: High-stakes decision, need strong ROI case

3. **Employers overwhelmed by applicant volume** (2-3x more screening calls than previous cycles due to AI-generated applications)
   - Translation: AI made the problem worse, not better

**Source:** file 'Research from TA Talent Acquisition Trade Association.txt', provided by V in conversation con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW
