# Glossary

## AI-mediated career companion
Careerspan's core product offering: an AI-powered platform that guides users through coaching-style conversations to capture comprehensive professional profiles (skills, values, mindset, stories) for improved opportunity matching and career development.

## Career Agent wedge
Careerspan's employer go-to-market strategy: structured progression from free pre-vetted candidate shortlists → pay-per-intro when companies want to meet candidates → subscription model for always-replenished curated pipeline of elite talent, initially focused on Product roles.

## Truths → Lenses → Interpretations → Decisions pipeline
Careerspan's data processing framework: captures source-backed facts/events/stories, applies contextual frames of reference (Source/Target role perspectives), generates reasoned judgments with confidence scores, and provides "fit" query answers with full traceability.

Aliases: T-L-I-D pipeline, T-L-I-D framework

## AISS semantic optimization
Action-Impact-Scale-Skill framework for intelligent resume and document rewriting that improves clarity and ATS alignment through evidence-based optimization without gaming algorithms or superficial keyword matching.

Aliases: AISS framework

## 3D résumé
Careerspan's dynamic profile concept: comprehensive representation of a person's professional capabilities beyond traditional 2D resume format, incorporating skills, values, mindset, and narrative stories for richer talent representation.

## B2B2C integrations
Business-to-business-to-consumer partnership model where Careerspan embeds reflective interview capabilities and guided coaching conversations into partner platforms and workflows.

## Coach-first design
Fundamental product philosophy prioritizing authentic, Socratic coaching conversations over algorithmic optimization, informed by founders' decade of coaching/education experience and emphasizing verifiable, primary-source data collection.

## Candidate-first, evidence-first
Core mission philosophy: the hiring crisis stems from informational problems (resume-centric, shallow signals) rather than algorithmic limitations, solved through rich qualitative data gathered via coaching-style conversations.

## Human data layer
Careerspan's vision of becoming the authoritative qualitative data layer that the market consults when talent meets opportunity, providing richer, more authentic talent intelligence beyond traditional resume filters.

## Multi-agent quality control
Technical foundation using multiple AI agents (input processors, evaluation agents, directors, extractors, QC) implementing structured coaching frameworks with multi-stage verification to minimize hallucinations and ensure data quality.

## Career Agent
AI-assisted talent broker representing knowledge workers, connecting candidates to employers proactively, flipping from reactive applications to curated introductions.

## Opportunity Matching Hypothesis
Core strategy positing that candidate-job fit should evaluate qualities like mindset, values, and potential, not just keywords or credentials, enabling better matching with qualitative data.

## Invisible Résumé
Dynamic, continuously updating profile that captures evolving career insights, stories, and self-assessments for predictive talent evaluation.

## Wedge Strategy
Phased go-to-market approach for enterprise entry: seed talent pool, engage target employers, deliver free value, then monetize via intros and subscriptions.
