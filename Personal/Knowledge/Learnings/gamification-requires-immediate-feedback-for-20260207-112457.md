# Gamification requires immediate feedback for ADHD brains - instant dopamine hits...

Gamification requires immediate feedback for ADHD brains - instant dopamine hits (growing trees, happy animations) work while delayed rewards (long streaks, leaderboards) fail. Design for immediate gratification.
