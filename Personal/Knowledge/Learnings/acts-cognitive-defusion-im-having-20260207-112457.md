# ACT's cognitive defusion ('I'm having the thought that...') is more effective fo...

ACT's cognitive defusion ('I'm having the thought that...') is more effective for ADHD emotional dysregulation than CBT-style cognitive challenging. Create distance from thoughts rather than arguing with them.
