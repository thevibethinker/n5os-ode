# B21: Key Moments & Turning Points

- Frame: Playful, casual reconnection between peers
- Implication: Relationship has history; this is celebration not business-only

## Inflection Point 1: Registry Latency Reality (00:52)
**Alex:** "If you have more than 100,000 domains in kind of a premium, there just isn't like a straight latency issue."
- Reveals: Technical constraints discovered through real operations
- Implication: Ola discovered hard limits through integration failures
- Shifted decision-making from theoretical ("premium domains") to practical ("keep it fast")
- This explains the entire top-20k strategy

## Inflection Point 2: Logan's Premium Domain Story (01:57)
**Logan:** "Maybe that's what. Not a surprise. I don't remember. I don't remember if I must have searched for it. It was either not available or it was astronomically expensive."
- Reveals: Personal user journey validates Alex's strategy
- Pricing barrier worked (prevented purchase); Logan tried and abandoned
- Later enabled via gift discount—shows retention/loyalty opportunity
- Real-world proof of premium domain economics
