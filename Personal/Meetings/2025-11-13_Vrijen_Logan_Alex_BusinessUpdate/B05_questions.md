# B05: Strategic Questions & Tensions

## Key Strategic Questions Raised

### 1. What is Careerspan's Definition of Success?
**Raised by:** Alex Garnepudi  
**Context:** Strategic fork between $2M, $5M, and $100M+ ARR models  
**Underlying Assumption:** Different company models (solopreneur job boards at $500k/year viable; but scaling to $100M requires different execution)  
**Status:** Not definitively answered; Logan acknowledged it's "a good question"

### 2. Has Careerspan's Success Definition Changed Since June?
**Raised by:** Alex Garnepudi  
**Context:** Last conversation was 5 months ago; company pivoting from B2C to B2B2C model  
**Implication:** May need new KPIs to track progress  
**Status:** Partially addressed (pivot is clear; new metrics less clear)

### 3. What Are the B2B Success Metrics Beyond Time-to-Hire?
**Raised by:** Logan (implicitly)  
**Context:** Industry standard metrics (time-to-hire, pipeline quality) don't capture full value prop  
**Tension:** How to measure when also targeting ATS replacement and recruitment fee displacement  
**Status:** Acknowledged but not resolved

### 4. How Can Careerspan Differentiate in "1% Success Rate" Job Market?
**Raised by:** Alex Garnepudi  
**Context:** Observed that even MBAs have <1% individual success rate applying to jobs  
**Implication:** Careerspan's differentiator can't be "better credentials"; must be structural  
**Logan's Response:** Fourth door model (bypass ATS) + 360 candidate positioning  
**Status:** Framework exists but execution metrics TBD

### 5. What Does Careerspan's 3-Year Survival Mean for Next Phase?
**Raised by:** Alex Garnepudi  
**Context:** Most startups die at 18 months; they've survived 3 years  
**Implication:** Different questions at this stage (not "will we survive" but "what scale do we want")  
**Status:** Acknowledged as milestone; strategic pivot in progress as answer

### 6. Is Ola/Hello.cv Discovering Genuine Product-Market Fit?
**Raised by:** Logan (curious)  
**Context:** 25k profiles built, 700k views/month; but unclear use cases  
**Alex's Response:** Early adopter behavior; real insights at 150k profile threshold  
**Tension:** Building features (job aggregation) before understanding user intent  
**Status:** Acknowledged uncertainty; treating as normal early-stage pattern

### 7. Can Job Matching + Registry Business Model Scale Together?
**Raised by:** Implicit in Alex's explanation  
**Context:** Ola is registry + job aggregator; latency/infrastructure constraints exist  
**Tension:** Adding job matching features while managing registry technical operations  
**Status:** Being managed; technical challenge acknowledged

### 8. What Should Careerspan's Go-To-Market Look Like in 2026?
**Raised by:** Implicit in Logan's marketing campaign comment  
**Context:** Haven't done "really any marketing"; now revving up campaigns  
**Implication:** First time testing paid acquisition; unclear unit economics  
**Status:** In planning phase; execution starting
