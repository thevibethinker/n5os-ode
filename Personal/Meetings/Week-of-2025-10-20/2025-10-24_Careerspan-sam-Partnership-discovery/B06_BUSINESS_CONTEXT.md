# B06: Business Context

## Market Positioning
Careerspan is positioning itself as a "low-cost, high-value" community sequencer. By providing automated career coaching, they capture deep data that traditional recruiters or job boards cannot access. They are shifting away from being a pure "career tech tool" for individuals and moving toward being a "sourcing infrastructure" for agencies and platforms like sam.

## Competitive Advantage
- **Data Depth:** 40+ minutes of conversation data per candidate vs. a 1-page static resume.
- **Semantic Evaluation:** Moving beyond keyword matching to "attribute analysis" and "vibe" detection.
- **Distribution:** Locking down specific high-prestige alumni networks (Cornell, Emory, McKinsey).

## Strategic Pivot
The meeting reflects a critical strategic pivot for Careerspan: moving away from the "VC game" (high-growth, dual-sided marketplace) toward a more specialized, partnership-driven model or an eventual exit via acquisition.
