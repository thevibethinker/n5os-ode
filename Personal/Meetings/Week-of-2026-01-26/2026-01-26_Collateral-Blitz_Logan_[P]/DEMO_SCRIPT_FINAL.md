## Voiceover Script

Every talent platform runs on the same scraped, stale data.

What if they were instead fueled by data candidates actually wanted to share?

Detailed insights into how they think, how they work, and how they relate to others.

That's Careerspan. Let me show you.

***\[INTO DEMO — show employer portal with job loaded\]***

You drop in a job. Within hours, you see who fits — with context a resume could never give you.

*\[Click into Donna's profile\]*

Rating and dealbreakers upfront. Then the full picture: elevator pitch, actionable analyses of strengths and weaknesses.

This is real candidate data. First-party. Opted-in. Legal. 

Here's how we do it

***\[Quick cut: candidate interface — 5 seconds\]***

We use a coaching-based modality to ask probing questions that stimulate recall and spark self-insight.

Users can talk. They can type. Hell, they can speak in 50 different languages if they want and get English back.

Why make them go through the effort?

***\[Back to employer view — Donna vs Donnatella side by side\]***

Same person. One acccoount only submitted a resume. One shared stories as well.

Look at the gulf in coverage scores. 

That 30 points reflects how much more of the underlying role requirements are captured by the account with stories. 

Candidates get that too; that's why 45% of our activated users share three or more stories. They come back because it works for them.

***\[Beat\]***

We know the next moat in hiring isn't more data. It's better data — data that's only been shared with you. 

It's that data that will keep talent coming back and the ability to rapidly test against that pool that will bring in employers. 

That's Careerspan. The Human Data Company for the Future of Hiring.
