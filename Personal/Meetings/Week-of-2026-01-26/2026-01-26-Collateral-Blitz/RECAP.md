# RECAP: Collateral Blitz Strategy Session

## Executive Summary
A high-stakes strategy session between **Vrijen** and **Logan** centered on a radical pivot in Careerspan's outreach methodology. Prompted by feedback that current materials are "too verbose" and "laborious," the team decided to shift to a **video-first strategy** using short (<60s) Loom demos. The core hook leverages the **Eightfold lawsuit** and the industry shift toward **first-party opted-in data** as a competitive moat.

## Key Decisions
- **Video Over Decks:** Replace text-heavy one-pagers with 45-60 second Loom walkthroughs showing the product in action.
- **CEO-Led:** Vrijen will record the demos personally to maximize founder-to-founder credibility ("the man of it all").
- **Strategic Hook:** Use the Eightfold lawsuit as a forcing function for companies relying on toxic third-party scraped data.
- **Segmentation:** Outreach is divided into two tracks: **Sourcing/Matching** (recruiting focus) and **Internal Mobility** (enterprise focus).

## The "Video-First" Blueprint
1. **The Scan:** Show the employer view—dropping a JD into the tool.
2. **The Mechanism:** Briefly mention Careerspan's unique first-party data capture and "bullshit detector."
3. **The Reveal:** Show real, anonymized results comparing profiles with and without narrative stories (Donna vs. Donatella).
4. **The CTA:** Direct outreach for a conversation based on the immediate visual proof of value.

  - [ ] Record the Loom demo showing real scan results.

## Strategic Impact
This pivot represents a shift from *explaining* Careerspan to *demonstrating* its outcome. By focusing on the "Hydraulic Flow" of first-party data, the team is positioning Careerspan not as a vendor, but as the infrastructure for the next era of talent intelligence.
