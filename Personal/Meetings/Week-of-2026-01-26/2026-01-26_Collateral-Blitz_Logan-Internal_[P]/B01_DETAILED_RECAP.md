# Detailed Recap: Collateral Blitz

**Date:** January 26, 2026  
**Duration:** 30 minutes  
**Participants:** Vrijen Attawar, Logan Currie

---

## Executive Summary

V and Logan conducted a focused "collateral blitz" session to develop outreach materials for Careerspan's sales pipeline. The core strategy centers on creating a 45-60 second demo video showcasing Careerspan's first-party human data advantage, supported by targeted email messaging for two distinct buyer segments.

## Key Decisions

1. **Asset Priority:** 45-60 second demo video is the lead asset
2. **Positioning:** Careerspan as "human data company" (not AI company, not recruiting tool)
3. **Segment Priority:** Sourcing/matching video first, internal mobility second
4. **Target Prioritization:** Reliance and Uber for internal mobility outreach
5. **Approach:** Lead with proof (real anonymized scan data) rather than claims
