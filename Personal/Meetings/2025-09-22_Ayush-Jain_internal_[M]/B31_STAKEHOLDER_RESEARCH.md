# B31 - STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

**Perspective:** Speaking as product-oriented team member in early-stage career tech startup

---

## Landscape Insights

### 1. **Job Sourcing Requires Manual Human Validation (Can't Automate Fully)**

**Observation:** Ayush expressed willingness to take on validating jobs, but Vrijen noted even API-driven job pulling requires manual work—can't just "vibe code" an API solution that checks job legitimacy

**Implication for Careerspan:** 
- Job quality is a differentiator (users trust Careerspan recommendations)
- But achieving quality at scale requires human judgment
- This is likely to remain a bottleneck as Careerspan grows

**Strategic Value:** 
- Suggests Careerspan needs either (a) more team bandwidth, (b) outsourced validation partners, or (c) ML-driven legitimacy scoring before manual review
- Early-stage constraint that becomes a scaling constraint

---

### 2. **Niche Focus (Product Manager Roles) is Defensible**

**Observation:** Vrijen explicitly chose PM roles as Careerspan's focus because "there's just enough subjectivity there"—hiring managers struggle more with PM evaluations than with, say, engineering roles

**Implication for Careerspan:**
- PM hiring is messy; traditional recruiting methods aren't great for it
- Careerspan's value prop works best in categories where traditional hiring underperforms

**Strategic Value:**
- Validates focusing narrowly before going broad
- Suggests expansion to similar "subjective hiring" categories (product design, go-to-market roles) might have similar PMF

---

### 3. **Third-Party Recruiting & Founders Are Primary Sales Channels**

**Observation:** Vrijen planning to reach out to founders, talent leaders, and third-party recruiting agencies—not just hiring managers directly

**Implication for Careerspan:**
- B2B2C model (serve recruiting partners who serve talent)
- Recruiting agencies may be easier to partner with than individual companies
- Founder networks are a starting point for credibility

**Strategic Value:**
- Shifts sales motion from cold outreach to relationship-based (smaller number of high-LTV accounts)
- Recruiting firms could become major distribution channel

---

### 4. **Operational Burden Falls on Small Team (Delegation Challenge)**

**Observation:** Vrijen acknowledged "we're bad delegators" but also expressed eagerness to offload to Ayush once capacity is clarified

**Implication for Careerspan:**
- Founder bottleneck is real; can't scale operations without trusting others with core functions
- Onboarding people to "quality judgment" (job validation) is critical

**Strategic Value:**
- This is a founder developmental moment—learning to delegate is a prerequisite to scaling
- Ayush's success (or struggle) here will signal whether Careerspan can hire operational people

---

### 5. **Pitch Event & Fundraising Push This Week**

**Observation:** Vrijen mentioned pitch event "this Thursday" and desire to "close some deals and place some folks" to accelerate revenue

**Implication for Careerspan:**
- Fundraising is active; suggests some investor interest or momentum
- Revenue placement (actually placing candidates with employers) is the immediate goal
- Time pressure is real (weekly operations, pitch prep, GTM execution)

**Strategic Value:**
- Suggests next 1-2 months are critical for demonstrating traction
- If Ayush can unblock job sourcing, it frees Vrijen for GTM/fundraising
- "Placing folks" = revenue; that's the milestone to hit
