# B01: Detailed Recap

## Overview
Vrijen and Logan held an impromptu internal strategy session to discuss Vrijen's latest advancements with "Zoe" (his AI automation protocol), reflections on AI's impact on the labor market (inspired by a Harvard lecture Logan attended), and fundraising/revenue strategy for Careerspan.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Zoe AI Protocol Demonstration
- **Automation Progress:** Vrijen showcased his latest workflow where a single command processes meeting transcripts to generate action items, deliverables, and summaries using GPT-5 Mini.
- **Fail-safe Mechanisms:** He implemented a "resume" rule to handle AI timeouts, ensuring the agent picks up where it left off.
- **Inter-Agent Coordination:** Vrijen noted a fascinating interaction between "Howie" and "Zoe" where the two AI agents negotiated rules for coordinating his schedule.

### 2. Market Macro-Trends: "Automatable Payroll"
- **Jeff Busking Lecture:** Logan shared insights from a lecture at Harvard about the 10X founder and "automatable payroll."
- **Labor vs. Software:** Logan highlighted a trend where enterprises are targeting labor budgets (payroll) rather than just software budgets, aiming to replace human roles with agents.
- **Social Impact:** Vrijen characterized the current AI wave as a new "Industrial Revolution" and argued that LLMs currently favor those who can "reason linguistically" and wield the tools effectively, rather than being truly democratizing.

### 3. Fundraising and Revenue Strategy
- **Go Ahead Ventures:** Vrijen proposed applying to Go Ahead Ventures, noting their good deck, powerful consumer numbers, and prominent advisors.
- **Fundraising Sources:** Discussion included reaching out to Rob (investor), New York State Ventures, and original investors to cobble together a bridge round.
- **The "Leaky Bucket" Concern:** Logan raised a strategic concern about the responsibility of taking more capital without a clear, proven revenue stream, referencing the "money into a leaky bucket" analogy.

### 4. Operations & Applications
- **WorkTech SHRM:** Logan shared feedback on a call with a WorkTech SHRM representative; they decided to apply for the program despite previous lack of responsiveness from the organization.
- **Advisor Rollout:** Plans to roll out advisors like Shajat and Rajesh soon to gain more visibility.
