# B01: Detailed Recap – Vrijen Attawar & Paula McMahon

## Executive Summary
Vrijen Attawar (V) met with Paula McMahon, a lead operator at the AI Collective (NYC), to explore synergy between Careerspan, the AI Collective, and Zo. The conversation moved from personal rapport (New York winters, climate) to deep strategic alignment on the future of "Applied AI" in New York vs. the Bay Area. Paula expressed strong interest in Careerspan's unique approach to "extracting human potential" via Socratic AI rather than just generating resumes.

## Narrative Flow

### 1. Introduction & Personal Rapport
*   Discussion on the arrival of "New York winter" and a shared preference for cold over heat.
*   Brief mention of climate policy (V's partner's field) and the "Dead Internet Theory."

### 2. Paula's Role & AI Collective Strategy
*   Paula is helping scale global operations for the AI Collective while managing the New York chapter.
*   **New York Thesis:** New York is focused on "Applied AI" and vertical innovation (healthcare, supply chain, etc.) rather than the "frontier lab" obsession of the Bay Area.
*   Upcoming events: New York Demo Night and various industry-specific vertical innovation showcases.

### 3. Careerspan Origin & Philosophy
*   V shared the Careerspan story: rooted in Singapore college admissions coaching and Singapore narrative design.
*   **Core Thesis:** Resumes are the wrong starting point. The real problem is the "intrinsic gap in people’s ability to examine and advocate for themselves."
*   Paula resonated with the "Socratic" nature of the tool, noting that she recently spent weeks manually "editing her soul" into Notion for her own career transition.

### 4. Strategic Collaboration Paths
*   **Programming:** V proposed career-related programming for AI Collective members focused on sophisticated AI career leverage.
*   **Zo Evangelism:** V introduced Zo as a "computer in the cloud" that replaces Notion/ChatGPT, suggesting it as a tool for the Collective's community.
*   **Information Exchange:** Mutual agreement to share articles and "hot takes" on the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

### 5. Industry Trends & Vision
*   Discussion on the "paradox of productivity" (AI making us feel more overloaded rather than free).
*   Paula noted the shift toward agents and MCP (Model Context Protocol) as the focus for the next 6-12 months.
*   V emphasized that AI is not powerful enough to replace human potential, but serves as a "miracle" for turning unstructured instructions into output.

## Conclusion
The meeting concluded with a commitment from V to send follow-up materials regarding Careerspan and Zo, and an open invitation for Paula to review his recent writing on Built In.
