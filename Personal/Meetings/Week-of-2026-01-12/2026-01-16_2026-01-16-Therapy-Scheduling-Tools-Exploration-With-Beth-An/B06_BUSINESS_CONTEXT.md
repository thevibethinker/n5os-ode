## Market Context: Therapist Scheduling

### Industry Characteristics
- Many solo practitioners
- High privacy requirements (HIPAA)
- Personal relationship is core to service
- Mix of tech-savvy and tech-resistant practitioners

### Key Requirements
1. **HIPAA Compliance** - Video, scheduling, and notes
2. **Calendar Integration** - Must work with existing email/calendar
3. **Privacy Controls** - Initials/codes instead of full names
4. **Personal Touch** - Clients value direct access

### Competitive Landscape
- Generic scheduling (Calendly, Acuity) - need HIPAA features
- Practice management suites - comprehensive but heavy
- Manual processes - still common due to privacy comfort
