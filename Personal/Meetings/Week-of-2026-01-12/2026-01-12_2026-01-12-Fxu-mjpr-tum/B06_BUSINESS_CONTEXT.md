## Business Models Discussed

### Advisory + Consulting Hybrid (David's approach)
- **Problem being solved:** Pure advisors leave companies unable to execute; pure consultants grind without strategy
- **Solution:** Advise (David) + partner with execution firms (Flow)
- **Referenced:** Wes Bush as example of advice-only consultant

### SaaS Subscription for Job Seekers
- **Concept:** Subscribe to job search tool until landing a job
- **Advantage:** Recurring revenue during active job search
- **Challenge:** Auth and data privacy concerns

### GitHub/Open Distribution
- **Concept:** Provide repo, users download and run in their own Zo instance
- **Advantage:** Avoids auth/data handling complexity
- **Disadvantage:** Not a subscription; one-time value transfer
- **V's note:** This is what Zo would likely support
