## Market Intelligence: Coach Persona

### Key Needs
1. **Client-centric views** - See all sessions for one person
2. **Session history** - Quick lookup during sessions
3. **Conversion tracking** - Especially for organizations
4. **Integration simplicity** - Zapier is too hard
5. **Sales resistance** - Many coaches uncomfortable with "selling"

### Tech Adoption Patterns
- Will struggle through complex setup if needed
- But prefers "just works" solutions
- Often assumes problems are their fault (even when they're not)
- Values training and support

### Sales Dynamics
- Coaches with sales training: comfortable
- Coaches without: "ick" factor
- Organizations working to address this gap
