## Market Context: Coaching Platforms

### Key Workflow Needs
1. **Client History View** - See all sessions with one person
2. **In-Session Lookup** - Quick access during meetings
3. **Session Counting** - Track packages, verify usage
4. **No-Show Tracking** - Document for accountability
5. **Conversion Analytics** - Funnel performance by coach

### Sales Funnel Reality
```
Signup → Amy (screener) → Coach → Conversion
         ↓ drop            ↓ drop     ↓ sign up
```
- Organizations actively working to improve conversion
- Some coaches uncomfortable with sales
- Training helps but mindset is barrier

### CRM vs. Scheduler Overlap
- Conversion tracking traditionally CRM function
- But many coaches don't have robust CRM
- Opportunity for schedulers to absorb this
