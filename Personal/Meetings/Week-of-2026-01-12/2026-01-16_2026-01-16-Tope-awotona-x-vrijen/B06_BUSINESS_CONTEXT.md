# B06: Business Context

## Calendly (Tope's Company)

### Company Overview
- **Product:** Scheduling platform (link-based scheduling)
- **Strategic Direction:** Expanding into relationship management platform
- **Stage:** Mature, market-leading position in scheduling
- **Funding Status:** Well-capitalized (previously raised significant venture capital)

### Current Initiatives Mentioned
1. **AI Note-Taker** - Gradually rolling out, not fully API-accessible yet
2. **Agentic Scheduling** - Building Howie-like product for non-link-based scheduling
3. **Relationship Management** - Expansion beyond pure scheduling

### Strategic Concerns
- **Existential threat:** Agent-based interviewing could reduce 15-20% of meeting volume
- **Market shift:** Link-based scheduling will persist, but agentic scheduling represents growth opportunity
- **Competitive pressure:** Howie and similar products capturing market Calendly doesn't currently serve

### Competitive Intelligence Gathered
- Calendly sees itself as needing to build in agentic scheduling to survive
- Interview scheduling is substantial revenue driver (15-20% of volume)
- Relationship management positioning suggests pivot toward CRM-adjacent space
