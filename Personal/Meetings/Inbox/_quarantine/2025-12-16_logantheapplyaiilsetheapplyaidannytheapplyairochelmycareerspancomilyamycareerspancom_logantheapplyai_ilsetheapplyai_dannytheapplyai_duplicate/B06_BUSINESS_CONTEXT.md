# B06: Business Context

The company is at a critical juncture where they are seeing employer interest but facing friction in candidate conversion.

## Strategic Shift
There is a consensus to move away from being a general "career coaching" or "resume killer" tool toward being a high-signal application platform for specific roles (Direct Apply). This is driven by the need to show immediate value to employers (applicants) and to streamline the product before potentially running out of "dev gas."

## Market Intelligence
- **Aviato (Conrad):** Exploring LinkedIn signals for hiring beyond standard banners. This data could inform internal product improvements or be sold as a novel signal to others (e.g., Dow Jones).
- **Competitor/Sector Trends:** Mention of companies like "Blue Sapphire" and others focusing on sourcing. Careerspan is seeing more momentum in non-sourcing related areas.

## Traction
- Multiple jobs are live on the board.
- Employers (e.g., Skillcapped) are asking for analytics (clicks vs. applications), signaling a shift to the "next phase" of customer needs.
