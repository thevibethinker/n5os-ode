# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Privacy as a Feature:** Ilse noted that their methodology works well for performance reviews and promotion tracking, but only if users feel their data is private.
- **The "Internal" Pivot:** Could the core technology be sold as an internal performance review / attrition prediction tool? (e.g., determining if people are likely to leave based on their "vibe check" equivalents).
- **Correcting the Understanding of Friction:** V's point that "your understanding of friction is wrong" to employers—employers often *want* friction to filter candidates, but it needs to be the *right* kind of friction (data-gathering vs. ergonomic hurdles).
- **Cadence as Signal:** Using the frequency and behavior of LinkedIn profile updates/posts as a leading indicator for "open to hire" before the candidate even knows it.
