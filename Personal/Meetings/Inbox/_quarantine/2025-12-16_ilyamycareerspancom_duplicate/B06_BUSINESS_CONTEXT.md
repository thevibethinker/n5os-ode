# B06: Business Context

## Market Intelligence: Teamwork Online
Teamwork Online is a dominant ATS in the American sports industry, used by most professional teams for back-office and front-office hiring. They possess a massive database (8 million candidates) but suffer from "shallow data." They currently monetize an MVP feature that allows candidates to pay to reach the "top of the stack."

## Careerspan Value Proposition
The central hypothesis is that "everything is going to be sourcing." Careerspan offers "100x visibility" and "deep data" that can transform a static ATS pool into a high-engagement marketplace. This is positioned as a defensive and offensive move for incumbent ATS players facing disruption from job boards.

## Valuation Target
V mentioned that Careerspan is "valuable in the order of at least a few million dollars" and that breaking the M mark would allow for returning capital to investors.
