# B01: Detailed Recap

Vrijen Attawar (V) and Ilya Kucherenko (Ilya) met to prepare for V's upcoming meeting with Davis Philippel, President of Teamwork Online. 

## Preparation for Teamwork Online Meeting
V outlined the opportunity with Teamwork Online, an ATS primarily serving American sports teams with a database of 8 million candidates. V plans to propose a partnership where Careerspan's deep data technology could transform their shallow candidate pool into a more valuable, dual-sided marketplace. V views this as a "meaningful" upgrade to Teamwork Online's existing MVP functionality that charges users to move to the top of the stack.

## Strategic Positioning
V and Ilya discussed positioning Careerspan as the tool for cultivating long-term, exclusive relationships with talent, emphasizing that "everything is going to be sourcing." They touched on how Teamwork Online, despite its current market dominance in sports, faces disruption from job boards and needs Careerspan to stay resilient. Ilya reinforced the value of Careerspan's "100x visibility" and "Careerspan difference" in candidate quality.

## Sales Philosophy and Mentorship
Ilya shared professional advice with V, emphasizing that the primary goal of early conversations is "selling the conversation" rather than closing a deal. He stressed the importance of sincerity, honesty, and leveraging the "Founder and CEO" title as a position of authority and trust.
