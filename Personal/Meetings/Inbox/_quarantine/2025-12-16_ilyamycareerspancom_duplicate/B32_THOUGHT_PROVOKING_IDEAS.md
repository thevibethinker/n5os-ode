# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The Sourcing Hypothesis**: Is "everything" truly going to be sourcing? If sourcing becomes the primary battleground, does the ATS become a commodity?
- **Relationship Cultivation vs. Candidate Stacking**: Careerspan proposes moving from candidate "stacking" (MVP logic) to relationship "cultivating" (Careerspan logic). 
- **The Founder/CEO as a Trust Mechanism**: Ilya's observation that the "CEO Card" is the highest in the deck because it bypasses the "salesman bias."
- **Data Depth over Breadth**: The hypothesis that deep data on 10% of a pool is more valuable than shallow data on 100% of it.
