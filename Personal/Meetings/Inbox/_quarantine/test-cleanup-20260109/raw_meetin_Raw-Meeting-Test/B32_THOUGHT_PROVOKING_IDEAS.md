## Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **Culture as the Operating System:** If culture is the OS of a company, then "output-centricity" is a bug that causes "innovation crashes."
- **Outcome vs. Output:** Shifting from measuring "how much we build" to "what we changed" requires a fundamental change in the definition of a "Senior" role.
- **Friction as Signal:** The idea that reducing friction (as seen in the 20% completion rate increase) actually *improves* signal by allowing the right candidates to finish the process.
