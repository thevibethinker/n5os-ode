## Raw Blurb (Type 2)

Careerspan delivers personalized career coaching at scale by combining AI-powered reflection with proven coaching frameworks. We help candidates articulate their unique professional value and match with opportunities where they’ll thrive—optimizing for match quality rather than just volume.

Our platform uses a multi-agent AI system to guide users through structured reflection, identifying strengths and accomplishments that are often overlooked. This narrative data is then translated into high-impact résumés and interview responses. Built on 20+ years of coaching expertise, Careerspan turns messy career transitions into structured, actionable frameworks.

We’ve seen significant impact with our thousands of users: 88% learned a new way to talk about themselves, and 97% felt more optimistic about their prospects after a session. By providing a "professional memory repository" that gets smarter over time, we’re building the career companion everyone deserves.
