**Subject:** Careerspan | Quality-of-Hire Prediction Infrastructure
**Type:** Email Blurb (150-250 words)
**Target:** VC Network (Post-PMF, $3M-$4M ARR focus)

### Raw Blurb

Careerspan provides quality-of-hire prediction infrastructure that turns messy candidate data into structured insights. We've built an AI-powered coaching platform that guides candidates through reflection—capturing ~200 discrete data points per person—to generate "why say yes/no" analysis that actually means something.

The problem: 99% of resume data is unusable, and AI can't fix it without richer extraction. We solve this by having candidates articulate their professional stories through guided Q&A, then mapping those narratives to specific role competencies. The result is a signal-rich profile that helps employers and VCs identify high-agency talent without wasting time on screening.

We've validated this approach with partners like Marvin Ventures (McKinsey alumni fund) and are in discussion with Emory's alumni network. Our methodology—built on 20+ years of coaching experience—helps 88% of users learn a new way to talk about themselves and 97% feel more optimistic after using the platform. We're pre-seed ($1M raised Q1 2024) and focused on scaling this two-sided marketplace where both candidates and employers win.

I'd love to connect with investors who understand the infrastructure play in hiring. Deck available on request.
