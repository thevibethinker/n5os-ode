A server is a computer that's always on, always connected, and always listening. Unlike your laptop (which shuts down), a server stays active 24/7. Think of it as a personal assistant that lives in the cloud, waiting for instructions—running automations while you sleep, storing files you can access anywhere, hosting websites that anyone can visit. Zo gives you your own server without the IT degree.
