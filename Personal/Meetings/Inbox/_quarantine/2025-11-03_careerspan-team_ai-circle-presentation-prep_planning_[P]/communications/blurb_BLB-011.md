# Zo: Your Personal AI Computer

Zo gives you a cloud server that runs continuously and thinks on your behalf. Instead of asking AI to help with tasks (like ChatGPT), your Zo instance *manages your workflows*—processing meetings, organizing information, running automations, building custom tools—all without manual setup.

**Why it matters:** Most AI tools let you chat or automate simple tasks. Zo lets you build a whole operating system—one that integrates with everything you use and stays loyal to you, not a company.

**The 30-second pitch:** It's AWS for normal people, powered by AI that actually understands your work.
