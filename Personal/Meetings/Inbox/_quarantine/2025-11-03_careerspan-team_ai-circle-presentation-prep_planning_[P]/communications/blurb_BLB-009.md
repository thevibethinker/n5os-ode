# Zo vs. ChatGPT/Claude: Agenticity Over Context

**ChatGPT and Claude are conversational.** They're brilliant at answering questions, but each conversation is isolated. You're starting fresh—they forget what happened yesterday, they only respond when you ask, and they can't actually change anything in your digital life.

**Zo is agentive and persistent.** It remembers your patterns, runs workflows continuously, takes action on your behalf, and learns from outcomes. Instead of "I need to ask AI to do this," it becomes "AI is already managing this."

**The gap:** ChatGPT excels at thought partnership and brainstorming. Zo excels at becoming part of your operating system. They complement each other—one is a thinking partner, the other is your digital agent.
