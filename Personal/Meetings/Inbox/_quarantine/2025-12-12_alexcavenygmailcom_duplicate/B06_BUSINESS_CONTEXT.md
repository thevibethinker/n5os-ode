# B06: Business Context & Implications

## Market Opportunity
The recruitment market is currently bifurcated between "blind appliers" (jaded users applying to everything) and those looking for a "more serious or better" process. Careerspan targets the latter by provocative users to tell stories, reflecting "intrinsic agency."
- **Size/Growth:** V noted a recent uptick in users starting and finishing stories (e.g., 7 finished today).
- **Customer Needs:** Founders need to cut through the "hundreds of resumes" to find the "seven people" worth reviewing.

## Competitive Position
Careerspan differentiates itself from "stodgy HR platforms" by focusing on human qualities and "authenticity" rather than just keywords or "Palantir style data hellscapes."
- **Differentiation:** Leading with the founder's authentic, grounded personality vs. the "aggressive tech bro" or "faceless enterprise platform."
- **Advantages:** Low screening-to-hire ratio (potentially reviewing only 7-10 people instead of 500).

## Strategic Direction
The strategy is shifting toward a more "problem-first" sales approach ("Selling the hole, not the shovel").
- **Goals:** Validate that people will actually tell stories to apply (Early validation achieved).
- **Approach:** Building in public, selling in public, and leveraging V's empathy as a brand asset.
- **Rationale:** Technical founders often have "ass communication skills," so V's social skill is a high-alpha differentiator.

## Commercial Model
The commercial model involves paying per job listing (some listings are currently free to build liquidity).
- **Pricing Considerations:** Not discussed in detail, but referral bonus structure was previously established (50% of first 2 months).
- **Cost Structure:** The "100 man-hour" cost of traditional hiring is the primary "hole" being sold against.

## Implications & Next Steps
- **Business Impact:** Successfully validating story completion allows Careerspan to move from "assumption" to "proof point."
- **Strategic Decisions Needed:** Finalizing the website restructure to better reflect the two-sided marketplace.
- **Next Action:** Logan to integrate "Show what you've got / Ask for what you want" messaging.
