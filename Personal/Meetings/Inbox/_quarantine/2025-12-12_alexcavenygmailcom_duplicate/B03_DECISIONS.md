# B03: Decisions Made

## Decision 1: High-Level Brand Framing

**DECISION:** Careerspan's core messaging will be structured around two parallel pillars:
- **For Candidates:** "Show what you've really got" (Beyond the resume).
- **For Employers:** "Ask for what you really want" (Qualities over keywords).
**CONTEXT:** Simplifies the two-sided marketplace value proposition into high-impact, symmetrical taglines that avoid the "Palantir data hellscape" vibe.
**DECIDED BY:** Vrijen Attawar (approved Alex's suggested framing).
**IMPLICATIONS:** These phrases will be communicated to Logan for potential integration into the website redesign.
**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Generic recruiting platform language (rejected as "boring" and "cringe").

## Decision 2: Sales Process Pivot: Immediate Locking

**DECISION:** V will shift from the "pleasant conversation + LinkedIn follow-up" model to "locking in" the next meeting during the initial interaction.
**CONTEXT:** Based on Alex's workshop insight that LinkedIn messages/emails are oversaturated and momentum is lost once the face-to-face or call ends.
**DECIDED BY:** Vrijen Attawar (following Alex's recommendation).
**IMPLICATIONS:** V will need to be "a little more obnoxious" (assertive) about scheduling on the spot.
**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Maintaining the current courteous but slower follow-up process.

## Decision 3: Strategic Differentiation through Authenticity

**DECISION:** V will double down on "selling/building in public" and leading with empathy as a differentiator.
**CONTEXT:** Alex highlighted that V's grounded and unassuming nature makes the "gems" of advice pop more and creates a unique brand identity compared to "tech bros" or "stodgy HR platforms."
**DECIDED BY:** Vrijen Attawar.
**IMPLICATIONS:** V will be more visible in talking about his deep understanding of the hiring "pain" and personal commitment to solving it.
**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Adopting a more "hoity toity" or "serious" persona to appear more capable (rejected as inauthentic).
