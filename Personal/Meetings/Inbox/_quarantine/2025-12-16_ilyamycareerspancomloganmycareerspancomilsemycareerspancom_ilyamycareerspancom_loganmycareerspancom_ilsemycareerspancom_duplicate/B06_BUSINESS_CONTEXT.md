# B06: Business Context

The company is currently in an opportunistic M&A / partnership exploration phase. They are evaluating several tech companies across the recruiting/HR tech stack:
- **Enterprise HRIS:** Darwin Box (High value, enterprise focus).
- **Job Seeker Tools:** Teal (Strategic alignment, cash-poor).
- **Sourcing/ATS:** Teamwork Online, Ellie AI, Clara.
- **Allies:** Superposition.

The core strategy is to demonstrate data value through partnership and pivot to acquisition discussions. There is an emphasis on "precision hiring"—breaking job descriptions into granular criteria and judging candidates based on specific evidence, which is seen as a key differentiator for large-scale enterprise hiring.
