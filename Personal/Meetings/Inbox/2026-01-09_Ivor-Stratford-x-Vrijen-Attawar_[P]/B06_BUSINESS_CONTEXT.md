## Market Dynamics Discussed

### Hiring/Recruiting Market
- **V's thesis:** ATS systems create fundamental limits on talent insight
- **Problem articulated:** "99% crap for 1% signal" in resume scanning
- **AI impact:** Even the 1% could be fake now; companies won't invest in true AI for low-signal input
- **Video interview problem:** Gets "flattened into summary" → equally useless

### Talent Market Power Shift
- **V's macro view:** Shrinking worker pool → power consolidation
- **Future state:** "99 people with no options and one person with 100 options"
- **Implication:** Companies need to attract/lock talent earlier (football academy model)

### College-to-Career Pipeline
- **Current state:** Job boards, shotgun applications
- **Ivor's vision:** Career journal from graduation to retirement
- **Time horizon:** 3-6 years for users to become "fans and advocates"
- **Business model tension:** Requires years to build; startups need shorter-term revenue
