# B06: Business Context

## Company: Careerspan

### Current Status
- Early-stage startup in talent/recruiting space
- Actively iterating on product-market fit
- Working through cash runway concerns ("We might run out of money before we can get sold doing it")
- Has actual users engaging with the product

### Value Proposition (Refined This Call)
**Core:** Talent scanning/discovery  
**Positioning:** "What we are is talent scanning, and that's literally the only thing that matters."

### Product Components
1. **Silent Scan:** Discovers candidates matching role requirements without candidate-facing touchpoint
2. **Employer Portal:** Provides employers/recruiters with candidate intelligence (vibe checks, strengths/weaknesses)
3. **Candidate Experience:** Coaching help + job board (being deemphasized in new positioning)
4. **Pre-list CSV:** Trust demonstration for recruiters showing quality of matched candidates

### Key Metrics
- **Response rates:** Primary proxy metric for demonstrating value to recruiters
- **User engagement:** People are actually using the product, which validates direction

## Market Dynamics

### Recruiter Landscape
- **Trust issue:** Recruiters don't trust AI and have been burned by software
- **High-end recruiters:** Placing $300K-$500K+ roles; main pain is getting candidates to respond
- **Mid-tier recruiters:** Need differentiation; all candidates look the same on paper
- **Information efficiency problem:** Market has gotten so efficient that traditional signals are commoditized

### The "Quiet Part" Problem
Employers have criteria they can't legally state (e.g., "I want someone who will work until midnight"). This creates a gap between stated job requirements and actual selection criteria that Careerspan could potentially address through silent scanning.
