# B03: Decisions Made

## Strategic Decisions

### D1: Talent Scanning as Core Value Proposition
**Decision:** Careerspan's primary value is talent scanning/discovery. All other features ladder up to this.

**Rationale:** After multiple customer conversations and pivots, the distilled insight is: "What we are is talent scanning, and that's literally the only thing that matters."

**Impact:** This reframes product priorities, marketing messaging, and technical roadmap.

**Decided by:** V (with Ilse's acknowledgment that this is now the go-forward direction)

---

### D2: Applicants Must Actively Click Submit
**Decision:** Applicants are not auto-submitted to roles. They must explicitly click submit after seeing the opportunity.

**Rationale:** Creates a "strong indication of them actually being interested" - validates response rate metrics and protects candidate agency.

**Impact:** UX flow requires explicit applicant action; response rates become a credible sales metric.

**Decided by:** Ilse (prior proposal), affirmed by V

---

### D3: Pre-List Serves as Trust Demonstration
**Decision:** The CSV/pre-list of potential candidates serves to demonstrate Careerspan's capabilities to skeptical recruiters, not as a security blanket.

**Rationale:** Gets AI-skeptical recruiters to "see that it does, in fact, generate some names worth paying attention to."

**Impact:** Positioning in recruiter conversations; need formal agreements about usage.

**Decided by:** V (with Ilse's caveat about preventing exploitation)

---

### D4: Need Recruiter Agreement Before Onboarding
**Decision:** Before onboarding recruiters, establish a formal agreement about how they can use candidate data.

**Rationale:** Prevent recruiters from adding candidates to their own mailing lists without approval; ensure Careerspan provides actual value vs. just being exploited for leads.

**Impact:** Sales/onboarding process; legal/compliance consideration.

**Decided by:** Both (Ilse raised, V agreed)

---

### D5: Silent Scans for Holly Delayed to Monday
**Decision:** The requested silent scans (Agave, Deep Tune roles) will be delivered Monday instead of today.

**Rationale:** Technical debt requires Ilse to rethink implementation given strategic pivot; scripts need updating.

**Impact:** V will communicate delay to Holly; Ilse prioritizes strategic reflection over immediate delivery.

**Decided by:** Ilse (with V's agreement)
