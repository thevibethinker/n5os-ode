# B03 DECISIONS

## Strategic Decisions

### 1. Two-Phase Outreach Timeline
**Decision:** Execute acquisition outreach in two phases
- **Phase 1 (Now → End of January):** Social visibility campaign — get in target line of sight via LinkedIn/Twitter
- **Phase 2 (First week of February):** Active outreach to targets based on Phase 1 momentum

### 2. Channel Division
**Decision:** Split social outreach by platform
- Logan: Twitter targets
- V: LinkedIn targets (may split if volume too high)

### 3. Prioritization Framework
**Decision:** Use three-dimensional scoring for targets
- **Proximity:** First/Second/Third degree connection
- **Warmth:** Hot/Warm/Temperate
- **Exit Type:** Money in Bank / Aqui-hire / PR Exit

### 4. Warm Intro Priority
**Decision:** Second-degree connections get warm intro treatment before cold outreach
**Rationale:** Higher conversion, better positioning

### 5. A-List Pressure Strategy
**Decision:** Use new interest to "drive up value chain" with existing warm leads
**Tactic:** Classic time compression — create urgency via multiple interested parties

### 6. Exit Type Classification
**Decision:** Classify targets by exit outcome, not just attractiveness
- Money in the Bank (cash acquisition)
- Aqui-hire Potential (team acquisition)
- PR Exit (reputation-preserving, minimal payout)
