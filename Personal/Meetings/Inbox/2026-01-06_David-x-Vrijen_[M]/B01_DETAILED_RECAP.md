# B01_DETAILED_RECAP: David x Vrijen

## 1. Executive Summary
Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan) and David Speigel met to discuss David's ongoing integration of Zo into his workflow, specifically focusing on automating the ingestion and processing of Fathom meeting transcripts. David shared his frustration with current manual processes and his attempts using Zapier. Vrijen provided a live tutorial on setting up direct API integrations within Zo to bypass third-party costs and latency. The conversation also spanned strategic networking (Sidebar/Super Interviews), health optimization (GLP-1s, 23andMe data), and the architectural philosophy of building a "second brain" through indexed meeting intelligence.

## 2. Key Discussion Points

### Zo Integration & Fathom Automation
- **The Problem:** David wants to consolidate all past and future Fathom transcripts in one place for querying and course content generation. He noted that manual copying is tedious and current Zapier workflows shifted him away from the agentic behavior he desired.
- **The Solution:** Vrijen demonstrated how to use Zo's **Developers** settings to store Fathom API keys and webhook secrets. He recommended using the **GLM 4.7** model for cost-effective, high-quality planning.
- **Architectural Shift:** Vrijen introduced the concept of an "Inbox" folder for staging raw data before processing it into structured intelligence blocks, emphasizing that raw transcripts are "iron" that needs to be processed into "aluminum" (higher-order data).

### Content Strategy & Course Gaps
- **Use Case:** David teaches a course and wants to identify gaps in his "superset" of content.
- **Workflow:** David already "flags" gaps during live calls by verbally noting when a topic isn't covered in his slides. He wants Zo to automatically extract these flagged moments and propose additional context/slides to make his curriculum additive over time.

### Networking & Strategic Introductions
- **Sidebar / Super Interviews:** David mentioned a call with a co-founder of Sidebar (a career growth platform). He sees a potential synergy between their "Super Interviews" product and Careerspan, particularly for recruiting firms and hiring companies. Vrijen expressed high interest in an introduction.

### Health & Bio-Hacking (The "GLP" Discussion)
- **Tirzepatide:** Vrijen shared his positive experience with oral Tirzepatide (GLP-1), noting benefits in appetite reduction, motivation, and cognitive sharpness, despite minor side effects like constipation.
- **Data Integration:** Vrijen showed David how he integrated 23andMe genomic data, Fitbit vitals, and medication protocols into Zo to generate a "Daily Protocol." He encouraged David to upload his own genetic data to Zo to investigate his high glucose/BMI readings.

## 3. Decisions Made
- **Tooling:** David decided to move away from Zapier for Fathom integration in favor of a direct Zo API setup using the secrets and environment variables Vrijen demonstrated.
- **Folder Structure:** David will implement a `Personal/Meetings/Inbox` directory structure to allow for a staging and processing pipeline.
- **Model Choice:** Agreed to use **GLM 4.7** for routine technical planning to optimize token spend.
