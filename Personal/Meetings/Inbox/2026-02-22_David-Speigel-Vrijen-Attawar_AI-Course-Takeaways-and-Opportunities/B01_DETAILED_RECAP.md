## Key Takeaways
- Maven success in this context is a **pipeline problem**: visibility gates (waitlist/listing), repeated experiments, and distribution compounding.  
- The **200 waitlist threshold** emerged as the practical milestone for discoverability strategy, with lightning lessons as the acquisition flywheel.  
- Borrowed audience is not optional at early stages; partner channels can materially accelerate legitimacy and signup velocity.  
- A differentiated opportunity was identified: **product + course hybrid** where learners build deployable capabilities each week, not just consume lectures.  
- Provisional concept direction: **“Build Your Zoffice”** / “hire AI employees,” aimed at translating AI engineering instincts for non-technical operators.  
- Follow-up ownership: David to continue tactical Maven inputs; Vrijen to refine concept framing and leverage relevant relationships for potential Maven category positioning.
