# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

## Idea 1: The "Savage" Tax
The "Savage Tax" is the cumulative productivity loss of highly technical or strategic people performing manual data plumbing (copy-pasting transcripts). Vrijen’s reaction suggests that in an agentic world, manual workflow is no longer just "work," it is a signal of missing infrastructure.

## Idea 2: Native Terminal Agents as the New IDE
The discussion of Claude code vs. Lovable highlights a bifurcation in AI tools: browser-based app-builders for the "no-code" crowd vs. native terminal-embedded agents for the "OS-first" crowd. Vrijen’s bet is on the latter—where the AI has deep file system access.
