# B21: Key Quotes & Strategic Questions

## Key Quotes
**"Take heed of these hypotheses and generate a generic block dedicated to it."**  
— Vrijen Attawar  
*Context:* V made the Zo Take Heed command that set the tone for B32, insisting the partnership hypotheses deserve their own, deliberately weird artifact.

**"Do you think a consumer-facing product like Gamma might be more open to that idea because they view things through that consumer lens?"**  
— Vrijen Attawar  
*Context:* The two were weighing whether consumer-first vendors (Gamma, Surf) respond better to usage-based bets than enterprise brands.

**"You guys are the App Store, right?"**  
— Ben Guo  
*Context:* Ben framed Zo as a platform with a curated catalog, reinforcing that new integrations should feel like packaged experiences instead of ad-hoc prompts.

**"I have a real scumbag approach to all things in life."**  
— Vrijen Attawar  
*Context:* Said in jest while describing his relentless experimentation, signaling that he is comfortable embracing the messy side of product-building.

## Strategic Questions Raised
- How do we make it compelling for Gamma and other consumer-facing partners to see Zo as their App Store, rather than another sandbox?  
- What is the best way to deliver digestible news or updates via text/SMS so the consumer hypothesis can be tested without a full UI rebuild?
