# B06: Business Context

## The Problem
Health tech sales reps struggle to synthesize deep medical data (surgical volumes, patient panels) with general medical knowledge (PubMed) and specific meeting goals to create effective sales collateral. This manual process is slow and inconsistent.

## The Solution (Bonfire)
A sales intelligence tool that automates "Sales Prep" by:
1. Identifying high-value targets (surgeons, hospitals).
2. Generating context-aware prep documents and collateral using AI agents that "speak" healthcare data and narrative story-telling.

## Market Positioning
Bonfire sits at the intersection of CRM/Sales Intelligence and Healthcare Data Analytics. They differentiate through "proprietary healthcare related context" that general-purpose sales tools lack.
