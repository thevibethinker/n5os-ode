# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

## Narrative Design in AI
V's background as a "career coach" and "narrative designer" is a secret weapon for AI consulting. The hardest part of agentic workflows isn't the data retrieval; it's the **synthesis into a story**. How can AI be taught "narrative design" for a CFO vs. a CMO?

## "Vibe Coding" as a Business
V mentions "vibe coding" as part of his consulting value prop. This suggests a shift from traditional software engineering to "intent-based engineering" where the architect's taste and prompting intuition are the primary drivers of value.

## The "Downstream" Pivot
Bonfire's move from "planning" (static) to "conversations" (dynamic) is a significant architectural shift. It moves the product from a dashboard that people check once a week to a tool that is mission-critical for every single call. This is the "active intelligence" vs. "passive intelligence" divide.
