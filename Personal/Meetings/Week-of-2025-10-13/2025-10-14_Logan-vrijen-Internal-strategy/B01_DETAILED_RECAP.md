## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Market Categorization and Strategic Pivot
Vrijen identified three primary categories for potential strategic focus:
- **Category A: Verification:** Validating candidate authenticity and skill.
- **Category B: Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS):** Strategic distribution and workflow integration.

### 2. The Verification Thesis
Vrijen proposed verification as the strongest lead, framing it as a solution to the "clear and present danger" of fake candidates and AI-cheating in interviews. 
- **The Product Hook:** Using Careerspan to cross-reference resumes against conversational stories to validate a candidate's grasp of their stated experiences.
- **Workflow:** Candidates do "two stories" via a Careerspan magic link before the recruiter/hiring manager interview. Careerspan provides a "confidence score" and flags inconsistencies.
- **Manual to Automated:** Ilse is currently experimenting with manual gut-checks (pre-filter) which can eventually be automated.
