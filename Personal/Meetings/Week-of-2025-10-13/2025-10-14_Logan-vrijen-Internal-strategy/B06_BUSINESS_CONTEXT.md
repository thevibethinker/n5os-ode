# B06: Business Context

## Market Timing
The "cheating candidate" and "AI-polished response" problem is reaching a fever pitch in technical recruiting. Companies are feeling "gaslit" by candidates who sound perfect but lack substance. This creates a market window for "Proof of Work" or "Proof of Knowledge" tools that go deeper than a standard background check.

## Competitive Landscape
- **Background Checks:** Checker, Tofu, First Advantage. These are well-funded but focused on criminal/identity records rather than behavioral/skill consistency.
- **Direct Competitors:** Recent funding from A16Z for similar players indicates a validated VC category. Careerspan's differentiator is its conversational data and "consumer knowledge."

## Strategic Risks
- **Integration Friction:** ATS systems are centralizing forces. If Careerspan isn't integrated, the manual effort for recruiters might be too high.
- **The "Gut" Factor:** Early-stage founders may reject the tool, believing their own intuition is superior (the "Founder Ego" trap).
