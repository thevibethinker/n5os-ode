# B03: Decisions

| Decision | Context | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pivot Careerspan away from VC scaling** | Careerspan will not seek VC money or follow a traditional employer-sales model. | Founders want to keep the tool free for job seekers and avoid the "grind" of selling to employers. |
| **Adopt Influencer/Brand Funding Model** | V and Logan will fund the product via their personal brands and expertise-led services. | Leverages their existing influence and allows for more disruptive, independent product growth. |
| **Focus on "Narrow Networks" (Nira)** | Nira will remain focused on small, high-trust group networks rather than broad internet scale. | Anti-LinkedIn positioning; focuses on trust and contextual relevance. |
| **"Pay It Forward" Collaboration** | Careerspan and Nira will collaborate via mutual shout-outs and community introductions. | Synergistic alignment between community-led growth (Careerspan) and network operating systems (Nira). |
| **Nira Team Size Constraint** | Target ARR of $20M with fewer than 15 people. | Commitment to a high-efficiency, engineering-heavy culture. |
