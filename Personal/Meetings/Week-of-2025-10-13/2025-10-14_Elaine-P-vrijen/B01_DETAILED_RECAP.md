# B01 Detailed Recap

## Overview
Vrijen Attawar (Founder of Careerspan) and Elaine P (Technical Program/Project Manager) met to discuss AI development philosophy, Careerspan's product evolution, and Elaine's career background and interest in LLMs. The conversation bridged technical AI architecture with career coaching expertise.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Careerspan Strategy & Evolution
- Vrijen shared that Careerspan is moving toward building individual brands and bootstrap funding instead of the VC route.
- The product is an AI career coach that uses conversational coaching to build a "centralized understanding" of a professional's story for matching with jobs and networking.
- They have decided to focus on candidate-side value, avoiding charging candidates or being beholden to employer desires.

### 2. AI Engineering Philosophy (The "Careerspan Way")
- **Simplicity over Sophistication:** Vrijen advocated for using the "dumbest possible AI" (cheaper models like GPT-4o-mini equivalent) and clever scripting/design patterns to simulate complex reasoning.
- **RAG vs. Scripting:** Careerspan avoids finicky RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) in favor of lightweight, constrained deployment to maintain consistency and lower COGS.
- **Data Depth:** Generic AI fails because it relies on shallow resume data. Careerspan's approach is to extract deep context through conversational deep-dives into experiences.

### 3. Technical Exchange on LLMs
- Elaine discussed her experience using RAG (uploading resumes to ChatGPT/Claude) and her frustration with generic outputs.
- Vrijen explained that LLMs are "deceptively bad" at many tasks without deep context and specialized instruction to prevent generic word substitution.
- Discussion on context windows, probabilistic nature of models, and the "multi-point matching problem."

### 4. Shared Backgrounds
- Both have international school backgrounds (IB system). Elaine worked at schools in Saudi Arabia and South Korea; Vrijen attended an IB school in Bangladesh.
- Vrijen met his co-founder Logan in Singapore a decade ago.
- Elaine's career pivots: Semiconductor industry -> International schools -> Software Program/Project Management.

## Conclusion
The meeting concluded with Vrijen promising to share advanced resources on LLMs with Elaine, reinforcing Careerspan's transition into an expertise-led brand.
