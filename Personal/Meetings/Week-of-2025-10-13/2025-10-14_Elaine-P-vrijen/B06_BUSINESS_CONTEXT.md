# B06 Business Context

## Careerspan Current State
Careerspan is at a strategic crossroads, transitioning from a product-first sales motion (to employers) to an expertise-first brand motion (bootstrapping). The core product is an AI Career Coach that solves the "multi-point matching problem" through high-granularity semantic data collection via conversational coaching.

## Market Thesis
- **Broken Incentives:** Current hiring models fail because they force candidates to be generic and employers to rely on shallow data (resumes).
- **AI Economics:** Software no longer scales traditionally because LLM outputs have high marginal COGS. Therefore, engineering discipline (using "dumber" models effectively) is a competitive advantage.
- **Deep Data > RAG:** Conventional RAG on PDFs is limited. Conversational "deep-dives" create "incomparable" data granularity that allows for truly non-generic rewrites and matching.

## Target User Relationship
The interaction with Elaine exemplifies Careerspan's new strategy: building authority by educating "technical" but frustrated users on the deeper mechanics of AI, thereby validating Careerspan's superior architectural approach.
