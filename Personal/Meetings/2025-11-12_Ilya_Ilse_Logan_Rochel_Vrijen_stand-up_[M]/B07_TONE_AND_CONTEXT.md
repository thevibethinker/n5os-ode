# B07 – Tone and Context

- Internal, execution‑oriented tone; team is comfortable challenging ideas and language.
- Mix of strategic thinking (market framing) and tactical planning (scripts, collateral, partnership details).
- Stable, collaborative team dynamic; decisions are made collectively and recorded explicitly (B40).
