### Concerns / Watch Points

**1. Ambiguity on Job Supply**
- **What:** Ray asked directly: "Do you have enough jobs?" Answer was nuanced but not crystal clear
- **Concern:** If 700 FOHE members join and there are only 10-20 roles, experience is bad
- **Watch Point:** Careerspan needs to provide demand forecast before finalizing pilot size

**2. Employer Variation in Application Process**
- **What:** Some employers require ATS integration, some don't; user experience is inconsistent
- **Concern:** Could confuse members; some paths have "skip the queue," others don't
- **Watch Point:** Pilot should probably start with employers who offer best experience (direct intro + no extra application)

**3. Role Type Concentration**
- **What:** Demand is heavily weighted to engineering and product; some FOHE members may not fit
- **Concern:** If pilot is positioned as "job opportunities for FOHE," but it's really "engineering/product jobs," expectations don't match
- **Watch Point:** Pilot scope should be explicitly role-typed (e.g., "Product/Engineering/Manager roles")
