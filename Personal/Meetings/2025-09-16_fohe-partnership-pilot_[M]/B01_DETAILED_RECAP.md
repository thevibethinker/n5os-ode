## Strategic Context

### Market Dynamics

The hiring landscape is undergoing rapid change. AI tools now make resume-matching trivial, which is rendering traditional ATS-based filtering obsolete. Major companies (Zapier head of recruiting confirmed) are either:
- Using internal tools only, ignoring applicant tracking systems entirely
- Posting jobs only for legal compliance, not actually reviewing inbound
- Overwhelmed by volume and unable to surface qualified candidates

This creates opportunity for Careerspan: **They're offering employers a way to find great candidates who would be buried in the inbound**, sidestepping the ATS lottery entirely. This positioning directly addresses employer pain.

### FOHE Partnership Opportunity

FOHE is a strong community partner with:
- Concentrated membership in product/engineering/business roles (where Careerspan has early demand)
- Trust with their community (important for adoption)
- Potential reach (large enough that pilot volume is real)

The risk Ray and Jake both surfaced: If we flood the zone with 700 signups and only have 10 jobs, the partnership becomes an aggregator (low value for community) and damages FOHE's credibility with members.

### Why This Matters for Careerspan

1. **Demand validation**: Direct relationship with employers gives Careerspan better data on what roles exist and what candidates are needed
2. **Community distribution**: FOHE is a trusted source; this partnership is distribution, not just referrals
3. **Flywheel potential**: More job seekers → better matching signal → more attractive to employers → more jobs → better retention
4. **Revenue model proof**: If the $300-500 warm intro model works with FOHE-sourced candidates, it scales
