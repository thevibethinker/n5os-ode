# B28: Strategic Intelligence

## Strategic Position
Careerspan is transitioning from passive candidate platform to active B2B service model with Magic Link product. Positioned at intersection of AI-powered hiring and anti-credentialism movement.

## Market Signals
- Multiple paying customers emerged organically for Magic Link ($200-300/month)
- Traditional recruiting pain points intensifying (application volume crushing founders)
- Speed-to-implementation becoming critical differentiator vs traditional recruiters
- Career changers/non-traditional candidates undervalued by existing ATS systems

## Competitive Advantages Being Built
1. **Pedagogically sound evaluation framework** - 70-page analysis per candidate (not corner-cutting)
2. **Voice-first founder experience** - WhatsApp voice messages vs lengthy intake calls
3. **Proprietary dataset** - Capturing how people describe solving org problems (not documented elsewhere)
4. **Cheap, scalable AI stack** - Built on GPT-4o-mini foundation enables sustainable margins

## Critical Strategic Opportunities
1. **McKinsey Founder Community Partnership** - 250 founders, official talent partner designation, ~400 jobs to populate job board with high-quality company names (ZocDoc tier)
2. **Acquisition Play** - Focus on user base growth + data accumulation for near-term acquisition rather than long-term sustainability
3. **Anti-FAANG Positioning** - Market recognizing that FAANG engineers are structurally misaligned for early-stage startups

## Strategic Risks/Tensions
- Compute costs on margin model require precise targeting (incentive = minimize waste)
- Product retention strong (40-50% feature adoption) but revenue model still stabilizing
- Non-technical founder facing technical scaling challenges (though Ilsa described as "demigod" AI lead)

## Decision Imperatives
- Push McKinsey partnership to closure (already delayed)
- Maintain quality bar on candidate evaluation vs pressure to ship faster
- Balance "keeping lights on" revenue generation vs long-term dataset value play
