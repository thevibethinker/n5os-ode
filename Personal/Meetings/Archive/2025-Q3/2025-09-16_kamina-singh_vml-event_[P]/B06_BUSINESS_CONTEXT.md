# Business Context

- **VML Event:** Inaugural, high‑visibility AI & future‑of‑work event; important for employer branding and talent positioning. VML leadership and HR/recruitment teams will attend, making it a strong wedge into enterprise relationships.
- **Northwell:** Major healthcare system (≈2M patients served annually) with a new Bloomberg‑funded health & sciences high school and significant internal workforce activity (layoffs, hiring, promotions). Alignment with Careerspan’s mission around career reflection and mobility is strong.
- **Careerspan Strategy Shift:**
  - Product engagement is strong, but prior GTM focused too heavily on universities (slow sales cycles, limited budgets) and direct‑to‑consumer (misaligned with desire not to charge job seekers).
  - New motion centers on **employers and professional communities**, using communities as talent pools and employers as paying customers.
- **Role of Partners like Kamina:** Trusted connectors who can bridge Careerspan into employers (Northwell, VML) and into high‑quality communities (universities, alumni groups, AI collectives).
