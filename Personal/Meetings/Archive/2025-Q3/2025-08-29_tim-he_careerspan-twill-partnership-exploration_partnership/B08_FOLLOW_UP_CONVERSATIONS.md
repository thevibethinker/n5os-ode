## Communication Strategy

### Tone & Style
- **Professional but warm:** Tim is an ally, not a vendor/client; relationship matters
- **Responsive:** Tim mentioned slow email response; set expectations for communication cadence
- **Transparent:** Share learnings from pilot; invite feedback; iterate together
- **Celebrate wins:** When candidates get matched or placements happen, share wins with Tim

### Key Messages to Reinforce
1. "Careerspan and Twill are natural partners in solving hiring problems"
2. "Tim's support and feedback is valuable to how we're building this"
3. "We want to move fast but not lose sight of quality for communities and candidates"
4. "Your success with Careerspan is our success"

### Escalation & Problem-Solving
- If pilot metrics are weak: Honest discussion on why; iterate or pivot approach
- If integration issues arise: Technical team engagement; rapid troubleshooting
- If Twill feedback is critical: Take it seriously; adjust offering or explain thinking
- If relationship stalls: Vrijen to reach out proactively (don't let momentum die)
