# Detailed Recap: Careerspan & Twill Partnership Exploration

## Meeting Overview
A partnership exploration call between Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan co-founder) and Tim He (Twill leadership) to discuss potential collaboration opportunities and alignment between Careerspan's talent marketplace and Twill's community-driven recruiting platform.

## Key Business Discussion Points

### Careerspan's Current Status & Go-to-Market Evolution
Careerspan has shifted its positioning from a technology-first approach (emphasizing behavioral insights and candidate data) to a market-driven solution addressing two core unmet needs:
- **Talent sourcing and cultivation** over extended periods
- **Quick access** to pre-qualified candidates through community channels

The company is building this capability by embedding within communities and expanding rapidly across multiple verticals including:
- Lenny's Product Community (primary focus)
- Future of Higher Education (FOHI)
- Alumni associations and related networks

### Product-Market Fit Indicators
- **User Base**: ~400 active users over 8-month period; ~800-900 total users with sufficient engagement for matching
- **Average user lifecycle**: 3 months, highly engaged
- **User behavior**: Users who engage once typically engage 3+ times; power users engage 10-20 times
- **Job distribution effectiveness**: 80-90% open rates on distributed jobs; 12-16% click-through rates (significantly above industry benchmarks)

### Revenue Model & Pricing Strategy
- **Current model**: Subscription-based, charged to companies
- **Value proposition**: Provide JD, receive high-quality candidate list within minutes
- **Pricing approach**: Flat subscription vs. per-name pricing was discussed; Tim advocated for flat subscription to avoid repetitive value calculations
- **Future aspiration**: Transition to placement-based pricing as the company matures

### Careerspan's Product Offering to Companies Like Twill
1. **Continuous talent pipeline**: Search database for specific role types or skills
2. **Community-fresh candidates**: Guaranteed new people entering pipeline due to rapid community expansion
3. **Auto-shortlist service**: Flexible search and outreach without consent concerns (all users opt-in; Careerspan handles intro process)
4. **Data-enhanced matching**: Matching combines resume data and conversation history from candidates

### Strategic Positioning
- Rejected traditional ATS positioning; believes matching systems will commoditize quickly
- Core strategy: **Aggressive community acquisition** as primary moat
- Organizational split: Logan (co-founder) focuses on community onboarding; Vrijen focuses on enterprise revenue generation
- Target customer profile: Recruiters and companies wanting to "max out their talent pipeline" via subscription access

## Partnership Dynamics with Twill

### Tim He's Perspective
- Expressed personal investment in Careerspan's vision and execution
- Noted alignment with Careerspan's philosophy about improving the hiring system
- Acknowledged Twill and Careerspan occupy similar space but are "not really competitors"
- Considering how to support Careerspan publicly through his following and community connections

### Potential Collaboration Areas
1. **Community participation**: Tim has two communities he's "loosely connected with" that could be interested
2. **Twill as customer**: Exploring how Careerspan could serve Twill's recruitment needs
3. **Public advocacy**: Tim considering using his platform and reach to promote/support Careerspan

### Twill's Context
- Operating a community-driven model with member incentives (kickbacks on placements)
- Loyalty driven through member benefits and referral incentives
- Tim acknowledged tension: giving kickbacks to members reduces company margins but builds loyalty
- Members speak positively about Twill's approach

## Fundraising & Growth Intentions

### Timeline & Capital Plans
- **Seed round target**: January 2025
- **Current focus**: "Bum rushing proof points" to validate go-to-market before seed
- **Timeline rationale**: Product is ready; primary challenge is monetization alignment with market needs

### Growth Strategy
- **Supply-side first**: Get communities/talent on platform; demand (company interest) will follow
- **Community onboarding pace goal**: 1-2 communities per week
- **Distribution ease**: Leverage consumer-facing product design (simple link-sharing or code-based onboarding)
- **Job supply**: Expanding with both proprietary/exclusive jobs and purchased job postings to signal robust pipeline

### Team & Execution Capability
- **Logan (co-founder)**: Community-focused, strong cultural fit and cultivation skills
- **Vrijen**: Learning HR/hiring domain deeply; taking lead on revenue generation and founder relationships
- **Geographic distribution**: Vrijen in Brooklyn, Logan in Boston, rest of team mostly in Europe

## Philosophical Alignment

### Careerspan's Vision
- The referral process is fundamentally broken (people want to help but lack time to know you)
- Existing systems force candidates into poor outcomes through ATS bottlenecks
- Market is ripe for wave of companies "faking" pro-candidate signals due to incoming backlash
- Real differentiation comes from authenticity in solving actual market problems

### Recruiting Marketplace Philosophy
- Reframe from "better matching technology" (commoditizing) to "better community integration" (sustainable)
- Both Tim and Vrijen share skepticism of traditional recruiting infrastructure
- Both value genuine network effects and member satisfaction as business drivers

- Discussion balanced between company updates and genuine partnership exploration
