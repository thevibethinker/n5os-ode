# Follow-Up Conversations

## Explicit Next Steps Identified

### 1. Product Testing & Feedback Loop (Krista Tan - Owner)
**Timeline**: Immediate, ongoing through partnership ramp  
**Scope**: Krista will test Careerspan's platform with Talent Collective members and provide feedback on user experience, feature gaps, and community-specific needs.

**Rationale**: This is the critical validation milestone for the partnership. Krista needs hands-on experience with the product to confidently recommend it to the Talent Collective community. Testing should include:
- Completeness of the candidate profiling/mental model generation
- Quality of resume generation and vibe check functionality
- Integration friction with Talent Collective's member communications channels
- Feedback on whether the "3D resume" positioning resonates with community members

**Success Metric**: Krista produces a pilot report on member engagement and qualitative feedback within 30-45 days.

### 2. Community Posting & Evangelist Engagement (Krista Tan - Owner)
**Timeline**: Post-pilot (45-60 days out)  
**Scope**: Krista will formally introduce Careerspan to the broader Talent Collective community through member communications, webinars, or featured content.

**Rationale**: This expands from product feedback to community adoption. By positioning Careerspan as a member benefit rather than a tool recommendation, Krista amplifies the reach and gives Vrijen/Logan direct insight into member needs.

**Expected Outcome**: Wave of Talent Collective members signing up for Careerspan, providing initial cohort for community-specific feature development (e.g., community-specific career frameworks, group coaching modes, etc.).

**Potential Format**: Virtual workshop or webinar where Krista demonstrates the product and invites members to test it; follow-up with case studies from early adopters.

### 3. Content & Code Partnership (Vrijen/Logan - Owners)
**Timeline**: 60-90 days post-initial commitment  
**Scope**: Careerspan provides content (career frameworks, interview prep guides, job search playbooks) and code-level integrations to Talent Collective's platform.

**Rationale**: This deepens the partnership from "recommend a tool" to "integrate our infrastructure." By providing content, Careerspan becomes embedded in the community's value delivery. Code integration makes it frictionless for members to access Careerspan's features without leaving the Talent Collective ecosystem.

**Specifics Not Fully Scoped Yet**: The conversation did not drill down on what exact content or integration architecture makes sense. This is a second-call agenda item.

**Potential Models**:
- **Content syndication**: Careerspan's career coaching content (transcripts, frameworks, guides) are featured in Talent Collective's resource library
- **API-level integration**: Talent Collective members can initiate Careerspan conversations or generate profiles directly from the Talent Collective platform
- **Co-branded materials**: Job search playbooks or career transition guides co-authored by both organizations
