# Business Context

## Careerspan / V
- Early‑stage company focused on careers and coaching, with significant experimentation behind it (multiple prior product directions and artifacts).
- Current pain: fragmented knowledge across Google Docs, Notion, AI tools, and emerging Zo‑based systems, leading to noisy search and difficulty keeping the team aligned.
- Strategic need: a disciplined, AI‑assisted cognitive operating system that keeps V and the team informed without overwhelming them, especially as AI tooling accelerates content creation.

## Bram / YCB
- Bram runs YCB, focused on personal library science and organizational knowledge systems.
- He is building a B2B consulting and implementation arm that helps organizations design better information flows and master ledgers.
- He maintains an open‑source boilerplate app that can act as a starting point for a high‑quality common base / ledger, suitable for customization by teams like Careerspan.

## Partnership Surface
- V is a strong fit for Bram’s early B2B profile: information‑dense, AI‑forward, and motivated to invest in better systems.
- There is clear conceptual alignment around the problem of unintentional hoarding and the need to curate a small number of very high‑quality, durable artifacts.
- Future collaboration could include: designing Careerspan’s master ledger, mapping ingestion points (email, Slack, docs, Zo outputs), and integrating Zo workflows with Bram’s or adjacent infrastructure.
