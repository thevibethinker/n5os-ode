# Decisions

- **Orientation toward AI + knowledge systems**  
  V commits (conceptually) to treat AI as an augmentor that supports human judgment rather than as a source of authoritative decisions or automatic filters.

- **Master ledger as north star**  
  V accepts the "master ledger" concept as a desirable target for Careerspan’s knowledge architecture: a tightly curated table of high‑value entries, not an exhaustive dump.

- **Need for clearer information vocabulary and policies**  
  V acknowledges that the team needs explicit definitions (what to store, search, synthesize, share, protect) before adding more tools or automation.

- **Reset and redesign of Zo system**  
  V validates his choice to wipe and reset his Zo environment and re‑approach it more deliberately, with an emphasis on reducing complexity and privileging high‑quality flows over clever prompts.

- **Role for Bram as advisor / collaborator**  
  Both parties align that Bram’s role is not to sell a specific tool but to help V and Careerspan think more clearly about information design, with potential consulting and/or implementation work to follow.
