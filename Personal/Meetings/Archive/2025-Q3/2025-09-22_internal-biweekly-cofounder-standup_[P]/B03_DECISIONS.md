# Decisions

## Strategic Decisions

1. **Interview framing with Workday AI leader**  
   - The interview will be framed around Kathy’s vantage point on hiring tech, AI integration, and career decision‑making, not as a narrow Careerspan product pitch.  
   - Flow: origin story (Logan) → algorithm reveal (Vrijen) → AI + hiring market and future‑of‑work insights.

2. **Community‑first distribution as GTM backbone**  
   - Careerspan will treat trusted communities (e.g., Superposition, Rise) as primary distribution channels instead of traditional job boards or pure direct sales.  
   - Non‑binding letters of intent (LOIs) with explicit rate structures will be used to build early proof points and consensus.

3. **Commission‑based SDR experiment**  
   - The team will test an external SDR contractor (India‑based) on a commission basis rather than hiring a fixed‑cost sales role immediately.  
   - This pilot will be used to validate outreach quality, cost per meeting, and scalability.

## Tactical Decisions

1. **Interview structure & facilitation**  
   - Use questions as loose anchors rather than a script; each co‑founder manages timing for their section and performs a soft, conversational handoff.  
   - Intros/outros will be recorded separately after the interview to reduce pressure and allow for better framing.

2. **Topic prioritization**  
   - Ruthlessly prioritize three core segments: algorithm reveal, AI integration, and hiring/HR‑tech future.  
   - Ethics is not treated as a standalone segment unless it emerges naturally from Kathy’s perspective.

3. **Bi‑weekly company standup reschedule**  
   - The regular company standup is moved from this slot to a later time/day to protect focus for Kathy interview prep and recording.

4. **Partnership validation sequence**  
   - Sequence will be: lock Superposition rates → meet Rise founder to validate fit and engagement → send LOIs to Superposition, Tim, and 1–2 other partners → use those commitments as proof when talking to additional communities.

5. **Demo and landing‑page direction**  
   - The existing product demo will be reused and iterated (e.g., potentially a product‑specific or role‑specific demo) instead of spinning up a generic off‑the‑shelf job‑board UI.  
   - Longer term, a dedicated landing page / mini job‑board for active roles is preferred over long, dense text blasts to communities.

## Deferred / Open Decisions

The following remain open and are tracked as debates in internal blocks (e.g., B47_OPEN_DEBATES):

- Final commission and partnership rate structure (margin vs. partner incentives).
- Long‑term GTM positioning (platform/data layer vs. embedded recruiter vs. community gateway).
- Realistic Braze/Sendgrid stabilization timeline and its impact on launch dates.

These will require additional data from pilots (SDR performance, community responses, infra readiness) before being fully resolved.
