# Deliverables

## Interview & Content Assets

- **Kathy Interview Flow Guide** (Owner: Logan)  
  - Single source of truth for the conversation arc (origin story → algorithm reveal → AI integration → hiring/HR‑tech future).  
  - Includes timeboxes, example bridge phrases, and notes on where ethics and market commentary can naturally surface.

- **Algorithm Reveal Talking Points** (Owner: Vrijen)  
  - Bullet‑point notes clarifying how to ask about Kathy’s decision‑making without making the conversation feel scripted.  
  - Intended for internal use during prep and live facilitation.

- **Intro/Outro Recording** (Owners: Logan & Vrijen)  
  - Short recorded segments to frame the conversation for listeners, recorded immediately after the interview while details are fresh.

## GTM & Partnership Materials

- **Non‑Binding LOI Templates for Communities** (Owner: Vrijen)  
  - Reusable letter templates for Superposition, Rise, and 1–2 additional communities.  
  - Explicitly specify proposed rates, expectations, and how Careerspan will work with the community.

- **Community Partnership Pitch Notes** (Owner: Vrijen)  
  - Talking points for calls with Tim (Superposition) and Giovanna (Rise), including metrics (e.g., Rise’s membership and engagement) and the "trusted recruiter" positioning.

- **Role‑ or Product‑Specific Landing Page Concept** (Owner: Logan)  
  - Outline or mockup of a focused landing experience for active roles (or for the revamped product) to replace long, dense community posts.

## Hiring & Operations

- **SDR Pilot Brief** (Owner: Vrijen)  
  - One‑pager describing the commission‑based SDR experiment: responsibilities, expected outreach patterns, initial commission assumptions, and pilot success metrics.

- **Braze/Sendgrid Integration Checklist** (Owner: Logan with Brady)  
  - Concrete list of conditions that define "stable enough" for GTM (e.g., ability to track magic‑link candidates, basic failure monitoring, and deliverability checks).

These deliverables collectively operationalize the meeting’s decisions: they turn a high‑level narrative (community‑first GTM + experimental hiring + flagship interview) into specific assets that can be created, reviewed, and iterated.
