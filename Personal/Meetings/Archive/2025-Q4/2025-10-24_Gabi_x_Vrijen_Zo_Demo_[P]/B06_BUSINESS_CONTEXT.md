# Business Context

## High-Level Context
- This meeting is an **early product demo / exploration** of Zo Computer as the substrate for a more coherent AI workflow system.
- V has been experimenting heavily with frontier models (ChatGPT, Claude) and is consolidating that work into a persistent, agentic environment (Zo) that can manage complex, interrelated workflows.
- Gabi is a senior operator/executive (exact title and company not specified) who has seen others build automations but has not yet invested heavily in her own due to time constraints and uncertainty about where to start.

## Problems Discussed
- **Fragmented workflows:** Tools like Zapier encourage many small, isolated automations that don’t capture the real, interconnected nature of founder/VC work.
- **Context and state overload:** Founders and operators must track large volumes of information—meetings, relationships, follow-ups, documents—largely in their heads.
- **Non-linear work patterns:** Real workflows branch, recombine, and loop back; they are not simple, linear "if this, then that" chains, making them hard to model in conventional automation tools.

## Proposed Solution Direction
- Treat Zo Computer as an **"AI operating system"** rather than just another chatbot:
  - Persistent file system and memory.
  - Always-on agent mode that can keep working between interactions.
  - Freedom to use multiple underlying models at true API cost.
- Build **one coherent, bounded system** on top of Zo that understands meetings, context, and follow-on workflows (summaries, intros, follow-ups) rather than dozens of disconnected workflows.
