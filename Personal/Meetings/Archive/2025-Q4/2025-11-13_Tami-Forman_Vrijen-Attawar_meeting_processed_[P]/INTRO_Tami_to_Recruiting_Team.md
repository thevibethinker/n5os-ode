## VERSION 3: LONG (280 words)

**Use case:** Detailed strategic brief with full context

**Subject:** Strategic Partnership Opportunity - Careerspan (Narrative-Based Matching Platform)

Team,

I met with Vrijen Attawar last week to explore a potential partnership between Women Back to Work and Careerspan. I want your technical and operational perspective before we move forward.

**Background on Careerspan:**
Careerspan is an AI-driven career platform that helps job seekers develop stronger narratives about their experience through guided conversational reflection. Rather than keyword-based ATS filtering, they match candidates to opportunities based on comprehensive profiles: hard skills, soft skills, career trajectory, and personal story. Their philosophy is "quality over volume"—they're building for curation, not mass candidate dumps.

**Why This Conversation Happened:**
Vrijen and I bonded over shared frustration with ATS dysfunction and the systemic waste of qualified talent. Careerspan's user base skews toward non-traditional candidates (career gaps, pivots, returners)—which overlaps significantly with our target population. Their approach to narrative-based evaluation could complement our technical vetting rather than replace it.

**Two Specific Use Cases Worth Exploring:**

1. **Returner Workflow Enhancement:** Could Careerspan's narrative matching layer into our existing HackerRank/cogility screening to give us richer candidate context earlier in the funnel?

2. **Internal Mobility (New Market):** Large enterprises struggle to match current employees to internal roles. Think SAP-scale companies hiring 20,000+ people annually but unable to efficiently redeploy talent internally. This could be a significant enterprise use case where our recruiting infrastructure + their matching platform create differentiated value.

**What I Need From You:**
Review the materials I'm forwarding (platform overview, case studies, internal mobility use case docs) and provide feedback on:
- Technical integration feasibility (API, data sharing, vetting workflow overlap)
- Whether this adds value or creates duplicate vetting steps
- Concerns about candidate experience or employer fit

**Timeline:** Please review by Nov 22 so I can schedule follow-up with Vrijen if this looks promising. If you see fundamental blockers, flag them earlier.

Thanks for taking a look at this.

Tami
