# Deliverables

## From Careerspan

- **Employer Impact Packet**  
  Short document or slide that combines candidate impact stats (e.g., Mass Eisenberg data) with early employer stories to show how Careerspan improves hiring outcomes.

- **Token/Cost Model Outline**  
  Clear estimates of token and infra costs for running a 250 / 500 / 1000‑person alumni program over six months, to inform commercial terms with Aspire.

- **Draft Employer Case Study**  
  A before/after narrative demonstrating how an HR or hiring manager’s throughput and satisfaction changed when using Careerspan.

## From Aspire / Husain

- **Alumni Program Proposal**  
  Written plan for the six‑month program: target cohort(s), Discord structure, programming cadence, roles/responsibilities, and success metrics.

- **Engagement & Metrics Plan**  
  Outline of how Aspire will track usage and outcomes (e.g., weekly active users, resumes/stories created, placements) and how often this will be reported back to Careerspan.

## Joint / Shared Outputs

- **Final Impact Report (Post‑Pilot)**  
  A 2–3 page narrative summarizing alumni outcomes, usage metrics, and hiring results, suitable for blog posts, employer outreach, and funder updates.

- **Partner‑Facing Story (e.g., Ripen)**  
  Reusable framing (building on `INTRO_Husain_Ripen_connector.md`) that explains how Careerspan + an alumni program partner collaborate to produce better hiring outcomes.
