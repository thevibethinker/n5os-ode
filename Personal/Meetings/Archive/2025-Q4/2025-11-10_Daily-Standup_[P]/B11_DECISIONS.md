### 3. Customer Experience Philosophy: Functional Over Complete
**Decision:** Launch when customer-facing experience feels complete, even if backend has "wiggle room"  
**Who Decided:** Ilya (articulated philosophy, team aligned)  
**Rationale:**  
> "If a customer clicks something, at least on their end, what is it like? Do they feel as though something has occurred?"

- Demo booking should send confirmation email
- Trial start should send confirmation email  
- Customer should feel process worked, even if internal work remains
- Perception of functionality is launch blocker; backend perfection is not

**Impact:**  
- **Positive:** Enables faster launch without waiting for full backend completion
- **Risk:** Team must deliver on confirmations sent (manage expectations carefully)
- **Philosophy:** Customer perception > internal perfection for MVP launch

**Category:** Strategic - Product & GTM Philosophy
