Strategic sponsorship discussion between Zo, Careerspan, and Oracle for a November 5th event targeting early-stage founders and builders. Oracle (represented by Andrew Zaneski, Director of New York office, and Chase Gagnon, Startup Liaison) explored partnership opportunity to provide venue and catering support.

## Event Specifications

### Event Purpose & Product Positioning
Zo positions itself as an AI operating system that addresses gaps in current market offerings:
- **Competitive differentiation**: Unlike platforms like Dia (browser-based) or locked-in solutions (Google Drive + Gemini, Notion AI), Zo provides a complete AI computer with both frontend virtual workspace and backend virtual machine
- **User autonomy thesis**: First-principles approach giving users access to any tool, ability to build and own everything, without vendor lock-in
- **Market fit exploration**: Event is a testing ground to validate whether founders/builders actually need and want Zo's value proposition

### Target Audience
- **Size**: 50-100 early-stage founders and builders
- **Demographics**: People actively building personal systems or MVPs; those frustrated with AI tool limitations; solo entrepreneurs
- **Curation**: Exclusive invitation-only application process (to be confirmed)
- **Compliance consideration**: Oracle flagged concern about government employee attendance (creates compliance liability for public sector company); Vrijen committed to checking exclusivity policy

### Event Flow & Program (2-hour format)
1. **15-minute entry/networking** (opening buffer)
2. **Ben's keynote**: "Why Consumer AI Feels Lacking" (identifies pain points Zo solves)
3. **Vrijen's keynote**: Practical demonstrations of processes built on Zo; real-world founder productivity use cases
4. **Happy hour + interactive exercise**: Attendees engage with board displaying AI improvement dimensions; participants add sticky notes capturing founder demand signals
5. **Purpose**: Qualitative market research to refine Zo's messaging and understand product-market fit signals

### Venue & Logistics
- **Original location**: Bushwick (Zo's office) - deemed suboptimal for attracting quality founder attendance
- **Target venue**: Manhattan location (significantly better for attendee quality and brand positioning)
- **Current handling**: Event organized by AI Circle, which handles distribution; Vrijen providing personal promotion
- **Potential timing options**: 6:45-8:45pm (primary) or 5:45-7:45pm (flexible alternative if needed)
- **Date**: November 5th (confirmed)

1. **Venue**: Manhattan office location (primary request; better positioning than Zo's Bushwick space)
2. **Catering**: Snacks and drinks (supporting the 2-hour happy hour format)
