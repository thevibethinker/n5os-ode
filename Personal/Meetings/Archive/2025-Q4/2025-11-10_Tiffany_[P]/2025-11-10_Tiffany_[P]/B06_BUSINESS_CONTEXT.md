# B06 – Business Context

## Zo (Tiffany’s side)

- **Stage:** Pre‑launch, approaching a major public “moment” after a long period of product‑focused building.
- **Launch Plan:**
  - A high‑level, theory‑oriented video from Robin and Ben on the future of computing and Zo’s philosophy.
  - A companion Twitter/X thread of concrete user stories showing Zo in action with real people and workflows.
  - Tight timing around mid‑November; missing this window pushes launch past Thanksgiving.
- **Go‑to‑Market Motion:**
  - Heavy emphasis on **community‑driven growth**: ambassadors, hacker houses, AI communities, and higher‑ed circles.
  - Desire for “social‑native” content that feels like organic discovery rather than corporate ads.

## Careerspan (V’s side)

- **Current Position:**
  - Consumer side: strong engagement metrics with thousands of users and high story‑creation rates.
  - Strategic shift: moving away from charging job‑seekers toward monetizing via employers (AI‑driven talent intelligence for hiring).
- **Key B2B Angle:**
  - Careerspan reframed as a **coaching‑first hiring tool**: candidates reflect and tell rich stories; employers get structured, high‑signal analysis for decision‑making.
  - Marvin Ventures (McKinsey alumni fund) is prepared to recognize Careerspan as a talent partner and sign an LOI, letting V approach their ~250 founders.
- **Why Zo Matters to Careerspan:**
  - V’s entire operating system for building and running Careerspan is now Zo‑based (N5/N5OS).
  - A successful Zo launch that features V credibly strengthens both brands and gives V more surface area to talk about his system and product.

## Intersection: Co‑marketing Surface Area

- V is both a **reference customer** and a **co‑builder**:
  - He can show Zo enabling dense, high‑leverage workflows (meeting intelligence, career systems) that are hard to replicate elsewhere.
  - He can route Zo into curated networks (VCs, higher‑ed, hacker houses) where Careerspan is also relevant.
- For Zo, V provides a story that bridges: non‑technical founder → sophisticated AI system → tangible business outcomes.
- For Careerspan, Zo’s launch is a platform to:
  - Introduce his Zo base layer and N5OS more broadly.
  - Signal that his operating environment is at the frontier of AI‑native work, reinforcing product credibility.
