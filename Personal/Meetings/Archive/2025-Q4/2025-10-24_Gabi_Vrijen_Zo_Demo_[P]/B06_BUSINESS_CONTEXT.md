# Business Context

- **V’s side:** Deep experimentation with AI tooling (ChatGPT, Claude, Zo) to build a personal cognitive operating system that handles meetings, follow‑ups, and complex, branching workflows.
- **Gabi’s side:** Running an early‑stage company with many responsibilities and limited time to invest in brittle, over‑customized automations.
- **Tooling Gap:** Most existing automation platforms assume **linear, deterministic flows**, while real founder workflows are graph‑shaped and context‑heavy.
- **Zo’s Positioning:** A persistent AI computer in the cloud, with:
  - A real file system.
  - Multiple model access.
  - Always‑on agents/routines.
  Offering a more natural substrate for the kind of interrelated workflows V and Gabi actually run.
