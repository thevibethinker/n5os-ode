- Gabi joins slightly late after handling urgent admin with an insurance broker and mentions juggling multiple upcoming insurance payments.
- V and Gabi briefly commiserate about vendors calling at inconvenient times.
- Gabi expresses strong curiosity about what V has built and confirms she is free until noon.
- V explains that Gabi is effectively one of the **first external people** (outside Logan) for whom he tried to set up AI‑based systems, making her an early design partner for his workflows.
- V describes his experimentation with ChatGPT and Claude: he has built advanced, highly customized workflows by asking unusual questions and chaining prompts in non‑standard ways, primarily focused on improving his productivity.
- He shares that these experiments led him to discover **Zo Computer**, which he’s become deeply excited about; he believes it has outsized potential and wants to actively support its growth.
- V frames Zo as a **"computer in the cloud you control by prompting"**—combining:

  - Access to multiple models (not just one provider).
  - Always‑on "agent" capabilities for running routines and automations.
- Gabi asks clarifying questions about what that actually means in practice; V commits to show concrete examples during the upcoming demo.

- V reframes the core founder problem as **context and state management**—keeping track of everything in his head and in scattered tools—and explains that his goal with Zo is to design a **bounded, coherent system** rather than a thousand isolated flows.
