## Raw Blurb (Type 2)

I'm Vrijen Attawar, founder of Careerspan. I help companies make career coaching accessible at enterprise scale by turning messy career problems into structured frameworks.

Before Careerspan, I spent a decade as a career coach for executives and mid-career professionals navigating major transitions—pivots, promotions, and re-entries. The pattern I saw was consistent: people usually know what they want but struggle to structure the path to get there. That insight became Careerspan’s thesis—career development scales when you combine AI-powered workflows with human coaching.

We work with Fortune 500 organizations to deliver personalized coaching using an AI + human hybrid. Our platform handles skills assessment, transition planning, and manager alignment for 10k+ employees daily. The goal isn't to replace human judgment—it's to structure the work so human coaches can focus on high-impact moments while AI handles the scaffolding.
