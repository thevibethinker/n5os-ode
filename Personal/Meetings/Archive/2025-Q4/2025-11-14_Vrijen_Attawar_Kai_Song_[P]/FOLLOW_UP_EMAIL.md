As promised, here's a brief overview of Careerspan for you to share with Rehab and the Ascendia team:

**Careerspan** is a hiring platform that uses semantic matching to evaluate candidates based on ~1,000 parameters, rather than keyword screening or resume parsing. We built this by pivoting from 2+ years of career coaching work (2,000 hours across 3,000+ signups), which gave us uniquely rich signal on what makes candidates successful.

Our approach helps companies:
- Replace expensive, rigid ATS systems with flexible semantic evaluation
- Move beyond video interviews that repel top candidates
- Access diverse talent pools (our users range from Hugo Award-winning sci-fi writers to Netflix execs to HVAC technicians)

**Why this might interest Ascendia:** We have high-fidelity candidate data that could complement Ascendia's interview outcomes. A potential data partnership could create a feedback loop where interview performance enriches our matching algorithm, and our candidate profiles provide Ascendia with better pre-interview context.

We're currently piloting with VCs who want standardized hiring for their portfolio companies, and partnering with the Future of Higher Education network (5,000+ ed leaders and operators).

Happy to explore how this could work for Ascendia's Asia market focus, or discuss other ways our data richness might be valuable.
