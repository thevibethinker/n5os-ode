He explained that if he sounds animated or aggressive, it's purely competitive drive—he "loves nothing more than beating competitors." He also shared significant personal context: having recently lost a parent after a difficult period, he gained perspective that "nothing's going to be harder than that" in work, creating a sense of urgency and resilience. He encouraged the team to give direct feedback without worry about hurt feelings, emphasizing his receptiveness to course correction.
