# Stakeholder Intelligence

## Konrad Kucharski (Aviato)
- **Role & domain:** Talent/people lead for Aviato; owns the recruiting narrative and screens a high volume of inbound due to the company’s emerging social push.
- **Key interests:** Wants tools that’ll make his existing people more effective instead of burdening them with another inbox, especially for the annoying CRM-style admin work he mentioned; cares deeply about predictability ("46% of hires fail by the 18 month mark").
- **Skepticism:** Wary of adding a siloed workflow and wants seamless handoffs into his current stack (Workday/careers button/webhooks).
- **Leverage points:** Already anticipates an influx from Eric’s social presence and is actively curious about offshore talent models and the "BS detector" consistency guardrail, which proves Careerspan is helping him cut through resume noise.

## Ilse Funkhouser (Careerspan / AI ops)
- **Role & domain:** Product lead responsible for the AI-powered candidate assessment experience and employer dashboard (she’s the one who "pokes the AI when it misbehaves").
- **Key interests:** Keeps the coaching dialog grounded in stories, customizable assessment criteria, and tight communication (e.g., letting hiring managers see the story-level justification for each skill).
- **Skepticism:** Does not want to build integrations before customers ask for them, but is open to lightweight automation (webhooks) if it’s clearly earned.
- **Leverage points:** Loves to demonstrate the "context sensitive" analysis per story, the radar graph candidate summaries, and the ability to publish the role and instantly share a link with applicants.

## Logan Currie (CareerFin)
- **Role & connection:** Co-founder of CareerFin; popped in to say hi on the strength of Vrijen’s introduction before hopping off.
- **Key interests:** Building goodwill with the Careerspan team and widening the partner network; offered quick encouragement before exiting the call.
- **Skepticism:** None surfaced because he joined purely for a short hello.
- **Leverage points:** Represents a warm network receptor for Careerspan’s recruiting relationships.

## Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan)
- **Role & domain:** Co-founder/host; frames the demo, asks thoughtful questions about Aviato’s hiring pain, and ensures the product’s voice stays people-first.
- **Key interests:** Making kindness visible in recruitment, reducing noise for hiring managers, and positioning Careerspan as the data layer for empathetic hiring.
- **Skepticism:** Keeps the team honest ("I don’t want to put pressure on you") and stresses the product should fit Aviato’s culture.
- **Leverage points:** Offers a straight path to partners (JD partner, automation notes) and keeps the conversation grounded in tangible follow-ups (e.g., sharing a draft link and the plan to keep stories portable for candidates).
