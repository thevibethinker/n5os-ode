# Tone and Context

## Emotional Vibe
- **Curious but Cautious:** Konrad was very engaged ("Very cool," "Value is clear") but kept coming back to the *logistics* ("How does this integrate?"). He didn't want a "cool toy" that created more work.
- **Proud:** V and Ilse were clearly proud of the product ("Pontificate for a second out of sheer pride").
- **Respectful:** High mutual respect ("Respect and work building this out").

## Subtext
- **"Don't Make Me Think":** Konrad's recurring question about workflow suggests he is overwhelmed. He wants a solution that *removes* cognitive load, not one that shifts it to a new dashboard.
- **The "Intern" Problem:** He specifically mentioned the "younger demographic" inbound being tough to screen. This implies the *quality* of their current inbound is low/mixed, making an AI filter highly attractive.
