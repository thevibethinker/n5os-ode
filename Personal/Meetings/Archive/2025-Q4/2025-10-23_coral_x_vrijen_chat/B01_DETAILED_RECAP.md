# Detailed Recap: Coral x Vrijen Chat

## Meeting Overview
Strategic partnership exploration meeting between Vrijen (founder of Careerspan) and Coral (creator, computer science/film student, builder of Fix Your Feed). Meeting focused on understanding Coral's product, career trajectory, and potential collaboration opportunities around career content creation and community building.

## Key Context & Background

### Participants
- **Vrijen**: Founder of Careerspan (career coaching platform); 33 years old; background in college admissions, McKinsey consulting, currently building AI-driven career product
- **Coral**: Creator building Fix Your Feed; CS/film student at FIU (senior year); recently went viral on TikTok (60k views, 500 followers); former career advisor for engineering students

### Fix Your Feed Product Overview
Coral built a quiz that matches students to career content in under 20 seconds on LinkedIn. The product:
- Got 3,000 visits organically
- Caught attention of LinkedIn's director of product
- Database of content creators curated by Coral based on actual student recommendations and her career advising experience
- Includes creators who may not be mainstream (e.g., professor with 5k followers teaching data structures) but provide real value
- Students are now requesting expansion to other domains: humanities, law, medicine

### Key Challenges Coral Identified
- Only knows "computer nerds" and tech creators; limited ability to expand beyond tech industry
- Faced pressure from LinkedIn product director and business stakeholders to monetize and scale
- Balancing product quality/trust with growth and monetization pressures

## Careerspan Product Demo & Strategic Alignment

Vrijen presented Careerspan's core value proposition:
- Narrative design engine that helps users articulate their achievements through guided coaching conversations
- Generates personalized resume bullets from user rambling/freeform input (vs. ChatGPT's "slop")
- Resume tailoring per job description
- Free to job seekers; monetization through employer placements (acts as talent agent)

### Why Careerspan Resonates
- Solves the problem that Vmock (mandatory tool at most universities) fails to address
- Careerspan already being adopted at Columbia, in discussions with Granola
- Focuses on helping users understand their own worth and articulate it authentically

## Critical Partnership Opportunities Identified

### Opportunity 1: University Adoption at FIU
- Coral has direct connection to Ruth (career director at FIU—"boss's boss's boss")
- FIU is largest Hispanic Serving Institution in US (50k students)
- Currently using Vmock (which Coral describes as ineffective)
- Potential Careerspan partnership could replace Vmock for 50k students
- Careerspan can offer 50% savings vs. Vmock's $20k charging model

### Opportunity 2: Creator/Community Monetization Model
Vrijen proposed a new revenue model for Coral:
- Use Careerspan as internal recruiting tool for Coral's community/followers
- When Coral connects community members to jobs through Careerspan, earn recruiting kickbacks (typically $20-40k per placement)
- Low-effort activation if community is already using Careerspan
- Potential to earn substantial income (few placements per year = significant revenue)

### Opportunity 3: Content Collaboration with Logan
- Logan Currie (Careerspan co-founder): 5k TikTok followers in 2-3 months; strong career content creator
- Potential collab to create career content on TikTok
- Audience overlap: both targeting students, creators, career-focused communities
- Logan could mentor Coral on TikTok growth and content strategy

## Thematic Insights from Conversation

### Philosophy Alignment
Both Vrijen and Coral prioritize:
- **Authenticity**: Building trust with communities; not compromising for quick monetization
- **Impact over extraction**: Helping people before monetizing
- **Community benefit**: Ensuring tools serve users' interests, not just business interests

### Coral's Mission
- Help Latina women in tech overcome imposter syndrome and lack of confidence
- Advocate for self-promotion and salary negotiation (gap she observed: men discuss salaries openly, women hesitant)
- Address systemic undervaluation of women (80% talent, 100% effort required for same outcomes)
- Fix Your Feed as tool to democratize access to quality career content for diverse students

### Vrijen's Approach to Careerspan
- Not "selling" to job seekers; product is free (monetizes through employer placements)
- Deliberately avoiding aggressive monetization to protect user trust
- Built on core narrative design principle: help people be better advocates for themselves
- Over 1,000 hours of recorded coaching; 3,000+ users
- Organic growth through social proof (all testimonials unsolicited)

## Notable Moments

1. **Trust as Asset**: Vrijen cautioned Coral to protect the organic trust/authenticity she's built, as monetization pressure will inevitably come
2. **Founder Realization**: Coral asked about Careerspan's background; Vrijen shared his 10-page concept written the day ChatGPT launched
3. **Reciprocity Discovery**: Coral shared observation about "law of reciprocity"—helping people who help you—aligning perfectly with both their philosophies
4. **Credential Insight**: Vrijen emphasized that while he's a McKinsey alum, he and Logan are "not really tech people," yet building a product competing with technically sophisticated alternatives

## Meeting Energy & Outcomes
- High rapport; mutual respect and philosophical alignment
- Coral to test/review Careerspan product and provide brutally honest feedback
- Vrijen to send screenshots/resources positioning Careerspan
- Ruth introduction to be facilitated (FIU career director)
- Logan/Coral TikTok collaboration to be explored
- Vrijen to provide exclusive promo code for Coral's community
