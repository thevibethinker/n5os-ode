## Collaboration Principles (Based on This Conversation)

### What Makes Good Collaboration:
1. **Value exchange, not transactional** - both gave/received in this conversation
2. **Authentic interest** - both genuinely curious about each other's work
3. **Complementary skills** - Vrijen (workflows) + Mujgan (research)
4. **Shared problems** - information overload, context switching, relationship management
5. **No pressure** - "open to bouncing ideas" not "we should definitely work together"

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Pushing too hard on Careerspan sales
- Asking for intros too early
- Treating relationship as transaction
- Not respecting AGI's stealth status
- Over-committing before trust is deep

### Green Lights to Watch For:
- Mujgan reaches out proactively
- She shares more about AGI's direction
- Asks for Vrijen's input on product decisions
- Introduces him to AGI team members
- Mutual content creation interest
