## Question Themes

1. **Behavioral vs. Technical Solutions:** Many problems (task management, personal relationships) are behavioral discipline issues, not pure technical gaps

2. **Trust & Discernment:** Users need AI with context AND judgment, but trusting AI judgment is hard

3. **Interconnectedness:** How to balance capturing everything vs. intelligent filtering

4. **Cultural Factors:** Founder loneliness and self-sacrifice celebration as systemic issues

5. **Switching Costs:** Memory/knowledge creates stickiness - how to overcome for better tools?
