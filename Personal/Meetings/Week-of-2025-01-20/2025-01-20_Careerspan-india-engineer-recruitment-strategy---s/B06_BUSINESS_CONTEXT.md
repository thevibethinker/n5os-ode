# B06: Business Context

## Company: Careerspan

### Business Model
- **Type:** Two-sided marketplace (job seekers + employers)
- **Product:** Career storytelling platform with enhanced candidate profiles
- **Key Feature:** Elevator pitch + resume + GitHub + deeper dive profiles
- **Revenue Model:** Not explicitly discussed (likely placement fees or subscriptions)

### Target Segments

#### Supply Side (Candidates)
- **Primary:** High-quality engineers in India
- **Initial Pool:** 65-80 engineers
- **Value Prop:** Better representation of strengths → faster job placement at competitive salaries
- **Salary Expectations:** $100-150k/year (very competitive in India)
- **Demographics:** Very active on Instagram and LinkedIn

#### Demand Side (Employers)
- **Primary:** US-funded startups
- **Deal Size:** $100-150k/year placements
- **Pain Points:**
  - Resumes don't paint complete picture of candidates
  - Compressed for time, generate poor JDs
  - Recruiters often write JDs without understanding needs
- **Desired Features:** Voice message input for job requirements

## Partnership Model

### Shivam's Recruitment Business
- **Role:** External recruitment partner, primary demand-side connector
- **Value to Careerspan:**
  - Access to high-quality Indian engineer pool
  - Relationships with funded US startups
  - Social channels (Instagram, LinkedIn) for amplification
- **Value to Shivam:**
  - Better candidate evaluation (beyond resumes)
  - Faster candidate matching
  - Competitive advantage in placement

### Lovable Partnership
- **Role:** Technical implementation partner
- **Value to Careerspan:**
  - Built intake form for customer demand side
  - Confirmed integration is "super easy"
  - Engineering team validated technical feasibility
- **Integration Flow:** Framer site → embedded intake form → Lovable → Careerspan payload

## Competitive Landscape

### Current State
- **Traditional Recruitment:** Just sending resumes, incomplete candidate picture
- **Problem:** Mismatch between what US companies think they need and how they access talent abroad
- **Gap:** No tools for deep candidate evaluation beyond resume

### Differentiators (Planned)
1. **Rich Profiles:** Elevator pitch + resume + GitHub + deeper dive
2. **Voice-Enhanced JDs:** AI converts founder voice messages to structured rubrics
3. **Social Proof:** Video testimonials from target demographic
4. **Speed:** Faster candidate-to-job matching

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Initial Rollout
1. **Supply Side:** Target 65-80 engineer pool with custom landing page
2. **Demand Side:** Use Lovable intake form for founder customers
3. **Conversion Target:** 30-50% of engineers create at least one Careerspan story

### Growth Strategy (If Traction Shows)
1. **Social Amplification:** Shivam to promote on Instagram and LinkedIn
2. **Proof Points:** Video testimonials from Indian engineers
3. **Showcase:** Top job with JD + Careerspan link to demonstrate value
