# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

* **The Micro-Community Recruitment Moat:** If recruitment shifts from broad platforms (LinkedIn) to high-trust micro-communities (Social Impact SF), the "curator" (Theresa) becomes the new recruiter. How does Careerspan scale the curation of these curators?
* **Founder Burnout Symmetry:** Both V and Theresa spoke about the "always on" pressure. Is there a way for Careerspan to automate the "recruiter" function so community leaders can provide career value without adding to their own burnout?
* **"Intent for Good" as a Market Filter:** Theresa's aggregation of nonprofit, political, and intent-for-good events suggests a specific psychographic profile. How can Careerspan’s AI match roles based on "intent" rather than just skill sets?
