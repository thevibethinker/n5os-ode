## B03_STRATEGIC_INSIGHTS

**Market Positioning:** Careerspan's boutique approach with superior data structure positions well against large incumbents (Greenhouse, etc.) who are forced into commoditized volume-based models. Large AI systems uneconomical for mass-scale analysis; Careerspan's selectivity is a feature, not a limitation.

**Identity Verification Trend:** Industry moving toward identity/background verification (Greenhouse + Clear partnership) as primary differentiator, suggesting hiring market is consolidating around verification vs. semantic candidate analysis. This represents either threat (if incumbents own verification) or opportunity (if Careerspan can integrate verification with semantic filtering).

**Fraud Sophistication:** Coordinated Chinese identity-building scheme in US community colleges demonstrates sophisticated, sustained effort to create verifiable fake personas. Using years of fake coursework to build credibility. AI amplification concern: LLMs could dramatically accelerate persona creation. Relevant to Careerspan: any system must assume fake candidates exist at scale.

**Market Dynamics:** Two customer archetypes emerging in ATS: high-volume/low-quality buyers (paying for full solutions with everything integrated) vs. quality-focused buyers (willing to integrate best-of-breed tools). Careerspan targeting the latter, which is smaller TAM but higher unit economics.
