## Short-term Deliverables (Weeks 2-3)

### 4. "Gangster Move" Proof of Concept
**Owner:** Vrijen  
**Deliverable:** One complete Gangster Move outreach sent to a real prospect

**Components required:**
1. **Target selection:** Identify 1-2 companies that:
   - Are Series A with recent funding
   - Have reposted a job 2+ times in 3+ months
   - Are in a space where Careerspan adds clear value

2. **Demo creation:** 30-40 second video showing:
   - Hook: "You reposted this Senior PM role 3 times in 5 months. Here's what we found:"
   - 2-3 anonymized candidate profiles from Careerspan matching
   - Close: "I'm the CEO. No obligation. Just tell me if this looks like caliber you want. If yes, let's talk."

3. **Email draft:** Write the email that frames the video
   - Personalization (why this company?)
   - Value statement (what we did)
   - Ask (can we talk for 15 min?)
   - No aggressive CTA

4. **Metric:** Get 1+ positive response within 2 weeks

## Medium-term Deliverables (Weeks 3-4)

### 7. Talent Call Pilot Launch
**Owner:** Vrijen  
**Deliverable:** One live talent call campaign

**Components:**
1. **Define target cohort:** 
   - Job category (e.g., Senior PM, Staff Engineer, Head of Product)
   - Geography (e.g., NYC/SF/Boston)
   - Profile: McKinsey alumni? Startup founders? E-commerce experts?

2. **Create campaign:**
   - Talent call landing page (short form, no job-specific role)
   - General interest form capturing:
     - Background, experience, goals
     - Why this cohort? (What's compelling?)
   - Social proof elements (testimonials, company logos, candidate success stories)

3. **Launch channels:**
   - LinkedIn (direct posting)
   - Slack communities (relevant professional groups)
   - Angel/VC communities (if applicable)
   - Email to warm network (if applicable)

4. **Sample deliverable:** Include a Careerspan-generated report as proof of value:
   - 1-2 candidate examples showing how the tool works
   - Anonymized but realistic profiles
   - How the tool evaluated them

5. **Measure:**
   - Applications received
   - Conversion to Careerspan profiles (%)
   - Engagement (how many candidates complete full profile?)

**Success criteria:** 50+ applications, 30%+ conversion to profiles
