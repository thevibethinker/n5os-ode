# Meeting Recap: Vrijen x Ilya Sales Coaching Session

**Attendees:** Vrijen (Careerspan Founder/CEO), Ilya (Sales Mentor, 20+ years experience)  
**Duration:** ~1 hour  
**Focus:** Sales strategy for Careerspan's hiring platform

## Overview
Ilya provided comprehensive sales coaching on how Vrijen can transition from a relationship-based sales approach to a consultative, execution-oriented strategy. The discussion covered CEO sales responsibilities, customer psychology, and a specific "Gangster Move" outreach tactic.

## Key Discussion Areas

### 1. CEO as Top Salesman (Foundation)
Ilya established that **the CEO must be the best salesman** in any company, using Elon Musk as an example. He emphasized CEOs who succeed are those who:
- Memorize value propositions at multiple depths (1-sentence, 2-sentence, full paragraph)
- Can articulate their message authentically in any context (supermarket checkout to formal meeting)
- Avoid sounding scripted while remaining consistent

**Vrijen's Reflection:** He acknowledged being comfortable with fundamentals but struggling with follow-through and final conversion due to imposter syndrome about not having corporate experience.

### 2. Psychology of Sales vs. Shopping
Central insight: **People hate being sold to, but love to buy.** 

Ilya illustrated this with evidence:
- Email spam filters, caller ID, billion-dollar institutions exist because people reject being sold to
- Black Friday deaths demonstrate people's love of shopping
- The key difference: Agency. Buyers feel they have control and choice; sales feels manipulative.

**Application to Careerspan:**
- **Inbound:** Let digital "bait" (website, G2 badges, content) attract prospects naturally
- **Outbound:** Create perception of agency—prospects must feel they're choosing to engage

### 3. Sales Tactics & Positioning

**Value Proposition Layers:**
- What Careerspan is NOT: "We're not another resume channel. We won't flood you with 1,000 resumes per role."
- What Careerspan IS: "We give you clarity into candidates no one else can provide. You see what you get—WYSIWYG."
- **Momentum trigger:** "We expected 100 employers, we got 1,000 this year."

**Outbound Framing for Series A Companies:**
The key pain point is **no false starts**—hiring missteps are catastrophic at Series A with investors breathing down their neck.
- Position as: "We eliminate false starts. You see deep clarity into candidates before hiring."
- Counter objection: "Even HR veterans make mistakes when tools are limited. This gives you the tool to get it right."

### 4. The "Gangster Move" Strategy
Ilya's most powerful concept: **Show up with solved problems before asking for anything.**

Example from his past: At a cybersecurity firm, he identified threats a prospect's own team was missing, packaged them in a compelling email with proof, and sent it unsolicited. The prospect's product manager opened it and forwarded to 20+ people internally within 2 hours—including company leadership.

**Application to Careerspan (discussed):**
- Identify companies that repost the same job (signals hiring failure)
- Run Careerspan's matching process on those roles
- Send a short video showing: "You reposted this role. We ran our tech on it. Here's the caliber of candidate you could be seeing."
- Include 1-2 anonymized example candidates
- Frame as: "I'm the founder. No strings attached. If you like what you see, we can talk."

**Key principle:** Sales pitch becomes 2 seconds—the value is undeniable.

### 5. Talent Call / Bait Job Strategy
Vrijen proposed a potential innovation: **Create "talent calls" without specific job posts.**

- Target McKinsey founder cohort job descriptions
- Post anonymized "talent calls" (e.g., "Looking for Senior PM")
- Create a general interest form for candidates
- Use Careerspan's tool to advocate for respondents effectively
- Frame explicitly: "We're scouting talent, not promising a specific role"
- Include social proof and a sample Careerspan-generated report

**Ethical considerations raised:**
- Need permission from hiring companies for job reposting
- Risk of "zombie postings" if companies don't authorize distribution
- Must secure consent from Marvin (their investor/partner)

**Path forward:** Explicit framing + consent = legitimate talent pipeline

### 6. Authenticity & Emotional Core
The conversation ended with deeper reflection on why Careerspan matters:
- Candidates historically aren't asked: "How was your day?"
- Many people never learned to advocate for themselves or take up space
- Careerspan solves a fundamental human problem: **being heard**
- This must remain authentic and central to messaging

Ilya reflected that truly great CEOs succeed because their care for the customer's problem is genuine and evident.

## Marketing Insights
Ilya shared that in past campaigns:
- Simply saying "new" outperformed technical claims like "AI" or "machine learning"
- Authenticity beats packaging
- Enthusiasm and genuine belief in the solution is the ultimate conversion tool

- Develop "Gangster Move" proof of concept (identify reposted role, create demo)
- Explore talent call strategy with proper consent framework
- Balance between consultative positioning and follow-through discipline
