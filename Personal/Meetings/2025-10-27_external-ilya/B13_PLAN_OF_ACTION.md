## PHASE 2: NEAR-TERM (Nov 4 - Dec 31, ~8 weeks)

**Focus**: Execute "juice the pipeline" strategy - generate authentic market response signals

**Specific Actions**:
1. **Pilot customer acquisition campaign**: Targeted outreach (warm network + cold) to get 10+ businesses using product for at least one hire
   - **Tactics TBD in Phase 1**: Could include warm intros through Logan/V/Ilse networks, targeted LinkedIn outreach, small paid campaign, conference/community presence
   - **Success metric**: 10+ active pilots with at least one hire processed per company
   - **"Juice" element**: Document conversion mechanics (what message resonated, what customer profile converted, what objections came up) to demonstrate market understanding to acquirers

2. **Case study/testimonial capture**: Extract feedback from pilot customers (good and bad) to demonstrate product iteration velocity
   - **Success metric**: 3-5 written case studies OR video testimonials showing real hiring outcomes
   - **Acquirer value**: Proves product works in real world, not just demo environment

3. **Pipeline building**: Identify 20-50 businesses with loose commitments to "use you for next hire"
   - **Success metric**: Documented pipeline with contact info, hiring timeline, warm relationship
   - **Acquirer value**: Demonstrates repeatable customer acquisition motion and future revenue potential

4. **Messaging refinement**: A/B test variations from Phase 1, double down on what converts
   - **Success metric**: Clear "winning" message with conversion data to back it up
   - **Acquirer value**: Shows company knows its positioning and can articulate value to market

**If-Then Logic**:
- IF warm network yields 5+ pilots quickly → prioritize case study capture and testimonial gathering
- IF warm network is tapped out → shift to cold outreach tactics and/or small paid acquisition
- IF pilot feedback reveals product gaps → decide whether to iterate product OR shift target customer to better fit current functionality
- IF one particular messaging variant converts significantly better → pivot all outreach to that framing

**Resource Constraints**:
- Timeline: 8 weeks to generate 10 pilots (aggressive but achievable with founder networks)
- Budget: TBD based on Phase 1 financial runway discussion
- Team time: Logan "miraculously free" schedule, V availability TBD, Ilse available for product support
