## 2. Leadership Persuasion Blurb

**Use case:** Package to present to leadership to secure partnership approval and long-term commitment

**Draft:**

**Careerspan for Emory Alumni: A Strategic Partnership Opportunity**

**Executive Summary**

Careerspan offers Emory a unique opportunity to deepen alumni engagement while delivering tangible career value at scale. Our AI-powered career coaching platform has demonstrated strong engagement and outcomes across multiple higher education partnerships, including Columbia SPS and UMass Amherst, with measurable impact on user advocacy and career discovery.

**Key Outcomes from Pilot Programs**

**UMass Amherst Study (100+ students):**
- 92% of students reported Careerspan positively helped them advocate for themselves
- 80% discovered new experiences and opportunities they hadn't previously considered
- Users engaged in hour-long coaching sessions, indicating deep commitment to the tool

**Columbia SPS Partnership:**
- Sustained engagement with 2-4% of users returning monthly for active coaching
- Strong retention with users reactivating and returning months after initial signup
- Cross-industry adoption from executive-level professionals to early-career talent

**Strategic Value for Emory**

1. **Alumni Engagement:** Careerspan provides an ongoing touchpoint that keeps alumni connected to Emory through meaningful career support — not just transactional asks.

2. **Differentiated Alumni Value:** In a crowded landscape of alumni benefits, Careerspan offers something genuinely useful: personalized career coaching that scales without human resource constraints.

3. **Data-Driven Insights:** We can provide detailed analytics on alumni career interests, engagement patterns, and outcomes, informing future programming and development efforts.

4. **Cost-Effective:** At $4-6 per hour of coaching, Careerspan delivers professional-quality guidance at a fraction of traditional coaching costs — approximately $1-2 per 20-minute coaching session.

5. **Community Building:** Our hiring-focused approach allows alumni to post jobs specifically for the Emory community, creating a virtuous cycle where alumni hire from Emory and Emory alumni find opportunities through the network.

**Proposed Pilot Structure**

- **Timeline:** Launch before holidays (mid-December 2024) with a 4-6 week observation period
- **Scale:** Initial target of 500 alumni, with flexibility to scale based on demand
- **Pricing:** Nominal licensing fee ($3-4 per alum) to align incentives and validate model, with potential for first 500 free to accelerate adoption
- **Success Metrics:**
  - 30+ day retention (benchmark: 30% from existing user base)
  - Engagement rate (benchmark: 40% story completion from new signups)
  - Alumni satisfaction and sentiment
  - Secondary engagement with Emory programs and initiatives

**Long-Term Vision**

Beyond the pilot, we envision a full-fledged partnership where Careerspan becomes an integral part of the Emory alumni experience:
- White-labeled branding to reinforce Emory connection
- Integrated email and engagement channels on behalf of Emory
- Alumni-to-alumni hiring marketplace built on the Emory network
- Ongoing data sharing to inform alumni strategy

**Next Steps**

We're prepared to launch immediately and will deliver a comprehensive persuasion package including:
- Detailed results from Columbia and UMass partnerships
- Testimonials from current users (including Emory alumni)
- Usage analytics and engagement benchmarks
- Implementation plan for pilot rollout

We believe this partnership aligns with Emory's commitment to alumni success and creates a durable competitive advantage in alumni engagement. We're excited to move forward and would welcome the opportunity to discuss how Careerspan can serve Emory's strategic goals.
