# B06: Business Context

## Company Overview

**Careerspan** is a human data company that transforms qualitative career conversations into structured, actionable data for employers. The platform collects first-party, opted-in candidate information through career coaching interactions, then enables employers to scan against this synthetic talent pipeline.

**Current traction:**
- 4,000+ users on the platform
- 2,000+ career stories recorded
- Demonstrated user engagement at scale

## Business Model

**Core value proposition:**
- **For employers:** Access to high-signal, first-party talent data that can be scanned and matched against job descriptions in real-time
- **For candidates:** Career coaching that results in richer, more discoverable professional profiles
- **Data advantage:** First-party, consented data vs. third-party scraped data

**Use cases:**
1. **External hiring (sourcing/matching):** Employers scan Careerspan's talent pool to find qualified candidates for open roles
2. **Internal mobility:** Large organizations identify internal talent for new opportunities or career development

**Revenue model:** Not explicitly stated in transcript; inferred as B2B platform or data access model
