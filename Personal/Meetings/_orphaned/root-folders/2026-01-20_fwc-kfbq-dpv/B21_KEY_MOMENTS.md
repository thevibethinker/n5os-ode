— Vrijen Attawar  
*Significance: This is the core strategic question underlying the entire AI-in-recruiting discussion. It surfaces the fundamental crisis of trust that AI creates for hiring systems. The question isn't just technical—it's existential for how organizations evaluate human capital.*

**"If I'm human and I make a judgment based on a CV, I also will fall for the faker trick?"**  
— Vrijen Attawar  
*Significance: Challenges the assumption that human judgment is inherently superior to AI judgment when both face the same problem (AI-generated fakery). Forces reconsideration of what "verification" actually means.*
