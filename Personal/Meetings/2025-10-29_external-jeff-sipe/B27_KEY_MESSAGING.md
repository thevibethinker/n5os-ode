## Messaging Don'ts

**❌ Don't Say** (to Agencies):
- "Eventually you'll compete with other agencies for talent" (too early—would scare them off)
- "We're experimenting with agency channel" (sounds uncommitted)
- "Our matching algorithm is proprietary" (they don't care about tech, care about results)

**❌ Don't Say** (to Jeff):
- "We're still figuring out if agency channel makes sense" (waste of his time)
- "Can you just make some intros and we'll figure it out?" (disrespects his time/network)
- "We need you to do the sales work" (if that's expectation, equity required)

**❌ Don't Say** (to Internal):
- "This is optional nice-to-have" (either commit or don't—half measures waste everyone's time)
- "Jeff will do all the work" (we need product readiness and operational capacity)

## Objection Handling

**Objection**: "Why not just use LinkedIn Recruiter?"
**Response**: "LinkedIn is where everyone goes—that's the problem. It's saturated. You need differentiated access to talent, not the same pool everyone else is fishing in. Our experience-based profiles give you candidates LinkedIn doesn't surface."

**Objection**: "Commission splits seem high"
**Response**: "Compare to your cost per hire through other channels. If we deliver qualified candidates that place faster with higher success rates, the commission is a bargain. Plus, zero upfront risk—you only pay on successful placements."

**Objection**: "We already have our own sourcing team"
**Response**: "Exactly—and they're spending hours filtering through unqualified applicants. We're not replacing your team; we're giving them refined gold instead of ore. Your team focuses on what they're good at (closing placements), we handle the sourcing and filtering."

**Objection**: "How do I know your candidates are actually better quality?"
**Response**: "Two reasons: (1) Experience-based profiles are much harder to fake than resumes—we validate through work history, not self-reported skills. (2) Start with one role. If our candidates aren't higher quality than your current pipeline, you owe us nothing."
