**What We Learned**:

**Evidence**:

- Premium pricing justified for hard-to-fill positions

- Long-term: Position for "talent agent model" where top talent commands athlete-level representation

**Confidence Level**: HIGH - Macro trend visible across recruiting industry

## Insight 5: Resume-Based Matching Hitting Limits ●●●●○

**Category**: Competitive Landscape & Product Differentiation  
**Signal Strength**: 4/5 (Confirmed - Industry limitation)

**What We Learned**:
Current recruiting tech (including competitors like Superposition) relies heavily on resume-based matching. Resumes are easily doctored—"everyone lies." Experience-based profiles are "much harder to deceive" and represent sustainable differentiation.

**Evidence**:
- Superposition has recruiting side but needs better matching technology
- General industry frustration with resume accuracy
- Jeff identifies experience profiles as Careerspan's long-run moat
- Measurement effectiveness (validating candidate claims) is differentiator

**Business Implications**:
- Experience-based profiles = core IP to protect
- Don't make it easy for competitors to replicate (no open API initially)
- Measurement infrastructure (verifying candidate experience) compounds advantage
- This becomes more valuable as candidate pool grows

**Confidence Level**: HIGH - Industry-wide limitation that Careerspan has addressed
