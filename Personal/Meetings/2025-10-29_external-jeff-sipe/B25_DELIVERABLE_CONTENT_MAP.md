### CONTENT 1: "Why Recruiting Agencies Are Desperate" (Thought Leadership)
**Format**: Blog post or LinkedIn article  
**Angle**: Market analysis on collapse of traditional sourcing channels  
**Key Points**:

- Agencies "out of ideas for how to get to talent"

**Audience**: Recruiting agency decision-makers, industry observers

### CONTENT 3: "The Talent Agent Model Is Coming" (Future Vision)
**Format**: Long-form article or presentation  
**Angle**: Where recruiting industry is headed in 5-10 years  
**Key Thesis**:
- Wage polarization accelerating (fewer, higher-paid roles)
- Top talent will command athlete-level representation
- Recruiting will evolve from "HR function" to "talent agent" model
- Platforms that enable this transformation will win

**Audience**: Forward-thinking agencies, VCs, industry analysts

**Strategic Purpose**: Position Careerspan as infrastructure for talent agent future

**Timing**: Not urgent; 6-12 month timeline

**Format**: One-page case study
