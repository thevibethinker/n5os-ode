1. **Validation of Market Thesis**: Three recruiting agencies (Superposition, LaunchSam, Matchbox) actively seeking Careerspan's talent pools validates that traditional recruiting tech is insufficient

2. **Human Psychology Barrier**: Jeff's 2010 story illustrates that even with clear ROI proof, recruiters won't adopt new tools due to loss aversion - suggests need for different go-to-market approach

3. **Long-term Vision Alignment**: Both parties see future of talent market moving toward "talent agent" model with fewer, higher-paid workers

4. **Strategic Network**: Jeff positioned himself as having established recruiting relationships AND having identified this exact market opportunity 2 years prior
