## Strategic Context

### Market Dynamics Creating Opportunity

**Recruiting Agency Desperation**: Traditional sourcing channels (LinkedIn, job boards) are saturated and ineffective. Agencies "out of ideas for how to get to talent" with inbound being "terrible." This creates urgent need for new talent access methods—exactly what Careerspan provides.

**Wage Polarization Accelerating**: Vrijen's 2025 thesis validated by Jeff—"senior hiring is going up and being paid more, junior hiring is cratering." Market moving toward fewer, extremely high-paid roles. This long-term trend supports eventual "talent agent model" where top talent commands athlete-level representation.

**Resume-Based Matching Hitting Limits**: Current recruiting tech (including Superposition, LaunchSam, Matchbox) lacks ability to distinguish quality candidates despite claims. Resumes are easily fabricated. Careerspan's experience-based profiles are "much harder to deceive mechanism" and "measure more effectively"—this is the long-term moat.

### Why This Matters Now

Three recruiting agencies (Superposition, LaunchSam, Matchbox) actively interested in Careerspan partnerships. They're motivated but lack good technology. Jeff's recruiter network is "fucking crazy" and he's ready to open doors. Timing is optimal because:
1. Agencies are desperate (traditional channels failing)
2. Product validation exists (magic linking working for founders)
3. Network access available (Jeff's relationships)
4. Differentiation is real (experience-based vs. resume-based matching)

### Positioning Evolution

**Short-term**: "Refined gold not ore"—Careerspan as quality filtering layer for agencies
**Long-term**: Marketplace where recruiters compete for top talent, with Careerspan taking commission from both sides (recruiter + talent), similar to sports agents or Hollywood talent agents
