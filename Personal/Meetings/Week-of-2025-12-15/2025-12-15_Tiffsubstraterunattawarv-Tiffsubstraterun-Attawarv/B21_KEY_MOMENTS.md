# B21 – Key Moments

   - Vrijen articulates the gap: “there’s no really good tool for staying on top of your networking.”
   - He describes Zo as uniquely able to **blend data from different sources** (email, LinkedIn, etc.) and support cadence‑based outreach to mentors, former managers, and other contacts.

   - Vrijen commits to **posting the event into Friends of Next Play** and asks for a link he can track (Short.io), turning a conceptual opportunity into measurable action.

5. **Seeding the AI Collective intro path**
