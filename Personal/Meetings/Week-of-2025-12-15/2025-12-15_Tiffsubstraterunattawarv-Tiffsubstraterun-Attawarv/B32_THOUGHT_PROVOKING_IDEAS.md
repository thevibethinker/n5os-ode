# Thought Provoking Ideas (B32)

### 1. The "App Store for Enrichment"
V's proposal to treat contact enrichment as a referral marketplace (Aviato cut) shifts Zo from a "Personal AI" to a "Platform Intermediary." It suggests Zo can curate a user's *data supply chain*, not just their personal tasks.

### 2. Networking as Data Blending
The idea that the "Social Graph" is currently broken because it's siloed in LinkedIn/Gmail/Text. If Zo becomes the *canonical join* for human relationships, it transforms from a tool into a "Personal CRM Operating System."

### 3. Community "Inventory" Management
V's framing of 80k members as "Inventory for events" treats community access as a commodity that can be tapped at specific intervals (every 3 weeks/month). This suggests a shift toward a programmatic approach to community-led growth (CLG).
