# B25 – Deliverables

> **Scope:** Concrete artifacts, tools, or materials discussed as outcomes or enablers of this meeting.

## 1. Networking framework write-up
- **Owner:** David
- **Description:** A written articulation of the three networking arenas (job-search, internal, personal/keep‑in‑touch) with example workflows and rules.
- **Purpose:** Serves as the conceptual spec for how the Zo kit should be structured and what behaviors it should encourage.

## 2. Example message and follow-up library
- **Owner:** David
- **Description:** A small library of anonymized or lightly-edited outreach and follow-up messages that have worked well across different scenarios.
- **Purpose:** Seed content for Zo personas, templates, and prompt snippets used by the system.

## 3. Pilot program outline
- **Owner:** V
- **Description:** A short document describing a proposed pilot series (sessions, outcomes, prerequisites, expected time investment) for communities like Next Play.
- **Purpose:** Basis for conversations with Ben Lang, the Zo team, and potential sponsors.

## 4. Zo kit specification draft
- **Owner:** V (with input from David)
- **Description:** A rough specification of the files, agents, tags, and integrations that would constitute the initial networking kit (e.g., person records, cadence rules, follow-up queues).
- **Purpose:** Bridges from conceptual framework to implementable Zo artifacts.

## 5. Partner/sponsor prospect list
- **Owner:** V & David (shared)
- **Description:** A shortlist of vendors (e.g., Condo, enrichment tools) and communities who might co‑sponsor or co‑market the series.
- **Purpose:** Inform outreach and help determine what optional integrations the kit should support from day one.
