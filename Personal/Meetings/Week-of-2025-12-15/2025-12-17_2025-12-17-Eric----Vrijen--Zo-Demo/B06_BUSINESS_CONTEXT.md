## Market Intelligence
- **VC Talent Sourcing:** Pre-seed/Seed VCs (like Consonant) are heavily burdened by "hands-on" support for portfolio companies, specifically in recruiting.
- **AI Sourcing Limits:** Experienced recruiters/investors are skeptical of AI for *evaluation* (spotting red flags), even if they accept it for *sourcing* (finding names).
- **Tool Fragmentation:** Users want to build "simple" things (scrapers) to avoid paying for multiple SaaS subscriptions (e.g., LinkedIn job change trackers).
