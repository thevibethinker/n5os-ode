# B01: Detailed Recap

## Overview
Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan) hosted a demo of Zo for Eric Rubin (Consonant VC). Eric, a non-technical VC who invested in the founders of Zo (Ben and Rob) during their substrate days, is interested in how Zo can enhance his productivity and investment workflows. Vrijen demonstrated how Zo enables non-technical users to build powerful agentic systems.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. The Value of Zo for Non-Technical Users
- Vrijen emphasized that everything he built on Zo was achieved without writing code, pushing the boundaries of what non-technical people can accomplish.
- He compared Zo favorably to other systems like Magnus, Cursor, and Lovable, noting its unique blend of UI, server, and agentic orchestration.

### 2. Eric's Current Workflows and Pains
- Eric operates as a "two-person shop" with limited resources.
- He currently uses Zo for simple tasks: Sunday SMS reminders for inbox look-backs and weekly meeting summaries.
- He expressed interest in building a LinkedIn scraper to track job changes in his network but wants to avoid the cost of commercial tools.
- A significant portion of his time is spent manually sourcing candidates for portfolio companies, looking for "disgruntled consultants" or early-stage startup employees ready for a move.

### 3. Sourcing and Filtering Challenges
- Eric finds manual sourcing necessary because he trusts his ability to spot "red flags" and "clues" in profiles that he believes AI might miss.
- Vrijen suggested that while automation (via APIs like Aviato) is possible, the core issue is that high-quality candidates often don't keep their profiles updated.

### 4. Careerspan as a Solution
- Vrijen pitched Careerspan as a complement to Eric's sourcing process.
- He described Careerspan as a "bat signal" that uses conversational coaching to friction-filter candidates, providing 10x more data than traditional forms.
- This would allow Eric to focus on high-signal candidates rather than manual filtering.

### 5. Advanced Zo Use Cases: Meeting Processing
- Vrijen detailed his own Zo-built meeting processing system.
- The system connects directly to the Fireflies API, extracts transcripts, and generates intelligence blocks automatically.
- This allows Vrijen to maintain high-fidelity records and stay on top of follow-ups without manual effort.
