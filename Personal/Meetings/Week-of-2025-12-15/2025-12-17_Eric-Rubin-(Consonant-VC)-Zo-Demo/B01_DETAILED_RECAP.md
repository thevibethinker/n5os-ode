# B01: Detailed Recap - Eric Rubin (Consonant VC) Zo Demo

## Overview

Vrijen met with Eric Rubin, founder of Consonant VC, for a demonstration of Vrijen's advanced Zo Computer configuration. Eric is an investor in Substrate (Zo) via his previous  fund and a current power user of the platform. The conversation spanned neighborhood advice, venture capital operations, hiring pain points, and a deep dive into Careerspan's value proposition.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Relocation & Brooklyn Advice

Eric is planning a move from the Upper West Side to Brooklyn in May/June, contingent on his fiance's medical residency placement (likely near Park Slope or Bushwick). Vrijen provided strategic neighborhood advice, recommending the Atlantic Ave/Downtown Brooklyn area for its connectivity, especially the 2/3 and G train accessibility.

### 2. Consonant VC & Zo Usage

Eric launched Consonant VC last year with an engineering partner. As a non-technical founder, Eric uses Zo for:

- **Sunday Lookbacks:** An automated SMS trigger that reviews his inbox and identifies pending responses.
- **Weekly Planning:** Summarizing his upcoming calendar via text.\
  He expressed a desire to unlock more complex workflows, particularly for LP relations and portfolio company (portco) support.

### 3. Hiring & Portfolio Support

Eric spends significant time manually sourcing talent for portcos on LinkedIn. He looks for specific profiles (e.g., consultants or startup veterans ready for a pivot) but finds current AI tools struggle with "sniffing out red flags" that humans catch easily. Vrijen introduced Careerspan as a "bat signal" for high-signal candidates, emphasizing its ability to filter for values and soft skills through conversational coaching rather than keyword matching.

### 4. Zo Advanced Workflows (Demo)

Vrijen demonstrated several high-leverage Zo capabilities:

- **Meeting Processing:** Connecting directly to Fireflies API to ingest transcripts and generate custom recaps.
- **Follow-up Generation:** Automated creation of "double opt-in" intro emails and blurbs.
- **Personas:** Using the "Strategist" and "Writer" personas for decision-making and content drafting.
- **Due Diligence:** A "Deep Research" flow that Eric noted would be highly valuable for LP pitching.

### 5. Personal Networking & Melissa's Job Search

The conversation turned to Vrijen's sister, Melissa, who is pivoting from hospitality/catering into food tech. They discussed companies like Resident, Culanistas (Katie Zion), and Freshline (Joseph). Eric offered to keep an eye out, and Vrijen committed to making introductions.

## Conclusion

The meeting reinforced the "generational" potential of Zo and established a strong strategic alignment between Eric's VC needs and Vrijen's Careerspan solution.
