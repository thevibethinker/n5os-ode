# B03: Decisions Made

## Strategic Decisions

### 1. Direct-Apply Product Focus (CONFIRMED)
**Decision:** Commit fully to direct-apply-centric product strategy  
**Rationale:** Users are confused by mixed messaging about general job help vs. specific company applications  
**Implementation:**
- Messaging across all touchpoints: "Prove yourself to [Company], not to Careerspan"
- UX flow redirects users directly to application page (not homepage)
- Onboarding emphasizes story-telling for specific roles
- Secondary use case (general job help) moved to "back of room" positioning

**Owner:** Ilse/Rochel (product); Ilya (messaging)  
**Timeline:** Immediate (prioritize over other features)

---

### 2. Resource Allocation: UX > Features
**Decision:** Invest remaining dev capacity in UX improvements, not new features  
**Rationale:** Funnel analysis shows friction at critical drop-off points (30 viewed, 5 applied)  
**What This Means:**
- Rochel focuses on application page landing and flow improvements
- Conversation improvements can proceed without backend dev (Ilse leading)
- Feature development de-prioritized
- Conrad's LinkedIn data exploration deferred unless strategic acquisition opportunity emerges

**Owner:** V/Logan (prioritization); Rochel (execution)  
**Impact:** Measurable: aim to increase Skillcraft applicant flow from 5 to healthy conversion rate

---

### 3. Candidate Outreach Campaign
**Decision:** Email non-applicants who viewed but didn't apply to understand friction  
**Details:**
- Target: ~30 people who clicked magic link + completed onboarding but didn't apply
- Approach: Both conversation option and written-feedback alternative
- Focus: Those with existing stories prioritized (higher intent signal)
- Owner: Logan (primary); open to Rochel assisting if available
- Follow-up: Rahul (product feedback) to join if users agree to calls

**Insight Sought:** Is drop-off due to UX friction, time commitment concern, or job mismatch?  
**Decision Logic:** Easy data point to validate funnel hypothesis before large UX redesign

---

### 4. Terminology & Messaging Refresh
**Decision:** Rebrand "Vibe Check" to "Pre-flight Check" (or similar)  
**Rationale:** "Vibe check" doesn't convey the actual purpose for direct-apply jobs  
**New Framing:** "Are you sure this role is worth your time? Here's what to tell stories about..."  
**Owner:** Ilse/Ilya  
**Scope:** Update language across:
- Job board descriptions
- Onboarding flow
- Notion templates (fix sync-block issue)
- Application instructions

---

### 5. Job Board Template Management
**Decision:** Do NOT use sync block for entire job description; generate per posting  
**Rationale:** Same JD appearing everywhere defeats purpose of individualized job listings  
**Current State:** Template updated but sync issue caused duplicate JDs  
**Implementation:** Either copy/paste template or template duplication (not sync block for JD)  
**Owner:** Rochel/Logan  
**Note:** Careerspan brief intro (separate from JD) should remain visible at bottom of each posting
