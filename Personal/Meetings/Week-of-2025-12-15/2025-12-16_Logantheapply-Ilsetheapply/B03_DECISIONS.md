# B03: Decisions

- **[D01] Strategic Pivot to Direct Apply:** The team will fully commit to an onboarding and messaging experience centric to "Direct Apply" / Magic Links. 
- **[D02] UX Re-prioritization:** Development energy will shift toward streamlining the application flow and the onboarding experience for "random" visitors arriving via job links, rather than general tool utility.
- **[D03] Holiday Coverage:** Ilsa is officially off from Dec 25th to Jan 2nd, with an "on-call only for fire" protocol.
- **[D04] Messaging Placement:** The Careerspan description will remain at the bottom of JDs, near the "click to apply" action, rather than in a separate top-level block, to minimize friction.
