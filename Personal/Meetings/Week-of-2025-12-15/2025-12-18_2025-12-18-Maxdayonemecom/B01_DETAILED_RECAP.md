# B01 Detailed Recap

Vrijen Attawar (V) and Max (Maximilian Kwok) met for a founder-to-founder "heart-to-heart" to discuss the state of hiring, AI's impact on the industry, and their respective company journeys.

Key Discussion Points:
- **Reflective Season**: Both noted the end-of-year atmosphere as a time for reflection and realignment. Max mentioned getting a "self-care" haircut.
- **Hiring Clusterfuck**: V expressed a strong belief that hiring is about to become a "clusterfuck" due to AI forces. He believes the industry is wrong to spend less time on candidates; instead, they should ask more of them to get better data.
- **Careerspan Origin**: V shared his journey from teaching test prep in Singapore to McKinsey, and how the launch of ChatGPT made him realize the importance of self-representation. Careerspan started as a coaching tool and is now pivoting to a matching engine/job board to charge employers.
- **Data Structure Issues**: Both agreed that the current resume-based data structure (ATS) is inherently flawed. Max noted that US hiring is more slog-like compared to the fluidity he experienced in Hong Kong/Singapore.
- **Human Element**: V emphasized that wrong hires kill companies, while the right hire (like their product designer) can save one.
- **Networking**: They discussed the "stink" of working overseas and having to prove oneself again in the US.
