# B06: Business Context

## Careerspan
- **Status:** Post-pivot (shifting from B2C career coaching to B2B employer talent signal).
- **Business Model:** Charging employers to access high-signal talent assessment data generated through candidate self-articulation/coaching.
- **Value Prop:** solving the "vacuum of fundamental candidate information" by asking for more qualitative data, not less.

## Day One (Max's Org)
- **Context:** An organization (Day One) focused on founder support and community.
- **Market Position:** Focused on high-signal talent and founder development; likely an ecosystem partner for Careerspan.
