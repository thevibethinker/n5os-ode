# B03 Decisions

- **Target Audience Pivot**: Focus initially on 'Option Maximizers' (Type A individuals not currently job hunting but looking to maintain network warmth) rather than just active job seekers.
- **Webinar Format**: Structure the session as: 20 minutes of 'plugging in' building blocks (pre-set instructions/files) followed by active execution/networking.
- **Data Source Strategy**: Acknowledge that historical data isn't critical for the 'Option Maximizer' group; the system can start 'fresh' from the point of installation.
- **Tone Methodology**: Use the 'neutral base + stylistic shift' transformation approach as the standard for generating high-quality outreach.
