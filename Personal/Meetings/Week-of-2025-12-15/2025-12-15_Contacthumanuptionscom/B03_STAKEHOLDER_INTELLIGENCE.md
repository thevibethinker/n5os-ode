# Stakeholder Intelligence (B03)

## 1. Kamina Singh — External champion and event operator

**Role / Position**
- Founder and operator of a high‑impact, multi‑school career networking series in New York.
- Trusted advisor and long‑time champion of Careerspan; has already invested significant time, relationship capital, and reputational risk.

**Interests & Motivations**
- Deeply mission‑driven around social mobility and outcomes for historically underserved students (CUNY, HBCUs, public institutions).
- Wants partners who *actually* deliver outcomes for students, not box‑checking programming.
- Values authenticity and hard work over institutional politics; strongly allergic to “excuse making” by career centers.
- Open to meaningful financial upside (commission on funding/acquisition), but frames money as secondary to impact and usefulness.

**Frustrations / Skepticism**
- Highly frustrated with higher‑ed administrators who: 
  - Refuse to pay small amounts (e.g., $20 tickets) while students pay six‑figure tuition.
  - Hide behind process, finals schedules, or union concerns instead of serving students.
  - Move at a “snail’s pace” on obviously valuable student‑facing tools.
- Skeptical of institutions that fail to act even when proof points (student testimonials, reviews) are overwhelming.

**Leverage Points**
- Credibility with students (hundreds of direct testimonials, 200+ reviews from the last event).
- Access to:
  - University of Mount Saint Vincent leadership (including Lawrence, now on staff).
  - BMCC and St. Peter’s decision‑makers.
  - National workforce organizations (Per Scholas, COOP, ASA, Strada).
- Willingness to proactively campaign for Careerspan if she has strong, clear metrics and collateral.
- Moral authority: she works extremely hard and expects the same; calling out institutional under‑performance resonates with funders and reformers.

**Risk Notes**
- Bandwidth is finite; she is juggling family health stresses, event operations, and multiple advisory relationships.
- If Careerspan dithers or fails to follow through on agreed next steps (email blast, metrics pack), her energy could redirect elsewhere.

**Risk Notes**
