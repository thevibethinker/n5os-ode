# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The "Game Hasn't Started" Paradox**: The idea that being "late" in a startup sense (timing, burn, exhaustion) can coexist with being "early" in a market sense (institutional incompetence leaving a total vacuum). The founder's internal clock vs. the market's stagnant clock.
- **Institutional Shame as a Sales Lever**: Kamina’s strategy of "shaming" career offices by name-dropping their peers in a list they aren't on. It suggests that in slow-moving bureaucracies, the fear of being left behind is more powerful than the desire for the product itself.
- **PR Exit vs. Value Exit**: The distinction between an acquisition that "looks good" (PR exit) and one that "pays well." The strategic decision to prioritize an exit that provides stability for the team/advisors even if the founders "missed the boat" on a massive windfall.
