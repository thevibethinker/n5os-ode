# B03 Decisions

This block captures the explicit decisions made during the pre-meeting sync regarding product adjustments and operational priorities.

## Product & User Experience
- **OpenAI Priority Queue Migration**: The team decided to move application processing (currently taking 10-15 minutes) to OpenAI's priority queue to improve speed and user experience, despite the decision potentially doubling application processing costs.
- **Story Completion Strategy**: Agreed to implement a "T-shaped" approach for story completion—collecting quick top-level basics first before selectively drilling into deeper details (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to reduce perceived friction for users.
- **UX over Analytics**: In a direct trade-off between fixing sign-up tracking (behavioral stats) and improving the applicant process UI/UX, the decision was made to prioritize UI/UX improvements to support current sales efforts.

## Data Collection
- **Requirement Visibility**: Decided to make it significantly clearer to users when they have satisfied "required" questions so they know they can move on without 100% completion of optional fields.
- **Audit of Dropout Behavior**: Agreed to investigate specific user data to determine if users are dropping out because they are voluntarily continuing into optional questions past the required minimum.

## Operational
- **Microsoft Teams Usage**: Confirmed the use of the Microsoft Teams web version for the upcoming Darwinbox meeting to avoid installation issues.
