# B06: Business Context

### Market Positioning: Darwinbox
Darwinbox is a unicorn HRMS provider primarily serving the Indian and SE Asian markets (Cambodia mentioned as an example). They are currently in a "diversification" phase, investing heavily in the US. Their technology is characterized as "helping process a lot of people" rather than high-end boutique HR tech.

### Product Philosophy
Careerspan (V's company) is facing a core architectural tension: **High-Fidelity Data vs. Low-Friction Entry**.
- The current system collects 9 required points plus optional deep dives.
- There is a hypothesis that users are over-extending themselves in the optional phase, leading to fatigue.
- The "Business Journaling" model (incremental collection over weeks) is identified as the ideal long-term solution, but the current market is not yet ready for it, necessitating a faster "one-shot" collection fix.
