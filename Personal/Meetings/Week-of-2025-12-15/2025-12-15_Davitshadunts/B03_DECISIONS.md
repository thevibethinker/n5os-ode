# Decisions (B03)

1. **Deep Tune is the primary near‑term target**
   - **Decision:** Among the Marvin Ventures roles discussed, Deep Tune will be the main short‑term focus because it explicitly supports visa sponsorship, unlike Yarn.
   - **Rationale:** Align pipeline with Davit's non‑negotiable immigration constraint while still pursuing a high‑quality technical role.

2. **Re‑engage Holly with a reset narrative**
   - **Decision:** V will re‑open the loop with Holly (the Deep Tune recruiter), clarify the prior scheduling mishaps, and re‑position Davit as a serious candidate.
   - **Rationale:** Without an explicit reset, Holly may misinterpret the missed calls as low reliability and quietly drop Davit.

3. **Use Careerspan as the canonical profile for advocacy**
   - **Decision:** Davit will continue adding stories and will run the Deep Tune, True Meter, and Zo roles through Careerspan; V will use the resulting narratives and fit scores as the primary material when advocating.
   - **Rationale:** A rich, structured profile is more persuasive to founders and recruiters than a generic CV alone.

4. **Leverage Warmer Jobs selectively**
   - **Decision:** Davit will consider signing up for Warmer Jobs, upload his LinkedIn connections, and add V so that V's first‑degree network can also surface opportunities.
   - **Rationale:** Combine Careerspan's depth (stories, fit) with Warmer Jobs' breadth (network‑indexed roles) to increase surface area for high‑quality introductions.

5. **Treat True Meter as a parallel AI‑native opportunity**
   - **Decision:** V will message Ali at True Meter about Davit's profile and keep True Meter's AI‑native backend roles in play, contingent on seeing strong Careerspan analyses for those roles.
   - **Rationale:** Diversify beyond a single recruiter‑mediated path while staying aligned with Davit's AI + backend strengths.

6. **Pursue Zo Computer if match score is strong**
   - **Decision:** Davit will explore the Zo infrastructure role via Careerspan, and if his score and narratives are compelling, V will personally recommend him to Zo's founders.
   - **Rationale:** Zo is a product V believes in and a place where his advocacy carries unusual weight; this is a potentially high‑leverage path for Davit.

7. **Shift resume customization into Careerspan**
   - **Decision:** Rather than manually maintaining ~8 resume variants, Davit will rely on Careerspan's resume generator once his stories and role analyses are complete.
   - **Rationale:** Reduce cognitive and time burden while raising quality and consistency of tailoring.
