He notes the difficulty of getting interviews due to visa sponsorship requirements and the high demand for "production-level" agentic/GenAI experience, which he missed due to being in school during the "boom."

V highlights the "Generate Resume" feature which semantically tailors resumes based on stories. Davit notes the pain of manually customizing resumes for 250+ applications. Davit agrees to spread the word about Careerspan to his struggling classmates at Columbia and within the Armenian community.
