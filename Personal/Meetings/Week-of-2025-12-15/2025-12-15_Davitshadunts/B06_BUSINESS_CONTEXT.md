# Business Context (B06)

## Careerspan & Marvin Ventures

- Careerspan operates as an AI‑assisted storytelling and hiring platform aimed at helping engineers articulate their experience via structured stories, then generating tailored narratives, resumes, and match scores for specific roles.
- V notes that Careerspan is an official talent partner for Marvin Ventures; many roles on the current job board come from this relationship.
- In this call, Careerspan is positioned both as:
  - A tool Davit can use directly (stories, STAR structure, resume generator).
  - An advocacy channel where V can represent Davit to founders and recruiters.

## Candidate & market conditions

- Davit is a full‑stack engineer with five years of industry experience plus current AI research at Columbia, but he lacks headline "production agentic AI" projects because he was in school when that ecosystem exploded.
- The macro environment is tough: he has already sent ~250 applications and sees many peers receiving few or no interviews.
- Visa sponsorship is a binding constraint; numerous early‑stage roles explicitly decline sponsorship, forcing a focus on the subset of companies (e.g., Deep Tune) that can support it.

## Employers and tools discussed

- **Deep Tune:** AI/engineering role where visa sponsorship is available; primary near‑term target, funneled through recruiter Holly and Marvin Ventures.
- **Yarn:** Initially interesting but ruled out due to lack of visa sponsorship.
- **True Meter:** Hiring multiple AI‑native backend engineers; V has a direct contact (Ali) and wants to recommend Davit contingent on strong role‑specific narratives.
- **Zo Computer:** Consumer‑facing AI product with an infrastructure role V is excited about; he has strong founder relationships and sees this as a high‑leverage option if fit is strong.
- **Warmer Jobs:** Separate paid tool where users upload LinkedIn connections to surface opportunities via first‑degree network; V offers Davit a discount code and proposes using it alongside Careerspan.

## Strategic angle for Careerspan

- This meeting functions as a live example of Careerspan's intended value proposition:
  - Turn scattered experience (industry + grad school) into coherent, role‑aligned narratives.
  - Reduce resume‑tailoring pain via semantically tailored resumes.
  - Use a trusted talent partner (V) and structured outputs (stories, fit scores) to open doors with founders and recruiters.
- Successful placement of Davit at Deep Tune, True Meter, or Zo would serve as a strong case study of Careerspan's ability to help highly‑qualified but constrained candidates break through in a difficult market.
