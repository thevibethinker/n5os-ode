# B06: Business Context

- **Business Model:** Careerspan / Apply AI platform facilitating job postings, applicant tracking, and "storytelling" (onboarding completion).
- **Tech Stack Issue:** Heavy reliance on Firebase (NoSQL) creates a "read explosion" problem for aggregate analytics. The system lacks a traditional relational database for roll-up data.
- **Growth Status:** Seeing an uptick in usage and distinct "two-story completion" patterns among users.
- **External Partners:** Skillcraft (David) is a key early founder user/profile.
