# B06 Business Context

### Teamwork Online
- **Model**: ATS/Job Board specifically for sports and entertainment.
- **Scale**: Connected to 1,500 organizations; 8 million candidate profiles.
- **Structure**: Family-founded (Buffy Philippel); currently led by Davis Philippel.
- **Vulnerability**: Potential disruption by job boards capturing top-of-funnel eyeballs.

### Careerspan Strategic Value
- **Valuation Mention**: V and Ilya discussed a theoretical valuation/exit target around $2M+ to return capital to investors.
- **Market Positioning**: Careerspan provides "100x visibility" into candidates, solving the "shallow data" problem inherent in traditional ATS platforms.
