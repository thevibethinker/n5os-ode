# B06: Business Context

- **Current Phase:** Traction building / "Hail Mary" phase. High focus on polish for potential "corporate benefactors."
- **Model:** Careerspan operates a job-seeking/application prep platform. Core value is reducing the time/effort of applications (e.g., "If you do two applications on Careerspan, you're already ahead").
- **Cost Structure:** Shifting from cost-minimization to experience-maximization ("sexy, not cheap"). Willing to eat higher API costs to reduce user friction.
- **Scale Indicator:** Monthly LLM spend is approaching/expected to hit the $3,500 - $5,000 range.
