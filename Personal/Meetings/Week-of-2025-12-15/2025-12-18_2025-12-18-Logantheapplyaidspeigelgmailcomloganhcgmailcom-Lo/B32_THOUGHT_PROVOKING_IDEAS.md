# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The "Venture Collective" Anti-Resentment Model:** The idea of building individually to *prevent* resentment and blocks while still maintaining a collective identity. This challenges the traditional startup co-founder model of shared ownership and shared risk.
- **Zo as an Enterprise Backend for Consumer Distribution:** Using Zo (a personal system) as the source of truth and backend for apps distributed through the ChatGPT App Store. This positions Zo not just as a workspace, but as a "headless" backend for consumer-facing AI products.
- **API Branding as Ecosystem Leverage:** The thesis that established brand names (like Aviato) can be "reference brands" in a new ecosystem (Zo) to create a moat, even if the underlying technology is similar to competitors.
