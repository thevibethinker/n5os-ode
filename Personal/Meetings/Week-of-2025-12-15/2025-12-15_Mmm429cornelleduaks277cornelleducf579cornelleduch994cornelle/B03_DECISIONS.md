# Decisions (B03)

> **Important:** This meeting was explicitly framed as an early‑stage evaluation. No purchase or pilot commitment was made.

1. **Include Careerspan as a serious candidate in the resume‑tool review**
   - **Decision:** Johnson CMC will treat Careerspan as a primary contender while it evaluates options to refresh its resume tools.
   - **Rationale:** The team sees value in an AI tool that starts from stories and semantics rather than only ATS keywords.

2. **Gather external references and outcome data**
   - **Decision:** Before moving forward, Johnson wants to speak with peer institutions and review concrete results.
   - **Actions implied:**
     - Vrijen to connect them with Diane (Columbia SPS) and UMass Eisenberg.
     - Vrijen to share survey results and proof‑point summaries (e.g., UMass study).

3. **Expect Johnson‑specific onboarding and training materials**
   - **Decision:** Any serious rollout would require:
     - Short “Careerspan 101” resources for students (video or guided demo).
     - Clear guidance for how Careerspan outputs map into Johnson’s resume template.
   - **Rationale:** Students often need hand‑holding on new tools; staff want to minimize confusion and rework.

4. **Preserve Johnson template and standards**
   - **Decision:** Even if Careerspan is adopted, Johnson will continue to enforce its own resume template; Careerspan should feed into that rather than replace it.
   - **Rationale:** Brand consistency and comparability across candidates remain non‑negotiable.

5. **Data‑handling expectations**
   - **Decision:** Any deployment must maintain current or stronger standards for:
     - Student data privacy and control (ability to delete data, own their account).
     - Non‑use of student data for external LLM training.
     - Clear boundaries on what information is shared with employers.

6. **Next Step (soft): Continued internal discussion post‑holidays**
   - **Decision:** The CMC team will digest this demo and compare Careerspan against other tools after the holidays.
   - **Rationale:** They are just beginning the evaluation cycle; they need time to synthesize, talk to references, and map tools to their early‑recruiting calendar.
