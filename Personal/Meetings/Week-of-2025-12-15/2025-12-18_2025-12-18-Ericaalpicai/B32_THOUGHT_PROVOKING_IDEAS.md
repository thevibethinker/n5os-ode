# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1. **The "Co-op Founding Team" Model**: The founder mentions a 5-person founding team with rotating presence in the US. V connects this to the idea of a shift from founder-led to "co-op" models where 6 people can run an 8-figure company by maximizing the "skill chunking" enabled by AI.
2. **The End of API Wrappers**: The transition from text-based API results to interactive UI components within the LLM interface (MCP Apps) suggests a future where the "app" is just a UI layer floating on top of standardized logic protocols.
3. **Zo as the "Private Backend" for Public LLMs**: The idea of using ChatGPT as the frontend and Zo as the secure, private compute backend for public apps. This flips the usual SaaS model (where the vendor owns the backend) to a user-owned backend model.
