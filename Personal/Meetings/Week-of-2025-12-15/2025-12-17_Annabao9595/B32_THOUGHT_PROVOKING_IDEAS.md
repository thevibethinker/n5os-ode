# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

*   **The "Subscription Shaving" Signal**: The idea that the primary value of an agentic OS isn't just new capability, but the *deflation* of specialized SaaS. Zo acts as an "arbitrage engine" for APIs.
*   **The Picture Frame Metaphor**: Shifting from "Platform" to "App-as-Frame." Users don't buy flexibility; they buy a solved problem that happens to be flexible enough to grow with them.
*   **Cognitive Backstopping**: Moving the AI discourse from "Automation" to "Cognitive Continuity." The goal isn't to replace the note-taking habit, but to ensure that if cognition fails (forgetting a detail), the system catches it.
