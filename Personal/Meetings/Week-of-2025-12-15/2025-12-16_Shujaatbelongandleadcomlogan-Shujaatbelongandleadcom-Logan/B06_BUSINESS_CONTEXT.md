# B06: Business Context

- **Market Trend**: Professionals are increasingly entering "founder mode" due to corporate shifts, creating a niche for AI-enablement that drives top-line/bottom-line growth rather than just "upskilling."
- **Ecosystem Positioning**: Careerspan's unique value is its candidate-side data, which is rare in a market saturated with employer-side tools. This makes them a prime candidate for licensing or acquisition by larger HR tech players.
- **Value Proposition**: The shift from "selling a course" to "helping evolve" (experiential/white-glove) is the winning formula for the mid-career segment.
