# B03 Decisions

*   **Pilot Selection:** The "Build Space 2.0" event will serve as the official pilot for **Flow State Canvas**.
*   **Vetting Mechanism:** The group agreed to use **Careerspan’s** story-based application model rather than traditional resumes to handle participant matching and onboarding.
*   **Space Partnership:** **Antler** will be the preferred partner for physical IRL space (NYC, Austin, SF) and the programmatic fireside chats.
*   **Community Integration:** The event will offer priority sign-up or a "dedicated board" for **Corridor X’s** community to ensure they feel valued and "in the spotlight."
*   **Scope of Work Definition:** The "hackathon" component will be a month-long "Launchpad" program rather than just a 48-hour event.
