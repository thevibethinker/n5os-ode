# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The "Rote" Inversion**: The idea that the larger a company becomes, the more its hiring becomes "rote," creating a massive value-gap for "semantic intelligence" that big players like Workday cannot easily fill due to architectural rigidity.
- **Bespoke at Scale**: A mental model for AI services that suggests the value isn't just automation, but the ability to apply high-cost "bespoke" human-level judgment (semantics) at "scale" (thousands of applicants) without the human cost.
- **The Passive Market Transparency Flywheel**: V's hypothesis that being the only company to show applicants *how* they are being judged could act as a gravity well for "passive" job seekers who want self-improvement data even if they don't get the job.
