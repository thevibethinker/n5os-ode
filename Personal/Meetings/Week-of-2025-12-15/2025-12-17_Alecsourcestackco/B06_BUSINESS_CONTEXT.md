# B06 Business Context

## Company Details (Careerspan)
- **Current State:** Transitioning from coaching-only to an AI-driven ATS platform.
- **Revenue/Model:** Collecting unique "self-reflection" data on candidates that competitors lack.
- **Strategic Goal:** Position for acquisition in 3-4 months (target price ~M).

## Market Context
- **Acquisition Wave:** HR tech is currently seeing many exits based on team and technology rather than just traction.
- **Competitive Pressure:** Resume builders and traditional ATS systems are "scared" of AI one-shot resume generation, creating an opening for high-signal solutions like Careerspan.
- **Getro Sunset:** Getro's decision to sunset its jobs data product creates a vacuum for portfolio job board management (e.g., for Marv Adventures).
