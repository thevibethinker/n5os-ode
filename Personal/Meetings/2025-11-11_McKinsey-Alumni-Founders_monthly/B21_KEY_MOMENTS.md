# B21: Key Moments

## 🎯 High-Impact Quotes

### On Gender Dynamics in Fundraising
> "I struggled quite a bit until my co-founder joined and it's like a very weird thing that happened."
> — **Abby** (Season)

**Context**: Discussing her fundraising experience as female founder before male co-founder joined. Honest acknowledgment of persistent bias.

**Significance**: Rare candid admission in group setting. Shows cultural shift toward naming these dynamics explicitly.

### On Focus vs. Opportunity
> "The hard part is getting less excited so we can just focus on one and not run around like a child in a candy store. Which of course I enjoy that very much myself."
> — **Roger** (Presenteasen)

**Context**: Discussing challenge of narrowing from many promising use cases in mental health/biometrics space.
