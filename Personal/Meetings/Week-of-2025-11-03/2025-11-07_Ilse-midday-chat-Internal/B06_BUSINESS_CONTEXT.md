# B06: Business Context

## Market Gap
Careerspan is identifying a "mid-signal" vacuum. Employers are often "burned" by low-quality resumes or referrals that don't scale, yet they find recruiters prohibitively expensive. This creates a window for a community-sourced alternative that provides higher signal than a job board but at a lower cost/friction than a recruiter.

## Sales Evolution
The transition from "Behavioral Matching" to "Community Curated Lists" reflects a shift from **Product-Led Growth** (selling the tech) to **Problem-Led Sales** (selling the outcome: "here are good people"). This shift has resulted in immediate "joy" and "biting" from the market.

## Strategic Risk
The primary risk identified is the "incumbent talent pool" — the need for a consistent, high-quality inflow of candidates. V’s strategy is to "swarm" specific high-signal communities (like Rise or product groups) to solve the supply side.
