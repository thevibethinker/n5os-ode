# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The Resume Decay**: The hypothesis that the "resume system is crumbling" and we are in a transition period before subjectivity-based matching becomes standard.
- **Recruiters as Insurance**: The perspective that recruiters aren't just sourcing tools, but an "externalization of the high cost of getting it wrong" for business owners.
- **The "Stardew Valley" Productivity Metric**: Using low-intensity, calming games as a necessary counterweight to the "marketing dollar war."
- **The "Fisherman's Net" Analogy**: Sourcing not by a broad net (job boards) but by visiting specific "parts of the water" (communities) to find specific "fish."
