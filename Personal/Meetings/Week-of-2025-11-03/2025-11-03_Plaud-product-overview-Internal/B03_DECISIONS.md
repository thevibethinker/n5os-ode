# B03: Decisions

## Systemic Decisions
- **Zo Naming:** The decision to name the company "Zo" was driven by SEO considerations and the logistical difficulty of single-letter branding (Z/Zed).
- **Target Audience Alignment:** The product is officially positioned for non-engineers, prioritizing accessibility and "indie hacking" use cases over traditional enterprise engineering.

## Workflow & Process Decisions
- **Transparency in Pricing:** Tokens are billed at the same rates Zo pays, ensuring a transparent cost structure for users.
- **Persona-Driven Execution:** Adoption of the multi-persona approach (Architect -> Builder -> Debugger) as the standard for major system changes.
- **Canonical Storage:** All meeting transcripts (regardless of source) must flow into the centralized Zo file system to enable intelligence generation.
