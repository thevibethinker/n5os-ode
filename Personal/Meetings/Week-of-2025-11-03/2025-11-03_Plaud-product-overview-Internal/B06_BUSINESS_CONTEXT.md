# B06: Business Context

## Product Positioning
Zo is positioned as more than just an LLM interface; it is a **cloud-based computer** with a persistent file system. This allows for long-term project management and the creation of "living" systems that evolve with the user's data.

## Market Context: The Evry Ecosystem
- **Monologue:** Targeting heavy dictation users.
- **Spiral:** Targeting LinkedIn-centric marketing and content creators.
- **Quora:** Positioned as a Superhuman alternative/competitor within the venture studio model.
- **Strategic Handoff:** Evry operates by hiring talented individuals to lead specific product lines with high independence, creating a fragmented but high-quality app ecosystem.

## Engineering Concept: Idempotency in N5
The mention of "idempotency" highlights the underlying technical philosophy of the Careerspan/N5 OS: building systems that are resilient to repeated execution and maintain a stable state. This is a crucial differentiator for a system meant to run background agents.
