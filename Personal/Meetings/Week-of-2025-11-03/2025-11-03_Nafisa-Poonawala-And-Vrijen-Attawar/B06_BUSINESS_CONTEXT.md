# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objective
The primary business objective is the successful demonstration of V's "N5" system (built on Zo) at South Park Commons. This event represents a critical opportunity for high-level exposure to the Zoho team and influential AI investors.

## Product Philosophy
- **Agentic Specialization:** Moving away from a single general-purpose AI toward a system of specialized personas (Builder, Researcher, Strategist, etc.) orchestrated by an "Operator."
- **Open-Faith Architecture:** V is adopting an "open" approach, sharing his core system designs to establish the "N5" framework as a standard for high-performance AI collaboration.
- **Value Anchor:** Meeting intelligence remains the most tangible "killer app" for the system, serving as the entry point for non-technical users.

## Market Positioning
Positioning N5/Zo not just as a tool, but as a "Chief of Staff" layer (referencing the Jared Dunn character) that is reliable and deeply integrated into the user's workflow.
