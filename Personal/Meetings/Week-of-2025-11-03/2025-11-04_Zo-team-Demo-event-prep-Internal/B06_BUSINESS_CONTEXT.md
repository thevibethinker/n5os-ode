# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objectives
- **Community Growth**: Crossing 100 signups is a significant milestone. The event aims to convert high-interest attendees into active users.
- **Brand Positioning**: Shifting from "intimidating vast open space" to "approachable magic moments" via pre-baked prompts and clear workflow examples.
- **Monetization/Sponsorship**: Identifying partners (Row, Oracle, MLH) to offset event costs and fuel growth.
- **Product Feedback**: Using V's friend (India) and early adopters to "put the repo through its paces" before broader distribution.

## Market Positioning
- Zo is positioning itself as a tool that "gives you wings" by solving information overload through deep personalization, contrasting itself with other tools that merely move unread content from one place to another.
