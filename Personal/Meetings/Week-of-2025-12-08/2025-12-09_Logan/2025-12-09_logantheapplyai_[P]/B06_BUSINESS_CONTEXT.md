# B06 Business Context

## The "Acquisition vs. Build" Tension
Careerspan/Apply AI is currently navigating a strategic pivot point. V expresses that they may no longer be trying to build a "viable long-term business" but are instead "trying to get it sold." This shift demands "guerrilla" tactics and "looking good" for buyers, which V feels is at odds with Ilya’s deep, process-heavy B2B pricing models.

## The Performance/Capital Ratio
The company is paying $10,000/month for a high-level strategist (Ilya) while experiencing a collapse in user activation metrics. There is an urgent need to align capital spend with actual growth (or acquisition-readiness) signals.

## Market Dynamics: HR/Recruiting Tech
V identifies that HR tech sales are "vibe-driven" and "fundamentally different" from standard tech sales. The reliance on resumes, Zoom calls, and relationship-based "people trade" makes the process-heavy approach (pamphlets/pamphleting) less effective than direct, scrappy outreach.
