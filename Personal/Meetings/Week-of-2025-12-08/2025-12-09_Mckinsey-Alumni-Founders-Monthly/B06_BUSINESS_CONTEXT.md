# B06 Business Context: McKinsey Alumni Founders Monthly

## Market Conditions
- **Fundraising Seasonality:** A clear consensus that the venture market effectively shuts down between Dec 20 and Jan 2. Founders are pushing for "wire dates" by Dec 19.
- **Dual-Use Tech Growth:** Increased interest in government contracting (US Army) suggests founders are looking for non-dilutive revenue streams to supplement venture capital.

## Industry-Specific Intelligence

### 1. Energy & Infrastructure
- **Verido AI** and **Capwell** represent a cluster of startups tackling infrastructure and emissions (methane capture).
- There is a high barrier to entry for "oil-friendly" VCs, which tend to be geographically concentrated (e.g., Houston).

### 2. Healthcare & Biotech
- **Latogen** (ancestry-based risk) and **Medrock AI** (concierge primary care) show a shift toward "personalized" and "frictionless" healthcare models.
- **Livelihood** is positioning itself at the intersection of GLP-1 medications and elite addiction recovery, a high-margin niche.

### 3. Construction Tech
- **Benchmark Labs** is leveraging weather forecasting as a risk mitigation tool for massive construction projects (Black & Veatch, Skanska), highlighting a move toward data-driven industrial operations.

## Community Value Proposition
- The community is moving beyond "networking" into "strategic enablement," providing direct paths to government contracts, philanthropic frameworks, and high-tier corporate sponsorships (JP Morgan).
- The "Showcase" format is proving successful for investor discovery, with high attendance retention rates.
