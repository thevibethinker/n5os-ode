# B01 Detailed Recap: McKinsey Alumni Founders Monthly (Dec 9, 2025)

## Executive Summary
The meeting served as the December monthly check-in for the McKinsey Alumni Founders community. Key discussions centered on end-of-year fundraising deadlines, upcoming community events (GiveWell strategy session, US Army dual-use tech engagement, and 2025 summits), and a round-robin of founder updates. Notable milestones included several founders closing seed/pre-seed rounds and new customer pilots in energy, healthcare, and oil & gas.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Community Updates & Events
- **Founder Dinner Recap:** A successful 15-founder dinner was recently held in New York at the Insularly office.
- **GiveWell Strategy Session:** An upcoming 30-minute session with Maggie (Senior Philanthropy Advisor at GiveWell) to discuss how founders can give back at any stage.
- **US Army Engagement (Jan/Feb):** Introduction to the "Fuse" program for dual-use technologies, covering awards from $15k to multimillion-dollar contracts. Specifically focused on the US Army (not Navy/Air Force).
- **2025 Summits:** Three major 150-person summits planned for New York, San Francisco, and London.
- **Sponsorship:** JP Morgan is interested in sponsoring upcoming events, potentially providing access to high-end venues like the new client center in NYC.

### 2. Fundraising & Market Sentiment
- **The "Holiday Deadline":** Multiple founders (Ulrich, Daniel) discussed the pressure of closing rounds before the December 19th holiday cutoff.
- **Investor Traction:** Founders noted that the recent showcase was highly effective, with high retention rates (60+ attendees throughout) and subsequent investor follow-ups.

### 3. Founder Round-Robin Updates
- **Ulrich (Benchmark Labs):** Closing seed round; focused on weather forecasting for top-tier construction companies (e.g., Black & Veatch, Skanska).
- **Daniel (Verido AI):** Automating due diligence for infrastructure (starting with energy). Raising a $2M pre-seed; seeking value-add angels/VCs.
- **Lance (Livelihood):** Addiction recovery platform using GLP-1s and coaching for elite professionals. Teeing up pre-seed; seeking connections to luxury rehabs and mental health investors.
- **Corey (Latogen):** Personalized medicine/telehealth based on ancestry-based risk. Partnering with Henry Ford Health Center. Preparing to raise in 1-2 months.
- **Vrijen (Careerspan):** Decentralized talent network and ATS solution for startups. Offered help with NYC networking and hiring strategy.
- **Thomas (Capwell):** Methane capture for oil/gas. Pilot starting in January; seeking oil-friendly VCs and customer intros.
- **Afraz (Medrock AI):** Concierge system for primary care to reduce bureaucracy. Validated a high-revenue model ($200k+ net per provider). Bottlenecked on patient consenting; seeking pre-med/gap-year hires.
- **Abby (Season):** AI platform for sports/gaming to convert fans to "super fans" using real-time intent data. Focused on reducing leakage in the fan economy.

## Strategic Outcomes
- **Community Maturation:** The shift towards philanthropic engagement (GiveWell) and government contracting (US Army) signals a broadening of the community's value proposition.
- **Resource Synergy:** Clear opportunities for cross-founder collaboration emerged, particularly between Afraz and Vrijen (hiring) and Daniel and Thomas (energy/infrastructure).
