# B07 Tone & Context: McKinsey Alumni Founders Monthly

## Meeting Dynamics
- **Atmosphere:** Informal, collegial, and highly supportive. The founders share a common language (McKinsey background) which allows for rapid context-setting and high-density information exchange.
- **Participation:** High-energy round-robin. Despite the small group (attributed to holiday travel), the engagement was deep, with members offering specific intros and tactical advice (e.g., hiring pre-meds, oil/gas tax credits).

- **The "McKinsey Way":** Frequent references to "BA-level talent," "strategy problem-solving," and structured networking indicate a group that values efficiency and high-signal interactions.
- **Mutual Aid:** There is a strong "pay-it-forward" culture. Vrijen’s open offer for NYC networking and the organizer’s proactive intro suggestions (Junaid for Lance) are representative.
