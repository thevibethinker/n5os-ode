# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The Psychology of "Free":** Explicitly listing what *isn't* charged for can be more powerful than just showing the price. It addresses "hidden costs" anxiety before it arises.
- **Time as the Ultimate Metric:** Shifting the narrative from "months to value" to "days to value" (ROI by EOW) transforms the product from a "strategic implementation" to a "tactical win."
- **Transparency as Onboarding:** Using the onboarding chart to show that "standard information package" is a "nothing burger" builds trust by demystifying the "exhausting setup" fear.
- **AI as a "First Draft" Skill:** V's advice to use AI not as a crutch, but as a "speed-to-draft" tool to liberate human energy for high-impact synthesis, is a useful mental model for non-technical entrepreneurs.
