# B06 Business Context

### Market Positioning:
Careerspan is positioning itself as a high-velocity solution in a market where traditional implementations take months. By emphasizing "ROI by EOW," they are targeting the "perceived barrier" of complex onboarding. This is a classic "wedge" strategy to get into corporate accounts by being the easiest and fastest vendor to work with.

### Product Strategy:
- **Value-Added Pricing:** The decision to list non-charged items is a tactical move to counteract price sensitivity.
- **Customizable Collateral:** The move toward templates suggests an account-based marketing (ABM) approach where the messaging is tailored to specific prospects.

### Operations:
The team is in a "shipping" phase, prioritizing speed-to-market and iterative feedback over document perfection.
