# B01: Detailed Recap - Ilya & V (2025-12-09)

## Executive Summary
Ilya shared a draft of a new "two-pager" marketing/sales PDF designed to streamline the sales process and articulate value quickly. The central theme of the document is "ROI by EOW" (End of Week), highlighting the speed at which Careerspan can demonstrate value compared to competitors who take months. V approved the direction, particularly the pricing transparency and the simplified onboarding timeline.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. The Two-Pager Sales Asset
- **Format:** A clean PDF view designed to match the customer's reading experience.
- **Visuals:** Discussion about embedding or linking videos. Ilya suggested linking to a corporate LinkedIn page instead of embedding large video files directly in the PDF.
- **Customization:** The document is designed as a template where company names and pricing grids can be swapped out easily depending on the prospect.

### 2. Pricing and "Liberties"
- **Strategic Padding:** Ilya added lines for "Unlimited Seats," "No Training Fee," and "No Setup Fee." Even though these aren't services they typically charge for, including them (at $0 or "included") justifies the overall price and makes the offering feel more valuable.
- **Packages:** The draft includes packages for different scales, such as 10 positions vs. smaller lightweight offerings.

### 3. The "ROI by EOW" Concept
- **The Hook:** Ilya realized that while previous companies he worked at took months to show value, Careerspan can show it in days.
- **Refinement:** V suggested shortening "ROI by end of week" to "ROI by eow" for a more punchy, parallel visual style in the onboarding chart.
- **Onboarding Chart:** A simplified visual showing the steps from contract signature to a live job description and strategy plan within a single week.

### 4. Content and Security
- **Data Security:** Ilya plans to include a concise statement on data security to provide peace of mind for corporate clients, even if it's rarely read in detail.
- **Feature Breakdown:** Currently in draft state with placeholder text (Lorem Ipsum), needing final polish on the descriptions.

## Consensus & Alignment
- V is "super happy" with the direction and the "ROI by eow" framing.
- V requested a "template version" and a "lightweight/month-by-month version" to start sending out to his network immediately.
