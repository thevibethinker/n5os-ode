# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objective
The meeting centers on the creation of a high-impact sales asset (the "Two-Pager") designed to reduce friction in the Careerspan sales cycle. The goal is to move from "conversations" to "closed contracts" by providing V's network with a clear, professional, and visually compelling pricing and onboarding roadmap.

## Market Positioning
Careerspan is positioning itself as the "ultra-fast" alternative to traditional recruitment or sourcing solutions. By measuring success in "days" rather than "months," they are targeting a significant pain point for corporate departments: the "total headache" of onboarding and slow time-to-value.

## Product Maturity
The discussion reveals that the core offering is stable enough to be packaged into standard "lightweight" and "enterprise" tiers. The shift from "liberties" to "on paper" indicates a move toward professionalizing the sales operations and establishing a clear value floor for the service.
