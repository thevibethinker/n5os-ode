# B06: Business Context

### Unit Economics of AI Analysis
The business is navigating the cost-performance trade-off of LLM-based candidate analysis. 
- **Variable Costs:** Every application triggers a specific API/compute cost ($0.30 - $0.40) to align candidate data against 30+ role parameters.
- **Scalability Risk:** A high volume of low-quality applicants could create a significant cost burden without corresponding revenue (if not managed by a filter).
- **Competitive Advantage:** The "super finite" or "dense analysis" identifies specific gaps that traditional ATS systems miss, but this precision comes at a literal cost.

### Product Positioning
The "Vibe Check" is evolving from a simple engagement tool into a critical infrastructure component for cost management and candidate filtering.
