# B06: Business Context

## Careerspan Unit Economics
The business is navigating the cost-heavy nature of "dense analysis." Every time a candidate applies to a job, Careerspan performs an alignment check against ~30 items. This creates a variable cost per application that must be managed.

## The "Apply" Funnel
1. **Resume/Story Ingestion:** Fixed cost per candidate for initial profile building.
2. **Vibe Check:** Low-cost, high-speed filtering stage.
3. **Dense Analysis:** High-cost, role-specific alignment (occurs only upon application).

## Strategic Moat
The value of Careerspan lies in its ability to perform "super finite" or "super dense" analysis that identifies gaps specific to a role. The competitive advantage is accuracy, but the business challenge is the cost of that accuracy. The Vibe Check is the mechanism proposed to scale this high-cost accuracy profitably.
