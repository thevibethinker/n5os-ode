# B03: Decisions

| Decision | Context | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vibe Check as a Mandatory Gate** | V explored the idea of requiring a certain "level" on a vibe check before expensive analysis occurs. | Prevents incurring high internal costs ($0.30 - $2.00+) for candidates with low alignment. |
| **Focus on High-Signal Filtering** | Commitment to the "1 candidate for 1 job" ideal as a strategic North Star. | Minimizes internal overhead and maximizes the value delivered to employers for the least work. |
| **Internal Cost Recognition** | Acknowledged that "dense analysis" against 30 JD items is the primary driver of application-stage costs. | Clarifies the unit economics of the Apply process for better business modeling. |
