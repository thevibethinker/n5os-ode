# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

## The "Greedy Scenario" as a Filter
Using extreme capitalism ("monocle mustache twirling") as a creative constraint to find efficiency. If we aim for 1:1 candidate-to-job matches, what parts of our current process are "flushing money down the drain"?

## The Cost of Specificity
Careerspan's analysis is role-specific and candidate-specific (the "gaps"). This level of personalization is the product's greatest value but also its greatest cost. How do we commoditize the *result* of this analysis while keeping the *process* exclusive?

## Vibe Check as "Train Dispatching"
The analogy of the Vibe Check as train dispatching for expensive analysis. It implies a high-speed, high-consequence decision point that dictates the flow of all subsequent resources. Can the Vibe Check eventually become so accurate that "dense analysis" is only needed for the final selection?
