# B03: Decisions

### Use of Vibe Check as a Cost Gate
- **Decision:** The Vibe Check should act as a pre-requisite or threshold.
- **Rationale:** Processing a full "dense analysis" for every applicant is too expensive. By using the cheaper Vibe Check to filter out the bottom 90% of applicants, the system saves significant internal compute/analysis costs.

### Cost Driver Identification
- **Decision:** Confirmed that "alignment" against the 30 job-specific items is the primary driver of application-time costs.
