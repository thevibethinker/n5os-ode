# B06: Business Context

## Market Macro
* **The "Signal-to-Noise" Crisis:** The recruitment market is currently plagued by "useless" ATS systems and bot-driven applications. This creates a massive opening for "High Signal" tools like Careerspan.
* **AI Fraud:** There is an increasing trend of fake LinkedIn profiles and AI avatars being used by candidates to game the interview process.

## Product Market Fit (PMF) Signals
* **High-Volume Agencies:** Strong PMF for agencies handling massive inbound.
* **Internal Teams:** Strong PMF for internal recruiters needing to save time while gaining rich information.
* **High-End Headhunting:** Potential PMF via "Value-Add Insights" (B08-style stakeholder intel) rather than just application processing.

## Partnerships
* **Existing Collaborations:** Superposition AI (AI headhunters) and Try Matchbox (Australia) are currently using/piloting the tool.
