# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Semantic Screening vs. Arbitrary Ranking**: The move away from "Harvard = Yes" toward "Actionable semantic evidence" as a hiring philosophy.
- **The "Proof of Humanity" Hurdle**: Using a tool like Careerspan not just for data, but as a barrier to filter out bots and low-intent applicants who won't spend 15 minutes talking.
- **Internal vs. External Recruitment Needs**: The tension between high-volume efficiency (Careerspan's current strength) and low-volume, high-touch headhunting (the recruiter's current mode). How does the "laddering up" feature eventually serve the latter?
