# B01 Detailed Recap

The meeting was a catching-up and product demonstration session between Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan) and an external recruiter (referred to as "mate").

### Key Discussion Points:
- **Recruiter's Performance**: The recruiter shared that his team has already hit their yearly target as of last month and might break their monthly record for the fourth time this year. He notes a high volume of activity with everyone "scrambling" for interviews before the Christmas break.
- **The Recruiting Landscape**: Both discussed the current state of recruiting, specifically the "uselessness" of Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and the influx of low-quality, bot-driven applications on LinkedIn. The recruiter mentioned encountering fake LinkedIn profiles and AI avatars in calls.
- **Careerspan Value Proposition**: Vrijen demonstrated the latest iteration of Careerspan, highlighting its shift from a simple ATS to a semantic evaluation tool. Key features mentioned include candidate storytelling (structured conversations), automated deal-breaker assessment, and "laddering up" of candidate abilities.
- **Market Alignment**: The recruiter provided feedback that Careerspan is highly useful for high-volume inbound roles (e.g., 0k–50k salary range) or in-house teams. However, for his current focus on "top 1%er" headhunting where volume is low, he feels it is less relevant, though he acknowledged the richness of the information it captures compared to traditional tools.
- **Roadmap & Future Features**: They discussed providing candidate insights to clients (concerns, interview topics) and potentially offering a "quick scan" service against the Careerspan talent pool. Vrijen noted that deep analysis (up to 40 pages) is already generated in the backend and just needs UI surfacing.
