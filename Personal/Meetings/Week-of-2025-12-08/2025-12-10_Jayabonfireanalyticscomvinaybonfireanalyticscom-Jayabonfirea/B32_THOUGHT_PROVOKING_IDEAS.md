# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

* **The Partnership-as-Diligence Model**: The idea that "anyone looking to acquire you would partner with you anyway." This reframes business development as a pre-M&A dating phase.
* **The Talent Call**: Keeping a "low-cost talent intake" open even when not actively hiring to build a "warm" bench of candidate stories.
* **High-Friction as a Feature**: In a world of LinkedIn "easy apply" spam, intentionally introducing friction (voice/chat stories) serves as an immediate signal of candidate intent and quality.
* **Founder Fatigue and Market Windows**: V's observation that a product can be "amazing" but still lose its "genre-defining" window to a competitor, leading to a shift in strategy from "growth" to "honorable exit."
