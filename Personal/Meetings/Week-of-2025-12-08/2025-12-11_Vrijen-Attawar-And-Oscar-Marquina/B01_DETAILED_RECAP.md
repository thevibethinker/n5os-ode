# B01: Detailed Recap - Vrijen Attawar & Oscar Marquina (2025-12-11)

## Executive Summary
Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan) and Oscar Marquina (Marvin) met to formalize the partnership between Careerspan and the Marvin community. The discussion focused on integrating Careerspan into the Marvin ecosystem (newsletter, perks page, WhatsApp) and transitioning Marvin's job board infrastructure from Getro to Careerspan's scraping and distribution platform.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Partnership Formalization & Community Integration
*   **Context:** Vrijen shared early success stories, noting that founders like Alan (Melly) and others are already using Careerspan. The "Founder Demonstration" event provided a strong signal of credibility.
*   **The "Marvin Sanction":** Vrijen emphasized the importance of being "sanctioned by Marvin" to build trust with founders.
*   **Integration Channels:** Oscar confirmed that Marvin will support Careerspan via:
    *   The Perks Page.
    *   The Marvin Newsletter (rotating basis).
    *   LinkedIn channel announcements (contact Ulrich for launch).
    *   WhatsApp community announcements (Oscar will handle the intro).

### 2. Infrastructure Transition (Getro → Careerspan)
*   **Getro Limitations:** Oscar noted that Marvin currently uses Getro primarily for its easy scraping capabilities rather than its candidate matching or ATS features.
*   **Careerspan Advantage:** Vrijen highlighted that Careerspan solves the "signal-to-noise" problem that traditional ATS systems (and by extension, Getro) struggle with, particularly in an era of AI-fabricated resumes.
*   **Permission to Port:** Oscar granted Vrijen "Network Admin" access to the Getro network to facilitate job exports and founder connections.
*   **Scraping Replication:** Oscar challenged Vrijen to replicate Getro's scraping efficiency. Vrijen confirmed Careerspan already has a provider that can sync data daily and could replicate the functionality within 2-3 weeks.
*   **Timeline:** Marvin is under contract with Getro until Q3 2026. The goal is to prove Careerspan's superiority well before that expiration to facilitate a more formal, exclusive arrangement.

### 3. Diplomatic Outreach Strategy
*   **The "Scout's Honor" Promise:** Vrijen committed to a highly diplomatic outreach strategy, being sensitive to not spamming founders.
*   **The "Marvin Signal":** Vrijen intends to approach founders as a "part of Marvin" with specific candidates, making the outreach high-signal and helpful rather than cold sales.
*   **McKinsey Legacy:** Both acknowledged the need to keep "McKinsey folks" (investors/partners) happy by remaining "above board" and professional in all interactions.
