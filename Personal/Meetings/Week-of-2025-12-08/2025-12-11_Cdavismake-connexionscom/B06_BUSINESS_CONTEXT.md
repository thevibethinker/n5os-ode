# B06 Business Context

- **Market Segment:** Early-career professionals, remote workers, and freelancers.
- **The Problem:** The "loneliness of the remote worker" and the insufficiency of the resume as a data point for hiring quality.
- **Connexions Competitive Edge:** A "mobile-first" approach that reverses typical social media mechanics, requiring conversation/meetings for "standing."
- **Careerspan Competitive Edge:** A conversational coaching modality that extracts high-nuance data locked away by traditional application methods.
- **Strategic Alignment:** Connexions focuses on the "top of funnel" networking/relationship cultivation, while Careerspan focuses on "deeper funnel" self-reflection and job application data.
