# B06 – Business Context

- **Company:** Careerspan – a product that job-seekers love using, increasingly also adopted by companies to power job postings and candidate flows.
- **Stage:** Roughly three years into the company; significant experimentation and multiple iterations; currently in a state of meaningful traction but constrained by founder energy and the fundraising environment.
- **Product usage mentioned:**
  - Candidates use Careerspan to navigate job searches.
  - Employers are starting to use Careerspan's tech to post roles and attract applicants.
- **Strategic environment:**
  - The team has fielded multiple inbound expressions of interest from potential acquirers who might:
    - Acqui-hire the team.
    - Absorb the technology.
    - Spin the team into a new initiative.
- **Resource reality:**
  - Team has already been narrowed to a lean core, with other teammates being helped into new roles.
  - Founders are balancing desire to keep building against personal burnout and life constraints.
- **Goal over next phase:**
  - Preserve value (users, tech, and team reputation).
  - Run a serious process to sell the company, ideally achieving at least a break-even monetary outcome plus a reputational "PR exit."
  - Set the founders up for a healthier foundation before any future startup.
