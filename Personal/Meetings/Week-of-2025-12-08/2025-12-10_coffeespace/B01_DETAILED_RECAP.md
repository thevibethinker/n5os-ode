# B01 - Detailed Recap: Vrijen (Careerspan) & Coffee Space

## Meeting Purpose
Introductory call between Vrijen Attawar and Bridget from Careerspan and a representative from Coffee Space (the "third co-founder") to explore alignment, shared problems in the hiring space, and potential technical or pipeline collaborations.

## Participant Summary
- **Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan):** Founder of Careerspan.
- **Bridget (Careerspan):** Careerspan team member (met V 10 years ago in Singapore).
- **Coffee Space Representative:** Third co-founder of Coffee Space (joined during a pivot).

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Coffee Space Evolution & Model
- **Pivots:** Started as mentor/mentee matching -> LinkedIn for founders -> Tinder for co-founders.
- **Current State:** Mobile app with 25,000 users and 1.5M interactions.
- **Expansion:** Moving from founder-matching to "Founding Teams" (Founding Engineers, Ops, Product, Design).
- **Livia Connection:** Livia (common connection via Harvard/i-lab) helped bridge the two teams.

### 2. Careerspan Evolution & Model
- **Pivots:** Started as a career coaching app focused on self-reflection.
- **Value Prop:** AI-native UX for structured self-reflection (15-minute conversations) to extract deep professional data beyond the resume.
- **Data Capture:** Not a "mock interview"; focuses on capturing raw "operator" data and context that resumes miss (hard/soft skills categorized via multi-agent system).
- **Current Partnerships:** Working with AI-native headhunters (Superposition, Matchbox) and McKinsey’s alumni fund (Marvin Ventures).

### 3. Shared Thesis on Hiring
- **The "Enemy":** Indeed and LinkedIn (David vs. Goliath dynamic).
- **Problem:** Resumes are "broken" and don't help founders discriminate between talent effectively. Founders are spending more time screening but feel less confident in the results.
- **Solution:** Introducing "meaningful friction" (like Careerspan's reflection process) to filter for high-quality candidates and surface data that predicts job performance better than a polish-focused interview.

### 4. Potential Collaboration Areas
- **Pipeline:** Careerspan acting as a pipeline for Coffee Space.
- **Tech Integration:** Coffee Space using Careerspan's tech to get deeper data on their existing 25k user base.
- **Signal Boosting:** Mutually supporting each other's efforts in the early-stage hiring niche.

## Outcome
Both teams recognized high "congruence" in their missions and agreed to explore how Careerspan’s deep data collection could enhance Coffee Space’s founding team matching service.
