# B06 - Business Context

## Market Thesis
- **The Failure of the Resume:** Both companies operate on the belief that the resume is "broken" in the modern AI-assisted application age.
- **The Founders' Burden:** Founders are the target customer because they are the most "signal-starved" and have the highest stakes for first hires.
- **AI-Native Advantage:** Both companies are leveraging AI not just to automate old processes, but to create new types of data (Reflection data for Careerspan, Interaction/Matching data for Coffee Space).

## Competitive Landscape
- **Incumbents:** Indeed and LinkedIn are viewed as the "Goliaths" providing high-volume, low-signal solutions.
- **Niche Focus:** By focusing on "co-founders" and "founding teams," these startups are capturing the high-value, high-complexity end of the market that LinkedIn fails to serve well.
