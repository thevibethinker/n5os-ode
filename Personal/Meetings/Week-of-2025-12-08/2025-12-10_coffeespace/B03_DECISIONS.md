# B03 - Decisions

## Tactical Decisions
- **Focus on Founding Teams:** Both organizations have independently decided to target the "Founding Team" niche (Founding Engineers, first hires) rather than general SMB or enterprise hiring.
- **Niche Overlap:** Acknowledged that their target users (early-stage founders) move quickly and avoid traditional ATS/HRIS burdens, which validates the current sales strategy.

## Strategic Realizations
- **B2B Pivot:** Both confirmed that monetizing job seekers (B2C) is non-viable for their specific models, cementing the employer/recruiter-pay model.
- **Friction as a Feature:** Agreed on the counter-intuitive strategy that *more* friction (via deep data collection/reflection) is actually a selling point for founders overwhelmed by high-volume, low-signal resume pools.
