# B06: Business Context

## The "Boomer" Opportunity
- **Product:** A Medicare-funded program provided "free" to patients.
- **Financial Catalyst:** A potential $1M revenue deal through a revenue-sharing agreement with a clinic.
- **The Manual Hurdle:** The "coding" for Medicare billing requires patient consent, which currently requires manual phone calls. This is the primary blocker to scaling from a 2-person team to a larger operation.

## Recruitment Landscape
- **High Noise:** The market is flooded with low-signal resumes, making traditional job boards (Indeed) a time-sink for founders.
- **Niche Talent Pools:** There is a specific opportunity in "leveraging" visa statuses (OPT) or educational milestones (Pre-med gap year) to find elite talent at non-VC prices.
