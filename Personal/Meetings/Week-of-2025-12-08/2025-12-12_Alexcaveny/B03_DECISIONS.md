# B03 – Decisions

> Note: No formal contracts or pricing decisions were made. This block captures working decisions / commitments that emerged implicitly or explicitly.

1. **Treat early “story completion” behavior as real signal, not an edge case.**
   - V accepts that 15–20% of candidates opting into story-recording is *strong* evidence that the core behavior is viable, not something to fear.

2. **Anchor Careerspan’s brand on authenticity and depth, not generic AI/HR language.**
   - Agreement that the product should lead with “show your whole self” (candidates) and “ask for what you really want” (employers), rather than generic “get to the top of the stack / skip the ATS” promises.

3. **Prioritize revisiting the website hero section and messaging.**
   - Decision that V will bring Alex’s feedback and the “two-sided promise” framing to Logan and use it to guide above-the-fold redesign.

4. **Adopt “sell the hole, not the shovel” as a core sales principle.**
   - V agrees that early conversations with founders should start from their hiring pain (“How’s your hiring going?”), not from pitching Careerspan’s feature set.

5. **Practice locking next steps during live conversations.**
   - V commits (at least in intent) to booking follow-up calls or job-intake sessions *while still on the call* when there is a fit, rather than defaulting to “I’ll email you.”

6. **Continue and deepen the coaching relationship.**
   - V confirms he will schedule **one more coaching session this month** with Alex.

7. **Use visual artifacts to communicate value to employers.**
   - Agreement in principle to create:
     - A Sankey-style visualization of time wasted in traditional hiring vs. Careerspan.
     - A one-page workflow comparison (traditional vs. Careerspan) for founder prospects.
