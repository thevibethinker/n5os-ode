# Decisions

1. **Careerspan will be listed on Marvin’s Startup Stack as a public offer.**  
   - You and Ethan agree that the offer should be visible to all Startup Stack users (not restricted to a private subset), with broad eligibility.

2. **Incentive structure: freemium, not pure discount.**  
   - The agreed structure is: **unlimited candidate filtering and matching for one role for three months** at no cost to founders, rather than a percentage discount on standard pricing.

3. **Positioning within categories.**  
   - Careerspan will be tagged under relevant categories such as **HR / hiring**, **recruiting / staffing**, **coaching / mentorship**, and **learning / continuing education**, so founders can find it via multiple search paths.

4. **Redemption via dedicated Fillout form.**  
   - Instead of a generic contact link, redemptions will flow through a specific **Fillout form** you control, ensuring you capture the right data from Marvin founders.

5. **Copy responsibilities and review loop.**  
   - You own the substance of the copy (company description, offer description, landing‑page details).  
   - Ethan will submit the offer for Startup Stack review and notify you once it has been approved and is live; you will then double‑check the public view and adjust copy later if needed.
