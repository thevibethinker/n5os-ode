# B01: Detailed Recap

## Overview
This meeting, titled "Sales Blitz," focused on refining the internal pricing strategy for Careerspan. Ilya Kucherenko presented an analysis of current pricing packages, emphasizing the need for variable pricing models that manage internal costs while remaining competitive.

## Key Discussion Points
- **Pricing Dynamics**: Ilya discussed the "pricing triangle" consisting of:
  1. Number of Job Descriptions
  2. Number of Seats (Users)
  3. Time (Contract Duration)
- **Constraint Management**: The core objective is to avoid offering "unlimited" access across all three dimensions simultaneously (Unlimited Job Descriptions + Unlimited Seats + Unlimited Time). 
- **Cost Risk Mitigation**: Ilya highlighted the "nightmare scenario" where an HR team could churn through 10,000 job descriptions annually, significantly driving up internal variable costs.
- **Sales Strategy**: The goal is to provide sales representatives with a flexible framework to "box in" agreements, typically through annual contracts or caps on specific dimensions based on client needs (e.g., limiting seats or time if job description volume is uncertain).
- **Package Iteration**: Ilya shared that he had landed on specific packages after exploring various pricing dynamics and overage scenarios.

## Conclusion
The session concluded with the introduction of refined pricing packages designed to weave through potential overages and protect Careerspan's margins while offering scalability to growing clients.
