# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objective
Careerspan is in a phase of professionalizing its sales and financial operations. The focus is shifting from "getting any customer" to "getting sustainable customers" by ensuring that high-volume users do not become unprofitable due to uncapped variable costs.

## Market Positioning
The company is realizing it is "not as expensive" as previously thought, suggesting room for strategic pricing adjustments that capture value from larger enterprise clients (e.g., 20-person HR teams).

## Internal Constraints
The primary internal constraint is the variable cost associated with processing job descriptions. The pricing strategy is being built specifically to "box in" this cost risk.
