# B03: Decisions

## Strategic Decisions
- **Abandoning Total Unlimited**: Decided against offering a package that includes unlimited job descriptions, unlimited seats, and unlimited time simultaneously.
- **Dimensions of Control**: Established that at least one of the three "triangle" dimensions (Job Descriptions, Seats, or Time) must be capped in every contract to mitigate internal variable costs.
- **Annual Agreement Default**: For "unlimited" volume scenarios, the primary constraint will be an annual agreement (Time boxing).

## Operational Decisions
- **Packaging Structure**: Ilya has finalized a set of packages designed to manage overages while remaining attractive to growing companies.
