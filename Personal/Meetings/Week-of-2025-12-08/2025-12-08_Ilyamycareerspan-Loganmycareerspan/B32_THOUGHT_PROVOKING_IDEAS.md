# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Can pricing constraints be framed as a "premium" feature?**: Instead of saying "you are capped at X JDs," can we frame it as "this tier is optimized for high-intensity 6-month hiring sprints"?
- **The "Uncertainty Pivot"**: Ilya noted that clients often walk into constraints because they don't know their future volume. Could we offer a "Discovery Tier" that is deliberately time-boxed to 90 days to help them find their right "Triangle" configuration?
- **Variable Cost Transparency**: Should the sales team have real-time visibility into the "variable cost burn" of existing accounts to inform renewal strategies?
