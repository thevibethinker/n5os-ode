# B03 Decisions

- **Decision 1**: Adopt the "Pricing Triangle" model (Unlimited JDs vs. Unlimited Seats vs. Annual Term) as the canonical constraint for all sales negotiations.
- **Decision 2**: Explicitly avoid "unlimited, unlimited, unlimited" contracts to protect against runaway internal variable costs.
- **Decision 3**: Use annual agreements to "box in" time as a fixed variable even when usage volume is high.
