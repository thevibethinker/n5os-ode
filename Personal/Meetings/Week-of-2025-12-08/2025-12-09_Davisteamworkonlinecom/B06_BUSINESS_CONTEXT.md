# B06: Business Context

## Market Thesis
The current recruitment market is suffering from "resume blindness." 99% of screening relies on self-written resumes which fail to capture true talent. AI can unlock the "hidden gem" market by acting as a high-volume, low-cost achievement coach.

## Product Positioning
- **Core Value Prop:** "Moneyball for hiring"—finding value where others aren't looking by leveraging deep achievement data.
- **Differentiation:** Unlike competitors (Dex, Jack and Jill), Careerspan focuses on a deep coaching pedagogy rather than a surface-level intake form.
- **Monetization:** Shifted from job-seeker coaching (low willingness to pay) to employer-facing signal generation (high value/bounties).

## Competitive Landscape
- **Direct Competitors:** Mention of "Dex" (meetdex.com) and "Jack and Jill." 
- **Observation:** Competitors are noted for having high valuations but "limited vision," often focusing on branding/forms over deep candidate data extraction.
