# B01: Detailed Recap - Vrijen (Careerspan) x Davis (Teamwork Online)

## Executive Summary
Vrijen Attawar (Founder of Careerspan) and Davis (Teamwork Online) discussed Careerspan's unique approach to recruitment data, specifically moving beyond the limitations of the resume through AI-guided self-articulation. The conversation covered Careerspan's pivot from a coaching app to an employer-facing data platform, the mechanics of its "stories" feature, and its current go-to-market experiments including job board bounties. Davis explored the platform in real-time, creating a profile and reflecting on how Careerspan's "vibe checks" and story-based assessments could solve the "blindness" of traditional screening.

## Core Discussion Points

### 1. The Careerspan Vision: "Moneyball for Hiring"
- **The Data Problem:** Vrijen articulated that while athletes' quality is easily conveyed through metrics, professional talent is often "data-poor" because it relies on self-articulation via resumes, which most candidates do poorly.
- **The Solution:** Careerspan aims to collect "more and better data" by using AI to patienty pull "hidden gems" and detailed achievements out of candidates through conversational coaching.
- **Economic Value:** Vrijen highlighted the 25x return on value compared to human coaches, offering a sophisticated coaching motion at approximately $4/hour.

### 2. Evolution and Business Model
- **The Pivot:** Originally a coaching app for job seekers, Careerspan shifted focus toward employers because job seekers are difficult to monetize and the achievement data being collected was found to be highly valuable for screening.
- **Current Motion:** The platform is currently operating a job board with "bounties" (around $1,000 for successful hires), appealing to founders who are flooded with resumes but lack high-signal candidates.
- **Competitive Landscape:** Discussion of conceptual competitors "Dex" and "Jack and Jill," though Vrijen noted they often function more as "intake forms" rather than deep coaching experiences.

### 3. Product Demonstration & User Experience
- **Guided Self-Reflection:** The core user motion is "Stories"—an AI-driven conversational interface that helps candidates articulate specific achievements.
- **Vibe Checks:** A capability where users dump in job descriptions to receive a high-fidelity fit analysis based on their accumulated stories and data.
- **Employer Signal:** Careerspan reintroduces the "effort signal" by showing employers how many stories a candidate has completed, indicating agency and interest beyond a simple click-to-apply.

### 4. Davis's Background and Real-time Onboarding
- **Background:** Davis is an alum of both Cornell and Emory. He works at Teamwork Online, a vertical hiring solution for the sports and entertainment industry (1,500+ companies).
- **Onboarding:** Davis created a profile during the call, checked out the "Crystal Ball" lead-gen tool, and began narrating a story about Teamwork Online's integration with a background check provider to test the system's "Stories" capability.

## Strategic Outlook
Careerspan is aiming to "catch up" and scale in the coming weeks and months, focusing on building long-term talent data sets that traditional ATS systems lack. Vrijen predicts a "talent war" where deep qualified data becomes the primary differentiator as job retention potentially shifts.
