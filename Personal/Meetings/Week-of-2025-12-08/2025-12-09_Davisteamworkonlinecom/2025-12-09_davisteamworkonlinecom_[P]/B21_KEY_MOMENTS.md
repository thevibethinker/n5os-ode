# B21: Key Moments

## The "Arsenal Connection"
Vrijen and Davis bonded early over a shared love for Arsenal FC and Arsène Wenger. Vrijen has a Wenger quote behind him: *"I believe the life should be to do it so well that it becomes art."* This established immediate rapport.

## The "Moneyball" Pitch
Vrijen's framing of Careerspan as "Moneyball for hiring" resonated well with Davis, given his background in sports recruitment.

## The "Hidden Gem" Philosophy
Vrijen's observation that "99% of the industry is blind" because they rely on resumes, and his goal to find value where no one is looking, served as the primary intellectual hook of the meeting.

## Real-Time Sign-Up
Davis actively creating a profile and interacting with the "Crystal Ball" tool provided a live "user test" environment, allowing Vrijen to explain features as Davis encountered them.

## The Storytelling Demo
Davis narrating a story about Teamwork's integration with background check providers served as a live example of the kind of "rich data" Careerspan seeks to capture.
