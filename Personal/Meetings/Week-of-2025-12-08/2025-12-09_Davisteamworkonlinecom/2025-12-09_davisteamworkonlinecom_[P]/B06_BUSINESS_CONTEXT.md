# B06: Business Context

## Market Thesis: "Moneyball for Hiring"
The central thesis of Careerspan is that the labor market is "blind" because it relies on the resume—a flawed instrument for self-articulation. By using AI to facilitate deep storytelling, Careerspan uncovers "hidden gems" (candidates who are high-talent but low-articulation).

## Product-Market Fit Observations
- **Monetization Pivot:** Job seekers have low willingness to pay for coaching, even if high-value. The value is actually in the *data* generated during coaching, which employers will pay for to reduce noise in high-volume applicant pools.
- **The "Effort Signal":** In an age where AI can write resumes and cover letters, "effort" (completing stories/conversations) becomes a primary differentiator for candidates.

## Competitive Landscape
- **Dex & Jack and Jill:** Identified as competitors with higher valuations but "narrower" visions. Careerspan aims to differentiate through deeper "pedagogy" and coaching facilitated discovery rather than simple intake forms.
