# B06: Business Context

- **Company**: Careerspan.
- **Stage**: ~4,000 users; 2 years of product development on the candidate side; currently expanding into the employer/marketplace side.
- **Business Model**: Transitioning from a coaching tool to a marketplace/matchmaking play. Focused on "narrative design" and "semantic alignment" rather than traditional keyword-based ATS systems.
- **Market Conditions**: Tech layoffs are at historic highs (worse than 2008); "Auto-apply" and "Chat GPT" resumes have created a signal-to-noise crisis in recruiting.
- **Partnerships**: Piloting with FOHI and Marvin Ventures (McKinsey alumni fund).
