# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1.  **The "No Ghost Jobs" Guarantee**: Can Careerspan turn "vetted employers" into a formal certification or badge that candidates can rely on across the platform?
2.  **Case Study vs. Feature Announcement**: The idea that founders respond better to seeing their peers solve pain points than to feature lists is a powerful principle for all future B2B marketing.
3.  **Short Attention Span Marketing**: Breaking "Big Announcements" into 5 micro-lessons/days. Could this become the standard Careerspan launch template for every new feature?
4.  **The "Plot Twist" Branding**: How to maintain high-end consulting/recruiting prestige while leaning into the "job board" utility? "We are a job board... jk, we're better."
