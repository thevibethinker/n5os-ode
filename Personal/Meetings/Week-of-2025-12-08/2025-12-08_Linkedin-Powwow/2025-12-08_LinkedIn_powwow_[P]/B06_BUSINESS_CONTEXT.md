# B06 Business Context

Careerspan is transitioning or expanding its identity to include a job board functionality that replaces traditional ATS (Applicant Tracking System) black boxes. The strategic challenge is maintaining their identity as a "premium talent partner" while utilizing the "job board" label, which is easily understood by the market. By vetting all employers, they are positioning themselves against the "ghost job" epidemic on major platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed. This move aims to capture both founder interest (efficiency/quality) and candidate trust (transparency/live roles).
