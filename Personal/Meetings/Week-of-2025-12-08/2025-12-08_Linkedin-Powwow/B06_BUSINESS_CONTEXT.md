# B06: Business Context - LinkedIn Powwow

## Core Mission Alignment
This meeting directly supports Careerspan's transition into a more visible platform for both founders and candidates. The launch of the "Direct Apply" job board functionality is a major product milestone that requires careful narrative positioning to avoid being seen as "just another job board."

## Market Positioning
- **The "Anti-ATS" Stance**: Careerspan is positioning itself against the "black box" of traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). The focus is on transparency and communication.
- **Vetting & Trust**: By guaranteeing "no ghost jobs," Careerspan is carving out a niche of high-integrity hiring, addressing a primary pain point in the 2024-2025 job market.

## Strategic Objectives
- **Founder Inbound**: Using case studies to attract other founders who feel the same recruitment "pain."
- **Candidate Trust**: Building a platform where candidates feel they "measure up" and aren't being ghosted by automated systems.
- **Brand Identity**: Maintaining the "we're more than just a job board" identity while capitalizing on the utility of a job board to drive traffic.
