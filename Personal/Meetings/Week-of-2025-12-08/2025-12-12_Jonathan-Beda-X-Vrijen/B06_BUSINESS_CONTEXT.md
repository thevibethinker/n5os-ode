# Business Context

## Jonathan’s Fund

- **Vehicle:** ~\$20M venture fund (name not specified in transcript) raised off the back of successful SPVs.  
- **Stage & Entry Point:** Post–product‑market‑fit, typically at **$3–4M ARR** with 30–40 employees and clear initial traction.  
- **Positioning:**
  - Intentionally **not** competing with Sequoia / Andreessen for the earliest, most contested deals.  
  - Instead, co‑investing alongside larger funds once there is enough data to reason about the business.  
  - Relying on bigger funds to lead and price rounds, while his fund adds value via talent, customer introductions, and founder support.
- **Macro Environment:**
  - 2021’s "easy mark‑up" world has ended; today, even strong companies face a hard fundraising market.  
  - Emerging managers like Jonathan must prove their **repeatability** without the benefit of quick paper markups.

## Careerspan

- **Core Product:** Story‑driven assessment and screening engine that turns candidate conversations into structured, high‑resolution data (skills, responsibilities, impact, preferences).  
- **Original Market:** Job‑seekers needing help articulating their stories and building a coherent, compelling narrative for applications and interviews.  
- **Current Market Shift:**
  - Moving away from monetizing job‑seekers toward **employers and ecosystems** (founders, VCs, talent platforms).  
  - Focused on solving the problem of **noisy inbound** and resume homogeneity for hiring managers.  
- **Strategic Belief:**
  - ATS systems that only see résumés are structurally incapable of delivering high‑quality AI screening at scale.  
  - The winning approach requires **deeper candidate data collection** up front, which Careerspan already does.

## Why This Conversation Matters

- Jonathan sits at a junction of **capital, founders, and talent problems**:  
  - He feels the hiring pain in his own firm and sees it across the portfolio.  
  - He understands both founder and investor constraints around time, cash, and expectations.
- If Careerspan can prove itself as a **portfolio‑wide talent signal layer**, Jonathan could:
  - Become an early, credible reference for a fund‑level product.  
  - Open doors to additional funds and founders facing the same hiring pain.
