## Key Decisions and Agreements

1. **Product-market fit validation**: Team shifted from selling "resume replacement vision" to proving concrete candidate matching ("we will show you candidates you like"). This is tactical but strategically sound—removes proof-point ambiguity.

   - **Rationale**: When promise is ambiguous, pressure on proof points is unbearable. Trading promise ambiguity for concrete proof is pragmatic

2. **ICP refinement needed**: Team will focus recruitment efforts on roles where soft skills matter more than technical tests (product, growth, operations) rather than engineering positions despite high demand

   - **Why it matters**: Creates urgency for both supply (candidates fill gaps) and demand (employers get shortlist fast)
