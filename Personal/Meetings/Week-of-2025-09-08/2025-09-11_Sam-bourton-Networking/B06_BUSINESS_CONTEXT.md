# B06 Business Context

### Market Landscape
The hiring market is shifting from transactional resume submissions to information-rich, agentic matching. Startups like Jack and Jill are seeing high acceptance of AI agents among Gen Z because agents "listen patiently" and provide a sense of being "heard."

### Problem Space
The "Articulation Gap": Candidates are often unskilled at job hunting and fail to translate their intrinsic value into a format (resume/LinkedIn) that employers can efficiently parse. This is described as a "fiber optic" communication problem.

### Careerspan Strategic Position
Careerspan aims to be a long-term "career repository" rather than a one-off placement tool. By collecting qualitative achievements over time, it builds a 3D profile of the candidate, creating an "intrinsic reason" to stay engaged with the platform beyond the job search.

### McKinsey/QB Heritage
The "firm" (McKinsey) serves as a common denominator. The "beach" culture during lulls and the loss of discipline in hyper-growth phases are shared observations that inform how both V and Sam view talent management and professional "flow."
