# Decisions Made

## Strategic Decisions

### Decision: Continue Using Zo Computer as Primary Productivity Platform
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen Attawar
- **Rationale**: "Zoe has pretty much become the centerpiece of my life. It's like basically the productivity unlock I was always looking for." After switching from Notion + multiple tools
- **Impact Assessment**: High impact - entire workflow now centered on Zo Computer
- **Supporting Evidence**: Consolidated meeting summaries, transcript processing, automatic compilation to SQL → periodic reports
- **Category**: Strategic - Technology Stack

### Decision: Do Not Fully Automate Relationship Caring via AI
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen Attawar  
- **Rationale**: "The act of asking someone to help me to be a good friend is really awesome. But if the act of being a good friend becomes fully automated, I think that's where it becomes problematic."
- **Impact Assessment**: Philosophical - defines boundary for AI assistance in personal life
- **Nuance**: AI should surface information and context to help maintain relationships, but the actual caring actions should remain human-initiated
- **Category**: Strategic - AI Boundaries

### Decision: Build Consulting Business for "Prosumer" Market
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan)
- **Rationale**: Target audience is "folks with very, let's just charitably call it relatively sophisticated taste" who are non-technical but want powerful workflows
- **Supporting Evidence**: "I quit being a management consultant at McKinsey to teach myself how to use Zapier and Airtable"
- **Market Validation**: This conversation itself validates the market - Mujgan represents exactly this user type at AGI
- **Category**: Strategic - Business Model

## Resource Allocation Decisions
