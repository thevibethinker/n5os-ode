### Competitive Positioning

**Careerspan's Differentiation** (validated by Edmund):
- **Latent Signal Discovery**: Captures non-obvious candidate capabilities through coaching engagement that resume databases miss entirely
- **Traffic Light Evaluation**: "Red/yellow/green" vs stacked ranking aligns with Edmund's stated philosophy and reduces false precision
- **Role-Specific Intelligence**: Particularly strong for emerging roles where title-holders ≠ talent pool (forward-deployed, GTM, solutions)

**Competitive Alternatives**:
- **Traditional Recruiting**: Contingency/retained search (Edmund sees Superposition as replacement for this)
- **Resume Databases**: LinkedIn Recruiter, Indeed, sourcing tools (Edmund: these only surface keyword matches)
- **Other AI Recruiting**: Gem, Lever, Greenhouse (ATS-focused), Karat/CodeSignal (assessment-focused) - none doing latent signal extraction via coaching

**Unique Advantage**: Careerspan is only player with 1500+ hours of candidate coaching interaction data that can reveal non-obvious signals. This is defensible moat because competitors would need to rebuild coaching infrastructure.

### Risk Factors & Mitigation

**Market Risks**:
1. **TAM Limitation**: If Careerspan only works for non-standard roles, addressable market smaller than full recruiting TAM
   - **Mitigation**: Validate that "non-standard" is growing faster than standard (AI-enabled roles, forward-deployed trend)

2. **Partnership Dependency**: Revenue tied to Superposition's success and willingness to continue revenue share
   - **Mitigation**: Establish direct candidate relationships, build multiple platform partnerships simultaneously

3. **Scaling Coaching Signal**: Current model based on 1500 hours of manual coaching - unclear if signal degrades with lighter-touch engagement
   - **Mitigation**: Test async coaching, recorded conversations, AI-augmented coaching to maintain signal quality at scale

**Execution Risks**:
1. **Speed Mismatch**: Edmund moving fast ("send this today"), if Careerspan can't keep pace he'll move to other priorities
   - **Mitigation**: Over-deliver on speed of initial follow-up, set realistic cadence for ongoing partnership

2. **Co-founder Alignment**: Micro Vandali not on call, risk that enthusiasm doesn't convert to execution buy-in
   - **Mitigation**: Structure proposal to be compelling to both founders (Edmund likes strategic vision, anticipate Micro may care about operational details)
