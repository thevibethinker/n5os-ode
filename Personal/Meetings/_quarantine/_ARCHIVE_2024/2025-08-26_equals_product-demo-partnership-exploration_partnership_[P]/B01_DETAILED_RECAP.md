# Detailed Recap: Equals Product Demo & Partnership Exploration

## Meeting Overview
Vrijen met with Graham Smith from Equals for a product demonstration and partnership exploration. The meeting focused on non-standard use cases for Equals' spreadsheet platform within Careerspan's workflow, specifically around job lead distribution and candidate management integration.

## Equals Product Context
Equals is a spreadsheet platform with pricing starting at $199/month. Their primary market focus is financial modeling and traditional spreadsheet use cases. The product includes:
- An AI layer for analysis and multi-data source views
- Data source integrations (Graham confirmed they use A5tran for connections)
- Plans for AI analyst features to build complex multi-data source views

## Careerspan Use Case Demonstrated
Vrijen showcased two specific non-standard use cases:

### Use Case 1: Job Lead Distribution Management
- Currently uses Google Sheets to manage all job lead distributions
- Manually extracts job descriptions and URLs from various sources
- Requires copying and pasting job information into the sheet for tracking and dissemination

### Use Case 2: AI-Powered Candidate Integration
- Careerspan's AI layer performs candidate matching and recommendation
- Output is manually placed into the spreadsheet for organization and workflow management
- The workflow involves ingestion from the sheet and output/excretion of results
- Need for agentic web search functionality to retrieve exact URLs and job descriptions automatically

## Integration Opportunities Discussed
Graham acknowledged that:
- Equals' edge cases are interesting and can materially change go-to-market strategy
- They encourage novel use cases through partnerships
- Current AI capabilities exist but more advanced features (AI analyst multi-source views) are in development
- Integration possibilities exist through their data source connection methods

## Key Outcomes

- **No SQL Requirements:** Vrijen won't need to connect a SQL database initially
- **Trial Duration:** One week to evaluate; friendly separation possible if needs aren't met
