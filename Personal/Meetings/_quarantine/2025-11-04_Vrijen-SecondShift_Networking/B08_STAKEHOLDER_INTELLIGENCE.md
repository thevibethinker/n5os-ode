**Company/Organization:** Second Shift  
**Product/Service:** UGC (User-Generated Content) growth engine + SaaS tools platform. Offers both done-for-you content production (creators produce branded content at scale) and self-serve tools for brands to manage their own campaigns.  
**Founders:** Yousef Abdel (also "Faze Apex", 29, been in content/creator space since age 14, ~15 years experience), Obeyda (last name not captured)

**4. Design Partnership Framing**
- **Observation:** Second Shift positioned early design feedback as mutual benefit ("we're building... give us feedback")
- **Why it mattered:** Removes friction for Careerspan to engage (not a sales pitch, a collaboration)
- **Signal:** Yousef explicitly asked about DIY fit before proposing partnership; tuned to Careerspan's positioning
