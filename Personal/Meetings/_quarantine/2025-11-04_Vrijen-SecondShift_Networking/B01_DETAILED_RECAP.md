## Strategic Context

**Second Shift's Value Proposition:** They've built a vetted creator network (~12+ creators mentioned) and UGC-focused tools. Moving from service delivery to SaaS. Have both DFY and self-serve tech options.

**Careerspan's Current State:** Focused on product community penetration (Lenny's Product Community, Reforge, etc.) as talent acquisition channel. Logan is the social/marketing engine (organic TikTok + LinkedIn growth). Limited paid content production to date (experimental Varun Rana video).

**Alignment Signals:** Both are product-focused founders. Second Shift explicitly called out product person appreciation. Careerspan's community-first positioning resonates with Second Shift's KOL/creator approach. No immediate revenue opportunity but strong partnership potential.

**Gap:** Vrijen delegated social/content decisions to Logan. Not clear if Careerspan has budget or bandwidth for content experiments. Second Shift sensing this (suggested DIY + tools approach rather than full service).
