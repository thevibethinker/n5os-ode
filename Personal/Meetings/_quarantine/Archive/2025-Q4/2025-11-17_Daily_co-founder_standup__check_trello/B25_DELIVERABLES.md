# B25: Deliverables

## Tangible Outputs from Meeting

### Approved Strategy: Careerspan Sponsorship + Job Drops Model
**What**: Simplified framework for Next Play partnership
- Zo hosts event with creative control
- Careerspan sponsors (no payment required)
- Careerspan announces 5-6 curated job listings at event
- Next Play members get 3-7 day early access before public posting

**Deliverable**: Event coordination framework + sponsorship positioning

10. **Post-Event Measurement Dashboard**

## Content Deliverables

### Communication Assets
- **Event sponsorship positioning**: Copy explaining Careerspan's role + job board value
- **Early access announcement**: Next Play members notification (email/Slack)
- **Job drop copy**: Narrative framing each job listing (e.g., "Early access: Engineering at Zo")
- **Founder testimonials**: 2-3 quotes from participating founders (optional but valuable)

### Process Deliverables
- **Email segmentation logic**: How to identify/target Next Play cohort
- **Job posting schedule**: Timeline (announce Day 1 to Next Play, public Day 8)
- **Fallback procedures**: If email unavailable, if founder commitments drop below 5, etc.

### Data Deliverables
- **Job performance data**: Clicks, applications, hires (post-event)
- **Founder satisfaction survey**: Did they see value in pre-screening + Careerspan pipeline?
- **Next Play member feedback**: Did early access incentivize Careerspan signup/usage?
