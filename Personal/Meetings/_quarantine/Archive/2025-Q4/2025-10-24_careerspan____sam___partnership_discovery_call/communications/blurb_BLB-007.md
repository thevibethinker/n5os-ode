# Partnership Opportunity: Careerspan

Hi Sam,

Great connecting with you. I wanted to share a quick overview of what we're building at Careerspan and how it might align with what you're working on.

**The Problem We're Solving**

Traditional recruiting is broken:
- Candidates get lost in ATS black holes and spray-and-pray applications
- Companies drown in unqualified applicants and struggle to find real talent
- Communities have engaged members but no infrastructure to help with career opportunities

**Our Solution: Community-Embedded Talent Infrastructure**

We partner with professional communities to create opt-in talent pipelines:

1. **For candidates**: Get discovered for relevant opportunities through communities they already trust (no cold applications)
2. **For companies**: Access engaged, qualified talent with genuine community signal
3. **For communities**: Add valuable career infrastructure without becoming a job board

**How It Works**

- Members create rich professional profiles through conversational AI (not resume uploads)
- Opt-in to being discoverable for opportunities aligned with their goals
- Companies search within partner communities for talent with real engagement signal
- Higher quality matches: 12-16% CTR vs. 3-5% industry standard

**Current Traction**

- 2.5 years building product
- Partnerships with Columbia, UMass, other universities
- Working with communities like Lenny's Product Community and Future of Higher Education
- Seed round planned for January 2025

**Why Partner With Us?**

- **Candidate-first approach**: Free B2C product ensures genuine engagement
- **Community-native**: We enhance communities rather than extract value
- **Proven model**: Strong engagement metrics and multi-year contracts
- **Long-term thinking**: Relationship-driven partnerships, not transactional deals

**Let's Explore**

I'd love to learn more about your goals and see if there's alignment. Happy to share more details, introduce you to community partners, or explore specific use cases.

Looking forward to continuing the conversation!
