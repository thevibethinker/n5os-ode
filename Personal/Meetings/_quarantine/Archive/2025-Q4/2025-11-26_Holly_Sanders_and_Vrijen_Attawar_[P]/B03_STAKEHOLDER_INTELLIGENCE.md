# Stakeholder Intelligence

## Primary Participants

### Holly Sanders
- **Role:** Founder of Matchbox (early‑stage recruiting marketplace/ATS hybrid focused on startup hiring).
- **Background & expertise:**
  - Deep experience in recruiting and working with VC‑backed startups; hands‑on operator who has built both relationships and product experiments in the ATS space.
  - Led development of an ATS platform with dashboards, calendar integration, interview stages, and an AI pre‑screening agent, plus a candidate network and a growing database of ~700+ early‑stage startups.
- **Current goals:**
  - Rebuild **traction and revenue** after a painful co‑founder breakup by filling concrete roles for clients and proving Matchbox’s value.
  - Evolve into a **home base for early‑stage startup roles**, where good but lesser‑known companies can attract strong talent.
  - Decide whether and how to monetise the existing ATS product (e.g., low‑friction self‑serve pricing) without overextending.
- **Constraints / pain points:**
  - Limited engineering bandwidth and lack of robust guardrails for AI systems; hesitant to ship AI‑driven guidance that might be wrong or mis‑calibrated.
  - Skeptical, change‑averse ATS customers: mid‑stage startups resist data migration; early‑stage companies don’t hire often enough to justify a heavy ATS switch.
  - Emotional and financial fatigue after a difficult co‑founder experience; needs quick, tangible wins.
- **Motivations & values:**
  - Strong bias toward *practical results* (placements, bounties, revenue) over theoretical elegance.
  - Cares about candidate experience and wants to avoid tools that over‑promise or provide noisy signal.
  - Values honesty and directness (willing to call legacy ATS vendors "bastards" while still acknowledging their dominance).

### Vrijen Attawar (V)
- **Role:** Founder of Careerspan; partner and potential infrastructure provider to Matchbox.
- **Position in this relationship:**
  - Acts as a **strategic collaborator**, not just a vendor—offering both product rails (Careerspan’s candidate story engine and employer portal) and distribution (founder/engineer communities, newsletter, job board).
  - Provides emotional support and pattern recognition from his own founder journey in a similarly hard market.
- **Leverage points:**
  - Built an AI‑powered **story‑based assessment system** that converts noisy inbound into high‑signal candidate profiles.
  - Owns a **job board and mailing list** with strong engagement metrics (~70–80% open, ~15% click‑through) plus curated networks of engineers and founders.
  - Can quickly turn roles into **Careerspan links** that filter for serious, reflective candidates and surface only the 2–5% worth deeper evaluation.

## Secondary / Off‑Call Stakeholders

### Matchbox Startup Clients
- **Who they are:** Early‑stage and growth‑stage startups (e.g., DeepTune, Yarn, Agave) hiring high‑value roles such as founding/back‑end/front‑end engineers.
- **What they want:**
  - High‑calibre candidates for critical roles, ideally delivered by a recruiter who understands startup realities and can move fast.
  - Minimal friction: many are already embedded in Ashby/Greenhouse and reluctant to switch systems.
- **Relevance:** Their satisfaction (placements closed, time‑to‑hire reduced) will determine whether Matchbox and Careerspan’s collaboration turns into durable, referenceable success.

### Careerspan Candidate & Founder Communities
- **Who they are:**
  - Mid‑ to senior‑level candidates who are willing to invest 20–60 minutes telling structured stories in exchange for improved odds of being taken seriously.
  - Founders and hiring managers who already trust V and Careerspan’s judgment and are open to experimenting with new funnels.
- **Leverage:**
  - Provide immediate distribution and a supply of candidates for Matchbox roles, especially in New York and similar hubs.

### Marianne Ray & other named candidates
- **Marianne Ray:** Senior engineer at Ginkgo Bioworks, open to a smaller team; a strong health‑tech‑adjacent candidate V can introduce to Holly.
- **Other unnamed candidates:** At least one computer‑vision‑oriented engineer and various product/engineering leads V can route into high‑priority roles.
- **Importance:** Early proof points—if one of these candidates lands via Careerspan‑Matchbox collaboration, it becomes a flagship success story.

## Relationship Assessment & Leverage Points

- **Trust level:** High. Holly is candid about co‑founder pain and business struggles; V reciprocates with transparent description of Careerspan’s own challenges and strategy. The tone is peer‑to‑peer, not transactional.
- **Strategic alignment:**
  - Both believe ATS incumbents are vulnerable on *quality of signal*; both see AI as a tool to deepen insight, not as hype.
  - Holly excels at **client‑side recruiting execution**; V excels at **candidate‑side storytelling and filtration**. The combination is naturally complementary.
- **Key leverage points:**
  - V can make Holly look powerful to candidates by surfacing curated roles quickly; Holly can make V look powerful to founders by closing hard searches.
  - If they successfully close a few bounties together, Matchbox gets narrative oxygen and cash; Careerspan gets a high‑credibility case study in a brutal market.
