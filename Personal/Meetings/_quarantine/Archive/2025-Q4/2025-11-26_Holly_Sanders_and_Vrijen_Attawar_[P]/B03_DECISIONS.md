# Decisions

1. **Use Careerspan as a signal filter in front of Matchbox’s roles.**  
   Holly and V agree that rather than building heavy AI matching inside Matchbox, Holly will lean on Careerspan’s story‑based assessment to separate high‑signal candidates from noise while Matchbox continues to own the recruiter/ATS workflow.

2. **Pilot collaboration on current high‑priority roles.**  
   They decide to start with a small set of live roles (e.g., DeepTune and Yarn’s engineering openings, plus other active searches) and treat these as a joint experiment in closing bounties using Careerspan links and Matchbox distribution.

3. **Short‑term focus on revenue over new funding.**  
   Holly decides that, despite inbound angel interest (including from senior leaders who saw her tech via accelerators and advisory work), she will *for now* prioritise closing 2–3 recruiting placements and rebuilding cash rather than chasing additional pre‑seed/seed capital.

4. **Defer heavy ATS product bets.**  
   Rather than committing immediately to a full ATS go‑to‑market, Holly will treat the existing ATS as optional infrastructure (possibly self‑serve at a low monthly price) and let actual demand and revenue traction guide whether she doubles down.

5. **Progress O‑1 visa plans only once finances improve.**  
   Holly intends to pursue an O‑1 visa and spend a few months in New York, but she decides to hold off on engaging more deeply with O‑1 service providers until she has more financial headroom from closed placements.
