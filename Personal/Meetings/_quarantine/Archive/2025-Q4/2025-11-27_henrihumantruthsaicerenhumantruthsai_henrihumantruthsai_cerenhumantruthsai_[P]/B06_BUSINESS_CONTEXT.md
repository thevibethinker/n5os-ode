# B06 – Business Context

## Careerspan

- **What Careerspan does (as surfaced in this call)**
  - Provides a **candidate story + resume analysis platform** that:
    - Captures conversational stories (spoken or typed, multi‑language) about real experiences.
    - Semantically aligns those stories and resumes to a decomposed role rubric (responsibilities, soft skills, hard skills).
    - Surfaces deeper signals about how candidates think, behave, and manage themselves.
  - Acts as a **first‑line screener** when inbound is overwhelming and traditional ATS tooling is low‑signal.

- **Differentiators emphasized**
  - **Semantic and behavioral focus**: goes beyond keyword matching to analyze how candidates describe their work.
  - **BS‑detection layer**: analyzes stylistic and semantic consistency to flag AI‑generated or inflated stories.
  - **Cross‑check with interviews**: can compare interview transcripts with prior stories for consistency.
  - **Support for non‑native English speakers**: multi‑language input normalized to English, helping Global South candidates represent themselves better.

- **Existing validation**
  - Works with **Aspire Institute**, regularly serving hundreds of candidates from Latin America, Southeast Asia, and South Asia in each cohort.
  - Internal examples (e.g., Donna vs. Donatella) illustrate how the system surfaces hidden alignment that resumes alone would miss.

## Henri & Ceren’s company (prospective client)

- **Stage and situation**
  - Early‑stage founder team (Henri and Ceren) with prior big‑tech experience (Google).
  - Have experienced **founder conflict** in the past but are now in a more stable phase.
  - Currently **capacity‑constrained** on hiring operations; have not opened public job postings because they cannot process the resulting inbound.

- **Immediate need**
  - Hire a **scrappy builder / full‑stack product engineer** who can:
    - Own back‑end and front‑end implementation.
    - Create the technical frame in which their data science and ML can shine.
    - Work closely with a non‑builder head of AI.
  - They expect to make **very few hires** (ideally interviewing no more than ~3 candidates), so every candidate interaction is high‑leverage.

- **Talent strategy**
  - Focused on candidates from the **Global South**, partly due to compensation constraints.
  - Strong emphasis on:
    - **Agency and self‑management**.
    - **Responsibility / ownership** (concerns about low ownership stereotypes in some younger cohorts).
    - Ability to **“think like western developers”** – i.e., product sense, creativity, and ability to collaborate closely with founders.

- **Hiring challenges**
  - **Inbound overload** if they post widely, without the internal capacity to screen.
  - Risk of **misrepresentation**: candidates overstating stack experience or using AI to fabricate stories around the published stack.
  - Need to balance **candidate friction vs. depth of signal** in a way that doesn’t repel strong builders.

## Fit between the two businesses

- **Problem–solution alignment**
  - Henri and Ceren’s key pain points (overwhelming inbound, lack of trustworthy signal, fear of faking, need to evaluate agency and product thinking) map directly onto Careerspan’s strengths.

- **Global South and fairness**
  - Their geographic focus aligns with Careerspan’s tested usage via Aspire Institute and multi‑language design.
  - Careerspan can help them distinguish between candidates who merely meet technical skill minimums and those who exhibit **mature self‑management and product judgment**.

- **Risk / unknowns**
  - Funding status, revenue model, and long‑term hiring plan for Henri & Ceren’s company were **not discussed** on this call.
  - Their eventual volume of hires beyond this first builder is unclear; this collaboration may be primarily a **pilot / proof‑of‑fit** phase.

- **Strategic upside**
  - If the engagement goes well, Henri and Ceren’s company can become a **case study for builder hiring in lean founder teams**, showcasing:
    - Story‑based assessment of product engineers.
    - Global South talent evaluation.
    - How Careerspan’s BS detection and interview cross‑checks work in practice.
