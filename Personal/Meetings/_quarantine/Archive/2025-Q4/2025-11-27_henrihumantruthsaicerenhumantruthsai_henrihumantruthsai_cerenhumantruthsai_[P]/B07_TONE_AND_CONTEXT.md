# B07 – Tone and Context

## Overall vibe

- The meeting is **constructive, optimistic, and forward‑looking**, with everyone aligned on experimenting together.
- There is no visible tension or disagreement; all friction points are treated as design parameters to tune rather than deal‑breakers.
- The call ends with light banter about disrespecting holidays by working through them, reinforcing the sense of cultural fit and shared work ethic.
