# Zo vs. Zapier: Why Natural Language Wins

**Zapier is powerful—but it's programming in disguise.** You're still connecting nodes, setting up conditions, handling edge cases. It works beautifully for straightforward tasks, but anything complex becomes a maze of configurations.

**Zo flips this:** Describe what you want in English, and AI figures out the steps. Your workflow gets smarter over time because AI understands context, not just IF/THEN rules. For simple tasks, they're equivalent. For complex information workflows, Zapier feels like writing assembly code while Zo feels like describing what you need.

**The real difference:** You're not programming a machine—you're delegating to something that thinks.
