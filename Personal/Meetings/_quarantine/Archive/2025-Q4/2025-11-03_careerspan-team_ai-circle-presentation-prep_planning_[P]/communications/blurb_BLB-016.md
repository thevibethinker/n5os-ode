Most AI tools are like really smart consultants—you ask a question, they give you an answer, then forget the conversation existed. Zo's AI is more like a colleague who lives in your workspace—it remembers what you built yesterday, it knows where your files are, it can work on projects over days or weeks without you constantly re-explaining context. That persistence and memory? That's agenticity. It's the difference between asking for help repeatedly versus having an AI that actually helps you build and maintain things over time.
