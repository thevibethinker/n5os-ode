# Why a Virtual Computer Matters: What Zo Enables

Zo is AWS for normal people—a personal cloud server that runs continuously and integrates your entire digital life. Unlike chatbots (which forget), automation tools (which require node-based programming), or vibe coding (which builds websites), Zo gives AI persistent access to your files, tools, and workflows.

**What this enables:**
- Build custom workflows in English instead of node diagrams
- Run AI processes continuously (not just on-demand)
- Integrate with everything you already use
- Own your data and keep it on your server
- Start simple (chat interface) and grow to full applications

**The difference:** You get a new kind of computer—one that thinks and acts on your behalf, not just responds when you ask.
