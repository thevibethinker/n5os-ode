Zo doesn't replace your existing tools—it works alongside them. Your Zapier workflows stay, your email keeps flowing, your spreadsheets remain. Zo adds a natural language layer for the complex coordination that node-based tools don't handle well. It's an alternative you choose for specific work, not a replacement that forces migration.
