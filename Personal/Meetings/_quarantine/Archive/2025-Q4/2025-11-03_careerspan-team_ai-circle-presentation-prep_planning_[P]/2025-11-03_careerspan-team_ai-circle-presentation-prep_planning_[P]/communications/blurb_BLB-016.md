Agenticity means the AI can take actions on your behalf without stopping to ask. Chat with ChatGPT, and it responds then waits. Give Zo an instruction, and it reads your calendar, processes yesterday's emails, generates a summary, and posts it—all while you're doing something else. The difference between having a conversation and having an assistant. It's what makes AI useful for real work.
