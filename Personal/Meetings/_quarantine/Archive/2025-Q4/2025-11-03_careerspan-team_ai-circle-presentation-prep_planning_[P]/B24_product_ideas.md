## Competitive Response Features

### vs. Lovable: Dynamic Data Integration
**Emphasis:** Sites that integrate with workspace data, not just static pages
**Example:** Articles folder automatically populates website section
**Message:** "Your website as view into your workspace"

### vs. Zapier: Accessibility
**Emphasis:** Natural language vs. visual programming
**Target:** People who found Zapier "too hard to figure out"
**Consideration:** What about Zapier power users? Migration path?

### vs. ChatGPT/Claude: Persistence & Memory
**Emphasis:** Living system vs. conversation session
**Example:** Workflows run continuously, maintain state
**Challenge:** Communicating value of "always-on" agent
