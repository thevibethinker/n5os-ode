## Risk Considerations & Monitoring Points

1. **User Adoption:** Quality gating (3+ stories) ensures signal, but may limit initial volume; monitor early adoption rates
2. **Data Privacy:** Ensure admin access and data sharing aligns with Careerspan's terms and user privacy policies
3. **Email Fatigue:** Auto-trigger emails + promotional messaging could drive unsubscribes; monitor engagement rates
4. **Attribution Complexity:** Tracking user journey across three platforms (Careerspan → Practice Interviews → Apply AI → job offer) requires clean data pipeline
