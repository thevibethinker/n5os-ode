# B06_BUSINESS_CONTEXT

- **Product:** Careerspan is using a Notion-based job board to broadcast open roles, closed hires, and talent calls. The board is intended to link David’s Notion page and the Zoe roles so that every position has context before the public sees it.
- **Talent strategy:** The trio of categories (coming soon, closed, open) plus a new “talent call” signal lets Careerspan operate like a concierge, collecting profiles in pockets even while the platform still lacks magic links for candidate communication.
- **Candidate communication gap:** Notion does not notify applicants when a role closes, so Careerspan must close roles manually, inform pending candidates through Slack/Linear, and clearly instruct employer partners that the hand-off includes owning follow-up once we pass introductions.
- **Urgency:** Candidates interpret silence as a “black hole,” so every published role needs a narrative (location, status, next step) before the job board link goes live to avoid losing trust.
