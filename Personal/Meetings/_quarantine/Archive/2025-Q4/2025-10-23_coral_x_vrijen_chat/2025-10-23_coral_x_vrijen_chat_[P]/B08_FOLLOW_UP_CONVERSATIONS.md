## Decision Tree: If Coral Feedback is Negative

**Scenario:** Coral tries product and gives critical feedback

**Response Protocol:**
1. Listen + understand specific concerns
2. Assess if feedback reflects product gap or user experience issue
3. Options:
   - **Product Gap:** Add to roadmap; circle back in 3 months
   - **UX Issue:** Quick fix; retest before proceeding
   - **Fundamental Misalignment:** Acknowledge; explore other partnership models (consulting, Fix Your Feed integration)
4. Maintain relationship regardless; reframe as valuable feedback

**Do NOT:** Abandon relationship if feedback is critical; instead, demonstrate responsiveness
