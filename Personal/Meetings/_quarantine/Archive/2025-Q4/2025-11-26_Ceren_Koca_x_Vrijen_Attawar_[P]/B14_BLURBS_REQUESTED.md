# Blurbs Requested / Blurb Opportunities

> Note: No one explicitly said "please send me a blurb" on this call. However, Ceren did suggest doing a **case study** and public **post** if Careerspan helps them hire, and both sides discussed promoting the role/link across networks. The blurbs below capture *implied* future needs so they are ready when asked.

## A. Blurb for Ceren to use when posting the Careerspan hiring link

**Short version (for WhatsApp/Slack/intro emails)**

> We’re hiring a senior product‑oriented engineer to help us build an AI‑powered research platform funded by the Gates Foundation. Instead of the usual resume drop, we’re using Careerspan’s story‑based application link below. If you’re excited about serious research, applied behavioral science, and practical AI, I’d love for you to apply.

**LinkedIn‑length version**

> We’re looking for a **senior product‑oriented engineer** to join our early‑stage, Gates‑funded startup building an AI‑enabled research platform. Our goal is to *amplify* professional researchers, not replace them, by combining rigorous methods with thoughtful automation.
>
> Instead of a traditional resume drop, we’re partnering with **Careerspan**, which uses a story‑based application to understand how you think, work, and learn—not just your tech stack. If that sounds like you, I’d love to hear your story.
>
> Apply here: [Careerspan link]

## B. Future case‑study blurb (once hire is made)

> We found our senior engineer through **Careerspan**, using their story‑based application instead of a traditional ATS. It let us see how candidates thought about real problems and collaboration, not just what was on their resumes. As a non‑technical founder building an AI research platform, that extra signal was critical—and it helped us make a high‑conviction hire under tight Gates Foundation timelines.

## C. Blurb for Ceren to introduce Careerspan to HR / people leaders

> I’m using **Careerspan** to hire for our Gates‑funded AI research startup because it gives me a much deeper view of candidates than resumes or keyword filters. Candidates go through a short, AI‑guided storytelling experience, and Careerspan summarizes their strengths, risks, and working style so I can focus interviews on real fit. If you’re rethinking how you evaluate talent beyond the ATS, I think you’d find it interesting.
