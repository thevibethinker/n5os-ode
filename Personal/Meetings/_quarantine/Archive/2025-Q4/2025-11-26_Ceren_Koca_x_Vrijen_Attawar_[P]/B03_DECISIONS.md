# Decisions

1. **Proceed with Careerspan as hiring partner for senior technical role**  
   - Ceren agrees in principle to use Careerspan’s storytelling‑based hiring flow to search for a senior product‑oriented engineer and, potentially, a technical advisor.  
   - Commercial model accepted: **~$200/month** to "rent the link" while the role is open.

2. **Run a focused JD and hiring‑profile session**  
   - Decision to schedule a short, high‑signal session with V (and ideally Ilsa) plus Ceren and Henry to:  
     - Clarify the role (senior product engineer vs. traditional CTO).  
     - Translate Ceren’s and Henry’s needs into explicit requirements, including non‑technical traits (startup brain, product brain, technical brain).

3. **Careerspan to activate employer account and job link**  
   - After the JD session, V will spin up an employer account for Ceren’s company and generate a live job link that can be posted across channels.

4. **Dual‑channel distribution strategy**  
   - Ceren will share the Careerspan job link through her own channels (LinkedIn audience, B2B network, founder communities).  
   - V and Logan will distribute the same link into curated technical and founder communities (e.g., FOHI, engineering networks, alumni/VC circles) where they already have high‑trust relationships.

5. **Pursue technical‑advisor support via Careerspan network**  
   - V and Logan commit to:  
     - Speak with Ilsa about potential advisory involvement and/or referrals.  
     - Compile a short list of credible technical advisors who have early‑stage startup experience and are comfortable with modest, time‑boxed engagements (~10 hours) rather than heavy ongoing commitments.

6. **Mutual support beyond this hire**  
   - If the collaboration succeeds, both sides agree in principle to:  
     - Explore a public case study highlighting how Ceren’s team "found our senior engineer through Careerspan."  
     - Exchange introductions: Ceren to HR leaders and people teams at large companies; V and Logan to founders, impact‑oriented orgs, and technical talent in their ecosystem.
