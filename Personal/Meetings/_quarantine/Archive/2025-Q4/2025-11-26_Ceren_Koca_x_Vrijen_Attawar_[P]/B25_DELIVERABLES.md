# Deliverables

## From Careerspan to Ceren

1. **Sharpened JD + hiring profile for senior product engineer**  
   - Output of a structured JD session including:  
     - Responsibilities and scope for the senior engineer relative to Henry.  
     - Required technical skills (stack, architecture comfort, product‑engineering mindset).  
     - Desired working‑style traits (startup brain, product brain, technical brain; bias toward pragmatism over cleverness).

2. **Configured Careerspan employer account**  
   - Live employer portal with Ceren’s company set up as an organization.  
   - Role configured with the above JD and screening criteria.

3. **Job application link (Careerspan story‑based flow)**  
   - A single URL Ceren can post across LinkedIn, communities, and direct outreach.  
   - Candidate‑side experience: guided storytelling conversation (ideally audio‑first) that yields rich STAR‑like examples.  
   - Employer‑side: summary views surfacing alignment, risks, and notable traits so Ceren can focus manual review on the top 2–5% of applicants.

4. **Candidate and advisor sourcing support**  
   - A short list of recommended **technical advisors** (with brief bios and why‑they‑fit notes).  
   - A distribution plan listing communities and channels where Careerspan will share the role, including copy snippets where helpful.

5. **Potential warm introductions**  
   - Intros to:  
     - Senior engineers and product‑engineers who are mission‑aligned and open to early‑stage risk.  
     - Other founders (especially in edtech/research tooling) who can share patterns and references.
