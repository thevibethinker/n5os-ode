## Acquisition Outreach Process Decision
**Decision:** Implement systematic 5-companies-per-week outreach model starting next week

**Context:**
- Acquisition/partnership conversations needed for growth
- Multiple target companies: Darwin Box, Future Fit, others identified
- Previously: ad-hoc, reactive approach (responding to inbound inquiries)
- Need: Proactive, scalable, consistent approach

**The Model:**
- Each week: Identify 5 target companies
- Action: "Blitz connect" to decision makers within those companies
- Week 2: New set of 5 companies (not repeats)
- Continuous rotation ensuring new opportunities each week
- ~10% of weekly effort allocated here

**Rationale:**
- Prevents "every week no forward motion" paralysis
- Creates rhythm: "We're moving with some conversation every week"
- Limits weekly cognitive load (5 targets = manageable)
- Ensures consistent momentum without overwhelming primary tracks (product sales + marketing)
- Scalable as company grows (could expand to 10+ later)
- Captures target company seasonality/timing naturally

**Execution:**
- First week (this week): Foundation-laying (marketing, messaging, partnerships)
- Week 2: Launch systematic 5-company/week outreach with agreed-upon story
- Continuous thereafter

**Messaging Consistency:**
- Will use standardized case presentation:
  - Team composition + skills + backgrounds
  - Progress to date
  - Forward roadmap (next few months)
  - Tech stack
  - Current business metrics
  - Integration timeline (February target)

## Sales Messaging Hierarchy Decision
**Decision:** Create tiered messaging framework (one-liner → paragraph → pager → demo)

**Context:**
- Need consistent way to convert prospects through pipeline
- Target: ~100 trial candidates
- Previously: scattered, ad-hoc messaging
- Two use cases: product sales (demo conversion) AND acquisition conversations

**The Framework:**
1. **One-liner** - Pithy, memorable description (conversational opener)
2. **One-paragraph** - Expanded value prop + key benefits (~3-4 sentences)
3. **One-pager** - Full positioning, problem, solution, team overview, differentiation
4. **30-minute demo** - In-person conversation to close

**Rationale:**
- Different audiences/contexts need different message depth
- Consistency across all outreach (product sales + partnership conversations)
- Easy to reference, reuse, and update
- Logan to develop with Elias this week
- Ready to deploy next week

**Execution:**
- Logan responsible for drafting with Elias
- Vrijen will use for partnership/Darwin Box responses
- Will be packaged for acquisition conversations too
- Updates weekly as product/strategy evolves (not waiting for perfection)

**Related Decision:** Don't wait for mythical "perfect" version; document current state, update weekly. Better to have 80% answer ready than 0% answer waiting for 100%.

## Jim Conti Call Strategy Decision
**Decision:** Position call as exploratory learning conversation, NOT a hard sales pitch

**Context:**
- Jim Conti: GP and Head of Talent at Hyde Park Ventures
- Original instinct: Pitch Careerspan to him for investment/partnership
- David (likely advisor/coach) advised different approach

**The Strategy:**
- Open: Transparent about Careerspan, what it does, why it exists
- Ask: "You're a VC involved in talent space—how would you perceive this?"
- Frame: "I'm trying to learn from you so I can pitch others better"
- Subtext: Show Careerspan in positive light while genuinely seeking insights
- Not: "Give us money" or "Partner with us" (first call)

**Rationale:**
- Jim has relevant expertise and perspective worth learning from
- Hard pitch on first call feels desperate, reduces his investment in conversation
- Learning approach makes him feel respected, positions Vrijen as thoughtful
- Creates better foundation for future collaboration if opportunity emerges
- Sets tone for deeper relationship over multiple conversations
- If he's impressed by Vrijen's approach, he'll likely help intro others

**Pre-Call Task:**
- Draft written agreement with Ulrich/Oscar confirming Hyde Park Ventures talent partner status
- Have this ready to reference if relevant (shows validation)
- But don't lead with it (exploratory conversation first)
