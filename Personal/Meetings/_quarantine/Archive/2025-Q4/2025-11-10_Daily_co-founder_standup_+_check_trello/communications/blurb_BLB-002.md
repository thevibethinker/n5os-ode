# Careerspan One-Paragraph Summary

Careerspan is a career development platform that helps talented professionals communicate their value more effectively—solving the problem where moderate self-promoters lose opportunities to less talented but more aggressive chest-beaters.

Most career development fails because it's either too generic (training programs) or doesn't scale (1:1 coaching). We built something different: AI-powered frameworks paired with human coaches that structure career conversations into actionable workflows. Skills assessment, transition planning, manager alignment—the frameworks that actually move careers forward, delivered at scale.

We started by recording 2,000+ hours of career coaching conversations across incredibly diverse professionals—science fiction writers, Netflix executives, HVAC technicians, fresh graduates. That coaching data became ~1,000 candidate parameters that capture who people actually are, not just resume keywords. Now we're bringing that same approach to enterprise teams managing retention and internal mobility, and to companies looking for talent matching that reduces resume noise down to 2-5% high-quality candidates.

Built for talented people who struggle to "play the game" as aggressively as their peers—helping them show up authentically while still getting recognized for great work.
