# Key Moments & Memorable Quotes

## "We're Building Chest Beating as a Service"

**Logan**: "If only there was a product for this."  
**V**: "Right? And here we are building chest beating as a service."  
**Logan**: "Yeah. I was literally about to say that."

**Context**: The most crystallized articulation of Careerspan's value proposition emerged organically. V and Logan were discussing how talented people (like Logan's brother Andrew, who makes $200k as an AI roboticist at Teradyne) often lose career opportunities to less talented individuals who are better at self-promotion. V captured the insight perfectly: the company's actual service is helping quiet achievers learn to market themselves effectively—literally becoming better at "chest beating" (professional self-advocacy and visibility).

**Significance**: This is quotable, memorable, and should anchor marketing messaging and pitch decks. It captures both the humor and the serious market insight simultaneously.

## Personal Narrative: Importance of Regular Connection

**V** on maintaining friendships post-college:  
"We need to, as a group, remember to come together every, like, year or two. Because it's really, like, amazing thing. I feel so lucky every time how, like, awesome my friends are from college. But it's like, I wish we just all had more chances to see each other."

**Logan** on creating recurring cadence:  
"Once you get a relatively regular cadence of a trip, you're like, 'we don't need a wedding to get this shit done.' It is really fun to kind of have something on the calendar."

**Significance**: Reveals founder mindset about relationships, intentionality, and systems thinking. The impulse to create regular cadence (not just event-driven connection) mirrors the structured weekly acquisition outreach strategy they're implementing for business. Suggests founder perspective on sustainable relationship-building applies to both personal and professional contexts.
