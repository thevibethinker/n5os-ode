## Aviato's Core Hiring Challenge

Aviato faces a scalability problem masquerading as a volume problem. They receive quality candidates (self-selecting through their intentionally high-friction application process), but lack systematic infrastructure to evaluate them at scale. The root issue: their "low friction for serious candidates" strategy requires manual review—each application demands cognitive overhead to assess cultural alignment and transferable skills beyond resume data.

### Why Careerspan Fits

Careerspan solves what Aviato cannot automate away: candidate evaluation at scale while preserving the cultural-fit emphasis that defines their hiring philosophy. The platform's core value isn't replacing human judgment; it's amplifying human discernment across hundreds of applicants without cognitive burnout.
