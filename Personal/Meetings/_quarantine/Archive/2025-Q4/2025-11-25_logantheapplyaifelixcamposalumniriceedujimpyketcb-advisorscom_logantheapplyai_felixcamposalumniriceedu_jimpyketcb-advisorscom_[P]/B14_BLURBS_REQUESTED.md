# Blurbs & Intro Text – Rice x Careerspan

> No one explicitly said "please send us a blurb" in this meeting, but V committed to follow‑up materials (email + demo) that will effectively function as shareable blurbs. Drafts below assume external audiences Felix and Jim may want to reach.

## 1. Short Blurb for SEE/CSE Students

**Audience:** Rice engineering undergrads in SEE/CSE and similar programs.

> Careerspan is a story‑driven career tool that helps you talk about your work in a way that actually lands. You speak your experiences out loud; Careerspan turns them into strong resume bullets, surfaces the skills you’re really showing, and helps you see which parts of a job description you already match—and where you still have gaps. It’s built for people who don’t have a stack of family connections or a perfectly polished narrative yet, and it’s free to use through the Rice Engineering Alumni partnership.

## 2. Short Blurb for Early‑Career Alumni

**Audience:** Rice engineering alumni in their first 5–10 years post‑graduation.

> Careerspan is an AI‑assisted career companion designed for real, messy careers. You record short stories about projects you’ve done; the system turns them into resume‑ready bullets, maps your skills against roles you’re considering, and highlights the gaps you may want to close before you apply. Instead of rewriting your resume from scratch for every opportunity, you grow a library of stories you can reuse and tailor. Rice Engineering Alumni is piloting access so you can try it at no cost while we explore a longer‑term partnership.

## 3. Blurb for Rice‑Affiliated Employers

**Audience:** Rice alumni and companies that hire engineers.

> Careerspan is a hiring tool built to surface great candidates who don’t always "win" on traditional resumes. Candidates who apply through Careerspan have already done the work of recording 2–3 structured stories about their experience. Our system maps those stories to your job description, flags potential deal‑breakers, and shows you where each person is strong or weak against the skills you care about. For Rice‑affiliated employers, this creates a way to reach Rice students and alumni who’ve put in real effort and can back up their interest with concrete stories.

## 4. Micro Blurb for Newsletters / Email Subject Lines

- "Careerspan: AI‑assisted storytelling and skill‑signalling for Rice engineers."
- "Turn your projects into interview‑ready stories in 20 minutes a month."
- "A pro‑candidate AI hiring tool, now piloting with Rice Engineering Alumni."
