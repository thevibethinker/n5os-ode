# BUSINESS_CONTEXT

## Revenue Model & Positioning
Careerspan is shifting from transactional candidate-coaching revenue to **portfolio-level B2B partnerships** with VC firms. The business model centers on becoming the talent-edge enabler for entire portfolio companies simultaneously, not individual consulting arrangements.

### Current Strategy: VC Portfolio Expansion
**The Model:**
- Pilot with single portfolio company (Sapphire) → prove value on 3-5 positions
- Demonstrate impact through top-tier candidate outcomes
- Expansion thesis: "If we deliver exceptional results on the first company, the VC wants the entire portfolio"

**Example Math from Meeting:**
- Sapphire has 3 open positions → source top 3 candidates
- 9 total candidates prepped through Careerspan system
- All 9 excel in interviews (via Jeff collaboration)
- Result: VC sees consistent excellence across all candidates
- Decision: Portfolio-wide deployment (all portfolio companies, all positions)

**Why This Matters:**
- Scale: Move from $5K-$20K per candidate to potential $500K+ portfolio contracts
- Longevity: Portfolio relationships are sticky; once embedded, retention is high
- Network effect: Success with one company becomes proof point for others in portfolio

## Partnership Architecture
**Careerspan + Sapphire/Elise + Jeff (Interview Coach)**
- Careerspan: Profiles, market intelligence, skill matching
- Sapphire/Elise: Client sourcing, hiring decision authority
- Jeff: Pre-interview preparation, coaching, final polish

**The Flywheel:**
1. Careerspan surfaces top candidates from network
2. Jeff preps them intensively for interviews
3. Candidates interview exceptionally well
4. Hiring manager attributes success to Careerspan's candidate quality AND preparation ecosystem
5. Portfolio expansion follows

## Revenue Implications
- **Current concern:** Jeff partnership structure — avoid sharing revenue (e.g., 25% cut per placement)
- **Preferred structure:** "Bring-in" model where Careerspan controls client relationships, Jeff is strategic collaborator
- **Justification:** Careerspan provides sourcing, system, and ongoing value; interview prep is enabler, not differentiator

## Competitive Positioning
Against traditional recruiting firms:
- Faster turnaround (Careerspan identifies + preps + delivers in ~6 weeks vs. 3-6 months traditional)
- Higher success rate (prep component ensures candidates perform in interviews)
- Better economics for client (outcome-based, not per-placement fees)

## Market Timing
- **Sapphire/Elise demo (Nov 26):** Proof of concept; they're actively hiring
- **Early December intake:** Real job descriptions, real candidates
- **Mid-December interviews:** Live validation; candidates performing at high level
- **Early 2026:** Portfolio expansion pitch to VC partner
