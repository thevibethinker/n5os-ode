- Careerspan now has five live employer engagements (two Zo roles plus three founder-backed openings) that will be the testbed for proving the ROI of automated candidate briefings, deal-breaker dashboards, and story-driven matching.
- The Aviato-powered workflow demonstrates the product’s vendor integrations: identify a profile, enrich with Aviato, and send an employer-tailored email that highlights why the candidate is the best hire, complete with radar charts for strengths/weaknesses.
- The core customer pain remains wasted recruiter time (Jeff Hyman’s pre-screening example), mismatched questioners, and the “garbage intermediaries” that prevent hiring managers and applicants from seeing the same story; Careerspan’s narrative is that its conversational coaching plus qualitative matching eliminates those inefficiencies.
- Marketing and outbound are being refocused under Ilia while the team sheds burn and self-funds, but new content/outbound partnerships (Roxine’s LinkedIn, the Cornell-mounted Clay Agency) can accelerate awareness once given dialing-in direction.
- Enterprise conversations are coming from multiple angles: Sapphire Ventures (as a customer), Future Fit AI, Darwin Box, and a former HireVue CEO exploring partnerships, plus staffing agencies that need a higher-volume matching engine.
