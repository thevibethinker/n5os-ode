# Decisions: Tim x Vrijen Catch-up

## Strategic Decisions Made

### Careerspan GTM Pivot (Vrijen)
**Decision:** Shift from traditional fundraising to direct-to-VC sales approach for Careerspan ATS platform.

**Rationale:** VCs are moving toward Series A-level hiring intensity (entire annual hiring in compressed timeframe). They are informed, savvy customers who understand hiring challenges and will buy solutions that demonstrably improve candidate filtering—unlike when they're approached as investors and "turn stupid."

**Supporting Evidence:**
- Sapphire Ventures demo scheduled (high-impact target)
- Marvin Venters (McKinsey Fund) already signing LOI
- Product shows clear PMF signals with VC customer interest

### Careerspan Team Structure & Resource Allocation (Vrijen)
**Decision:** Reduce to skeleton crew while maintaining product distribution capability.

**Supporting Actions:**
- Let go of additional team members beyond previous layoffs
- Brought on Ilya for sales and scalable marketing automations/videos
- Pivoting from high-touch sales to scalable video/content-driven distribution

**Rationale:** Product is in "fucking decent shape" for distribution and cash generation without full headcount. Reduces burn while maintaining execution capability.

### Careerspan Product Distribution Strategy (Vrijen)
**Decision:** Target both founder market (100s of dollars per setup) and VC market simultaneously.

**Approach:**
- Smaller-stage founders: low-friction onboarding ($100-200+ per account)
- VCs: higher-value contracts with better value prop than existing ATS solutions
- Cherry Tree channel partnership with Tim for founder distribution

### Zo Infrastructure Monetization (Vrijen)
**Decision:** Pursue 50/50 partnership model to scale custom Zo workflow installations.

**Details:**
- Seeking fresh talent (post-college, scrappy) to become "installer"
- Installer handles ground pounding, relationship cultivation to build network
- Vrijen provides repo and 15-20 minute technical coaching
- Target customers: VCs and tech leaders interested in Zo workflows
- Benefits installer: access to powerful tech contacts, network-building opportunity

**Rationale:** 
- Vrijen lacks time to handle individual installations at scale
- Installer gains valuable network access and opportunity to build reputation
- Creates natural relationship with Zo team and partners
- VC customers use sophisticated tools and are willing to pay

### Tim's Career Direction (Tim)

**Context:**
- Left Twill (~2 weeks prior) due to misalignment on venture-scalable business model
- Cherry Tree building equity calculator + co-founder agreement generator (launch next month)
- Actively interviewing with multiple companies (keeping options open)
- Exploring growth/GTM roles at well-funded startups

**Context:**
