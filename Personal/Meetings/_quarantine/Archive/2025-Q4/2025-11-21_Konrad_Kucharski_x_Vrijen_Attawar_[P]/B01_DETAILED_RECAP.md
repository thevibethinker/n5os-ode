## The Problem: Hiring in the AI Age (05:00 - 12:00)

- **V's Question:** What is painful about hiring right now?
- **Konrad's Answer:**
  - **Efficiency:** "Can I make my people more efficient?"
  - **Person vs. Tool:** Trying to find the balance. He notes that for sales (CRM), tools often add *more* admin work instead of less. He wants tools that actually save time.
  - **Ramp Time:** Fear of hiring someone who looks good on paper/interview but fails 6 months later.
- **V's Response:** Cites the stat "46% of hires fail by 18 months." Positions Careerspan as the solution to this "noise."

## The Demo: Careerspan for Employers (12:00 - 25:00)

- **Ilse Demos:**
  1. **Ingesting the JD:** System breaks down a job description into \~30 customizable skills/responsibilities (Soft & Hard).
  2. **"Telling Stories":** Candidates don't fill out forms; they talk to the AI coach (20-25 mins).
  3. **Matching:** The system cross-references candidate "stories" against the JD criteria.
- **Key Feature - The Resume Gap:** Ilse shows an example ("Donatella") where the resume scores low (62/100) because it misses keywords (e.g., React), but the *stories* might reveal transferrable skills.
- **Konrad's Question:** "What if I don't have a job description?"
  - **Answer:** You need a semblance of one, but V mentions a partner agency that helps draft them.

## Deep Dive: Assessment & BS Detector (25:00 - 35:00)

- **Konrad's Q:** "Is this analyzing the PDF resume?"
  - **V's A:** It analyzes *both* the resume and the stories (semantic analysis).
- **The "BS Detector" (Consistency Check):**
  - Ilse explains how the AI checks for consistency in voice/style across stories and interviews.
  - Example: A "Senior PM" shouldn't sound like a "Junior PM."
  - This prevents candidates from "20x-ing themselves" just for the interview.
