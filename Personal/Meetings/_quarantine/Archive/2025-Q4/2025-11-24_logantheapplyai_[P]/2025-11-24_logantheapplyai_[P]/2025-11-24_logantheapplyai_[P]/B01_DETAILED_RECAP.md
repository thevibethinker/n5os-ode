## Overview
This stand-up was redirected into a working session to turn the Next Play/Ben Guo connection into a marketing and hiring experiment for Careerspan. Vrijen and Logan sketched how to use a single high-signal community as a launch pad for a digital series (“Hack Your Career”) and to demonstrate a low-touch passive hiring workflow, while also locking in the immediate operational and marketing steps needed to make both moves real.

## Chronology
1. **Opening check-in:** The call starts with light banter about desk setups, feng shui, and the usual crew checking in, which buys a few quiet minutes to warm up before transitioning to strategy.
2. **Bringing Ben back into the funnel:** They revisit why Ben Guo/Next Play matters—he runs a selective, $12-per-month community filled with director- and VP-level talent, he is comfortable with events, and he has the VC and operator connections (Lightspeed, Lorimer, Sapphire) that Careerspan needs.
3. **Event and content ideas:** Logan frames a “Hack Your Career” series (two-part digital webinars featuring Ben and Zoe) to showcase collaboration with Next Play, Enrich, and Well Fed, and to make the case that Careerspan is a tactical + strategic partner who can shoulder most of the work; they plan to present those options in a bulletized menu before asking for a short call.
4. **Passive hiring + talent-call experiment:** They brainstorm making Careerspan links the default “always recruiting” CTA on founder sites, seeding the job board with early-access signals, and timeboxing a talent call experiment (10 positions, founders get a three-day head start) while ensuring Ilsa understands the accountability and compute requirements (reset portal password, add a dummy Chair AI job entry for demos).
5. **Marketing and candidate timing:** Logan is building out low-budget Instagram and paid campaigns with Ilya this week so they can command December’s earning window, then shift to candidate marketing in the latter half of the month. They also keep “dark arts” outreach alive by replicating the Aviato leapfrog and using Ben’s introductions to VCs to secure more demos.
6. **Next steps:** Vrijen commits to sending the Ben/Ben Lang outreach with a menu of collaborative options and to pushing for a call after Thanksgiving, while Logan will help plan the two-hour blitz that crystallizes the experiment, and Ilsa will do the one-time portal work before the experiment kicks off.

## Strategic threads
- **Next Play as a channel:** Use Ben’s credibility with Lightspeed, Lorimer, and high-signal founders to demonstrate value, run a “hack your career” digital series, and attach Careerspan to the kinds of community events Ben already values.
- **Passive hiring net:** Treat founder websites as always-on talent calls, show them how to embed Careerspan links, and measure the viability of that workflow before layering more automation or paid marketing on top.
- **Marketing cadence:** Run paid and organic campaigns this week, book demos during the pre-holiday window, and pivot to candidate recruitment messaging in December once the proof points stack up.
- **Operations discipline:** Keep the experiment low-lift for Ilsa by resetting passwords and adding illustrative dummy jobs, scope compute as a secondary issue, and only celebrate the experiment if it stays within a two-week, 10-position time box.
