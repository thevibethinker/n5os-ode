# B06 – Business Context

## Stitch Sense AI

### Business Overview
- **Company:** Stitch Sense AI (Alexis & Misha).
- **Location:** Based in Dubai / UAE.
- **Domain:** Factory optimization – AI-driven scheduling, quality, and operations support for manufacturing (initially textiles/CPG-adjacent factories).

### Stage & Traction
- **Stage:** Pre‑revenue but approaching first deployments.
- **Commercial pipeline:**
  - **1 committed production contract** with a factory.
  - **~4–5 additional factories** in queue (LOIs or strong verbal commitments) contingent on V1 delivery.
- **Team:**
  - Founders with big-tech background plus a **newly hired technical team** that is both talented and cost-efficient.
  - Hiring quality is materially better than their initial attempts, thanks to hard‑earned experience.

### Capital Structure
- **Bootstrapped:** To date, funding has come from founders’ own savings.
- **Clean cap table:**
  - No external investors.
  - No employee or contractor equity issued.
- This makes them an attractive target for **first-money-in** investors and programs like YC, should they choose to pursue them.

### Strategic Questions
- **If / when to raise:**
  - How to time the first raise relative to:
    - V1 launch.
    - First factories going live.
    - Initial revenue and proof points.
- **Instrument choice:**
  - How to balance SAFEs vs. priced rounds vs. convertible notes.
  - How to navigate cross-border issues (UAE entity, potential non‑UAE investors).
- **Investor mix:**
  - Non‑US high-net-worth angels vs. US VCs vs. accelerators.
  - Niche, sector-focused VCs (e.g., XRC Ventures, RVC Capital) vs. generalists.

## Careerspan

### Business Overview
- **Company:** Careerspan (V).
- **Domain:** Career coaching and AI-powered interviewing / assessment.

### Product & Traction
- Built a **deep conversational interviewing AI**:
  - Average activated user spends roughly **an hour** talking to the system.
  - Quality is competitive with or better than existing UK market leaders.
- Recently sold an initial batch of **job-inventory slots**, generating about **$1k** quickly by having employers post Career span links instead of using traditional ATS flows.

### Capital & Structure
- Previously raised approximately **$1M** from a family friend investor in India, via a priced round with significant legal overhead.
- Currently exploring a potential **reverse acquisition**:
  - Led by a **former CEO of HireVue**, who took HireVue from ~$1M to ~$40M.
  - Contemplating folding Careerspan tech and team into that company, with V or Logan running the combined entity.

### Strategic Questions
- How to balance:
  - Continuing to scale Careerspan independently.
  - Taking an acquisition/"reverse bailout" path that offers stability and new upside.
- How to leverage Careerspan’s interviewing tech as a **licensable engine** for other products (e.g., Human Truths AI).

## Shared Ecosystem & Opportunities

- **Human Truths AI (Saren Koca):**
  - Gates Foundation–backed AI interviewing/research platform that is struggling to ship product fast enough.
  - Represents an immediate opportunity for Careerspan’s interviewing engine and, indirectly, for Stitch Sense AI via shared credibility and network effects.

- **VC Ecosystem Contacts:**
  - **XRC Ventures:** Interested in factory/production plays; could be a fit for Stitch Sense AI once early traction is visible.
  - **RVC Capital:** CPG and production specialist VC; potential later-stage partner as Stitch Sense AI moves into brand/buyer relationships.

- **Tooling / Infrastructure:**
  - Zo is positioned as a **personal AI computer in the cloud** that V uses for:
    - Meeting processing and intelligence (this block).
    - CRM and contact enrichment.
    - Experimentation and internal tools.
  - Alexis & Misha may choose to adopt Zo as their internal backbone for pipelines and automations.
