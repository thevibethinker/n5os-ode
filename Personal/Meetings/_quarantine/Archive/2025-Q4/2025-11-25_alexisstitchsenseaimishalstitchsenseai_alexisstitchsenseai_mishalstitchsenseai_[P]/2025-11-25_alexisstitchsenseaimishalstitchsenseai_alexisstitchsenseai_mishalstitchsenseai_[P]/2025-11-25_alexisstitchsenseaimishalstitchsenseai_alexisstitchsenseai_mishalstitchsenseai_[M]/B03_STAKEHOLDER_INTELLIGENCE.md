## 4. Investor Psychology Themes (Meta-Intelligence)

- **FOMO-Driven Behavior:**
  - Most investors are ultimately **FOMO-driven gamblers**; they need to feel this is the **best deployment of capital this year**.
- **Return Narrative:**
  - Investors care less about whether the product is morally good and more about **how the company will return capital** (exit path, multiples, path to liquidity).
- **Valuation as Forward Commitment:**
  - The valuation chosen now becomes the **target they must grow into** by the next round.
- **Preferred Investor Profile for Stitch Sense AI:**
  - High-net-worth angels and non‑US investors who:
    - Are excited by factory/AI as a niche.
    - Are comfortable with higher valuations in exchange for asymmetric upside.
    - Don’t require heavy reporting or micromanagement.
