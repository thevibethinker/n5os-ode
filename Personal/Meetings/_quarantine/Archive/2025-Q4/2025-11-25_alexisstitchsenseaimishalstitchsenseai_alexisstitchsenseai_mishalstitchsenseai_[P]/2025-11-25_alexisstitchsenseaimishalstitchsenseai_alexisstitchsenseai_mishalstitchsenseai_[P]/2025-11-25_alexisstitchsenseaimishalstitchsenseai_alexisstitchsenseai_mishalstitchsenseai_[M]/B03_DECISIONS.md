# B03 – Decisions

## Fundraising Strategy
- **No immediate raise.**
  - Alexis & Misha confirm they are **not currently raising**; this conversation is for preparation.
- **Anchor first raise to product + revenue milestones.**
  - They agree that the first serious raise should come **after V1 ships and converts LOIs into revenue**, ideally in a quarter where revenue, case studies, and narrative all line up.
- **Instrument preference: SAFEs first, priced rounds only when forced.**
  - Default plan is to use **SAFEs**, particularly with **UAE-based investors**, and avoid priced rounds unless jurisdiction (e.g., India) forces it.
  - They will treat **Indian capital** as a last resort given legal/FDI overhead.
- **Avoid stacking SAFEs blindly.**
  - Decision to **model dilution** and avoid signing multiple SAFEs on inconsistent terms without understanding cumulative impact.

## Capital Sources & Positioning
- **Primary early capital from non‑US investors and angels.**
  - Strategy: raise from **non‑US angels / HNW individuals** who lack access to deals like Stitch Sense AI, enabling higher valuations with fewer structural constraints.
- **US VC and accelerators as optional, not mandatory.**
  - YC/Antler and US VCs are treated as **strategic options**, not defaults.
  - Any engagement with YC would need to clear a **high bar** on brand/optionality benefits vs. dilution and distraction from factories.

## Process Design
- **Adopt a time-compressed fundraise window.**
  - Plan to:
    - Spend the coming months saying **"we’re not raising yet"** while building a list of interested investors.
    - Open a **short, time-boxed window** (e.g., two weeks in a target month) during which they formally raise and investors must decide.
- **Formalize a lead pipeline.**
  - Alexis acknowledges the need to move pipeline tracking out of his head into a structured system (CRM, stages, metrics) before raising.

## Intros & Relationships
- **Future intros to specialist VCs.**
  - V will introduce Alexis & Misha to **Darshan (XRC Ventures)** and **RVC Capital** when timing aligns with their chosen raise window and milestones.
- **Pursue Human Truths AI collaboration.**
  - Alexis will introduce V to **Saren Koca (Human Truths AI)** so they can explore licensing or integrating Careerspan’s interviewing tech into her platform.

## Personal & Operating Principles
- **Only take money from investors who add value beyond capital.**
  - Reaffirming Misha’s father’s principle: *money comes and goes; it has no loyalty* — any investor must bring value beyond the cash itself.
- **Plan cash deployment before taking capital.**
  - They internalize V’s regret about not having a clear post-raise deployment plan and treat **explicit use-of-funds planning** as a non-negotiable before signing term sheets.
