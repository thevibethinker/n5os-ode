# Partnership Opportunity: Careerspan x Community Platforms

**The Problem We Solve Together**

Recruiting platforms face a fundamental challenge: candidates don't want to be "in the market" on traditional job boards, but they *do* want to be discoverable to the right opportunities. Communities have engaged members but lack infrastructure to monetize talent connections effectively.

**Careerspan's Model**

We embed within professional communities to create **opt-in talent pipelines**:

1. **Community members join** through a simple link or code (consumer-grade UX)
2. **Share career interests once** (role preferences, skills, availability)
3. **Get matched automatically** as relevant opportunities arise
4. **Companies subscribe** for access to community-fresh candidates

**What Makes This Work**

- **Trust transfer**: Candidates join through communities they already trust
- **Continuous pipeline**: New members = new candidates automatically
- **Zero friction**: No resume uploading, no job board navigation
- **Genuine opt-in**: Candidates actively want to be discoverable

**The Partnership Value**

**For your community:**
- Tangible member benefit (career opportunities through trusted channel)
- Revenue share potential on successful placements
- Enhanced community value without building recruiting infrastructure

**For Careerspan:**
- Access to engaged, high-quality professional networks
- Authentic community integration (not just another sponsor)
- Rapid pipeline growth through trusted channels

**For candidates:**
- Discovered through communities they value
- No ATS black holes or resume spam
- Context-rich matching (we know their community, interests, goals)

**Our Approach**

We're not competitors to community-driven recruiting platforms—we're infrastructure. Think of us as the backend that makes talent discovery work seamlessly within the communities you've already built.

**Current Traction**

- 800+ users across Lenny's Product Community, FOHE, alumni networks
- Onboarding 1-2 communities/week
- Companies see 80-90% open rates, 12-16% CTR (3-5x industry standard)
- Seed round planned for January 2025
