# Careerspan: Community-Embedded Talent Marketplace

**What We Do**

Careerspan is building a new model for talent discovery by embedding directly within professional communities. Instead of relying on traditional job boards or ATS systems, we cultivate talent pipelines where professionals already gather, learn, and connect.

**How It Works**

- **For candidates**: Join through your community (Lenny's Product Community, Future of Higher Education, alumni networks). Share your career interests and skills once, then get matched with relevant opportunities automatically.

- **For companies**: Subscribe to access our talent database. Search by role, skills, or community. Get high-quality candidate lists within minutes, continuously refreshed as new community members join.

**Why It's Different**

Traditional recruiting forces candidates into ATS bottlenecks and resume black holes. We flip the model: candidates opt-in through trusted communities, share rich context about their goals, and get introduced to companies that genuinely match their interests.

**Traction**

- 800+ engaged users across multiple communities
- 80-90% open rates on distributed jobs (vs. 20-30% industry standard)
- 12-16% click-through rates
- Users who engage once typically engage 3+ times
- Onboarding 1-2 new communities per week

**The Vision**

We're fixing the broken referral process. People want to help each other find great opportunities, but lack the time and context to make meaningful introductions. Careerspan provides that infrastructure—at scale, embedded where professionals already spend their time.
