# Careerspan: Helping Job Seekers Find Great Opportunities Through Community

**What We Do**

Careerspan helps professionals navigate career transitions through deeper storytelling and community-embedded discovery. We're building infrastructure that makes it easier for talented people to be found by great companies—without the ATS black hole.

**How It Works**

1. **Audio-Driven Conversations**: Instead of filling out forms, candidates talk through their career journey. Our platform reflects back learned mental models of their professional experiences.

2. **3D Professional Profiles**: Go beyond bullet points to capture the full story of someone's capabilities, values, and achievements.

3. **Community-Embedded Discovery**: Partner with professional communities (like Talent Collective!) to create opt-in talent pipelines. Members get discovered for relevant opportunities through networks they already trust.

4. **Vibe Checks**: Test alignment between candidates and roles based on values and achievement patterns—not just keyword matching.

**Why Talent Professionals Care**

- **Middle-signal alternative**: Higher quality than job boards, more scalable than pure referrals
- **Candidate-first approach**: Free B2C product ensures candidates are genuinely engaged
- **Community alignment**: We don't replace communities—we add value by solving the career discovery problem

**Current Traction**

- 2.5 years building product
- Multi-year contracts with Columbia, UMass, other universities
- Strong engagement metrics (12-16% CTR vs. 3-5% industry standard)
- Seed round planned for January 2025

**Partnership Philosophy**

We believe the best partnerships are relationship-driven with long-term ROI focus. If your community cares about helping members find great opportunities, let's talk about how Careerspan can add value.
