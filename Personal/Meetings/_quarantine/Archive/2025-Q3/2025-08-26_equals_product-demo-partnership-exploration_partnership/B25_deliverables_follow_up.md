## Key Discussion Points for Follow-Up Meeting

### Discussion Themes
1. **Workflow Integration Success:** How well did Equals fit into current Google Sheets + AI layer setup?
2. **Feature Gaps:** What capabilities did you want that weren't available?
3. **Ease of Use:** Compared to other tools, how's the learning curve?
4. **AI Integration:** Was it possible to connect to your AI layer? Any blockers?
5. **Time Savings:** Did it reduce manual work (specifically URL/description copying)?

### Decision Points to Cover
1. **Trial Outcome:** Did it solve the stated problem?
2. **Contract Terms:** If positive, what terms would work for Careerspan?
3. **Timeline:** When would you want to implement if moving forward?
4. **Feature Roadmap:** What's most important to see next (agentic search, etc.)?

### Risk Mitigation for Follow-Up
1. **If trial unsuccessful:** "Thank you for the feedback. What would need to be different?"
2. **If partial success:** "Can we scope a smaller implementation? What's the MVP?"
3. **If technical blocker:** "How do we solve the integration issue? Do we need to escalate?"
