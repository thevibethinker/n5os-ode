# B03_Decisions

## Key Decisions Made

### 1. Friends & Family Trial Exception
- **Decision**: Vrijen accepted a special trial arrangement outside normal Equals trial policy
- **Rationale**: Graham recognized Vrijen's non-standard use case (HR/recruitment workflow) as strategically valuable for Equals' go-to-market learning
- **Terms**: Basic tier ($199/month) with one week of free access to evaluate before commitment

### 2. Pricing & Contract Structure
- **Decision**: 1-year annual contract commitment ($199 × 12 = ~$2,388/year)
- **Tier**: Base tier without guided implementation (reserved for higher-tier customers)
- **Flexibility**: Agreed to part ways amicably if product doesn't meet needs after trial period
- **Risk Mitigation**: One-week trial window reduces commitment risk for Vrijen

### 3. Communication Channel
- **Decision**: Slack channel established as primary communication method
- **Logistics**: Graham will post workspace credentials and login info via Slack
- **Follow-up**: Fixed meeting cadence (same time next week, 3:30 PM) for trial check-in and feedback

## Strategic Implications
- **For Equals**: Vrijen positioned as innovative user who may influence product roadmap through novel use case feedback (job distribution matching + candidate extraction workflows)
- **For Careerspan**: Low-cost trial to validate Equals' capability as tool for HR workflow automation and candidate management
- **For Partnership**: Potential for expansion if trial succeeds (Vrijen indicated expectation of "more BI stuff in the coming year")
