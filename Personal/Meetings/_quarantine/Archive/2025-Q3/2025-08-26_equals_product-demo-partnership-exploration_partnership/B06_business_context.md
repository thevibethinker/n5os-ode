# B06_Business Context & Implications

## Careerspan's Use Case: Non-Traditional Spreadsheet Workflow

### Problem Statement
Careerspan's hiring/recruitment process currently relies on:
- Manual copy-paste operations between multiple Google Sheets
- Job lead distribution sheet with URL management
- Manual extraction of candidate data from AI-generated recommendations
- Labor-intensive process managed by Vrijen (non-technical founder) and head of AI

### Proposed Solution
Equals spreadsheet with AI layer could automate/simplify:
- Job URL ingestion and organization
- Candidate data extraction from structured sheets
- Integration with Careerspan's internal AI matching system
- Multi-source data aggregation (if Equals develops web scraping/agentic capabilities)

## Business Implications for Careerspan

### Efficiency Gains
- Reduction of manual data entry and copy-paste operations
- Potential for agentic web search to auto-retrieve job descriptions (future feature)
- Integration with existing AI recommendation system

### Strategic Value
- Positions Careerspan to scale hiring workflow as "user base expands"
- Lower-cost trial reduces risk of tool evaluation
- Contributes to overall HR tech stack modernization

### Risk/Limitation
- Equals' base tier lacks guided implementation - requires Vrijen to self-serve configuration
- Primary use cases are financial modeling; HR/recruitment is edge case
- Feature development roadmap may not prioritize Careerspan's specific needs
