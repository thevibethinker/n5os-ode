# B06 – Business Context

- Colin’s case illustrates a **structural weakness** in current hiring stacks:
  - Even with multiple interviews, technical screens, I‑9 verification, and background checks, sophisticated identity fraud can slip through.
  - When fraud is discovered, organizations may seek a scapegoat rather than addressing systemic failures.
- For Careerspan, this reinforces the need for:
  - Better **fraud detection and verification layers** that go beyond paperwork.
  - Tools that capture richer behavioral and narrative evidence, making it harder for fraudulent candidates to fabricate histories.
  - A coaching‑first model that can support both candidates and hiring managers navigating high‑risk situations.
