# B21: Key Moments

## The "Meaning Level" Argument
> "In fact, I would argue [other tools] don't really exist on that meaning level. And that's I think the big advantage that we bring." — Vrijen Attawar

## Moving the Counselor Stack
> "My hope is that we just move you higher in the problem stack. Right. At a higher level of abstraction instead of being the nuts and bolts sort of level. Working on resumes, you're able to operate more at the goals, objectives, strategy level." — Vrijen Attawar

## The Labor Problem
> "I've always been intrigued by using AI in a new and different way for careers. My interest has always been in doing resumes differently and better because it's so much. It's so laborious for us and it's so laborious for students." — Michael Maher

## The Matching Pivot
> "We've reoriented the product... more towards hiring... as long as folks have done the two stories that they should on Careerspan, they will immediately be able to apply to that employer and we actually have a portfolio portal for that employer if they would like." — Vrijen Attawar
