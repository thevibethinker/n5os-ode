# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1. **The Anthropomorphic Feedback Loop:** The idea that a bot's effectiveness in habit tracking is not in its utility but in its *anthropomorphization*—the sense of obligation one feels to respond to a "human-like" text.
2. **Degrees of Freedom in Career Strategy:** A mathematical/geometric model for career pivots where "distance" from your core (domain + role) determines your "fundability" and "acceptability" in the market.
3. **The "Slam Dunk" vs. "Moonshot" Dichotomy:** A reflection on whether the "growth" from a moonshot is more valuable than the "winning" of a slam dunk, even if the moonshot "fails" financially.
