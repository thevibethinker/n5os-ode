# B06: Business Context

- **Company:** Careerspan.
- **Product:** AI-led career coaching and talent validation engine that builds semantically rich candidate profiles via voice/text storytelling.
- **Business Model:** 
    - **Pivot:** Moved away from candidate monetization to employer/recruiter fees.
    - **Fees:** Takes a cut from recruiters for jobs sourced through the platform.
    - **VC Integration:** Middleware for VC talent pools to keep "inbound eyeballs" warm and track candidates over years.
- **Funding/Stage:** Early journey; has partnerships with Marvin Ventures (McKinsey Alumni Fund) and Superposition AI.
- **Target Partners:** Ribbon (Interview intelligence), VCs (Sequoia mentioned as an archetype), Recruiting Agencies.
