# B07 Tone & Context: Vrijen & Rochel - Job Applying Powwow

## Emotional Vibe
- **Resilient Solidarity:** The meeting feels like a peer-to-peer mentorship session. There is a shared understanding of the "startup struggle" and the emotional toll of job searching.
- **Candid & Protective:** Vrijen is very candid about his opinions on other companies (Boardy, LinkedIn) and protective of Rochel’s internal sense of value.
- **Holiday Anticipation:** A lighthearted layer of holiday planning provides a domestic, humanizing backdrop to the professional discussion.

## Subtext & Dynamics
- **"The Signaling Game":** Much of the discussion revolves around how to "play" the signals of the tech industry (McKinsey, Marvin, A16Z) to bypass standard HR filters.
- **Imposter Syndrome:** There is a recurring theme of Rochel needing to "step up" to her new peer status (e.g., with Ryan) and Vrijen coaching her to own her founding-team success.
- **Product Belief:** Despite being a job seeker, Rochel’s belief in the Careerspan product remains high ("I know how useful it is"), though she struggles with the friction of self-work.

## Key Phrases/Vibe Markers
- "How the sausage is made" (referring to hiring/startups).
- "Transplants only Christmas."
- "The signaling game is the name of the game."
- "I can fix him" (Vrijen joking about a rival founder).
