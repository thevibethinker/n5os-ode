# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Data Supremacy vs. Commodity Scraping**: The idea that most data companies are structurally destined for failure because they lack a proprietary source or unique "supremacy," essentially just repackaging the same public LinkedIn graph until the capital runs out.
- **The "Manual-to-Product" Debt**: The strategic risk/opportunity of founders who sell a manual methodology as software (selling the "fucking product" before it exists). It creates immediate PMF but massive engineering debt and a high-risk transition period.
- **The "Alpha" of Seed vs. Mental Stability**: The observation that while Seed stage offers higher theoretical "alpha," the cost in mental health and lack of foundational structure often makes Series A/B the more rational choice for mid-career specialists.
