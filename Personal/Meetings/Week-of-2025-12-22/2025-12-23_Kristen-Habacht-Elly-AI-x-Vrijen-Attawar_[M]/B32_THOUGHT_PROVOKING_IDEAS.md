# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The "Data Agnosticism" Advantage:** Careerspan's insight that data collected *independent* of a specific job application is higher fidelity because it removes the "angling" incentive. This creates a "Pure Talent" profile that is universally applicable and more honest.
- **Hiring as a Trade-Off Engine:** Moving away from stack-ranking (1-10) to "bundles of skills" and "trade-offs." This acknowledges that no candidate is "better," only "differently fit" for a specific context, which is a significant departure from standard ATS logic.
- **LinkedIn's Strategic Suicide:** The idea that LinkedIn's pursuit of "social media network" status (engagement/ads) is actively destroying its core utility as a professional network (utility/matching), creating a massive opening for "Superhuman for LinkedIn" overlays.
