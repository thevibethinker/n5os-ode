# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

*   **Model Asymmetry:** The idea that using "the absolute dumbest models" (4o-mini) in multiple small steps can produce a more "intelligent" and certainly more resilient output than single-shot high-end models.
*   **The "Story" as Signal:** The concept that the *willingness* to complete a high-effort "story" reflection is itself a stronger signal of candidate quality than the content of the resume.
*   **Separation of Concerns:** V's observation that having a "separation between who deals with the candidate and who deals with the employer" creates a better psychological space for candidates to be honest and reflective.
