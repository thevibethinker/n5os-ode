# B14: Blurbs Requested

### Blurb 1: For Ribbon Partnerships (Software/Product Focus)
Careerspan is the first hiring platform built on the premise that the resume is an insufficient and unauthenticated data structure. We use structured AI-reflection sessions to build a high-fidelity mental model of a candidate’s experiences. By integrating with Ribbon, we can create a "Triangle of Integrity"—cross-referencing candidate stories against live interview performance to eliminate signal noise and provide recruiters with actionable "Why Yes/Why No" insights.

### Blurb 2: For VC Talent Partners
Careerspan is transforming the VC talent portal from a static job board (like Getro) into a proactive sourcing engine. We allow VCs to define archetypal profiles (e.g., "The Founding Engineer") and use our AI to continuously assess and warm up inbound talent until they meet the threshold for a portfolio intro. We currently partner with funds like Marvin to build higher-integrity talent pipelines.
