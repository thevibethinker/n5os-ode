# B05: Questions Raised

## Product & Technology Questions

**Q: How does CareerSpan actually decipher a candidate's soft skills?**  
Status: Answered  
Context: Christine, having recruited for years, wanted to understand the mechanism behind moving from raw "story" data to valid skill assessment.  
Answer: The AI is provided with a specific rubric to assess skills and context for interpreting their presence. It includes human-in-the-loop points where candidates can correct or refine the AI's narrative before submission.

**Q: Does the recruiter get this data and then share it with hiring managers?**  
Status: Answered  
Context: Clarifying the workflow and the persona of the end-user on the employer side.  
Answer: Yes, the employer portal provides highly actionable breakdowns, including "Why Yes" and "Why No" insights, which are intended to be shared to reduce the cognitive load on hiring managers.
