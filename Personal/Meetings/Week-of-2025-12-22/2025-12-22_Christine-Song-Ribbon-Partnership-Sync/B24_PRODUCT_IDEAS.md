# B24: Product Ideas and Feature Requests

This block extracts specific product feedback, potential feature integrations, and new product directions discussed during the meeting between Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan) and Christine Song (529 Society/Ribbon).

## Feature Requests

**1. Cross-Platform Integrity Triangle (HIGH)**  
Requested by: Vrijen Attawar  
Use case: Integrating Careerspan story data with Ribbon interview data to create an "iron triangle of integrity." This would allow employers to triangulate what a candidate says in a structured coaching session against their performance in a live or recorded interview, making it nearly impossible to fabricate experience.  
Impact: HIGH  
Current status: Needs development (Proposed as a "Skunkworks" integration project).

**2. VC Talent Pipeline Sourcing Engine (HIGH)**  
Requested by: Vrijen Attawar  
Use case: Transitioning Careerspan from a simple job board to a mid-layer talent engine for VCs. Instead of a static "Getro-style" billboard, VCs would use Careerspan to build long-term archetypes for roles (e.g., Founding Engineer). Candidates would "check their fit" and remain in a pipeline until their skills/story evidence match the VC’s specific archetype.  
Impact: HIGH  
Current status: Exists/Refinement (Partnership already inked with Marvin/McKinsey Alumni Fund).

**3. Actionable "Why Yes/Why No" Insights (MEDIUM)**  
Requested by: Vrijen Attawar  
Use case: Moving beyond semantic scoring to provide recruiters and hiring managers with a synthesized "Strategic Positioning" report. Instead of forcing recruiters to decipher resumes, the product generates explicit strengths and weaknesses based on story evidence versus resume claims.  
Impact: MEDIUM  
Current status: Exists (Featured in the Donna/SaaS PM demo account).

**4. Soft Skill Assessment Rubric Validation (MEDIUM)**  
Requested by: Christine Song  
Use case: Providing a transparent way for recruiters to understand how soft skills are being "deciphered" by the AI. This includes human-in-the-loop points where candidates can correct flawed interpretations of their narratives before submission.  
Impact: MEDIUM  
Current status: Exists/Needs refinement.

## New Product Ideas

### Careerspan for "High-Stakes" Personal Hiring
Inspired by Christine's observation that "it really counts who the person is that you want to share your life with," there is a potential pivot or sub-product for vetting high-trust personal roles such as:
- **Nanny/Childcare Hiring**: Using story-based vetting to assess character and reliability beyond a background check.
- **Dog Walkers/Home Services**: Vetting for integrity in roles that require home access.
- **The "Dating" Use Case**: A "Bio-Log" or "Story-Log" for high-intent dating to ensure alignment before the first meeting (jokingly suggested by Vrijen’s father, but noted as a potential "listening engine" application).

### Video-to-Insight Compression
Discussion centered on the inefficiency of video interviews (Ribbon’s current model). An idea was sparked to move away from the "visual third dimension" (which recruiters don't have time to watch) and instead use the Careerspan engine to compress video transcripts into the same "coaching-based" data points used in the story portal. This would allow Ribbon users to "read" the value of a video without watching it.

### Peer-Network "Confidentiality" Layer
Drawing from the 529 Society model, a feature for Careerspan could involve "Anonymous Peer Vetting," where senior operators can have their stories validated by other senior peers in a confidential network before the data is ever shared with a CEO or potential employer, mitigating "workplace politics" risks.
