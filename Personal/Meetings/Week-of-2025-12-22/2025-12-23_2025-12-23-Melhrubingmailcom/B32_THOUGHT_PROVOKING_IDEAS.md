# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Artificial Gap Closing:** The idea that a candidate's primary job in a pivot is not to "do the work," but to artificially close the cognitive gap between their current self and the employer's "ideal candidate" via narrative engineering.
- **Hiring as a Diet:** The concept of managing a job search like a diet, balancing stable "normal" opportunities with high-leverage "outsized bets" (screamer shots) to maintain momentum without burning out.
- **Vibe Coding as a Career Lever:** Positioning vibe coding not as a technical skill, but as a "badass" industry identity bridge for talented generalists.
