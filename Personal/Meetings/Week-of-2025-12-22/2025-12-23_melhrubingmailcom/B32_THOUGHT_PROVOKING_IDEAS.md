# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas
- **Career as a "Diet"**: V's analogy of hiring/job seeking as a diet consisting of a mix of standard opportunities and "outsized bets" (the 40-yard screamer).
- **Artificial Gap Closure**: The idea that a candidate must "artificially" close the skills gap in an employer's mind by providing a compelling narrative that mitigates perceived weaknesses.
