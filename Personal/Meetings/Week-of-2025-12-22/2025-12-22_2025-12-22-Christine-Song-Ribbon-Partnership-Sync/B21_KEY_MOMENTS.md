# B21: Key Moments

*   **The "Death of the ATS":** V's opening thesis that the resume is an insufficient, unauthenticated data structure set the tone for the technological necessity of Careerspan.
*   **The Resume Generator Arrogance:** V's claim to have "the only actual working resume generator on the planet" because it relies on *knowing* what the person did (via stories) rather than just "upgrading bullets."
*   **The Ribbon Realization:** Christine acknowledging that "people don't have time to watch videos," validating the need for the Careerspan "Vibe Check" / summary layer.
*   **The "Iron Triangle":** V's framing of the integration (Careerspan + Ribbon + Interview) as a foolproof validation system.
*   **529 Society Pitch:** Christine's description of "lonely at the top" and the need for peer networks that trump "remote isolation."
