## RIBBON TEAM BLURB

**Subject:** Careerspan <> Ribbon | High-signal candidate validation

Careerspan is a career development platform built for enterprise teams—combining AI-powered "storytelling" workflows with human coaching to deliver personalized guidance at scale.

Most hiring systems struggle with candidate authentication because resumes are two-dimensional and video is time-consuming. Careerspan solves this by using structured AI sessions to build a mental model of a candidate's experience, collecting narrative evidence that validates their skills. It provides the deep semantic "Why Yes / Why No" logic hiring managers need without requiring them to watch hours of tape.

We see a powerful "iron triangle" here between Ribbon's video snapshots and Careerspan's validation data. We currently work with organizations like Superposition.ai and the Marvin McKinsey Alumni Fund to move beyond keyword matching into true candidate integrity.

## VC TALENT PARTNER BLURB

**Subject:** Building a persistent talent pipeline with Careerspan

I'm Vrijen Attawar, founder of Careerspan—focused on making career coaching and candidate validation accessible at enterprise scale.

We're helping VC firms solve the "untapped inbound" problem: the gap where you have thousands of talented people in your network but no way to know who is actually ready for a founding role today. Careerspan acts as a persistent "mid-layer" where candidates build a mental model of their experience over time through AI-driven coaching. For a talent partner, this means you can set a "founding engineer archetype" and have the system automatically flag when a candidate in your pool has reached that level of readiness through their story evidence.

It's a significantly higher signal than a standard job board or ATS. We're currently building these data layers for specialized talent communities to ensure that when a role opens, the right candidate is already validated and ready.
