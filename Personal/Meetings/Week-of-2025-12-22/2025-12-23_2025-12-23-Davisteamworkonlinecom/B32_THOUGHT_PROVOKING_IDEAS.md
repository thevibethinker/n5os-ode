# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

*   **Signal as Data:** The idea that the *act* of a candidate being willing to complete a Careerspan story is itself a higher-fidelity signal than any text they actually produce.
*   **Candidate/Employer Separation:** The strategic value of having a "neutral" third party (Careerspan) handle the "squishy" self-reflection side, while the hiring platform handles the transactional ATS side.
*   **Low-Fidelity Architecture Strength:** Building sophisticated outputs from "dumb" mini-models to ensure architecture resilience—counter-intuitive in an era where everyone is rushing toward the most expensive, frontier models.
