# B06: Business Context

## Teamwork Online
*   **Core Business:** Hiring platform specifically for Sports and Entertainment.
*   **Market Position:** 25 years in the field; dominant player in a niche where candidate supply massively outweighs job demand.
*   **Revenue Models:** MVP Access (Candidate subscription), Job Posting fees, and Executive Search.
*   **Focus Areas:** Resume parsing reliability and involving AI in "the right places" to make the platform cohesive.

## Careerspan Partnership Fit
*   **Value Prop:** "Translating" non-traditional backgrounds (athletes) into white-collar roles via structured self-reflection.
*   **Strategic Alignment:** Teamwork Online has the volume; Careerspan has the signal/depth. The goal is to grow through this partnership rather than raising more venture capital.
