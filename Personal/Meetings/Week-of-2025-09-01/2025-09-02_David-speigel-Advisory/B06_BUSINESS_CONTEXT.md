# B06: Business Context

### Market Positioning
Careerspan is positioning itself as more than just a job board; it is a "career agent" for candidates and a high-signal "shortlist" generator for employers. The strategy relies on tapping into professional communities to access "passive" seekers who are of higher quality than those on public job boards.

### Supply and Demand Balance
The current strategic focus is heavily on the **supply side** (candidate acquisition via communities). The hypothesis is that having 200-300 new, high-quality, product-focused candidates per month creates enough leverage to charge employers for access (shortlists or warm intros).

### Strategic Assets
*   **Custom Job Distribution:** A mechanism to provide exclusive opportunities to communities.
*   **Career Agent Model:** A shift from transactional job hunting to long-term candidate management.
*   **Gap Filling:** A feature to help candidates improve their marketability, increasing the overall quality of the Careerspan supply.
