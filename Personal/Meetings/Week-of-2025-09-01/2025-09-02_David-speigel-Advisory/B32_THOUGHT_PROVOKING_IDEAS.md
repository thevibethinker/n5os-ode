# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

*   **The Career Agent as a Service:** Can Careerspan effectively function as a "sports agent" for the top 10% of technical talent, rather than just a recruitment tool?
*   **The "Anti-Job Board":** How do we lean further into the "exclusive" nature of the job distribution to make communities feel like they are providing a rare, high-value asset?
*   **Passive Engagement:** Is the "gap filling" mechanism enough to keep passive candidates coming back daily, or do we need a more "social" or "skill-building" loop?
*   **Advisory Proof Points:** Could the "incentive commission" model for advisors become a standard way Careerspan interacts with all its fractional support?
