# B06: Business Context

The meeting serves as the kickoff for an educational series intended to prepare professionals for the "AI Agent Era." The primary business driver is the efficiency paradox: as AI models become more capable at "execution," the primary bottleneck in organizations shifts from "capacity" to "coordination/agreement." Jacob argues that companies failing to transition to a "Super IC" model will be out-competed by smaller, faster teams that leverage agents for all repetitive knowledge work.
