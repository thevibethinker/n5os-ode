# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

*   **The "Agreement" Bottleneck:** In the future, projects won't be limited by the *capacity to do work* (thanks to AI), but by the *difficulty of getting humans to agree* on what should be done.
*   **Taste as the Primary Skill:** As execution becomes a commodity handled by agents, the Super IC's value shifts entirely to "taste," "judgment," and "creative direction."
*   **The 1-to-60 Ratio:** Jacob's personal ratio of 1 human to 60 agents in marketing provides a concrete benchmark for what "high leverage" actually looks like.
*   **The Death of the Career Ladder:** If junior execution roles disappear, how do people "level up" to become Super ICs? This creates a potential mentorship/training gap in the future economy.
