# B01: Detailed Recap

Jacob Bank, CEO of Relay App, leads the first session of an "AI Agent MBA" series. The core thesis is that AI agents will fundamentally reshape knowledge work, regardless of whether models improve beyond their current state.

### Key Predictions and Framework (The "Step" Model)
*   **Step 0: AI is Ready.** Current models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet, GPT-4o, etc.) are already capable of writing, researching, and synthesizing better and faster than most people.
*   **Step 1: Death of Junior Execution Roles.** Repetitive, "text-in, text-out" roles with low autonomy (e.g., turning outlines into articles, rote support macros, basic report writing) will cease to exist.
*   **Step 2: Rise of the Super IC.** The new norm will be senior "Super Individual Contributors" who manage dozens of specialized AI agents. These generalists will span traditional boundaries (marketing, sales, support).
*   **Step 3: Small Teams > Big Teams.** Lean teams of Super ICs will outperform large organizations because they bypass the "coordination tax" and organizational friction of large groups.
*   **Step 4: Elimination of Bureaucracy.** Managers who exist solely to coordinate large numbers of people will become obsolete as the total headcount per project shrinks.
*   **Step 5: The Future Company.** High-agency, high-trust environments where small groups produce high-quality work with massive leverage.

### Personal Example
Jacob shares that Relay App operates with only 10 people but achieves the output of a 20+ person team. He personally handles marketing, support, sales, HR, and finance by managing roughly 60 AI agents, specifically noting his marketing team consists of just himself and 60 agents.
