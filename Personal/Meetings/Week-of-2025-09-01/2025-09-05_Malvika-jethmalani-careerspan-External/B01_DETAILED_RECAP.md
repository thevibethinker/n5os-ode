# B01 Detailed Recap

The meeting centered on a strategic discussion between Malvika Jethmalani (Careerspan) and an external consultant/coach regarding Careerspan's mission to transform the recruitment industry by moving beyond the traditional resume.

### Key Discussion Themes:

1.  **The Death of the Resume**: Malvika posits that we are at a transformative moment where AI is saturating the market with perfectly tailored resumes, making them obsolete as a filtering mechanism. Careerspan aims for a "candidate-centric" vision that captures the totality of an individual’s qualitative strengths through storytelling.
2.  **Product & Engagement**: Careerspan offers a free tool that records and analyzes candidate stories (averaging nearly an hour per user). Malvika highlighted strong engagement, particularly among women in education who often struggle with "bragging" about their accomplishments.
3.  **Go-to-Market & Strategic Partnerships**:
    *   **HR Communities**: The consultant recommended joining specific HR and Talent Acquisition communities (Transform, People Over Perks, Culture Geeks, Talent Collective) to find the primary ICP.
    *   **Competitive Landscape**: The consultant noted Boardy as a conceptual competitor gaining popularity.
    *   **Reverse Hiring Events**: Careerspan is facilitating events where candidates are matched to founders based on qualitative story vetting.
4.  **Monetization & Community Integration**: Malvika detailed a model for community owners (like the consultant’s "Women Shaping AI" group) to act as talent pipelines, potentially earning placement fees and offering private job distribution features.
5.  **Immediate Outreach**: Malvika will provide a trial code for the consultant's VC client and a dedicated code/blurb for the "Women Shaping AI" community. A follow-up with Logan (Careerspan co-founder) was proposed to explore deeper community integration.
