# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1.  **The "Death of the Resume" as a Timing Play**: Malvika notes people have said this for decades, but argues AI makes *this specific moment* the tipping point. Is the saturation of "perfect" resumes the final nail in the coffin?
2.  **Psychology of Self-Promotion**: The finding that groups "inherently bad at bragging" (like educators) thrive with AI-led coaching. Can AI bridge the confidence gap for marginalized or humble talent pools?
3.  **Reverse Hiring Events**: The "pseudo arranged marriage" for talent. Moving away from open job boards toward curated, high-signal matching.
4.  **Community as Recruitment Agency**: Turning community leaders into decentralized recruiters. Does this disrupt the traditional agency model by leveraging existing high-trust networks?
