# B06 Business Context

### Market Thesis
Careerspan operates on the hypothesis that AI-generated resumes have destroyed the resume's value as a filtering tool. They are pivoting toward **qualitative signal extraction** through AI-led coaching conversations.

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
*   **Corporate**: Talent Acquisition leaders and HR professionals.
*   **Strategic Partners**: High-signal community owners (alumni associations, professional networks, niche communities like "Women Shaping AI").

### Competitive Positioning
*   **Boardy**: Identified as a conceptual/overt competitor. Malvika notes Careerspan is "encroaching" on similar events-based matching (reverse hiring).
*   **Differentiator**: Focus on long-form storytelling (1 hour per user) and narrative positioning statements for specific job applications.
