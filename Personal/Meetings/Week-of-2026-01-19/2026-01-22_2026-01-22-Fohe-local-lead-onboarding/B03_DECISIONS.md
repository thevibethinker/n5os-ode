# B03: Decisions

**Meeting:** FOHE Local Lead Onboarding  
**Date:** 2026-01-22

## Explicit Decisions Made

### Event Announcement Protocol

**Decision:** Adopt three @channel message strategy for event promotion  
**Owners:** Local Leads (with guidance from Oleksandra)  
**Implementation:**
1. First @channel: Few weeks before event
2. Second @channel: One week before event
3. Final @channel: One day before event

**Decision Criteria:** Proven by past local leads to generate highest engagement and follow-up rates

**Flexibility:** High-frequency organizers may reduce to two @channels at Ray's discretion. Minimum two recommended.

### Access Permissions Boundaries

**Decision:** Grant @channel permissions to local leads with strict limitations  
**Owners:** Oleksandra (granting), Local Leads (adhering)

**Boundaries:**
- ✅ Allowed: Manage local chapter with @channel
- ❌ Prohibited: Create new channels or groups
- ❌ Prohibited: Use permissions for any other purpose

**Confirmation Required:** Written consent acknowledging understanding of restrictions

### Data Access Restrictions

**Decision:** Local leads receive view-only access to master tracking systems  
**Owners:** Oleksandra (provisioning), Local Leads (adhering)

**Prohibitions:**
- Do not edit the Google Sheets master tracking system
- Do not modify any data in the shared systems

**Rationale:** Sheet feeds newsletter automation; errors would break downstream systems

### Event Tool Selection

**Decision:** Local leads may choose event management tools  
**Owners:** Individual Local Leads

**Guidance:** Many use Luma, but choice is flexible. Templates and tips available in FOHE Local Lead Google Drive.

### Local Lead Networking Program Participation

**Decision:** Jviassy and V join local lead networking program  
**Owners:** Oleksandra (adding to program), New Leads (participating)

**Program Details:**
- Monthly Donut tool matches
- 30-minute peer conversations
- Purpose: Collaboration and tip-sharing

### Onboarding Action Items

**Decision:** Immediate post-call actions required from new leads  
**Owners:** New Leads (completing), Oleksandra (sharing resources)

**Required Actions:**
1. Submit bio this week for welcome message distribution
2. Complete Google form (demographics, phone number, name)
3. Review and sign volunteer agreement
4. Provide written consent for @channel/manager status usage

### Email Communication Protocol

**Decision:** BCC mandatory for group emails  
**Owners:** Local Leads (executing)

**Rationale:** Privacy protection when using local group aliases
