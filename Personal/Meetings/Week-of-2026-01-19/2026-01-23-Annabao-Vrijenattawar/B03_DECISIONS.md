## Decision 5: Quarterly Email Newsletter

**DECIDED BY:** Anna Bao (proposed direction), group agreement

## Decision 7: Volunteer Program Structure & Roles

**DECISION:** Four distinct volunteer roles will be established: Event Creator, Connector, Greeter, and Neighborhood Ambassador.

**CONTEXT:** The volunteer program aims to distribute event and community workload beyond Anna and Vrijen. Survey showed strong interest across all roles.

**DECIDED BY:** Anna Bao (designed framework), group agreement

**IMPLICATIONS:** Listservs and coordination processes will be created for each role. Volunteers commit to single events, not ongoing obligations.

**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Andrew Young Events' event-focused model was referenced, but Fohi's approach emphasizes embedded locality and neighborhood distribution.

---

## Decision 8: Low-Tech Connector Format

**DECISION:** The Connector role will operate via a low-tech manual process: volunteers post their expertise and availability in Slack; interested community members sign up directly via the volunteer's calendar link.

**CONTEXT:** Ray and Jake are hesitant about new tools beyond Slack and email. The focus is on curated, expertise-based connections rather than random matching. Donut already provides general coffee chat matching.

**DECIDED BY:** Anna Bao (proposed), Vrijen Attawar (aligned), consistent with leadership constraints

**IMPLICATIONS:** Minimal technology overhead. Relies on manual coordination. Differentiates from Donut by being expertise-specific rather than random.

**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Automated matching systems and new platforms were discussed but rejected in favor of Slack + Google Sheets approach.
