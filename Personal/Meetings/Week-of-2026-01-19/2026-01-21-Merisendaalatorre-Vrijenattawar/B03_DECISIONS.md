## Decision 1: ACE Fitness to pursue hybrid technical hiring model

**DECISION:** ACE Fitness will pursue a hybrid technical hiring model that combines in-house technical leadership with cost-effective development resources.

**CONTEXT:** Merisenda is seeking advice for ACE Fitness's AI development initiative. After learning from Careerspan's experience, she acknowledged the value of having a technical point person in-house while also exploring options for more affordable development talent.

**DECIDED BY:** Merisenda Alatorre (implied through acknowledgment and interest in pursuing the suggested path)

**IMPLICATIONS:** ACE Fitness will need to budget for at least one in-house technical lead and consider partnerships for development resources. This approach balances control and accountability with cost efficiency.

- Fully outsourced model (rejected based on Careerspan's advice about needing in-house oversight)
