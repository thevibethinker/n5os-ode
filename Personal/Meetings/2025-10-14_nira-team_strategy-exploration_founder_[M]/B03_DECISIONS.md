# B03 – Decisions

## Strategic decisions (Careerspan)
- **Decide not to pursue a classic VC‑scale B2B hiring SaaS path in the near term.**
  - V and Logan agree they do not want to spend years selling into employers or charging job seekers directly.
  - Instead, they will prioritize building a strong brand and community around "AI x careers" and use that to fund and grow Careerspan as a free or highly accessible product.

- **Position Careerspan as a high-signal, free tool that can plug into ecosystems like Nira.**
  - Rather than optimizing for enterprise revenue, they will optimize for depth of value and reach among high-agency users.

## Collaboration decisions (Nira × Careerspan)
- **Mutual agreement to treat each other as flagship, values-aligned partners.**
  - Both sides affirm that they want to work together, even though Careerspan’s business model is shifting.

- **Agreement for V and Logan to become early, power users of Nira.**
  - They will onboard onto Nira, upload their networks, and actively experiment with it as a personal/professional network OS.

- **Agreement to explore Nira’s ambassador program.**
  - Fei proposes a revenue-share ambassador model where selected users invite their networks to Nira and share subscription revenue.
  - V responds positively and is open to participating as an early ambassador once details are set.

- **Decision to feature Nira in V’s upcoming "anti‑LinkedIn" content.**
  - V commits to incorporating Nira into an "anti‑LinkedIn playbook" and related public-facing work, effectively endorsing Nira to his audience.

## Product & ecosystem decisions
- **Shared commitment to deeper-data integrations in the future.**
  - Once Careerspan exposes an API, both sides intend to explore integration where Careerspan’s qualitative data powers Nira’s dynamic profiles and outreach.

- **Agreement to run joint experiments around modern networking and hiring flows.**
  - They will co‑design experiments (e.g., curated candidate pipelines, staying top-of-mind in networks) that combine Nira’s network OS with Careerspan’s assessment/reporting capabilities.

## Open decisions / TBD
- Commercial terms beyond the high-level ambassador revenue split.
- Concrete integration roadmap (timelines, owners, technical responsibilities) once Careerspan’s API is live.
- How to operationalize a consistent "anti‑LinkedIn" narrative across both products (branding, shared content, events).
