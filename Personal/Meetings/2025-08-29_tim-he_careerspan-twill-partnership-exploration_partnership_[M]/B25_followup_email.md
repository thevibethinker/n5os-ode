I know you wanted to noodle on it and compare against your internal sourcer costs. A few things that might help your analysis:

**What you get:**
- Unlimited monthly scans of our database for any JD
- Pre-matched, scored candidate profiles

- Consistent quality signal (unlike inbound, which varies wildly)

**Why this model works for both of us:**
- You get predictable sourcing capacity without scaling headcount
- We get the proof point we need: "high signal talent sourcing actually works at scale"

**Flexibility:** If $2K doesn't work, or if you want to pilot with a different structure, I'm open. The key for us is just getting enough volume to validate the matching quality + get real feedback.

What excites me most about this isn't the sourcing fee—it's that we're solving a real founder pain point that Twill understands better than anyone. Founders are burnt out on inbound. They don't want more noise. They want faster, higher-signal options.

Your community + our matching system = that middle ground. That's the magic.

I think there's an opportunity here for both of us to win, without it being zero-sum.

### 4. Communities

You mentioned having two communities in mind that might be interested. Even if those don't work out, I'd love to stay in your orbit. Your insights on community dynamics are valuable, and I genuinely think our approaches are more complementary than competitive.
