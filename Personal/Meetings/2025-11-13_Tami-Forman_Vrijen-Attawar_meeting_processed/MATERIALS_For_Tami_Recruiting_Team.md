## OPTION 2: PLATFORM OVERVIEW (Deck-Style Bullets)

**FOR TAMI TO INCLUDE AS ATTACHMENT OR INLINE:**

### Careerspan Platform Overview

**Core Value Proposition:**
Narrative-based candidate matching through AI-guided reflection. Helps candidates articulate their career stories → creates deeper profiles for matching.

**How It Works:**
1. Candidates engage with conversational AI (voice or text)
2. Platform guides reflection on experiences, skills, motivations
3. Creates rich narrative profiles (not just resume data)
4. Matching engine connects to relevant opportunities

**Traction:**
- 3,000 lifetime signups
- 800+ with 20+ minutes platform engagement (strong retention signal)
- Performance-based revenue model (aligned incentives)

**Differentiation:**
- **Not a recruiting agency** - they're a platform for candidate development
- **Curation over volume** - "I will give you three names every week" vs. ATS floods
- **Narrative-first** - captures *why* and *how* someone works, not just *what* they've done

**Potential Fit with Women Back to Work:**

1. **Pipeline Enhancement:**
   - Use Careerspan for pre-screening/narrative gathering
   - Reduces recruiter time spent on initial candidate vetting
   - Complements existing HackerRank/cogility workflow

2. **Internal Mobility (New Revenue Stream):**
   - Large employers want to retain talent by helping employees find internal roles
   - Careerspan built specifically for this (match employees to internal opportunities)
   - Could be value-add service we offer enterprise clients

**Technical Questions to Evaluate:**
- API integration with our existing stack
- Data privacy/security (candidate info handling)
- Scalability (can it handle our volume?)
- Cost structure (performance-based model details)

## OPTION 3: INTERNAL MOBILITY DEEP-DIVE (Strategic Brief)

**IF TAMI WANTS TO EMPHASIZE INTERNAL MOBILITY USE CASE:**

### Internal Mobility: Strategic Opportunity

**The Problem Large Employers Face:**
- Employees leave because they don't see internal growth paths
- HR departments lack tools to match employees to internal roles
- Retention costs less than recruiting, but internal mobility is manual/broken

**Careerspan's Solution:**
- Employees use platform to develop career narratives and explore internal opportunities
- AI matches employees to internal roles based on skills + motivations + culture fit
- Employers see better retention + reduced external recruiting costs

**Why This Could Be Valuable for Women Back to Work:**
- **New revenue stream:** Sell to enterprise clients as retention tool
- **Differentiation:** Most staffing firms focus on external hiring; internal mobility is underserved
- **Client stickiness:** Becomes part of HR infrastructure, not one-time transactional hire

**Partnership Model to Explore:**
- Women Back to Work brings enterprise relationships
- Careerspan provides internal mobility platform
- Revenue share on enterprise accounts that adopt internal mobility solution
