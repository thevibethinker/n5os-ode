# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1.  **"Referral Quality at Inbound Scale":** Can a software/service actually bridge the trust gap of a referral with the raw volume of inbound? How much of that is signal (data) vs. sentiment (process)?
2.  **The "Diet" Metaphor for Hiring:** "If you only rely on one source, you're going to have a bad time." Is over-reliance on referrals a form of intellectual/talent malnutrition?
3.  **Designing Backwards from Outcome:** Applying the "Outcome -> Emotional Experience -> Story" framework not just to pitches, but to product features. What emotional experience does a Careerspan user need? (e.g., Certainty, Agony-relief).
4.  **The "Effort Proxy":** The idea that inbound applicants show "agency and reflection" in a way that referrals might not. This flips the traditional view that referrals are higher quality simply because they are vetted.
