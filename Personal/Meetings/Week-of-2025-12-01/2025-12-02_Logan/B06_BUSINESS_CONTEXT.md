# B06 Business Context

### Messaging Strategy
The core business problem identified is the "mis-hire" and the over-reliance on referrals among founders. Founders trust referrals but they lack scale. Careerspan aims to bridge this by providing "referral quality" (deep insights) at "inbound scale" (accessing the full talent pool).

### Partnership Strategy (Emory)
The Emory proposal is moving from a general pilot to a more structured "Alumni Employer" model. The goal is to create a flywheel where Careerspan becomes the default for Emory alums to hire other Emory alums/students, facilitated by "magic links" that subsidize the cost of initial roles.

### Market Positioning
Careerspan is leaning into a "New York Founders" identity. The "Make Inbound Great Again" slogan is a tactical joke designed to resonate with a specific demographic of high-growth founders who are frustrated with current ATS systems and low-signal recruiting.
