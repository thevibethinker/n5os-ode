# B06 Business Context

## Melly's Current Situation
Melly is a small team (3 core members) struggling with "Founder's Dilemma" in engineering. They have a brilliant "brain" (founding engineer) but cannot find "soldiers" to execute the roadmap. They are stuck in a cycle of failed trials and offer recusions, likely due to a combination of budget constraints ($100k cap) and reliance on low-cost overseas markets without sufficient vetting infrastructure.

## Careerspan's Strategic Entry
Careerspan is moving from a coaching-only solution to a two-sided marketplace. This pilot with Melly serves as a perfect case study for Careerspan's ability to:
1. Provide "anonymity" for founders bound by restrictive recruiter contracts.
2. Filter for "thinking capacity" through conversational AI.
3. Reduce the "resume tax" (the time spent reading 99 bad resumes to find 1 good one).
