# B06: Business Context

## Melly's Current Trajectory
Melly is a small, creative-led team with a significant engineering bottleneck. They have high user demand (feature requests) but a dysfunctional hiring pipeline. The company is operating with a "non-technical founder + founding engineer" model, which is struggling to scale its offshore/non-US engineering efforts due to poor candidate quality and high churn.

## Careerspan's Value Prop for Founders
The meeting highlights a critical "founder-to-founder" sales motion:
1. **The Problem:** The current market allows low-quality candidates to "spray" applications, wasting founder time.
2. **The Solution:** A high-friction (but high-value) AI-driven application process that measures "thinking capacity" and "subjective fit."
3. **The Pivot:** Careerspan is moving from being perceived as an "aggregator" to a "two-sided coaching and matching solution."
