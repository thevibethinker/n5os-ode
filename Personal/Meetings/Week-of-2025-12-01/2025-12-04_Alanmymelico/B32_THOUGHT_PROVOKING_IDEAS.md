# B32 Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The "Technical Linchpin" as a Service:** Vrijen's observation that overseas talent needs a stateside "brain" to draw the straight lines. Could Careerspan or Corridor X formalize this "Linchpin-as-a-Service" for non-technical founders?
- **Recruitment Anonymity as a Feature:** Founders often get stuck in bad exclusive contracts. Careerspan's ability to post "anonymously" but with high signal could be a killer feature for founders trying to bypass legacy recruiters.
- **Friction as a Quality Filter:** In a market where candidates "spray and pray," forcing a 40-minute "hoop" (that is beneficial to the candidate) is the ultimate signal of motivation and self-awareness.
- **The "Resume Tax" Elimination:** The next phase of HR Tech isn't better resume reading (AI parsing); it's making the founder read *fewer* resumes by creating a high barrier of high-signal data.
