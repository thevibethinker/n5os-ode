# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Alignment
- **Product Strategy:** Shift from simple job matching to a persistent career platform that rewards time investment.
- **Monetization:** Experimenting with variable pricing models (trial sprints, time-boxed contracts) to align with diverse employer needs.
- **Technology:** Focus on "page analysis" and "macro trends" to identify friction in the user flow.

## Market Positioning
- Positioned as the antidote to "Workday fatigue" by providing a centralized, reusable data store for applications.
