# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Alignment
The meeting reinforces Careerspan's shift from a simple job board to a **Career Concierge/Automation Engine**. By emphasizing "passive applications," the company is positioning itself as a long-term utility rather than a transactional job-seeking site.

## Market Positioning
*   **The "Anti-Workday" Stance:** Leveraging the universal frustration with repetitive application forms to highlight Careerspan's data-persistence model.
*   **Boutique Partnerships:** Focusing on high-margin bounty deals (25%) with specialized firms (like the Australian ATS/Matchbox clone) rather than chasing mass-market employer scale initially.

## Revenue & Growth
*   **The "Trial" Hook:** Recognizing that annual contracts are a high-friction sale, the team is moving toward time-boxed trials (2-3 months) to demonstrate value quickly and secure lower-resistance buy-in.
*   **Sourcing as a Service:** The business is increasingly acting as a data pipe, where Careerspan sources and pre-vets candidates, and partners (like Try Matchbox) handle the procedural hiring steps.
