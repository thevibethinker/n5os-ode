# Business Context

## 43North

- **Type:** VC fund + startup accelerator operating as a non-profit.
- **Backer:** State of New York.
- **Mandate:**
  - Drive **job creation** and economic development.
  - Attract and scale tech companies in and around **Buffalo**, with a focus on revitalizing downtown.
- **Talent Reality:**
  - Works with a portfolio of founders who often struggle to identify truly strong technical talent amidst large volumes of look-alike candidates.

## TechBuffalo (Sister Organization)

- **Type:** Workforce/talent-focused non-profit adjacent to 43North, within the same foundation ecosystem.
- **Mandate:**
  - Support the local tech talent pipeline, especially **university and early-career** candidates.
  - Connect regional employers with emerging talent.
- **Shared Problem with 43North:**
  - Traditional resumes and standard LinkedIn profiles provide **weak differentiation**.
  - Employers and ecosystem partners need better, higher-signal views of candidates’ real abilities.

## Careerspan Fit

- **Core Capability:**
  - Structured, story-based assessment that turns candidate experiences into **high-resolution signal** for hiring decisions.
- **Strategic Fit Here:**
  - Acts as a candidate-signal layer that could sit between:
    - University/early-career technical talent, and
    - Portfolio founders / regional employers in the 43North + TechBuffalo network.
  - Maps directly onto the "LinkedIn 2.0" concept Nick describes: a way to see beyond resumes for technical roles.

## Why This Matters Now

- 43North and TechBuffalo are actively thinking in **systems**, not one-off placements.
- If Careerspan can demonstrate value in this context, it could become:
  - A reference implementation for other ecosystem partnerships.
  - A source of high-leverage distribution and validation for the product.
