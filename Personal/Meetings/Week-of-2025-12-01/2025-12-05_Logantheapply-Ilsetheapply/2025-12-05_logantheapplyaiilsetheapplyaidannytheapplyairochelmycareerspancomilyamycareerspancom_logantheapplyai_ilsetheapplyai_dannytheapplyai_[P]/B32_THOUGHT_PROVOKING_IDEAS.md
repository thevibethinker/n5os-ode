# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

## Viral Distribution as Partnership Bait
Vrijen's TikTok content led to a direct inbound from a major player (Teal). This suggests that "viral" or "top-of-funnel" awareness can be used as a strategic signal to bypass traditional BD layers and reach CEOs directly. Can this be systematized?

## "Proof Points" over Paperwork
The team explicitly decided *not* to formalize a partnership with Superposition via contracts until they had demonstrated recurring value. This "execution-first" approach to BD reduces friction and ensures that when the contract is signed, the leverage is clearly in Careerspan's favor.

## Link Rental as a Revenue Model
Vrijen mentioned "paying us a nominal link rental fee." This explores the idea of the job link itself being a valuable asset that employers pay to use, rather than just a tool for application. It treats Careerspan's tech as a "utility" for the hiring process.

## Brand Equity Borrowing
Small companies (Careerspan) can effectively "borrow" the trust of larger companies (Superposition) by positioning themselves as the "evaluation/matching" engine that allows the larger partner to scale their own value-add. It’s a win-win that solves the "too new to trust" problem.
