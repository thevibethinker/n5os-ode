# B06: Business Context

## Market Position
Careerspan is actively seeking to leverage "brand equity" from established partners like Superposition to overcome the "trust factor" hurdle associated with being a new player in the market. The outreach from Teal validates Careerspan's growing visibility (driven by TikTok/LinkedIn) and positions them for potential high-level collaborations in the career tech ecosystem.

## Partnership Ecosystem
- **Superposition:** Acting as a "wedge" for market entry. The relationship is shifting from purely informal collaboration to a potential revenue-sharing model based on link rental fees and customer referrals.
- **Teal:** A potential "big player" partnership. Teal's massive user base (2M job seekers) makes this a high-stakes conversation that could significantly alter Careerspan's scale.

## Operational State
The company is transitioning from "ad-hoc" activity to "formalized processes" (standardizing the job board, setting up Pipedrive). The current focus is on "noise" (outbound visibility) and "reps" (sales cycle iterations) to build the data needed to define internal sales stages.
