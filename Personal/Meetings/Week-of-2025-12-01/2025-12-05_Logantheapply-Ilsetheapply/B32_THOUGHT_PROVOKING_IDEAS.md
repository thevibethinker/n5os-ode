# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

### 1. The "Link Rental" Model
Could "Link Rental Fees" become a standardized SaaS-like revenue stream for Careerspan? Instead of just placement fees, charging for the infrastructure of the evaluation itself creates a lower-friction, recurring-style baseline.

### 2. Branding as a Shield
Vrijen noted that founders are "always a little hesitant because we're new." Using Superposition's brand equity as a wedge is a smart tactical move. Are there other "brand anchors" Careerspan can align with to decrease the perceived risk of using a new evaluation tech?

### 3. TikTok to CEO Pipeline
The Teal CEO reaching out via TikTok highlights the non-linear path of B2B sales in the current creator economy. How can Careerspan further systematize "making noise" to attract high-leverage inbound interest rather than relying solely on outbound?

### 4. Evaluation vs. Admin
The partnership separates "Admin" (Superposition) from "Evaluation" (Careerspan). This clarity of labor might be the key to scaling: if Careerspan *only* focuses on being the best evaluation matching engine, it can integrate with any number of "Admin" or "Sourcing" partners.
