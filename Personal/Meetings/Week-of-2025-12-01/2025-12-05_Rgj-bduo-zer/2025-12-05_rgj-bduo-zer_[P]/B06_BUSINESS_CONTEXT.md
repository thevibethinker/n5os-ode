# B06: Business Context

## The Problem
A highly capable engineer is currently in a "treading water" phase—working in the service industry while job hunting. This leads to fatigue and avoidance of the job search (the "Lifer Trap").

## The Opportunity
The engineer has a strong network, including a former manager who is now a CTO in a high-growth sector (Biotech). There is also an opportunity to build "Indie Hacker" utility apps to maintain technical continuity and generate cash flow.

## Market Positioning
V advises that while the engineer's "personality" and "quirkiness" are strengths in a networking-driven search, the resume must still satisfy the "consensus-building" requirement of an organization. This means it needs to be both "flashy" for human reviewers and keyword-optimized for technical reviewers (and ATS).
