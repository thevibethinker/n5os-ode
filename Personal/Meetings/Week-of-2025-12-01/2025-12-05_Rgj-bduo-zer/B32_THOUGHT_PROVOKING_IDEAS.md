# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

1.  **The "Air Traffic Control" of Software:** Could the restaurant window role ("bridge between kitchen and servers") be a powerful metaphor for Product Engineering or Engineering Management?
2.  **The Exhaustion Trap as a Biological Moat:** Does the exhaustion of a "survival job" act as a biological deterrent to social/economic mobility? How do you solve for "self-management" when the body is truly tired?
3.  **The "Personality" Premium in Engineering:** In a world where high-level technical skills are increasingly commoditized or AI-assisted, is "being someone people want to grab a beer with" the ultimate 1% differentiator?
4.  **Resumes as Consensus-Building Tools, Not Fact Sheets:** Shifting the perspective of a resume from "this is what I did" to "this is the evidence my advocate needs to convince the rest of their team."
5.  **Indie Hacker Partnerships as Resume "Fillers":** Is building "utility products" the new "portfolio," and should it be optimized for cash or for resume "continuity"?
