# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objective
The core objective is "opening the funnel" by leveraging the intense passion of current power users ("rabid users"). The "Zo Club" system is a direct response to the need for scalable, grassroots community building that doesn't rely on traditional, high-cost acquisition channels.

## Market Positioning
Zo is positioning itself against the "influencer/KOL" model of education. Instead of users watching a YouTuber and then using a separate tool, Zo aims to integrate education (how to use AI), community (clubs), and implementation (the Zo machine) into a single, un-intermediated experience.

## The Prosumer Shift
Vrijen identified a critical cultural trend: "Consumerism fatigue." People are becoming averse to buying decisions. Zo capitalizes on this by shifting the user from a "consumer of apps" to a "builder of solutions." The business goal is to cultivate "Zo prosumers."
