# B03 – Decisions

## GTM Strategy Pivot
- **Decision:** Shift primary outreach focus to **Platform Managers** at VC firms.
- **Rationale:** These individuals are responsible for hooking founders up with resources and are currently underserved by existing tools like Getro.

## ICP Definition
- **Decision:** Target **mid-sized funds** (Fund 2 or 3).
- **Rationale:** Small funds (Fund 1) lack the support staff to manage the tool; massive funds (Alumni, Sapphire) are too large for initial rollout. Mid-sized funds have enough portfolio companies to benefit from the "smoothing function" of a constant talent stream.

## Strategic Partnership Integration
- **Decision:** Proceed with **Superposition AI** as a candidate-side API.
- **Rationale:** Aligns with the "Internet of Careers" vision and allows Careerspan to pipe candidates into existing recruiter systems without requiring employers to switch their entire workflow.

## Phased Rollout for Large VCs
- **Decision:** If engaging with massive funds like Alumni, implement a **phased rollout**.
- **Rationale:** The scale is too large for an immediate, full-portfolio launch.
