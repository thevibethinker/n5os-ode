# B01: Detailed Recap

Vrijen shared updates on a recent AI builder event and a promising conversation with a VC platform person at Alumni Ventures. The discussion focused on a significant shift in Careerspan's Go-To-Market (GTM) strategy: targeting VC firms directly as a "platform" or "talent" solution. 

### Key Themes:
- **VC Talent Solutions:** Vrijen notes a strong signal from VCs interested in providing talent support to their portfolio companies (Portcos). Current solutions like Getro are perceived as mere "job boards" that don't solve the quality or filtering problem.
- **The "Trash Heap" Metaphor:** Both Vrijen and Alex discussed the "inundation of trash" in traditional hiring pipelines. Careerspan's value proposition is its ability to maintain quality and candidate standards via AI.
- **Partnerships:** Careerspan signed a partnership with Superposition AI. The Founder is open to Careerspan acting as the candidate API, piping candidates into their system.
- **ICP Refinement:** Vrijen identified "Platform Managers" at VC firms as the primary target persona. There was a debate about whether to target massive firms like Alumni Ventures (high volume, but complex) versus mid-sized funds (first or second fund) where the value of a "smoothing function" for lumpy hiring is more apparent.
- **Strategic Vision:** Vrijen reinforced his vision of the "Internet of Careers"—positioning Careerspan as a candidate API.
