# B06: Business Context

The conversation marks a pivotal moment where Careerspan is transitioning from a direct-to-employer sourcing tool to an "Infrastructure/API" play for VC ecosystems. 

### Strategic Pillars:
1. **The Internet of Careers:** The long-term vision of Careerspan as a standardized API for candidate data.
2. **VC Platform as Distribution:** Leveraging VC platforms (who are incentivized to help Portcos but lack the tech to do it well) as a massive top-of-funnel for candidates.
3. **The Quality Differentiator:** Countering the market cynicism (the "trash heap") by proving that AI can actually maintain standards at scale.
4. **Unit Economics:** Vrijen acknowledges internal stress regarding OpenAI credits and runway, making the VC platform play (with its potential for high-volume data capture) a critical strategic bet.
