# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The Internet of Careers:** What does the world look like when candidate identity is portable via an API rather than siloed in thousands of ATS trash heaps?
- **Expectation via Technology:** How much of the "low expectation" of VCs is a direct result of being forced to use Getro? Are we solving a hiring problem or a "lack of imagination" problem?
- **Smoothing Functions:** Is Careerspan's real product "hiring" or is it "predictability for venture ecosystems"?
- **The "Look Good" Incentive:** In B2B SaaS, the emotional goal (looking competent/generous to your boss/partner) is often a stronger driver than the functional goal (hiring). How do we weaponize that for VCs?
