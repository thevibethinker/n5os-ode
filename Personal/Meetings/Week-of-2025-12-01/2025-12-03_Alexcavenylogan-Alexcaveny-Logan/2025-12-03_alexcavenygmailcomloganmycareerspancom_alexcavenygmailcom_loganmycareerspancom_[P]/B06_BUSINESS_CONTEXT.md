# B06: Business Context

Careerspan is moving away from generic job board positioning and towards a **high-signal talent platform** for VCs. 

### Strategic Context:
- **Market Timing:** VCs are under pressure to provide tangible value to Portcos beyond capital. Talent support is the most requested but hardest to scale service.
- **Competitive Opening:** The dominant player (Getro) is viewed as low-value/high-volume. There is a "trash heap" of low-quality candidate applications that VCs and Portcos are tired of digging through.
- **The "Candidate API" Hypothesis:** The long-term business value may not be a standalone UI, but a high-fidelity intelligence layer that integrates into existing ATS/Recruitment workflows.
- **Unit Economics vs. Market Pull:** There is a tension between the high cost of AI-driven sourcing and the undeniable market demand for better candidates. V is using external signals (VC interest) to justify the investment to the internal team.
