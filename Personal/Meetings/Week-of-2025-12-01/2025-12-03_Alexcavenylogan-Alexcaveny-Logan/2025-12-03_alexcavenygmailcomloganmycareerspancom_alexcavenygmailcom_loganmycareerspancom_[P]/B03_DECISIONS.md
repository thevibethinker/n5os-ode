# B03: Decisions

- **ICP Priority:** Focus GTM efforts on VC **Platform Managers** rather than general partners or founders directly.
- **Target VC Segment:** Prioritize mid-to-large VCs (Fund II/III+) that have a "platform" or "talent" function and enough portfolio companies (100+) to justify a continuous pipeline.
- **Product Integration Path:** Accept a "Candidate API" model where Careerspan pipes data into recruiter systems (e.g., the Superposition AI partnership) as a valid compromise to reach the market.
- **Messaging Pivot:** Frame the offering as a way for VCs to "distinguish themselves to their Portcos" (optics) while ensuring the back-end delivers high-quality candidates (utility).
