# B06 – Business Context

## Competitive Landscape
- **Getro:** Currently the dominant player in VC job boards. Critiqued for lack of filtering and high noise (10,000+ jobs with no curation).
- **The "Trash Heap" Problem:** Hiring managers and VCs are cynical because traditional pipelines (LinkedIn, basic job boards) are inundated with low-quality applications. Careerspan must "climb over" this reputation.

## The VC Talent Value Prop
- **Stated Need:** VCs need to show portcos they are adding value to justify management fees and distinguish their "platform."
- **Hidden Need:** Actual quality hires that move the needle for portcos.
- **Smoothing Function:** Individual companies have "patchy" hiring (hire after a raise, then stop). A VC portfolio of 100+ companies provides a constant, aggregate demand for talent, making a dedicated pipeline efficient.

## Vision: The "Internet of Careers"
- Vrijen’s long-term goal is for Careerspan to be an API (the candidate side of the equation) rather than just a standalone destination. This allows for deep integration into the existing recruiter ecosystem.
