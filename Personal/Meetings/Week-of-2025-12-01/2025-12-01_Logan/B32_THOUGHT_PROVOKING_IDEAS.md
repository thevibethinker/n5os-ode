# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

*   **The David Blaine Strategy:** Can a startup with significant debt exit purely on the basis of "narrative engineering" and "FOMO stacking"?
*   **Reverse Shrinking:** The idea that if partnerships offset half the salaries, the runway extends (Feb to April). It’s an interesting way to view capital efficiency in the endgame.
*   **"Stitching Together an Asset":** The realization that disparate university pilots and community relationships are actually a "channel" that might be more valuable than the core "scanning" product.
*   **The Ethical Exit:** How does one prioritize the "soft landing" of a team member (Ilsa) when the financial stakes for the investors are so underwater?
