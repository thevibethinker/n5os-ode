# B06: Business Context

Careerspan is at a critical juncture, navigating the "Tale of Two Cities" dynamic: high reputation and pilot success vs. significant debt ($2M) and limited runway.

### Market Positioning:
*   **The Problem:** High volume in recruitment makes differentiation difficult for VCs' portfolio companies.
*   **The Solution:** Careerspan's ability to provide "dedicated, high-quality eyeballs" through established university and community relationships.
*   **The Shift:** Moving from a tool that *scans* to a platform that *channels* talent. This reduces the technical and operational cost of scanning while maintaining the high-value access to candidates that VCs and large employers crave.

### Financial Reality:
*   The business model is essentially being re-engineered to facilitate a clean exit. By reverting the cap table and using debt instruments, the founders are clearing the path for an acquisition that can satisfy the primary investor without the complexity of activated SAFEs.
