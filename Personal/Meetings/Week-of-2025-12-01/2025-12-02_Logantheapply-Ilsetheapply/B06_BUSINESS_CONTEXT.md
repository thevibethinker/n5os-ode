# B06 Business Context

## Market Positioning
Careerspan is differentiating itself by rejecting the "volume-based" resume scanning model. The market (specifically VCs) is signaling a high value for "talent pool access" and "eyeballs" on roles, rather than just tools for filtering.

## Sales & Growth
The company is moving from demo-based feedback to active lead generation via social media (Meta/Instagram). The current theme is "X-Ray Vision," focusing on the clarity and quality of the matching process.

## Product/Market Fit Challenges
There is a recognized tension between companies that need a "slow burn" high-quality hire and companies like Theron that have urgent, funding-dependent hiring needs. The product may be more optimized for the former, or needs to adapt to handle the pressure of the latter.
