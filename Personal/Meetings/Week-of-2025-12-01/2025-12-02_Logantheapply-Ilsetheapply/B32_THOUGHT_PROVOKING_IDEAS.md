# B32 Thought Provoking Ideas

- **The "Slow Burn" Paradox**: Is Careerspan inherently a tool for companies that *aren't* in a rush? If funding is dependent on a hire (e.g., Theron), does the "quality-first" approach become a liability or a luxury?
- **VCs as Distribution, not just Buyers**: By integrating with tools like Getro, does Careerspan move from being a "tool for founders" to a "standard for VCs"?
- **"X-Ray Vision" for Hiring**: How can this marketing theme be translated into a literal UI feature that justifies the "Quality over Quantity" claim?
- **Mass Scanning vs. Curation**: The decision to kill mass scanning is a bet on the human-in-the-loop/curation model. How does this scale technically compared to the competitors offering "500 resumes for every job"?
