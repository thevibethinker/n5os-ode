# B06: Business Context

## The Problem
The hiring market is currently flooded with "AI slop" resumes—low-signal, high-volume applications generated by LLMs. This has decreased the reliability of resumes and increased miss-hiring rates by ~15%.

## The Careerspan Solution
Careerspan provides a **qualitative signal** through guided self-reflection. By mapping these reflections against job descriptions, it identifies "real" skills that the candidate has actually reflected upon, rather than just keywords listed on a resume.

## Partnership Opportunity
Partnering with Healthy Gamer GG (an organization with deep psychological/coaching expertise and a massive young-male audience) provides:
1. **Validation:** Academic/outcomes research to prove Careerspan improves career "preparation" and outcomes.
2. **Distribution:** Access to student populations and university career centers.
3. **Synergy:** Careerspan's "asynchronous coaching" tools complement Healthy Gamer's existing human-led coaching interventions.
