# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

## 1. "Qualitative Data at Scale"
The core hypothesis of a potential study: Can LLMs transform "troves of qualitative data" (structured reflections) into a standardized, quantitative signal that predicts career outcomes better than a traditional resume?

## 2. "The Death of the Resume Signal"
If the marginal cost of a "perfect" resume is now zero (AI-generated), does the future of hiring depend entirely on "coached reflection" or some other form of proof-of-human-effort?

## 3. "Life Purpose as a Career Metric"
Healthy Gamer's shift to the **PROMIS** scale for "life purpose and agency" suggests that career coaching is moving toward a more holistic, psychological metric. How can Careerspan explicitly track "Agency" as a secondary outcome of its reflection process?

## 4. "The Massachusetts Connection"
Leveraging the geographical overlap between Healthy Gamer, UMass Amherst, and V's network to create a "Regional Innovation Study" that could attract state or local development funding.
