## Blurb 1: Careerspan for VC Talent/Platform Teams
**Type:** Email Blurb (Type 2)  
**Subject:** Careerspan  
**Audience:** VC Talent/Platform Partners  
**Purpose:** Strategic positioning of Careerspan as a proprietary talent asset for funds.

Careerspan is a career development and matching platform that helps VC platform teams support portfolio hiring while building their own proprietary talent database.

Most funds struggle to provide high-leverage hiring support without simply becoming a manual recruiting agency. Careerspan solves this by giving portfolio companies "talent funnel" links that route candidates through a structured, Socratic reflection process. Instead of a lazy one-click apply, candidates provide deep qualitative signal on their decision-making and strengths—data that the fund owns and can reuse across the entire portfolio.

We’re moving away from the "Getro model" where data is siloed and surface-level. Careerspan surfaces the top 2–5% of candidates who are not just a keyword match, but a behavioral fit for the specific stage of a startup. It allows funds to capture high-intent talent early, reducing dependency on expensive external search firms.

I’d love to show you how we’re structuring these "always-on" funnels for other NYC-based founders.
