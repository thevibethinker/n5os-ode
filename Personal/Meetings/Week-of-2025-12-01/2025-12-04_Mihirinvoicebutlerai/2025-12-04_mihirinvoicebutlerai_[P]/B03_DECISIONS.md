# B03: Decisions

## Strategic & Tactical Decisions

### 1. Invoicing Mechanics (Careerspan)
- **Decision:** Stick to manual Stripe payment links for now.
- **Rationale:** Careerspan only has ~5 customers; automation overhead is not yet justified (threshold set at 25-30 customers).
- **Tooling Choice:** Avoid "Stripe Invoicing" features due to fee structure; prefer QuickBooks Invoicing for formal requests or direct Stripe credit card charges.

### 2. Contract Structure
- **Decision:** Transition from monthly to annual contracts.
- **Rationale:** Higher quality revenue and better positioning for acquisition valuation.

### 3. ICP Target (Careerspan)
- **Decision:** Focus on VC Platform/Talent teams as a primary Ideal Customer Profile.
- **Rationale:** High-value recurring revenue and solves the "data ownership" pain point VCs have with existing talent board tools.

### 4. Team Resource Allocation
- **Decision:** Maintain a skeleton crew for Careerspan.
- **Rationale:** Focus on acquisition-readiness and capital efficiency (avoiding fundraising for now).
