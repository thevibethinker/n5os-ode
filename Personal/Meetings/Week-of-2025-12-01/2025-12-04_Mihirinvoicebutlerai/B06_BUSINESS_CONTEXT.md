# B06 Business Context

### Careerspan's Market Positioning
*   Moving away from charging job seekers.
*   Pivoting to an employer-side "matching" product.
*   **The "Un-ATS" Approach**: Positioning as a filter that provides qualitative depth (Socratic method) rather than just keyword matching.
*   **Strategic Moat**: Independent talent database for VCs, solving the problem where VCs lose their data when using third-party scrapers/job boards.

### Invoice Butler's Advice
*   Startups should optimize for "scrappy" revenue collection early on.
*   Avoid Stripe's invoicing fees (0.5% or more) by using payment links until volume justifies the cost or complexity.
*   Acquisition attractiveness is heavily weighted by the shift from monthly to annual recurring revenue.
