# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

- **Intent vs. Attributes:** How much does "high intent" (someone really wanting a specific job) offset a lack of perfect "on-paper attributes"? Can AI quantify the value of passion/conviction?
- **Headless Recruitment APIs:** The shift from two-sided marketplaces to "High-taste consumer-facing candidate products" that function as APIs for employer tools.
- **Reasoning vs. Rules:** The technical and philosophical argument that recruitment is a "dynamical system" that cannot be solved with if-else statements, but requires LLM-based reasoning over context deficits and spikes.
- **The "One-Two Punch":** Using small, high-conviction communities (Marvin, NY Founders Club) as the "hardest test" for new recruitment AI tools before scaling.
