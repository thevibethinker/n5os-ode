# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Objective
To validate a collaborative recruitment model where Careerspan handles the "high-touch" AI intake (Voice Agent) and the partner side handles the "high-scale" candidate story-matching and distribution.

## Market Positioning
- **The "Two-Punch" Strategy:** Combining Careerspan's elite intake with the partner's community-driven candidate portal.
- **Anti-Pattern:** Explicit rejection of "if-else" recruitment software. The goal is to build a "dynamical system" that mimics human recruiter reasoning.
- **Commercial Goal:** Establishing Careerspan as a viable ATS alternative/feeder for high-end technical talent in NYC.

## Risks & Constraints
- **Cost of Data:** Ilsa noted the high cost of profiles (e.g., 30 cents/profile from PDL) and the need for cost-effective intent mapping.
- **Integration Friction:** Currently manual handoffs; requires proving the value of the "LLM-legible JD" before automating.
