# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The Audit Trap:** Small startups often get "Audited Financials" requests from investors or banks that may be overkill. Clarifying the *purpose* of the request (e.g., is it just for tax nexus, or for a $10M debt facility?) can save thousands in CPA fees.
- **Contractor Concentration Risk:** With 62% of expenses going to contractors, the company's IP and operational continuity are highly decentralized. Is there a plan to bring "rockstar" contractors in-house to build equity?
- **Burn vs. Runway:** With cash dropping from $56k to $17k in one month, the "Officer Loans" are likely the primary life support for the business. How many more "seed-to-table" hits can the personal balance sheets take?
