# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The Three-Stage Job Search Framework**: Discovery (Stage 1), Alignment (Stage 2), Execution (Stage 3). This modular view of the career coaching market allows for cleaner collaboration between "competitors" who actually specialize in different segments.
- **Founder-Only "Safe Spaces"**: The specific value of a community where participants don't have to explain basic context or motives—allowing for higher-velocity problem solving.
- **Integrity in AI-Enabled Services**: Emily's comment about Vrijen being the only "AI job finder guy" she'd host highlights the market's skepticism toward low-quality/gimmicky AI job tools and the value of a "human-in-the-loop" or high-integrity approach.
