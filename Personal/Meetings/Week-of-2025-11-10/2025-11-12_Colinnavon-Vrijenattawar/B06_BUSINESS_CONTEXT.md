# B06 Business Context

## Market Intelligence: The "Fake Candidate" Epidemic
This meeting provides a visceral, first-hand account of a growing threat in the tech hiring market:
*   **Identity Theft in I-9s:** Bad actors are passing background checks and I-9 verifications using stolen identities.
*   **AI/Remote Assistance in Interviews:** Candidates are utilizing earbuds and remote handlers to pass technical interviews.
*   **The "Scapegoat" Risk:** Companies are firing internal recruiters for these failures rather than addressing systemic flaws in the verification process.

## Careerspan Strategic Alignment
*   **Hypothesis Validation:** Colin's story confirms Logan's theory that standard video and technical screens are no longer sufficient to guarantee candidate authenticity.
*   **Product Solution:** Careerspan's focus on "structured rubric-based assessments" and "merit versus ranking" addresses the exact noise that allowed these bad actors to slip through at Amwell.
*   **Advocacy:** Colin represents a potential advocate for Careerspan, as he now has a "burn the boats" reason to want a more secure, merit-based hiring system.
