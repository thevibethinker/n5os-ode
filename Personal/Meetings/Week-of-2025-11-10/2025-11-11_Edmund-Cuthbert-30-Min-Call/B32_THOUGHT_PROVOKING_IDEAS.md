# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **Talent as a Traffic Light:** Moving away from stack-ranking (thermometer) to trade-off space evaluation (traffic light).
- **Forward-Deployed Engineer Alpha:** The idea that "latent signals" for customer-facing engineers are best captured via AI coaching/interaction rather than static resumes.
- **The API for Sourcing:** Shifting from a full-stack marketplace to a "picks and shovels" API (intent-driven People Data Labs).
- **AI Talent Agents:** The future transition to sports-agent-style representation for top-tier AI-enabled talent.
