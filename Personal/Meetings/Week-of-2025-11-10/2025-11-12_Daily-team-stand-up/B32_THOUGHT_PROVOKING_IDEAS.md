# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The Myth of Superiority**: Can we truly kill the "stack rank" mentality in hiring? If we shift to "combinatorics," how do we help founders make a final decision when faced with two excellent but different "combinations"?
- **AI as an Empathy Hedge**: The idea that AI can help us see someone "at their best" is a powerful reversal of the usual "AI is cold/impersonal" narrative. How do we lean into AI as a tool for radical empathy in the hiring process?
- **Quantifying the "Black Art"**: Ilse described soft skill quantification as a black art enabled by AI. Is there a danger in making this "black art" too transparent, or does the mystery add value to the brand?
- **Transparency in the Loop**: Ilya's disclosure about using ChatGPT raises a question: Should all "human-generated" content in a tech company be "stamped" with its origin, or is the final output all that matters?
