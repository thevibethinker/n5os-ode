# B06: Business Context

Careerspan is at a critical juncture of defining its market position against traditional recruitment tools. The team is moving away from the "factory farming" approach (stack ranking and tests) and towards a more nuanced, qualitative-to-quantitative analysis. 

### Strategic Differentiators:
- **Talent Combinatorics**: Moving beyond "superior vs. inferior" to "best fit for the role based on a mix of facets."
- **Soft Skill Quantification**: Utilizing AI to perform analysis that was previously impossible or purely subjective.
- **Human Advocacy**: Positioning the tool as a way for recruiters to "see candidates at their best," aligning with the emotional and professional goals of HR specialists.
