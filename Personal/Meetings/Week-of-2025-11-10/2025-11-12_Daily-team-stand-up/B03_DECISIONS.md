# B03: Decisions

- **Messaging Framework**: Use "empirical data" as a robust descriptor for the information gathered from candidate resumes and stories.
- **Strategic Pivot**: Avoid leading with "behavioral science" as a standalone pitch to HR personas who are weary of proprietary methodologies; instead, focus on "talent combinatorics" and "seeing people at their best."
- **Video Strategy**: Proceed with two distinct video flavors (Technology-led vs. Buyer-centric) to test resonance with different personas.
- **Workflow Ethics**: Accepted transparency regarding the use of AI tools (ChatGPT) for drafting assistance as long as it is disclosed.
