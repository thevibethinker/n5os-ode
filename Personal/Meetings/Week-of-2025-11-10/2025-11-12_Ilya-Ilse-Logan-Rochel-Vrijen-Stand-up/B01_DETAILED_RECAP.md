# B01 Detailed Recap

## Overview
The meeting was a daily stand-up involving Ilya Kucherenko, Ilse Funkhouser, Vrijen Attawar, and Logan Currie. The primary focus was on marketing messaging, technical terminology for AI-driven talent analysis, and Ilya's progress on employer-facing video scripts.

## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Terminologies and Buzzwords for Talent Analysis
Ilya sought "buzzwords" or scientific terms to describe Careerspan's analysis engine to cater to different personas:
- **Technical/Early Adopters:** Resonate with terms like "AI analysis," "machine learning," and "deep learning."
- **Professional/HR Skeptics:** Need "hard science" or "empirical data" backing.
- **HR Artistry/Matchmakers:** Value the "artistry" of matchmaking and talent combinatorics.

### 2. Market Messaging and Positioning
Vrijen shared insights from conversations with HR professionals:
- **The "Methodology" Trap:** HR folks are often inundated with proprietary methodologies. Selling purely as "behavioral science" can sometimes lead to friction or skepticism about the underlying methodology.
- **Mental Models:** HR professionals are accustomed to conventional tests (MBTI, Enneagram).
- **The Combinatorics Frame:** Vrijen introduced the concept of "talent combinatorics" or "skill combinatorics"—moving away from stack ranking toward understanding the unique mix of facets a candidate brings.
- **Seeing People at Their Best:** A successful frame for HR is the idea of Careerspan acting as a "hedge" that allows them to see candidates at their best, rather than a "factory farming" approach to processing people.

### 3. Progress on Video Deliverables
Ilya reported being close to a "grand finish line" with two storyboarded videos for employers:
- **Flavor 1 (Technology-Centric):** Focuses on the new technology and how it solves problems.
- **Flavor 2 (Buyer-Centric):** Uses the classic marketing playbook of putting the HR professional at the center of the story.
Ilya plans to use an audio generator to hear the scripts aloud for final refinement.

### 4. Use of AI in Content Creation
Ilya was transparent about using ChatGPT to help overcome a writing block on a technical paragraph. Vrijen validated this, noting that the transparency and care Ilya put into the process was reassuring.

## Technical Nuances
Ilse clarified that while "empirical data" (knowledge obtained through systematic observation) is an accurate term because the system quantifies observations, Careerspan doesn't strictly adhere to a single peer-reviewed school of thought yet. The quantification of soft skills via AI is a relatively new field ("black art").
