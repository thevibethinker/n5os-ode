# B06 Business Context

## Strategic Positioning
Careerspan is actively refining its "Pitch to Employers." The company is moving away from the "Behavioral Science" label as a primary hook because it triggers requests for specific (and often proprietary) methodologies that HR teams find burdensome. Instead, the focus is shifting toward:
1. **The Human Element:** Seeing candidates at their best.
2. **Anti-Ranking:** Moving from stack ranking to "Combinatorics."
3. **Efficiency:** Empowering the HR professional rather than replacing them.

## Market Sentiment
- **HR Professional Fatigue:** They are tired of being "processors" and want to "cultivate relationships."
- **Skepticism of AI:** There is a need for "hard science" or "empirical" validation to satisfy more savvy buyers.
- **Founder Preferences:** Founders prefer the "combinatorics" view as it aligns with how they actually think about building teams.

## Content Roadmap
The immediate goal is the production of high-quality, short-form (60s) videos that can be used for employer outreach and sales.
