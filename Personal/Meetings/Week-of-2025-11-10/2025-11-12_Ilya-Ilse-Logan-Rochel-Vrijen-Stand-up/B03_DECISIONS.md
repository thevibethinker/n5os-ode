# B03 Decisions

## Terminology & Branding
- **Decision:** Use the term "Empirical Data" to describe the insights gathered from resumes and candidate stories.
- **Rationale:** It sounds more robust and accurate to the systematic observation the AI performs, distinguishing it from generic "data."
- **Decision:** Adopt "Talent Combinatorics" or "Skill Combinatorics" as a high-level framing for the analysis engine.
- **Rationale:** This resonates with founders and differentiates the product from traditional "stack-ranking" tools.

## Content Strategy
- **Decision:** Proceed with two distinct video scripts for the employer-facing marketing.
- **Rationale:** To test two different angles: one highlighting the technology/AI innovation and the other focused on empowering the HR professional's workflow.

## Internal Workflow
- **Decision:** Full transparency regarding the use of AI (ChatGPT) in drafting scripts is accepted and encouraged within the team.
- **Rationale:** Ensures traceability and maintains the "human-in-the-loop" quality standard.
