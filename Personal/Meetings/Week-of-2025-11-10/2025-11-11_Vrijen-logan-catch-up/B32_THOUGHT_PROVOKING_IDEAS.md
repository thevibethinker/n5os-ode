# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

*   **"The Love Language of Roasting":** The idea that for certain personalities, the absence of a roast is a sign of lack of intimacy, and that aggressive banter is a "complex flower" of affection.
*   **"Millennial Bougieness as a Weight":** Assessing neighborhoods not by infrastructure but by the "net asset value of designer dogs."
*   **"The Bible Study Exception":** The phenomenon where personal "legitimacy" and authenticity allow individuals to bypass a person's general skepticism toward religious or social institutions.
*   **"Artisanal Kindling Doors":** A humorous critique of modern/old-house aesthetic choices that prioritize "vibe" over basic functionality like soundproofing.
