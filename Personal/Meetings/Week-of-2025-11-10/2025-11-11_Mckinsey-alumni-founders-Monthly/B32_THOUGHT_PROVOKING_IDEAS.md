# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **Acquire to Automate:** Alex Chumbley's strategy of acquiring traditional businesses (clinics) specifically to "rewire" them with AI. Is it more efficient to buy the cash flow and automate from within than to sell automation to existing owners?
- **The ROI of Voice Intelligence:** Deeya's use of voice AI to gather frontline insights. Could "automated ethnography" be the primary way we identify automation opportunities in the future?
- **AI Expert Partner vs. Vendor:** Ahmed's positioning. In an era of commoditized API calls, is "staffing top-notch experts" the only way to maintain premium pricing in the data labeling market?
- **Non-E-commerce CDPs:** Season's goal of building Intercom-like engagement for sports/gaming. How does community engagement differ fundamentally from e-commerce transaction-led relationships?
