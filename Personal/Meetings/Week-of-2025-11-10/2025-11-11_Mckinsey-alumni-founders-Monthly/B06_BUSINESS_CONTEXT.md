# B06: Business Context

## Strategic Alignment
The McKinsey Alumni Founders community operates as a high-trust network where founders leverage their shared consulting background to validate business models and facilitate warm introductions. The meeting revealed a strong trend toward "Agentic AI" and specialized document/data processing.

## Market Trends & Signals
- **The "Unbundling" of Consulting:** Both Deeya and Alex are building products that specifically target the automation of roles traditionally held by junior consultants or back-office staff.
- **Specialized Data over Commodity Labeling:** Ahmed's success indicates a market shift away from low-cost data labeling toward expert-led "AI model improvement" services.
- **Vertical SaaS + Acquisition:** Alex's model of acquiring a clinic to "rewire" it with AI suggests a shift toward vertically integrated AI strategies.
- **Niche Ag-Tech Adoption:** Olivier's traction with plant-health scanners suggests a growing appetite for hardware-enabled data insights in traditional industries like agriculture.
