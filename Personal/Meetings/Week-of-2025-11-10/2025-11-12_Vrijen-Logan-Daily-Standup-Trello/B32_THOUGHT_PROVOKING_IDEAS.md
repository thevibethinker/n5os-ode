# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

*   **The "Compute Efficiency" Narrative:** Using the high cost of AI computation as a strategic reason for maintaining a small, curated user base. This turns a common startup "weakness" (lack of scale) into an operational "strength" (precision and cost management).
*   **Alumni "Pay-to-Check":** The idea of charging alumni or networks specifically for the ability to surface talent within their own ranks. This leverages "in-group" trust and affinity as a monetization layer.
*   **"Flexible" LOIs:** The tactic of requesting an LOI on "back-outable" terms specifically to establish pricing legitimacy with third parties (VCs) without locking into an acquisition.
*   **Hiring Signal Versatility:** The observation that a single AI storytelling tool can capture high-fidelity signals from vastly different roles (Authors vs. Technicians), suggesting a "universal" talent signal.
