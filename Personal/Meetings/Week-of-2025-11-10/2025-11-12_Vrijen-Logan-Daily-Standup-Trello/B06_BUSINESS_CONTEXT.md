# B06: Business Context

The meeting highlights Careerspan's current phase: transitioning from a pure "build" mode to active "market validation" and "pricing discovery."

*   **VC Services:** By seeking an LOI from Marvin Ventures, Careerspan is attempting to monetize its value to venture capitalists (likely as a sourcing/diligence tool) rather than just a B2C or B2B job seeker tool.

*   **Ecosystem Integration:** The focus on "alumni networks" and "community partnerships" (Fohy, Nicole/Roll Out) suggests a "bottom-up" distribution strategy via established communities.
*   **Exit/Strategic Interest:** The dialogue with Hamoon (Future Fit) indicates that Careerspan is "on the radar" for larger workforce development players, even if the current interest is speculative.
