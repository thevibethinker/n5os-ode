## Decision Criteria & Checkpoints

### **Checkpoint 1: Nov 15, 2025 (Skeleton Crew Validation)**
**Question:** Can we build a viable product with just 2 of us?

**Go/No-Go Criteria:**
- ✅ **GO:** Product spec defined; ops footprint reduced by 50%+; customer retention acceptable
- ❌ **NO-GO:** Scope creep returning; ops footprint still requires 3+ people; customer churn

**If GO:** Proceed to end-of-year decision (likely sale/fund path)
**If NO-GO:** Activate Opportunity #2 (consulting pivot) immediately

---

### **Checkpoint 2: Dec 1, 2025 (Acquirer Interest Validation)**
**Question:** Are acquirers still interested? At what valuation range?

**Success Criteria:**
- ✅ **3+ warm conversations** completed
- ✅ **Valuation range** identified ($X-YM range)
- ✅ **Acquirer appetite** clear (for Jan-Q1 close)

**If Strong Interest:** Position for quick sale (Jan close possible)
**If Weak Interest:** Determine if Scenario 3 (founder fund) is viable vs. consulting pivot

---

### **Checkpoint 3: Dec 15, 2025 (Final Decision Framework)**
**Question:** Which of 3 scenarios is best path forward?

**Decision Matrix:**
| Scenario | Path Viability | Founder Energy | Go/No-Go |
|----------|---|---|---|
| Product + Skeleton Crew | Strong | Medium | GO |
| Sale / Acquisition | Strong interest | Medium | GO |
| Founder-Funded Continuation | Unknown capital | Medium | MAYBE |
| Consulting Pivot | Strong demand | Low (burnout) | FALLBACK |
