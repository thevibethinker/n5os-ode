## Threat Landscape

**Emerging Competencies:**
- Large language models making data extraction commoditized (anyone can extract with GPT)
- Careerspan's advantage: intentional system design, not proprietary tech

**Market Fragmentation:**
- Career coaching: Saturated with individual coaches and platforms
- Talent data: Crowded with LinkedIn, Indeed, and specialized recruiting tools
- Careerspan's position: Differentiated but in fragmented market

**Funding Compression:**
- If macro environment tightens, acquirer appetite could drop
- B2B SaaS multiples under pressure (possible risk to valuation)
