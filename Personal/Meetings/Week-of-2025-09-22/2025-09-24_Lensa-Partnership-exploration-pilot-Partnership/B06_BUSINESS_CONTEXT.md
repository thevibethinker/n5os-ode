# B06: Business Context

## The Partnership Thesis
The partnership bridges two different scales of the recruitment market:
1. **Lensa (The Aggregator):** Operates at massive scale (25M jobs) but faces the "incestuous" traffic patterns of the job board industry where users bounce between platforms.
2. **Careerspan (The Signal Provider):** Operates at high-fidelity/low-volume, capturing "fresh" and highly engaged candidates from elite communities.

## Value Exchange
- **For Careerspan:** Moving from expensive and messy manual scraping to a structured, high-quality API/XML feed from Lensa significantly reduces technical debt and improves recommendation quality.
- **For Lensa:** Access to Careerspan's 75-80% open rate audience provides a source of high-intent candidates that traditional job boards struggle to reach.

## Market Positioning
Vrijen's focus on "Product Roles" as the V1 entry point is a strategic bet on the subjective nature of product hiring playing to Careerspan's data-rich matching strengths.
