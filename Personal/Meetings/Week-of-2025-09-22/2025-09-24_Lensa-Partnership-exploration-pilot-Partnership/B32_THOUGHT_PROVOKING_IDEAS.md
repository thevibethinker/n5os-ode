# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

## "Always Job Ready" Culture
Vrijen mentioned cultivating a state where professionals are always "ready to be scouted" by logging stories and reflections monthly, regardless of their current employment status. This shifts the recruitment paradigm from "periodic desperation" to "continuous visibility."

## The "Signal" vs "Traffic" Divide
The industry mostly trades in "traffic" (bouncing users), but Careerspan is attempting to trade in "signal" (validated excellence). Can this high-signal, low-volume approach actually disrupt the aggregator-dominant market at scale?

## Subjectivity in Product Hiring
Vrijen's bet that "the more subjective nature of picking for product is going to play to our strengths." This suggests that AI matching should lean into subjective storytelling rather than objective keyword matching for high-level roles.
