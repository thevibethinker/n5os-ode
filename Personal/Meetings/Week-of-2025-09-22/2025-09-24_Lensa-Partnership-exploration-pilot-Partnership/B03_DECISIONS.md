# B03: Decisions Made

## Decision 1: Launch Partnership Pilot for October

**DECISION:** Careerspan and Lensa will initiate a partnership pilot starting in October to test traffic quality and engagement.
**CONTEXT:** Careerspan wants to replace manual job scraping with a clean API/XML pipeline, and Lensa wants to acquire high-signal users for their job alerts.
**DECIDED BY:** Mai Flynn (Lensa) and Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Careerspan will be set up as a "Master Publisher" on Appcast. A testing phase will begin with specific commercial and volume guardrails.
**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Continuing with manual scraping (rejected as inefficient).

## Decision 2: Initial Commercial Terms (CPC & Budget)

**DECISION:** The pilot will start with a 15-cent CPC (Cost Per Click) and a starting budget of $2,500 per feed.
**CONTEXT:** Standard starting rates to establish an ROI baseline and determine if Careerspan's high-engagement traffic justifies the spend.
**DECIDED BY:** Mai Flynn (Lensa).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Lensa will assign two analysts (one for direct content, one for lead gen) to monitor the ROI and provide feedback on scaling.

## Decision 3: Job Feed Filtering Strategy

**DECISION:** Lensa will provide filtered XML feeds/API access narrowed down by specific categories to avoid overwhelming Careerspan's V1 matching system.
**CONTEXT:** Careerspan's current system is cost-inefficient for processing millions of jobs and currently focuses only on Product roles.
**DECIDED BY:** Mai Flynn (Lensa) and Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Initial feeds will be restricted to "Product" roles in SF, NYC, and Boston.

## Decision 4: User Flow and Application Path

**DECISION:** Candidates on Careerspan will be routed to Lensa for the application path/registration.
**CONTEXT:** Lensa operates on a user acquisition model; they need users to register for job alerts to monetize them via email/push notifications.
**DECIDED BY:** Mai Flynn (Lensa) and Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Careerspan will use Lensa links as the primary application path in emails/platform recommendations to ensure traceability and monetization.

## Decision 5: Pilot Content Scope

**DECISION:** The pilot will test all types of Lensa content—organic, syndicated, and direct agency content—simultaneously.
**CONTEXT:** To determine which channel provides the highest value and best match for Careerspan's specific candidate pool.
**DECIDED BY:** Mai Flynn (Lensa) and Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Low-performing content channels will be shut off after the initial test data is reviewed, and budgets will be reallocated to successful channels.
