# B03: Decisions

## Pilot Program Configuration
- **Initial Focus:** Product roles only.
- **Geographies:** Boston, San Francisco, New York.
- **Cadence:** Repeat/automatic pipeline to minimize manual "tinkering."
- **Application Path:** Jobs will be distributed via email with the Lensa application path/magic link to ensure traceability.

## Financial & Technical Terms
- **Platform:** Careerspan to be registered as a "Master Publisher" on Appcast.
- **Pricing Model:** Pilot test at **15 cent CPC** (Cost Per Click).
- **Budget:** Starting budget of **$2,500 per feed**.
- **Support:** Careerspan will be assigned two analysts (one for direct content, one for lead gen).
