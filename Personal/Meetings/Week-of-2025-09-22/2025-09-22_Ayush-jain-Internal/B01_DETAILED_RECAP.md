# B01: Detailed Recap

Vrijen (V) introduced Ayush to "Zo," a cloud-based operating system he uses to automate job aggregation for Careerspan. While the system can scrape roles efficiently, V explained that the primary bottleneck is the manual verification of these roles—checking legitimacy, capturing Job Descriptions (JDs), and organizing them into a master spreadsheet.

The meeting focused on delegating this "job distribution" workflow to Ayush. Careerspan aims to provide a curated list of product management (PM) roles to its users to maintain engagement. V outlined a weekly cycle where he scrapes the data on Sundays, Ayush validates and processes ~50 high-quality roles (primarily PM, with some software engineering) on Sunday afternoon/evening, so they are ready for Elsa to distribute on Monday mornings.

Beyond job processing, V discussed expanding Ayush's role into Go-To-Market (GTM) activities. This includes monitoring social platforms for hiring managers or founders posting PM roles and initiating outreach strategies. V also shared updates on Careerspan’s business health, mentioning an upcoming pitch event and the momentum gained from notable product leaders backing the company.
