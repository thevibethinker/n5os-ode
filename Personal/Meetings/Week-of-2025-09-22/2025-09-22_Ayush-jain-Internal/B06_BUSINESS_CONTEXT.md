# B06: Business Context

### Strategic Niche
Careerspan is focusing on the **Product Management** market due to the high degree of subjectivity in hiring for these roles, where human (or curated) signal provides a competitive edge over automated boards.

### Operational Model
The company operates a "Freemium/Value-Add" model for job distribution:
1. **Curated Scraped Jobs:** Distributed weekly to keep the community engaged and "happy."
2. **Direct Placements:** Roles where Careerspan has a direct relationship with the employer, handled more manually.

### Financial/Growth Status
- **Revenue:** Lower than target, but "starting to come together."
