# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

- **The "Subjectivity Edge":** Can a recruiting business be built entirely on the ability to interpret and filter roles that are too "subjective" for traditional algorithms?
- **Vibe-Coding Bottlenecks:** V and Ayush both use "vibe coding" (LLM-assisted development). Is the "manual validation" step a failure of the current AI tools, or is it the indispensable human element in a high-trust community?
- **Founder Fatigue vs. Automation:** Vrijen's obsession with "Zo" represents a common founder trap: spending time building the "perfect system" to avoid the "grunt work," even when the grunt work is what's currently required for revenue.
