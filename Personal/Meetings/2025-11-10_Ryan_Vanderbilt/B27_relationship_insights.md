**Opening Moves**:
- Reference specific insight from previous conversation (shows V was actually listening)
- Share something V learned since last talk (demonstrates ongoing growth)
- Ask about something Ryan mentioned (Blend, mentoring, specific project)

**Middle Game**:
- Explore shared intellectual interests (user psychology, UX challenges, career development)
- Ask for specific feedback where Ryan's expertise is relevant
- Share challenges V is facing without pretending to have all answers

**Closing Moves**:
- Offer something concrete V can do for Ryan (intro, resource, advice)
- Leave door open without being pushy ("Love to continue this conversation when you have time")
- No hard asks unless Ryan offers first

**What NOT to Do**:
- Don't lead with product updates unless they're directly relevant to previous feedback
- Don't ask for intros until relationship has deepened significantly
- Don't pretend confidence on topics where V is actually uncertain
- Don't let conversation become one-sided (V talking at Ryan)
