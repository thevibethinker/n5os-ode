# Key Insights

## Product Positioning Insights

### What Doesn't Work
- **Behavioral science buzzwords**: HR professionals are saturated with proprietary methodologies and will ask for proof/peer review
- HR mental models are anchored in conventional tests (MBTI, Enneagram)
- Difficulty mapping "qualitative information → reliable insights" in their worldview

### What Works
1. **Skill Combinatorics Over Stack Ranking**
   - Founders resonate with evaluating "what combination of skills/attitudes makes someone qualified" vs. ranking people hierarchically
   - Frame: "What does Ilsa bring vs. Ilya vs. Rochel?" not "Ilsa > Ilya > Rochel"
   
2. **Seeing People at Their Best**
   - Addresses latent guilt in HR about "factory farming" hiring processes
   - HR professionals want to cultivate relationships, not just process people
   - Provides hedge: judge by best performance, then validate in interviews

3. **Empirical Data + Soft Skills Quantification**
   - Quantifying soft skills was "black arts" before AI (self-reporting only)
   - Careerspan's system is differentiated by heavy soft skills focus (no hard skills assessments)
   - Novel capability: systematic observation → empirical data

## Strategic Business Insights

### Superposition AI Model
- Recruiting firms that don't want sourcing headcount are viable partners
- Magic link as infrastructure layer: they post/attract, we qualify
- Mutual referral networks can expand reach without direct sales effort
- Flat-rate-per-link pricing aligns incentives

## Team Culture Insights

### Tool Use Philosophy
- Team embraces AI augmentation when transparent and traceable
- "No different than prosthetics" - using available tools doesn't diminish craft
- Priority: ship quality work over ideological purity
- Writer's block + deadlines = valid use case for AI assistance

## Marketing Content Development
- Two-script approach allows A/B testing different buyer personas:
  - Technology adopters (AI-forward messaging)
  - HR professionals (empowerment messaging)
- Audio mockups help validate pacing and tone before production
- 60-second format requires ruthless editing and clear value proposition
