Think of a server as your personal computer that never sleeps—always on, always accessible from anywhere, storing your files and running your programs even when you're not looking at it. Your laptop turns off. Your server doesn't. That means your AI can work overnight processing information, your websites stay live 24/7, and your data stays in one place you control. It's the difference between a workspace that disappears when you close the lid and one that's always there, always ready.
