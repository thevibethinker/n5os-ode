Yesterday's AI Circle talk explored why consumer AI isn't delivering on its promise—and what comes next. We discussed how most AI tools bet on context windows (making conversations smarter) while Zo bets on agenticity (giving AI a persistent workspace where it can actually build things over time). Demos showed workflows impossible on other platforms: zero-touch information processing, self-enriching personal systems, team coordination with persona switching. Key insight: the difference between AI as consultant versus AI as colleague living in your digital infrastructure. Thanks to everyone who joined—great questions on integration flexibility and complexity trade-offs.
