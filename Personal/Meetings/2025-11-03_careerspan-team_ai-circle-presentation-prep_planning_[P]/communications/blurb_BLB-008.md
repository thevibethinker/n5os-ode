# Zo vs. Lovable: More Than Beautiful Websites

**Lovable is brilliant at vibe coding—generating polished UIs fast.** It's the right tool if you want to build a website and ship it.

**Zo is a full computing environment.** You can build websites too, but that's just one option. The real power is deeper: integrate with your files, run workflows continuously, connect to your entire ecosystem of tools, store data your way, and even host other people's open-source software. Lovable says "build prettier." Zo says "build anything."

**They're different layers:** Lovable is UI-forward. Zo is systems-forward. Many teams use both—Lovable for frontend polish, Zo for the backend that powers it.
