# Zo vs. Browser Automation: Safety, Control, and Ownership

**Browser automation tools give AI access to your personal browser and data.** It sounds powerful, but it comes with a problem: your AI is touching everything you access, reading your emails, clicking through your accounts. You're giving a visiting agent the keys to your entire digital life.

**Zo flips the security model.** Your Zo server is isolated, dedicated, controlled. You decide what data flows into it and what AI can do. Nothing touches your personal accounts or browser—your Zo instance is a separate workspace that you own completely.

**The practical difference:** Browser automation feels powerful but risky. Zo feels safer because you're in control. Your data stays yours.
