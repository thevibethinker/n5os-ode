## Decisions Made

1. **Pilot Structure:** Roll out to up to 500 alumni (or unlimited with Emory code tracking)
2. **Pricing:** Target $2-4/license for pilot phase, or $3,000-5,000/year for broader rollout
3. **Timeline:** Launch within 3 weeks (before holidays), 6-week observation period for initial data
4. **Marketing:** "End the year on a high with Emory" / "Start the year on a high note" messaging
5. **Deliverables:** Vrijen to package Columbia + UMass Eisenberg results, testimonials from existing Emory alumni users, and persuasion deck
6. **Distribution:** Through Emory Connects platform, potentially via Salesforce Marketing Cloud
7. **Alternative Option Considered:** First X free with benchmark-triggered commitment for larger deal
