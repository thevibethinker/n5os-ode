# B01 Detailed Recap: Fohe Partnership Pilot

## Overview
Vrijen Attawar and Logan Currie (Careerspan) met with Ray Batra, Jake Weissbourd, and Shivani Mathur (Fohe) to discuss a potential partnership pilot. The discussion centered on validating Careerspan's value proposition, evidentiary basis for matching, and the logistics of a joint member blast.

## Discussion Points

### 1. Value Proposition Validation
Ray Batra sought to distill Careerspan's value for job seekers into three core pillars:
- **Common App for Jobs:** Fill out once, considered for many.
- **Better Matches via Stories:** Qualitative data surfacing through "Stories."
- **Coaching/Support:** Guidance provided along the job search journey.
- **Queue Skipping (The X-Factor):** Logan added that the primary benefit is getting a "second look" from hiring managers, bypassing the standard ATS lottery.

### 2. Evidence & Matching Efficacy
- **Case Studies:** Logan and Vrijen highlighted instances where candidates who were "ATS-invisible" based on resumes became top-tier candidates once their "Stories" were incorporated.
- **Statistical Lifting:** Vrijen mentioned data indicating a significant increase in visible soft skills after Careerspan interactions, particularly for non-technical roles where resumes fail to capture structural qualitative strengths.
- **KPIs:** Tracking focuses on a three-stage pipeline: Pre-screening/Co-selection, Interview progress, and Offers accepted (the "gold standard").

### 3. Supply and Demand Logistics
- **The "Blast" Concern:** Jake raised a critical supply-side question: if 5,000 members are emailed and 700 sign up, are there enough jobs?
- **Hybrid Job Pool:** Vrijen explained their strategy of using a mix of "Direct Careerspan Jobs" (sourced directly with queue-skipping) and "Aggregated/Scraped Jobs" (high-standard curated listings) to ensure every user receives relevant recommendations.
- **User Engagement:** Vrijen shared metrics: ~50% Day 30 return rate and ~12-15% click-through rate for job recommendations, indicating high user satisfaction with matching quality.

### 4. Revenue Model
- **Paywall Strategy:** Careerspan is currently free for members, but they are considering a paywall for the "Direct Sourced/Queue-Skipping" job functionality after an initial 1-2 month trial.
- **Revenue Share:** Vrijen confirmed that revenue from Fohe members would be shared with Fohe.
