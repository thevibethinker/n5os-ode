# B06 Business Context: Fohe Partnership Pilot

## Strategic Alignment
This partnership represents a high-potential channel for Careerspan to acquire high-quality users (Fohe members) who typically possess qualitative strengths that are undervalued by traditional ATS systems. For Fohe, this is a value-added service that helps their members find better-aligned career opportunities.

## Market Positioning
Careerspan is positioning itself as a "status quo bypass." By using Stories to provide qualitative "lift," they are attacking the inefficiency of the resume-based ATS lottery. The partnership with Fohe validates this approach within a niche community of high-potential individuals.

## Financial Context
The revenue model is shifting toward a freemium approach. Acquisition through Fohe is a "supply-first" move that requires a corresponding "demand-side" (employer) scale. The discussion of revenue sharing indicates a long-term interest in Careerspan as a sustainable business partner for professional communities.
