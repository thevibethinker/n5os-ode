# B03 Stakeholder Intelligence: Fohe Partnership Pilot

## Participants

### Careerspan Team
- **Vrijen Attawar:** Founder. Focused on the philosophical alignment of qualitative matching and the revenue/partnership structure.
- **Logan Currie:** Focused on the operational mechanics of the employer pipeline, case studies, and the "queue-skipping" value prop.

### Fohe Team
- **Ray Batra:** Lead interrogator on value proposition and evidence. Wants to ensure the "essence" of the value is communicable and backed by data.
- **Jake Weissbourd:** Focused on scalability and member experience. Specifically concerned about the "Supply vs. Demand" mismatch during a large-scale launch.
- **Shivani Mathur:** Interested in the utility of "Stories" (resume vs. interview aid) and the current platform behavior for users.

## Stakeholder Needs & Sentiment
- **Fohe Priority:** Protecting member trust. They need to ensure that a "blast" results in actual job opportunities, not just a crowded waitlist.
- **Careerspan Priority:** Proving that their qualitative data ("Stories") creates a measurable "lift" in hiring outcomes for Fohe's non-technical/qualitative-heavy membership.
- **Sentiment:** Highly collaborative and inquisitive. The Fohe team is doing due diligence but shows clear interest in the "Common App" and "Queue Skipping" concepts.
