# B03 Decisions: Fohe Partnership Pilot

## Key Decisions Made

### 1. Value Proposition Pillars
The group agreed to frame the value proposition around four pillars:
1. **Common App functionality** (efficiency).
2. **Qualitative matching via Stories** (depth).
3. **Queue-skipping** (the competitive edge/guaranteed look).
4. **Coaching/Support** (guidance).

### 2. Job Supply Strategy
To mitigate Jake’s concern about job scarcity during a flood of signups, the pilot will explicitly differentiate between **Direct Sourced Jobs** (where Careerspan has an active relationship and queue-skipping applies) and **Aggregated Recommendations** (high-quality curated leads).

### 3. Revenue Sharing Principle
Vrijen and Ray agreed in principle that any paywall revenue generated from Fohe members (specifically for long-term access to the direct-sourced job feed) would be split between the two organizations.
