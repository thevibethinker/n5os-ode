# B06: Business Context

## Value Proposition Development
The company is currently in a phase of proving its core matching system. The debate between "archetypes" and "placements" reflects a strategic choice to prioritize high-signal, objective success (hires) over narrative-based potential (archetypes). This is driven by a need to gain employer trust through tangible results.

## Metric Framework
Logan introduced a clear three-stage funnel for tracking product success:
1. **Pre-screening success** (Validation of matching algorithm)
2. **Interview stage** (Validation of candidate quality)
3. **Offer stage** (Validation of ultimate business value)

## Market Engagement
The "Careerspan Select" initiative demonstrates a strategy of capturing high-intent referrals ("friends of friends") to seed the system with quality talent while maintaining a low-friction entry point (3-minute Typeform).

## Team Growth & Dogfooding
The proposal to join professional communities (like Lenny's) indicates a culture of learning and a belief that the team's professional growth directly informs product-market fit.
