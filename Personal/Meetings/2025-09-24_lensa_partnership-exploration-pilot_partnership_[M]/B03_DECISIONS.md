# Decisions

1. **Proceed with a structured test between Careerspan and Lensa.**
   - Both parties agree in principle to run an initial traffic and jobs distribution test.

2. **Use Appcast as the technical integration layer.**
   - Careerspan will become a master publisher on Appcast.
   - Lensa will send XML feeds to Careerspan via Appcast.

3. **Commercial test parameters (initial).**
   - Pricing: Start at **$0.15 CPC** on Lensa’s side.
   - Budget: **$2,500 per feed** as an initial test allocation.
   - Lensa will assign **two analysts** (direct content + lead‑gen content) to monitor performance and advise on scaling.

4. **Job scope and targeting.**
   - Start with **product roles** only, focused on major hubs (Boston, SF, NYC) where Careerspan’s early audience is likely concentrated.
   - Lensa will provide **filtered feeds** so Careerspan doesn’t need to process the entire multi‑million‑job inventory.

5. **Candidate flow and routing.**
   - Careerspan will route **application paths to Lensa**, not to its own native paths, so Lensa gets clear attribution and can monetize registrations.
   - Careerspan will continue using its matching engine and high‑engagement email flows to drive quality clicks.

6. **Evaluation and scaling approach.**
   - Lensa will evaluate the test primarily on **ROI of acquired users** (engagement with job alerts, downstream monetization).
   - If performance is strong, both sides are open to **scaling budgets and broadening job categories** beyond product.
   - Careerspan is willing to **adjust matching thresholds** slightly if necessary to increase volume without breaking user experience.
