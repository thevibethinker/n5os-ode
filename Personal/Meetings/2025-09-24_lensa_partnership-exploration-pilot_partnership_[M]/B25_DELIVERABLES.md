# Deliverables

## From Lensa
- **Appcast onboarding package**
  - Master publisher setup information for Careerspan.
  - Credentials or links needed to access the XML/API feeds.
- **Initial job feeds**
  - Filtered job feeds focused on **product roles** in Boston, SF, and NYC (with potential to expand later).
  - Clarity on which content types are included (organic, syndicated, agency, etc.).
- **Commercial documentation**
  - IO or agreement confirming **$0.15 CPC** and **$2,500 per feed** test budgets.
- **Analyst introductions**
  - Email introducing the two analysts (direct content + lead gen), including how reporting and optimization will work.

## From Careerspan
- **Technical integration of feeds**
  - Internal pipeline capable of ingesting and normalizing Lensa’s filtered job feed.
  - Mapping of Lensa job attributes to Careerspan’s matching schema.
- **Campaign configuration**
  - Email/job recommendation flows that route clicks to Lensa application paths for attribution.
  - Tracking configuration (UTMs, IDs) agreed with Lensa’s analytics team.
- **Test plan and internal documentation**
  - Internal definition of test success metrics (quality/fit, engagement, downstream hiring signals where possible).
  - Operational notes on throughput limits (e.g., ~70–80 jobs/week initially) and how scaling will be handled.

## Joint
- **Launch + review cadence**
  - Target start in **October** with a clear launch date.
  - Agreed schedule for first performance review (e.g., after 2–4 weeks of data) to decide whether to scale or adjust the test.
