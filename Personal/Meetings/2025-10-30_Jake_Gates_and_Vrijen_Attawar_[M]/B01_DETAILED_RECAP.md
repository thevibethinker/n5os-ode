**V's Reframing (Key Teaching Moment):**

**Problem with "What's Unique About You" Framing:**
- It's "unintentionally unhelpful"
- There's "nothing unique under the sun about any of us"
- We're all just "a patchwork of values and qualities and things we pick up along the way"
- Individual things aren't unique, but the aggregate IS unique

**Better Framework: "Play Your Own Game"**
- Sports analogy: Do teams play to counter the opponent or play their own game?

- Don't obsess over being unique – obsess over being clear about who you are

**Reframed Question:**
