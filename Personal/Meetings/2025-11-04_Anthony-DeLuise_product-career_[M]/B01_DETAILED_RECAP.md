## Discussion Topics

### Higher Ed Sales Challenges
- Both acknowledged difficulty selling into higher education market
- Highly relationship-based, requires insider knowledge and "belonging"
- Universities have misaligned buyer/user dynamics:
  - End users (students/parents) have high UX standards
  - Buyers (administrators) have low/no standards
- Visit Days positioned as "enrollment tool" not generic events platform
- Strategic differentiation from EventBrite, Hopin, etc.

### Career Tech Landscape Critique
Anthony's observations on current tools:
- Many solve non-problems (auto-apply features)
- Cold DM automation tools unnecessary for experienced professionals
- After 5+ years of experience, networking > applications
- Value in having agency to do own research and outreach

### Careerspan Product Evolution
**Previous Experience (3+ months ago):**
- Anthony tried early version
- Liked voice-based story capture concept
- Captured more context than typing
- Didn't return after initial trial

**Current Product (as demonstrated):**
- **New framing:** "Get to the top of the stack"
- **Vibe Check feature:** 
  - Paste job descriptions for analysis
  - Shows missing skills vs. resume
  - Identifies skills needing more story evidence
  - Provides strategic positioning language for outreach
- **Job distribution:** Limited curated jobs released weekly through platform
- **Monetization:** Jobs distributed through Careerspan (not charging candidates)
- **Auto-reevaluation:** Profile rescored as candidates add new stories

**Product Philosophy Discussion:**
- Most career tools lie about having "context" (only resume data)
- Careerspan captures deeper context through story methodology
- AI-driven inquiry pushes thinking in uncomfortable directions
- "The parts we're most insecure about is where the substance lies"
- Value in working on career when you least need it

### Career Transition Insights
- With accumulated career stories, difficult to synthesize for new direction
- Recent experience feels more important than early career
- Would be valuable to have tracked career arc over time
- Natural language storytelling was differentiating feature for Anthony
