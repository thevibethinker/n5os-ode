# B06 – Business Context

- Careerspan is refining its employer‑facing story while building marketing assets.
- Market reality: HR is saturated with vendors claiming unique "behavioral" or "psychometric" science; skepticism is high.
- Careerspan’s differentiation:
  - Focus on **combining skills and experiences** rather than ranking candidates on a single axis.
  - Use AI to quantify soft skills and narrative evidence.
- Superposition AI represents a potential **distribution partner** that handles recruiting while Careerspan powers assessment.
