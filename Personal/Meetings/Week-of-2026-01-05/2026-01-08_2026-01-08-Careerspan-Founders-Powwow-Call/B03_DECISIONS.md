# B03: Decisions

## Strategy & Messaging
- **Messaging Pivot**: The team decided to focus on a narrative of "expanding access" by pulling from specific, high-signal communities. This will be framed as a strategic evolution rather than a craven rebrand.
- **Engineer Focus**: Vrijen decided that the platform MUST figure out how to get engineers on it to avoid operating in a shrinking market.

## Product & Outreach
- **Engagement Metric**: The team decided to prioritize "open rates" as the primary value signal to recruiters, rather than "personalized outreach content."
- **Privacy/Vibe Boundary**: Decided NOT to pursue hyper-personalized emails that use user stories in a way that could be perceived as "creepy" or invasive, particularly for the engineer demographic.
