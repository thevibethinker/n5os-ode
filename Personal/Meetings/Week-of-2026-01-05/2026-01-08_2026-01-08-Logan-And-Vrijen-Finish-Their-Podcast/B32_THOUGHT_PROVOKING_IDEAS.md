# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

1.  **Confidence vs. Evaluation:** The reframe of AI scoring from "evaluating a person" to "measuring visibility into a person" is a profound shift. It acknowledges that AI's best use is in resolving uncertainty (visibility) rather than making subjective value judgments (quality).
2.  **Scalable Friction as a Filter:** The concept that Careerspan uses "scalable friction" as a feature rather than a bug—selecting for those with the high agency to navigate that friction—suggests a model where the *process itself* is the primary validator of talent.
3.  **The Human Anchor:** V's alignment with "Agents of Purpose"—the idea that machines should handle 100% of the mechanics so humans can handle 100% of the meaning—poses a question: what happens to the concept of "work" when mechanics are entirely invisible?
