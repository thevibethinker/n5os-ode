### V's Careerspan Pitch

**The Problem:**
- ATS systems don't actually work for scalable talent sorting
- "Lying by omitting" about their limitations
- Resumes are inadequate data points for decision-making
- AI has made the "marginal cost of bullshit zero" - perfect resumes can be generated

**Careerspan's Solution:**
- Uses Socratic dialogue to help candidates "exposit about themselves"
- Collects information, breaks into data labels for mental models
- Candidates tell stories to back up skills claimed
- One-click apply vs. resume parsing
- "Information is already actionable - just need to decide whether to buy it"

### Product Demo Walkthrough
V shares screen and demonstrates:
1. **Candidate side:** Story capture, skills matching, gaps identification ("stories needed")
2. **Positioning narrative:** AI-generated advice on how to position yourself
3. **Match scores:** Shows candidate-job alignment (e.g., 90% match)
4. **Employer portal:** Single-click apply, story completion, visual difference between candidates with/without stories
5. **Key insight:** "This is not a reflection of how qualified someone is. This is how much visibility do you have into them."

### Jeff's Resonance Points
