# B03: Decisions Made

## Decision 1: Shift to Synthetic Talent Community Model

**DECISION:** Careerspan will transition from an open-access platform to a "synthetic talent community" with closed public signups.
**CONTEXT:** Vrijen argued that the current open availability dilutes the incentive for community partners to promote the product. By making it exclusive ("privileged access"), it becomes a genuine perk for partner communities (e.g., Next Play) and reinforces the brand as the "densest concentration of high-agency talent."
**DECIDED BY:** Vrijen Attawar (Proposed), Logan Currie (Aligned with caveats on implementation).
**IMPLICATIONS:** Public signups will be closed; new users must join via partner community codes or private invite links. Messaging will shift toward exclusivity and "high agency" metrics.
**ALTERNATIVES CONSIDERED:** Geographic limiting (Boston/New York only), which was discarded as too narrowing/onerous compared to the community-code approach.

## Decision 2: Prioritize Sourcing as the Lead Value Proposition

**DECISION:** The team will lead sales and product focus with "Sourcing" as the primary capability.
**CONTEXT:** Client feedback consistently indicates that sourcing is the most desired but unsolved component of the hiring stack. Internal verification that sourcing is "possible" (confirmed via Ilsa) allows the team to return to previous prospects who requested this feature.
**DECIDED BY:** Logan Currie and Vrijen Attawar.
**IMPLICATIONS:** Immediate sales outreach will focus on those who previously passed due to lack of sourcing. The product roadmap will prioritize "scanning" mechanics (e.g., scanning candidates against roles and presenting results).

## Decision 3: Flexible Scanning Fee Structure

**DECISION:** Implement a dual-option pricing model for candidate scanning.
**CONTEXT:** To lower the barrier for companies to try the product, Careerspan will offer two paths: 1) A flat fee for the scan results, or 2) A free scan with a higher success fee if a candidate is accepted into the company's pipeline.
**DECIDED BY:** Vrijen Attawar.
**IMPLICATIONS:** This allows for rapid generation of "proof points" and candidate success stories even if the initial B2B conversion is slow.
