# B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

## Idea 1: Talent Liquidity vs. Investment Status
David’s comparison of candidates to real estate ("liquid vs. illiquid investments") suggests that the "sourcing" problem is actually a **market-timing** problem. Sourcing the "working but willing" is the "alpha" because they are illiquid to the broad market but liquid to the high-signal scout.

## Idea 2: The "Emperor Has No Clothes" Era of Recruiting
The pivot from "Matching" to "Vetting/Sourcing" signals a collapse of faith in black-box AI algorithms. The market is returning to the value of **curated pools** and **human-verified signals**, even as it tries to use AI to scale that verification.
