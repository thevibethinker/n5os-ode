# B32: Thought-Provoking Ideas

## The "Knowledge Exchange" vs. The "Panel"
Vrijen's suggestion to move from a standard panel to a "knowledge exchange" is a significant shift in event design. **Question:** How can we facilitate this in a way that feels organic rather than forced? Perhaps using pre-event surveys to map "expertise" to "questions" among the 40 founders?

## Wellness as a Brand Values Signal
Amy mentioned partnering with wellness beverage brands to show founders that "we also care about your health." **Idea:** Can Careerspan incorporate "Founder Wellness/Mental Health" as a recurring theme in its talent community strategy? It's a high-resonance topic for founders that differentiates Careerspan from more "transactional" recruiting agencies.

## The JP Morgan Partnership
JPM is interested in events where they can "bring founders they want to bring into their business." **Insight:** This event isn't just a Careerspan lead-gen tool; it's a platform for JP Morgan's relationship management. Leveraging this dual-value could lead to a permanent "founder salon" series co-sponsored by the bank.
