## Key Strategic Insights

### 1. Systems of Remembering & Forgetting

**Core Idea:** Need formal processes for both remembering and forgetting to develop good behavioral habits.

**Personal Edge Identified:**

- "One of the edges that I have is that I don't carry things with me"
- Ability to blank out memory allows for fresh thinking
- Whether through defense mechanism or intentional process, this creates strategic advantage

**Implication:** The N5 system should support this edge by:

- Capturing what needs to be remembered (external memory)
- Allowing strategic forgetting (not requiring constant recall)
- Supporting habit formation through automated processes

---

### 2. Prediction & Hypothesis Tracking

**Requirement:** System should track:

- Personal predictions
- Business hypotheses
- Market hypotheses

**Purpose:** Build predictive capability and learn from forecast accuracy over time.

**Implementation Needs:**

- Timestamp predictions/hypotheses
- Track outcomes
- Analyze accuracy patterns
- Identify blind spots

---

### 3. Extraction & Ticketing System Requirements

**Critical Features for Meeting Processing:**

1. **Speaker Identification:** System must distinguish between speakers

   - Identify V among multiple speakers
   - Distinguish "Careerspan folks" vs. "their folks" (external parties)

2. **Side Attribution:** Track which organizational side made statements

   - Internal team perspectives vs. external stakeholder perspectives
   - Critical for CRM and relationship intelligence

**Why This Matters:**

- Accurate attribution enables better prediction tracking
- Separating internal vs. external perspectives improves strategic analysis
- Supports hypothesis generation from stakeholder conversations
