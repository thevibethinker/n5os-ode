# The Sacred Texts: Productivity in the Age of AI

**Version Status**: Living Framework (Work in Progress)
**Core Philosophy**: Knowledge as intentionally curated sacred texts, not consumed information
**Target Practitioner**: Type-A, high-achieving, ADHD, productivity-obsessed knowledge workers

---

## 🎯 The Central Metaphor: Knowledge as Sacred Texts

### What This Means

In the modern era, we don't suffer from lack of information—we suffer from context collapse. The problem isn't finding information; it's maintaining the right context in your mind and knowledge base when needed.

Knowledge is not consumed like news; it is sacred and must be intentionally curated in a canonical, trusted knowledge base we call "sacred texts."

### What Sacred Texts Are Not

- They are not your entire inbox, notes dump, or ephemeral capture
- They are not low-quality, unprocessed information
- They are not arbitrary documents without intent or validation

### The Five-Phase Flow of Modern Productivity

\- Capture → Review → Ingest → Leverage → Tidy

### Phase 1: Capture

The point of entry for all information, usually a flexible inbox (email, notes, photos, clips, meeting records).

\- Concrete Example: Using N5 OS, meetings are automatically captured in transcripts queued for review.

### Phase 2: Review

A light filtering where captured data gets validated for importance, relevance, and potential.

\- Concrete Example: Keyword tagging scripts in N5 automatically suggest which segments are important.

### Phase 3: Ingest

Validated data is cleansed, annotated, and formally added to sacred texts.

\- Concrete Example: The incantum_engine translates natural language commands into actions for ingestion.

### Phase 4: Leverage

Sacred texts are powerfully queried and cross-contextually used in workflows, decisions, and content creation.

\- Concrete Example: Listclassifier.py enables information reuse by classifying elements as needed.

### Phase 5: Tidy

Automated maintenance of sacred texts to preserve integrity, update links, and prune redundancies.

\- Concrete Example: n5_safety.py runs periodic data integrity checks and backs up the system.

## 📚 Concrete Examples of Existing Functionality

### Self-Maintaining Systems

- **n5_safety.py**: Protects knowledge base with tiered file locks and automated backups.

### Pattern Recognition & NLP

- **incantum_engine.py**: Maps natural language input to system commands facilitating ingestion automation.

### Intelligent Classification

- **listclassifier.py**: Classifies and indexes items to support cross-context leverage.

### Automated Workflows

- **n8n_processor and task_intelligence**: Implement workflow orchestration for zero-touch automation of scheduling, notifications, and content processing.
