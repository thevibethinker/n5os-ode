   - When Vrijen proposed defining the minimal product two people could operate, Logan immediately understood and agreed.
   - Why it resonated: Removes impossible burden of "keep all options open." Creates permission to say no.
   - Signal: Quick engagement, no hesitation, immediate "that's exactly" language. Logan came into meeting exhausted but this clarity energized him.

2. **Profitability-First Over Growth-At-All-Costs**
   - Vrijen's statement "with Zoe, I was like partially that's what did the selling for you on the idea of selling" landed hard.
   - Why it resonated: Logan had already been thinking about selling/exiting; hearing Vrijen willing to consider profitability model instead of perpetual growth chase validates his fatigue.
   - Signal: Logan noted "I just need a break from Baldwin" (ambiguous reference to struggle). Resonance around permission to rest, not keep pushing.

3. **AI as Intellectual Horsepower Multiplier**
   - Vrijen's observation about design patterns and building shitloads of code through disciplined implementation.
   - Why it resonated: Logan can see Vrijen's perspective but also notes the gap—top 5% of humans needed for this level of vibe coding; most people just want the product, not the craft.
   - Signal: Extended discussion, intellectual engagement, validation of "yeah this is hard." Not defensive, but clear-eyed about difficulty.

4. **Consulting/Project-Based Pivot as Viable Path**
   - Logan unprompted brought up that they (Vrijen + Logan) are both "consultants at heart." Sees opportunity in project-based AI training for companies.
   - Why it resonated: Opens new possibility space that feels within reach and energizing rather than continuous product grind.
   - Signal: Generated ideas (2-month spin-up for McKinsey-style validation framework), mentioned Andrew's positive feedback. This is how Logan thinks when excited—possibilities multiply.

5. **Clarity Over Chaos**
   - The hibernation/reset date removes speculation and gives both founders explicit decision points.
   - Why it resonated: Logan's exhaustion partly stems from uncertainty ("what are we doing?"). Having January 1 as reset point + three explicit paths = relief.
   - Signal: Shifted from fatigue-dominant to strategic-thinking-dominant partway through call.
