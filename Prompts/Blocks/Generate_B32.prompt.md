# Generate Block B32: Thought Provoking Ideas

Identify 1-3 highly provocative themes, strategic "weirdness," or original mental models that emerged during the meeting.

## Guidelines
- **FORBIDDEN**: Actionable tasks, tactical follow-ups, or standard meeting recaps. If it can be a task, it belongs in B05 or B02.
- **FOCUS**: Socratic challenges, contradictions, and "What if?" scenarios that challenge current assumptions or open new mental horizons.
- **THRESHOLD**: If no truly provocative or original ideas exist, DO NOT generate any output. An empty response is a success signal, meaning the meeting was purely operational.
- **CRINGE FILTER**: Avoid shallow AI-platitudes. If the idea isn't truly novel or surprising in the context of Careerspan and V's worldview, skip it.

## Output Format

### [Provocation/Idea Title]
- **The Spark**: What was said or implied that triggered this thought?
- **The Provocation**: A challenging, Socratic question for V that forces deeper thought.
- **Truth Anchor**: A direct quote or verifiable reference from the transcript.
- **Why it's B32**: Explain why this is a conceptual spark and not a tactical task.

---

## Requirements
- Minimum 1 provocation, maximum 3.
- Must be rooted in the meeting transcript.
- Strictly conceptual/strategic, never tactical.
