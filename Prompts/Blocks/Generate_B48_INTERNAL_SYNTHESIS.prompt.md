# Generate B48: Internal Synthesis

Synthesize strategic insights, organizational intelligence, and high-level themes from internal meetings.

## Purpose

Internal meetings contain strategic signals that go beyond individual action items or decisions. This block synthesizes the meta-patterns, organizational health signals, and strategic insights.

## Input Format

Meeting transcript from internal discussions containing strategic themes, organizational patterns, or high-level insights about company direction.

## Output Format

**REQUIRED YAML FRONTMATTER:**
```yaml
---
created: {YYYY-MM-DD}
last_edited: {YYYY-MM-DD}
version: 1.0
provenance: {agent_id or conversation_id}
---
```

**REQUIRED STRUCTURE:**

```markdown
# B48: Internal Synthesis

## Strategic Themes

### {Theme Name}
- **Pattern**: What pattern emerged in discussion
- **Significance**: Why this theme matters strategically
- **Implications**: How this might affect future decisions
- **Trend Direction**: Is this theme growing or diminishing

## Organizational Health Signals

### Positive Signals
- **Team Alignment**: Areas where team is well-synchronized
- **Energy & Momentum**: What's driving positive energy
- **Capability Growth**: Where team is getting stronger
- **Cultural Strengths**: Positive cultural patterns observed

### Areas for Attention
- **Misalignment**: Where team perspectives diverge
- **Energy Drains**: What's creating friction or frustration
- **Capability Gaps**: Where team needs to grow
- **Cultural Tensions**: Patterns that need addressing

## Meta-Insights

### Decision-Making Patterns
- **How We Decide**: Team's natural decision-making style
- **Information Needs**: What data/input team values
- **Risk Tolerance**: Team's approach to uncertainty
- **Speed vs. Thoroughness**: Team's natural balance

### Communication Patterns
- **Information Flow**: How information moves through team
- **Feedback Loops**: How team gives and receives input
- **Conflict Resolution**: Team's approach to disagreement
- **Knowledge Sharing**: How expertise gets distributed

## Strategic Positioning

### Market Position Insights
- **Competitive Understanding**: How team sees competitive landscape
- **Value Proposition Evolution**: How team's thinking on value is changing
- **Customer Understanding**: Insights about customer needs/behavior

### Internal Positioning
- **Resource Allocation Philosophy**: How team thinks about priorities
- **Growth Strategy**: Team's approach to scaling
- **Risk Management**: Team's approach to downside protection

## Forward-Looking Intelligence

### Emerging Opportunities
- **Market Opportunities**: New possibilities identified
- **Capability Opportunities**: Ways team could grow stronger
- **Partnership Opportunities**: Relationship possibilities

### Potential Challenges
- **Market Risks**: External threats or challenges
- **Internal Risks**: Team/organizational vulnerabilities  
- **Resource Constraints**: Limitations that could bind

## Strategic Questions Surfaced

- **{Strategic Question}**: Core question raised about direction
- **{Operational Question}**: Question about how team operates
- **{Market Question}**: Question about market or customers
```
