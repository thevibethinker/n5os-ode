# Generate Block R07: Prediction

**Purpose:** Capture forward-looking observations about trends, technologies, market shifts, or societal changes — things external to V's direct work but relevant to his worldview.

**Domain:** Macro trends, industry shifts, technology predictions, societal changes, "what's coming"

---

## Input

The raw reflection text is provided in conversation context.

---

## Output Format

```markdown
## R07: Prediction

**Signal:** [What V observed or is reacting to]
**Prediction:** [The forward-looking claim — be specific about timeframe if mentioned]
**Confidence:** High / Medium / Low / Speculative
**Basis:** [Why V thinks this — evidence, pattern recognition, gut]
**Implications:** [What this means if true — for V, for Careerspan, for the world]
**Watch For:** [What would validate or invalidate this prediction]
```

---

## Quality Standards

- Capture the PREDICTION clearly — what specifically is V saying will happen?
- Distinguish between observation (what V saw) and prediction (what V thinks it means for the future)
- Include timeframe if mentioned ("in 5 years", "by end of 2026", "soon")
- Note the confidence level honestly
- If V is just observing a trend without making a prediction, note that
- If the reflection doesn't contain predictions, output: `R07: Not applicable — reflection lacks predictive content`
