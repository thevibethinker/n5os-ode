## Be Ready Platform

### Mission
Social enterprise focused on helping immigrants settle in Canada through employment, housing, and credit support.

### Business Model
- **Structure**: Not-for-profit
- **Revenue**: Not VC-track, mission-first
- **Partners**: Housing and credit scoring services
- **Funding**: Canadian IP filing grants accessed

### Platform Details
- **Talent Pool**: 1,200 candidates (after 1 year)
- **Employer Base**: 145 companies
- **Success**: 45 placements
- **Tech Stack**: Custom algorithm (close to IP filing)
- **Dev Team**: 2 developers (startup visa immigrants themselves)

### Target Market
- **Talent**: Immigrants to Canada (Express Entry, international students, startup visa)
- **Employers**: SMEs under 200 employees (manufacturing, food processing, tech)
- **Roles**: Broad (IT, marketing, various) - excludes union and fractional
- **Geography**: Ontario currently

### Value Proposition
**For Talent**:
- Resume upload + 1-minute video + 500-word pitch
- NO job applications (passive talent pool)
- Soft skills training (interviews, language, business etiquette)
- NOT technical training ("they already have the technical skills")

**For Employers**:
- Search by job description
- Green/yellow/red match scoring
- Anonymized profiles (names scrubbed)
- Interview request from platform
