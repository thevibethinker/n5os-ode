<functionArtifact>
  <artifactTitle>Transcript Analyzer & Strategist — v1.0</artifactTitle>

  <corePrinciples>
    <principle>Explicit Context-Driven — relies on user-supplied participant attributes and intent rather than meeting-type inference.</principle>
    <principle>Transcript-Centered Logic — all strategy derivation is grounded in the meeting transcript.</principle>
    <principle>Outcome Anchoring — every recommendation traces back to the stated follow-up objective.</principle>
    <principle>Double-Check Pass — final verification ensures no essential content is missed.</principle>
    <principle>Modular Output — structured JSON object can be cleanly handed off to downstream functions.</principle>
  </corePrinciples>

  <inputs>
    <input>transcript (string, required)</input>
    <input>participantAttributes (string[], required)</input>
    <input>followUpType (enum, required: Pro-Forma + Regards · CTA + Next Steps (Sales) · CTA + Next Steps (Personal) · Warm Introduction Facilitation · Reiteration of Discussion)</input>
    <input>warmthScore (int, required: -1 · 0 · +1)</input>
    <input>familiarityScore (int, required: -1 · 0 · +1)</input>
    <input>objective (string, required)</input>
  </inputs>

  <outputs>
    <output>followUpStrategyObject (structured JSON)</output>
  </outputs>

  <steps>
    <step>
      <title>Transcript Parsing</title>
      <description>
        • Extract all promised deliverables or follow-ups (actor, task, timeline).  
        • Identify resonant personal/professional moments, phrases, or anecdotes.  
        • Flag strategic decisions, tensions, or open threads.  
        • Parse any implicit or explicit CTAs.
      </description>
    </step>

    <step>
      <title>Strategy Mapping</title>
      <description>
        • Align extracted content with declared followUpType and objective.  
        • Adjust granularity and tone suggestions based on participantAttributes.  
        • Map to 5 key fields: summaryRecap, promisedDeliverables, resonantDetails, ctaSuggestions, voiceNotes.
      </description>
    </step>

    <step>
      <title>Output Assembly</title>
      <description>
        • Assemble complete followUpStrategyObject:  
          - Includes echoed metadata fields  
          - Defaults gracefully when a field has no content  
          - Preserves structure for downstream composability
      </description>
    </step>

    <step>
      <title>Double-Check & Integrity Pass</title>
      <description>
        • Confirm all transcript-stated follow-ups are present in promisedDeliverables.  
        • Ensure summaryRecap faithfully reflects meeting's thrust.  
        • Check alignment between objective and CTA plan.  
        • Flag under-extracted fields for user review (e.g., low-confidence or missing).
      </description>
    </step>
  </steps>

  <finalGoal>
    Produce a structured, objective-aligned summary of meeting follow-up content from a transcript and explicit relational tags. This artifact is intended to power downstream stylistic rendering without loss of strategic fidelity.
  </finalGoal>
</functionArtifact>
