# Founder Intake — One-Pager Prompt (v0.1)

You are tuning a hiring rubric for your next founding hire.

Please answer succinctly:
1) Stage & 90-day outcomes (what must be true in 90 days?)
2) Top risks next 6 months (what could sink us?)
3) Must-win strengths (where do we need outsized ability?)
4) Team complement (existing strengths / gaps)
5) Evidence preference (portfolio vs trial vs live interview)
6) AI posture (how important is AI fluency? examples of good/bad)
7) Non-negotiables (anti-signals)

Output requested:
- Tuned weights for each criterion on the chosen rubric (0–1; must sum to ~1)
- Notes on critical gaps driving these weights
- Optional: loop length preferences (live/async minutes)
