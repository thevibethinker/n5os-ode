# AI Fluency Signals — Resume & Artifact Tells (v0.1)

Purpose: Assess implied AI fluency from passive signals before interviews.

Positive indicators
- Clear, role-appropriate structure; concise bullets with consistent tense/voice
- Evidence links with context (repos, case studies) rather than generic claims
- Style consistency across sections (edited beyond raw AI output)
- Explicit notes on prompts, templates, or automations used in portfolio

Negative indicators
- Boilerplate phrasing repeated verbatim across sections
- Formatting artifacts typical of raw AI exports (odd spacing, list styles, capitalization)
- Vague claims with no links, no metrics, or no specifics
- Incoherent prompt-like phrasing inside resume bullets

Recording the signal
- Use scorecard notes (InterviewNote) tagged `implied_ai_fluency` with 1–2 sentences and an artifact link/screenshot when possible.
- This is a signal, not a verdict. Confirm or refute during structured AI-fluency questions.
