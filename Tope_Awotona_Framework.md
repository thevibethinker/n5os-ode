# Tech CEO Worldview Assessment Framework

This framework is designed to decode the operating system of a technology leader by triangulating their public statements, strategic decisions, and digital footprint.

## 1. Core Philosophy (The "Why")
*   **Founding Mythos & Drive:** What is the origin story they tell? Is it rooted in efficiency, democratization, frustration, or vision? (e.g., "scratching your own itch" vs. "changing the world")
*   **Value System:** What virtues do they signal? (e.g., transparency, speed, craft, radical candor, customer obsession)
*   **Techno-Optimism Level:** Where do they sit on the spectrum from "software eats everything" to "human-centric enablement"?

## 2. Strategic Cognition (The "How")
*   **Product vs. Sales DNA:** Do they view the world through the lens of product excellence (builder) or market capture (seller)? *Hypothesis for Tope: Strong Sales DNA.*
*   **Growth Levers:** What do they believe is the primary engine of growth? (PLG, Enterprise Sales, Community, Brand)
*   **Decision-Making Models:** Do they rely on data, intuition, consensus, or first principles? How do they handle failure/pivots?

## 3. Operational Stance (The "What")
*   **Talent & Culture:** How do they define "high performance"? What traits do they hire for? Remote vs. In-person?
*   **Capital Efficiency:** Bootstrapped vs. Venture-backed mentality. (Tope is famous for bootstrapping long-term).
*   **Moat Construction:** How do they articulate their defensibility? (Network effects, switching costs, brand).

## 4. Current Obsessions (The "Now")
*   **Macro View:** How are they reacting to the current tech climate (AI shift, efficiency era)?
*   **Recent Discourse:** What specific topics are they posting/speaking about *right now* on X and in interviews?

## 5. Shadow/Blindspots (The "Inverse")
*   **Contrarian Bets:** What does he believe that few others do?
*   **Criticism Response:** How do they handle pushback or crisis?
