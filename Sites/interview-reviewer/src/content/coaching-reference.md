## General Interview Principles

### Preparation
- Research the company, role, and interviewers
- Prepare specific examples using STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Have questions ready for the interviewer

### Communication
- Listen actively before responding
- Be concise and structured in answers
- Use specific examples rather than generalities
- Acknowledge what you don't know honestly

### Body Language & Presence
- Maintain appropriate eye contact
- Show engagement through posture and nodding
- Manage nervous habits

### Common Pitfalls
- Talking too much or too little
- Being vague instead of specific
- Negativity about past employers
- Failing to ask thoughtful questions
- Not connecting experience to role requirements

### Post-Interview
- Send thank-you note within 24 hours
- Reflect on what went well and what to improve
- Follow up appropriately if no response
