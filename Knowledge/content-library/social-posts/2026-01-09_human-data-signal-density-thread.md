## Context & Strategic Alignment

This thread extends Ali Ansari's thesis about the trillion-dollar human data market by introducing **signal density stratification**:

- **Commodity tier:** Annotation, labeling, basic RLHF feedback
- **Premium tier:** Concentrated metacognition from top performers — how they think when paths aren't obvious

**Careerspan alignment:** This is the exact thesis Careerspan is building toward — capturing high-signal professional narratives that contain concentrated learning signal about problem-solving, negotiation, and stakeholder navigation from proven performers.

**Key positions expressed:**
1. Not all human data has equal learning signal density
2. Premium-tier data = out-of-distribution thinking/frameworks (underrepresented by definition)
3. Top performer success narratives are a critical answer to the signal density problem
4. Meaningful margins will come from structured access to proven judgment
