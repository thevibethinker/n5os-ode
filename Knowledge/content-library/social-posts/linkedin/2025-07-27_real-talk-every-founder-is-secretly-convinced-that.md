# Real talk: Every founder is secretly *convinced* that they're working on a mo...

Real talk: Every founder is secretly *convinced* that they're working on a moonshot. "
""
"As though they're just three pivots away from curing cancer."
""
"You start with an AI-powered to-do list."
" → Add a social graph"
" → Add a blockchain layer"
" → ???"
" → Boom. Oncology, disrupted"
""
"We all know a founder like this."
""
"Hell, most of us *are* this founder at some point."
""
"And sure, most of us are actually just building the 9th CRM for fractional teams."
""
"Or some kind of Slack-integrated Notion for people who hate both Slack and Notion."
""
"But somewhere in that delusion is a kernal of something pure."
""
"The belief that even a silly little productivity app might unlock a future no one--not even we ourselves--could have predicted. "
""
"Because let’s not forget:"
"🎯 Twitter started as a podcasting company"
"🎯 Shopify started as a snowboard shop"
"🎯Airbnb started as an Air-Mattress-as-a-Service"
"💪 And Craigslist… has somehow never pivoted. Because they're legends."
""
"So mock the founder delusion if you want—just don’t forget: Delusion isn't a bug in a founder's OS, it's a feature.
