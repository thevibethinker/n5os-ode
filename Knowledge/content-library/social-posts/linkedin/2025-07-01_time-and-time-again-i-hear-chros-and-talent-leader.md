# Time and time again, I hear CHROs and talent leaders surface this problem on ...

😰 Time and time again, I hear CHROs and talent leaders surface this problem on our calls:"
""
"The already tenuous signal provided by the resume is being diluted further by a deluge of AI resumes."
""
"🤷 Not that the resume told us much to begin with -- let alone was processed on a more meaningful level than "Keyword present Y/N""
""
"The reality: 46% of hires fail by the 18-month-mark. ~89% of the reason they churn is because of a mismatch in factors like coachability, EQ, motivation, etc. "
""
"Only 11% failed because of a "hard skills" mismatch, which is what the resume is best suited to identifying."
""
"😓 My takeaway? We're actually damn good at figuring out if someone can *do* the job. Where hiring really fails is assessing whether said person can do the job *at that org* and *on that team*."
""
