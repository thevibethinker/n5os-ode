# I'll do this one more time for the people in the back.

I'll do this one more time for the people in the back. "
""
"Your resume bullets are probably costing you interviews right now..."
""
"Here's my bulletproof formula for crafting resume bullets that *actually* get noticed:"
""
"1. Impact first → Show exactly HOW you moved the needle ("Achieved 20% revenue increase", "improved user engagement by 12%")"
""
"2. Scale matters → What "level" are you operating at ("assisting C-suite", "supporting 12-person team", "completed in 2 months".)"
""
"3. Skills in action → Don't just list skills, show them in use ("Leveraged interpersonal communication", "Applied conflict resolution techniques")"
""
"4. Numbers, numbers, numbers → Quantify EVERYTHING (See examples from #1)"
""
"Remember: Vague bullets = instant rejection. Be specific or be skipped! 📝
