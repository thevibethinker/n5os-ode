# Personal Library of Alexandria

I'm convinced that as AI becomes better able to manage context/memory, there will be tremendous alpha in constructing your own personal Library of Alexandria.

Just imagine the possibilities—sequestering a highly curated set of knowledge/data/perspectives/etc in one place and then being able to draw from it like a well of infinite wisdom.
