# But Who Replies to the Reply Guy?

But who replies to the Reply Guy? 

Who, in all these years of Twitter, has had the common humanity to engage with the one poaster who needed attention most?

Not the crypto shill with her millions. 

Not the D-tier tech twitter celebrity with his blue check. 

But the Reply Guy—unfollowed and unloved—who sits alone in his notifications at 2 AM waiting to be in someone's mentions. 

He has committed no crime save the great one of having an only above-average opinion and insufficient rizz to overcome it.

He has ratio'd no one, yet is ratio'd by all.
