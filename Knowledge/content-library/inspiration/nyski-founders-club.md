# NYSKI Founders Club Inspiration

This entry captures the design patterns and aesthetic of the NYSKI Founders Club landing page.

## Design Analysis

### Why Inspirational
- **Professional founder aesthetic**: High-end, exclusive feel through dark tones and high-impact typography.
- **Good typography hierarchy**: Clear distinction between massive display headers and clean body text.
- **Effective social proof**: Testimonial integration with group photos builds immediate trust.

### Design Patterns
- **Full-bleed dark hero**: Dark overlay on video background ensures high text contrast and moody atmosphere.
- **Glassmorphism CTA card**: Semi-transparent elements provide depth and modern feel.
- **Clean 3-column grid**: Features are easily scannable through a structured layout.
- **Social proof quote**: Italicized testimonials integrated directly into the flow.
