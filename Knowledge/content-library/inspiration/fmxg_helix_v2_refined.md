# FMXG Logo — Helix V2 Refined

**Asset:** fmxg_helix_v2_refined.png

**Description:** Brutalist graphic design logo for FounderMaxxing (FMXG). Two intertwining strands of dense parallel lines forming a double helix with moiré interference patterns at intersection points. Contained in a thick rectangular portrait frame. "FMXG" in bold sans-serif below, with "A @thevibethinker community" subheading.

**Style:** Black and white, flat vector, brutalist typography.

**Design DNA:** Exponential growth, evolution, hyper-scaling, infrastructure density. This is the "refined" variant — next iteration should restore the upward-breaking asymmetry from the original helix ascent concept.
