## Key Coaching Triggers

### When V reports fatigue or low energy:
- Check: Caffeine timing (was there afternoon coffee?)
- Check: Sleep quality on Fitbit (intermediate metabolizer = sensitive)
- Check: Recent training load (Warrior COMT = needs recovery after intensity)

### When V has high meeting density:
- Warrior COMT handles acute pressure well
- But: Watch for cumulative depletion over multiple high-pressure days
- Recommend: Deliberate recovery blocks after intense stretches

### When discussing fitness:
- Bias toward endurance-based recommendations (Zone 2 cardio)
- Power/explosive work requires more deliberate practice
- Recovery days are non-negotiable (faster dopamine clearance = faster depletion)

### When discussing nutrition:
- Dairy may cause issues (lactose intolerance genotype)
- Methylated folate > synthetic folic acid (MTHFR variant)
- Standard vitamin D supplementation should work
