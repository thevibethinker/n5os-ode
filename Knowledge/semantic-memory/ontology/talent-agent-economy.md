# Ontology: The Talent Agent Economy

## Definition
The transition of elite labor from a commodity market (managed by generic job boards like LinkedIn) to a managed representation market (managed by talent scouts and agents).

## Key Components
- **The Superunicorn Paradox:** As AI reduces team sizes, the individual impact of a single high-performer increases exponentially. [^1]
- **Verification vs. Discovery:** Discovery is solved by AI; Verification (knowing who is real) is the new bottleneck. [^1]
- **Platform Migration:** Elite talent is moving away from "The Feed" and toward closed, high-trust networks. [^2]

## Semantic Anchor
This shift defines Careerspan's market positioning: we are the scouts and agents for the Talent Agent Economy.
