## Synthesis

### Cross-Pattern Observations

1. **Community distribution is the dominant theme** across all GTM meetings
2. **Trust & proof requirements** appear consistently - stakeholders want demonstration before commitment
3. **Integration friction** is a recurring barrier (mentioned by Usha, Sofia, Allie)
4. **Pricing sensitivity** varies by stakeholder type (communities vs. sales prospects)
5. **Candidate signal quality** matters more than volume (soft skills, referrals vs. inbound)

### Recommended Next Steps

1. **Community pilots:** Prioritize low-friction trials with 2-3 communities (Usha, Krista models)
2. **Proof-first approach:** Develop sample shortlist/scan demonstrations vs. heavy sales collateral
3. **Integration strategy:** Default to white-label/API approach vs. ATS integration
4. **Pricing experiments:** Test bounty/warm-intro fees before subscription models
5. **Signal quality:** Build soft-skill and behavioral fit capture into matching
