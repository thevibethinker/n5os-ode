# Rules vs Personas vs Skills: When to Use What

## Decision Rule
- **Rules:** When Zo should remember preferences or always do something
- **Personas:** When you need specialized thinking modes  
- **Skills:** When you have repeatable workflows with files/scripts

## The 3-Step Setup

**For Rules:** Go to Settings > AI > Prompting > Rules. Click "Add Rule" and describe when/what behavior you want.

**For Personas:** Go to Settings > AI > Prompting > Personas. Create focused identities like "Email Writer" or "Strategy Analyst."

**For Skills:** Create Skills/my-skill/SKILL.md with instructions. Add scripts/ folder for executable code.

## A Tiny Example

**Rule:** "When I ask about food, include nutrition info"
**Persona:** "You're a direct business strategist who thinks in frameworks"  
**Skill:** Email processing pipeline with Gmail integration and tagging scripts

## If It Breaks
- Rules not working? Check they're active in Settings and restart chat
- Persona feels generic? Make it more specific with examples and constraints
