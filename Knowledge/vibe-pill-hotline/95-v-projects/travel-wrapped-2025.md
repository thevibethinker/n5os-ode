# Travel Wrapped 2025 — Gmail Travel Infographic

A viral prompt for Zo Computer that generates a personalized 2025 Travel Wrapped infographic from your Gmail data.

## How It Works
1. Zo scans your Gmail for travel-related emails (flights, hotels, bookings)
2. Extracts destinations, dates, and trip details
3. Generates a visual infographic summarizing your year of travel

## Why It Matters
Shows the power of Zo's Gmail integration + image generation working together. A single prompt that produces a shareable result — the kind of thing that makes people go "wait, it can do that?"

## Use Case for Callers
If someone asks "what's a cool first thing to try with Zo" — this is a great answer. Connects Gmail, uses AI analysis, produces a visual output. All from one conversation.
