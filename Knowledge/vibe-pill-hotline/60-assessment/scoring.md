# Assessment Scoring

## Trigger Phrases
- "What does my score mean?"
- "How do I interpret this?"
- "What should I focus on?"

## Scoring Method

After asking the four assessment questions, calculate the average score:

| Answer | Points |
|--------|--------|
| A | 1.0 |
| B | 1.5 |
| C | 2.0 |
| D | 3.0 |

**Total possible range: 1.0 - 3.0**

---

## Score Interpretation

### Score < 1.5: Level 1 Focus

**Profile**: Every conversation starts from zero. No persistent context. Manual everything.

**Diagnosis**: You're working hard, not smart. Each AI interaction reinvents the wheel.

**Priority**: Master Level 1 tactics before anything else.
- Delay the Draft
- Clarification Gates
- Adversarial Probing

**Quick Win**: Next conversation, spend 5 exchanges building context before asking for anything.

---

### Score 1.5 - 2.5: Level 2 Focus

**Profile**: Some Level 1 skills. Ready for persistent context.

**Diagnosis**: You're getting value from AI but context doesn't carry forward. Still too much setup per session.

**Priority**: Build your Level 2 environment.
- Custom instructions
- Memory settings
- At least one persona

**Quick Win**: Set up custom instructions with your top 5 preferences. Use them for a week.

---

### Score ≥ 2.5: Level 3 Ready

**Profile**: Good Level 1 skills. Solid Level 2 environment. Ready for pipelines.

**Diagnosis**: You're getting consistent value. Now it's time to make AI work when you're not there.

**Priority**: Build your first pipeline.
- Connect a data source
- Create a scheduled task
- Automate one repetitive workflow

**Quick Win**: Identify your most repetitive AI task. Build a scheduled workflow to handle it.

---

## Coaching Notes

**Don't skip levels.** Level 3 without Level 2 creates brittle automation. Level 2 without Level 1 skills means you can't diagnose when things go wrong.

**Progress isn't linear.** Someone might have sophisticated Level 1 skills but no Level 2 setup. Meet them where they are.

**The goal is the Meta-OS.** All three levels working together. But it builds one layer at a time.

---

## Follow-Up Questions by Level

**For Level 1 Focus:**
"What's the last thing AI gave you that felt generic? Let's talk about how to prevent that."

**For Level 2 Focus:**
"What do you find yourself explaining to AI repeatedly? That should be in your persistent context."

**For Level 3 Ready:**
"What's a task you do every week that AI could do while you sleep? Let's design that pipeline."
