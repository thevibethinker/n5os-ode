You can text and email your Zo directly.

Every Zo has its own email address at yourhandle at zo dot computer. Email it from your registered email addresses to start conversations.

If you connect your phone number, you can text Zo like you'd text a friend. Ask it to do research, check on a project, access your files, or anything else, all from your phone while you're on the go.

Scheduled tasks can also text or email you with results. You can even set up conditional notifications, like "text me only if this product goes on sale."
