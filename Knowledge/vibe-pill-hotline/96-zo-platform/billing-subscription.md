# Billing and Subscription

Zo uses a subscription plus credits model. Your plan gives you a monthly credit allowance, and you can buy more credits if you need them.

To manage your subscription, go to Billing in Settings. You can see your current plan, change plans, buy credits, view usage, and manage payment methods.

Credits are used for AI responses and tool calls. Different models and tools cost different amounts. You can track exactly what's using your credits in the Usage tab.

If you run out of credits mid-cycle, you can purchase more instantly. Your allowance resets each billing cycle.

Zo also supports gift credits — you can send credit gifts to other Zo users.
