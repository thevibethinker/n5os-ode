Zo gives you access to all the leading AI models. You can switch between models depending on what you're doing.

Language models are used in the chat for conversations, coding, analysis, and tool use. Zo includes models from Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, and other providers. The Zo team evaluates models to offer the best ones.

Zo also has built-in models for image generation, video generation, and audio transcription.

On the free plan, you can use free models only. On paid plans, you get the full model selection. You can also bring your own API keys from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Cerebras, or Groq, which lets you use models at your own cost.
