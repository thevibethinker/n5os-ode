If you're new to Zo, here are the best things to try in order.

First, add some files. Upload documents, notes, or PDFs and chat with Zo about them.

Second, connect an integration. Gmail or Google Calendar are great starting points. Ask Zo to check your schedule or summarize recent emails.

Third, save an article. Send Zo a URL in the chat and it will save the whole page. Great for building a knowledge base.

Fourth, create a scheduled task. Try something simple like a daily text message reminder or a morning news summary.

Fifth, build a site. Ask Zo to create a personal dashboard, a to-do app, or a landing page. It's surprisingly fast.

The pattern with Zo is: the more you use it, the more useful it gets. Your files, integrations, and automations build on each other.
