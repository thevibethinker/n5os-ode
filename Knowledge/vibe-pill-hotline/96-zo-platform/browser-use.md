Zo has its own web browser. You can access it from Settings, then Integrations.

The browser lets Zo view and interact with websites. You can sign into sites using Zo's browser to give your AI access. Once logged in, Zo can browse and interact with those sites on your behalf.

For example, you could sign into X, formerly Twitter, and ask Zo to summarize your feed. Or sign into a work tool and have Zo pull data from it.

Zo can also browse the web without being logged in. It can search the web, read articles, search Google Maps, search Twitter, find research papers, and more, all through built-in tools.
