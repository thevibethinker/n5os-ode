# Quick Wins by Level

## Level 1: Conversation Tactics (Start This Week)

**Delay the Draft (5 min setup)**
- Add "Ask me 5 clarifying questions first" to your next AI request
- Notice the quality difference immediately

**Adversarial Probing (No setup)**
- After AI gives you output, ask: "What are the 3 biggest weaknesses?"
- Use this as your new standard practice

**Threshold Rubric (Next conversation)**
- Try: "Don't recommend anything until you understand my budget, timeline, and constraints"
- Watch AI become more thorough

## Level 2: Environment Setup (This Month)

**Custom Instructions (15 min setup)**
- ChatGPT: Add 3 preferences in Custom Instructions  
- Claude: Create a project with your work context
- Zo: Add rules for how you want AI to behave

**Memory Building (Ongoing)**
- Tell AI to remember key facts about your role, industry, preferences
- Ask weekly: "What do you remember about my situation?"

**First Persona (30 min)**
- Create a "Teacher" persona that explains things at your level
- Use it next time you need to understand a complex concept

## Level 3: Pipeline Beginnings (Next Quarter)

**Automated Report (Start simple)**
- Set up one scheduled task that runs weekly
- Example: "Summarize my calendar for next week every Sunday"

**Data Import (Pick one source)**
- Connect one regular data source to your AI environment
- Start with calendar or email integration

**Building Block Hunt (Explore)**
- Find one GitHub repo or template relevant to your work
- Adapt it for your specific needs

## The Progression Strategy

Start with Level 1 tactics this week. Master them before moving to Level 2 setup.

Each level builds on the previous one. Level 3 pipelines work best when you have Level 2 personalization and Level 1 conversation skills.

## Success Metrics

**Level 1:** AI asks better questions and gives more relevant answers
**Level 2:** You stop re-explaining yourself every conversation  
**Level 3:** Systems work when you're not actively managing them

## Time Investment

**Level 1:** Minutes per conversation (immediate ROI)
**Level 2:** Hours of setup (pays dividends for months)
**Level 3:** Days of building (transforms how you work)

The Meta-OS emerges from sustained attention across all three levels.
