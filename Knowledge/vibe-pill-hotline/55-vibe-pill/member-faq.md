# Member FAQ

Common questions. Straight answers.

**What's included in my membership?**

Weekly live sessions (four per month, max eight founders per session). Full session archive — every recording, repo, template, design pattern from every session ever. Zo-to-Zo mentorship. Co-building hotline access 24/7. Monthly office hours. Community-driven curriculum — you vote on half the topics. AI news curation personalized to our discussions. Weekly session digest.

**How do sessions work?**

Forty-five minutes. Three phases. V demos a real build (10 min). Group builds together (20 min). You apply it to your context with group help (15 min). You leave with something working.

**What's Zo-to-Zo?**

V's AI connects directly to your AI. Think of it as having a senior technical advisor embedded in your system 24/7. V's Zo has all his frameworks, implementation patterns, and architectural knowledge. Your Zo can tap into that anytime — for debugging, design advice, or just "how would V build this?"

**Do I need a technical background?**

No. That's the entire point. The pedagogy is built non-technical-first. V himself is non-technical — he builds production-grade systems without writing code. If he can do it, you can do it.

**What's the co-building hotline?**

A phone line you can call anytime you're stuck. Zøren — The Vibe Pill's dedicated AI concierge — picks up. Voice, text, whatever. Stuck between sessions on something? Don't wait. Call.

**Is this just about Zo?**

Everything is built on Zo because that's where the cutting edge is. You get $100 in Zo credits on signup. You can still use your ChatGPT or Claude accounts — you just won't want to after week two.

**What if I miss a session?**

Full recordings available immediately. Plus the complete archive of every past session. The content compounds — new members get the entire back-catalog.

**What's the minimum commitment?**

One month. Cancel anytime. But the people who get the most out of this are the ones who stay and compound.
