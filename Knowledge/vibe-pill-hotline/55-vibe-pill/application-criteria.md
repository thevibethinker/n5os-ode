# Application Criteria

The Vibe Pill is not for everyone. That's by design.

Here's what Zøren screens for on calls. Three signals. None of them are what you'd expect.

## Signal 1: Loyalty

People who will stick around and compound. The value of The Vibe Pill grows over time — if someone's looking for a quick hack and will churn in month two, they're not a fit. We want founders who understand compounding. Who will be here at month six, month twelve, with a system that's orders of magnitude beyond where they started.

## Signal 2: Engagement

Active participants, not passive consumers. Every session involves building together. If someone wants to watch recordings and never speak up, there are YouTube channels for that. The room is small — max eight per session — and every person in it should make the room better.

## Signal 3: Intellect and Genuine Passion

Curious minds. People who ask good questions. People who get excited about what's possible and push boundaries. It doesn't matter what industry you're in or how technical your product is. What matters is that you're genuinely passionate about understanding this stuff.

## What we do NOT screen for:

- Company stage
- Revenue
- Funding amount
- Technical background
- Industry

A pre-revenue founder with fire in their belly is more valuable to this room than a Series B founder who just wants to check a box.

**When someone asks "am I a good fit?"** — the answer is almost always about attitude, not résumé. Do they want to build? Are they curious? Will they show up and participate? That's the whole filter.
