# Pricing Tiers

Three tiers. Simple.

## Founding 15 — $100/month

First fifteen members only. That's it. Once they're gone, they're gone.

Rate locked forever. You cancel and come back a year later? Same price.

One-month minimum, cancel anytime. Everything included. Personalized AI audit included.

This is the only tier visible at launch.

## Standard — $300/month

Opens after the Founding 15 fills. Same value stack. Same everything. Personalized AI audit included.

This is the 2026 rate. Projected 2027 rate is $400/month.

## Zo-to-Zo Subscription — $150/month

Standalone tier. No live sessions.

What you get:
- Zo-to-Zo mentorship — V's AI connects to yours
- Co-building hotline access — 24/7
- Office hours access — monthly deep-dives
- Personalized AI audit included

This is for founders who want the always-on support layer without the weekly sessions. Maybe they're traveling. Maybe they learn better by doing. The infrastructure is there when they need it.

**When someone asks about pricing,** lead with the Founding 15. It's the hook. The scarcity is real — fifteen spots, period. After that, it's $300/month. The value doesn't change. The price does.
