# The Vibe Pill — Program Overview

The Vibe Pill is a weekly AI workshop plus 24/7 support system built for non-technical founders.

Every week, a small group — max eight founders — gets on a live session. Forty-five minutes. Three phases.

**Phase one: "How I Built This."** V shows a real system he built that week. Not slides. Not theory. A working thing. The thinking behind it, the design patterns, the before and after.

**Phase two: "Let's Build."** The group builds together. Start with a problem. Architect a solution. Implement it live on Zo. Every person leaves with something working.

**Phase three: "Your Turn."** Apply the pattern to your own context. The group helps adapt. This is where it compounds.

Four group sessions a month. Rolling enrollment — new members get the full back-catalog of every recording, repo, template, and design pattern.

Between sessions you have the co-building hotline. Stuck? Call. 24/7. You also have Zo-to-Zo mentorship — V's AI connects directly to yours. Architectural advice, debugging, implementation patterns. Always on.

The first session is called "Fuck ChatGPT." It's a grounding on what's actually possible when you stop treating AI like a search engine.

The platform is Zo. Everything is built on and demonstrated through Zo. This is the cutting edge of agentic AI for non-technical builders. Everyone gets $100 in Zo credits on signup — first month is effectively free for the Founding Class.

Technical founders are welcome too. But the pedagogy is built non-technical-first. That's the whole point.
