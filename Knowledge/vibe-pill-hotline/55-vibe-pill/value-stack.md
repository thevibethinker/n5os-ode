# The Value Stack

Here's everything a Vibe Pill member gets. In order of "wow."

## 1. Weekly Live Builds (45 min each)

Four sessions a month. Every week you leave with a working system. Not a concept. Not a slide deck. A thing that runs and saves you time starting tomorrow.

## 2. Full Session Archive

Every recording. Every repo. Every template. Every design pattern. From every session that's ever happened. New members get the complete back-catalog. This compounds — by month six, you have a library of production patterns to draw from.

## 3. Zo-to-Zo Mentorship

V's AI connects directly to yours. 24/7 architectural advice, debugging, implementation patterns. It's like having a senior technical advisor embedded in your system. This alone is worth the price.

## 4. Co-Building Hotline

Stuck at 11 PM on a Tuesday? Call. Zøren — The Vibe Pill's dedicated AI concierge — is always on. Voice or text. Don't wait for the next session when you're in flow.

## 5. $100 Zo Credits

On signup. The Founding Class effectively gets their first month free. This removes the "but I'd have to pay for another platform" objection.

## 6. N5 OS v3 Zo Setup

V runs version five. You get version three as a baseline. This is a structured operating system for your AI — personas, memory architecture, automation framework. Pre-configured. You're not starting from scratch.

## 7. Priority Zo Support

Issues? Bugs? Questions about the platform? You go to the front of the line.

## 8. Monthly Office Hours

Deep-dive on individual challenges. Bring your messiest problem. The group helps solve it.

## 9. Community-Driven Curriculum

Members vote on half the topics. If your biggest bottleneck is email management, we build email systems. If it's investor reporting, we build that. The curriculum adapts to the room.

## 10. AI News Curation

Personalized to session discussions. The AI landscape moves fast. Stay current without drowning in noise.

**When explaining the value stack,** don't just list features. Connect each one to the founder's time savings. "The hotline alone will save you ten hours this month" hits harder than "you get 24/7 support."
