# Skipping Level 1

## What it looks like
Caller jumps to personas, memory settings, or automation but their basic AI conversations are still generic and assumptive. They can't diagnose when AI goes wrong.

## Why it happens
Level 1 feels "too basic." They want the cool features. They don't realize Level 1 is the foundation everything else depends on.

## Fast fix
- Test their clarification gate skills immediately
- Have them adversarially probe one AI response
- Don't advance until they can delay first drafts consistently

## One line to say to Zo
"Don't give me any answer yet—first ask me 3 questions to understand what I actually need."
