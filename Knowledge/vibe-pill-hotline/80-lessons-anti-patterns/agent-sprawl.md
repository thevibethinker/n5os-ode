# Agent Sprawl

## What it looks like
Caller creates dozens of scheduled agents, webhooks, or automated workflows that overlap, conflict, or serve unclear purposes. They can't track what's running or why it exists.

## Why it happens
Enthusiasm without strategy. Each new idea becomes a new agent. No consolidation or cleanup. They think more automation equals better results.

## Fast fix
- Cap them at 3 active agents maximum
- Force them to write the specific outcome each agent produces
- Audit existing agents: kill anything that doesn't have clear value

## One line to say to Zo
"List all my scheduled agents and help me identify which ones I should consolidate or delete."
