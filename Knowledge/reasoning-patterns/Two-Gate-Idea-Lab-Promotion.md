# Two-Gate Idea Lab Promotion (Reasoning Pattern)

## When to use
When you want **very low friction idea capture**, but you *don’t* want to accidentally commit to deep work.

## Core move
Separate the workflow into two different “gates”:

### Gate 1 — Capture (always open)
- Log the idea quickly (minimal required fields).
- Do **not** open a big exploration thread.

### Gate 2 — Promotion (manual)
Promotion requires an explicit decision and is triggered only when at least one of these is true:
- The idea is **time-sensitive**
- The idea is **high leverage**
- The idea is **mentally sticky** (recurs / distracts)

## Decision shortcut (5-question triage)
Ask:
1. Leverage
2. Time sensitivity
3. Confusion-to-clarity gap
4. Emotional charge / stickiness
5. 3-minute next action exists

Rule of thumb:
- ≥3 “yes” → promote now
- 1–2 “yes” → add to triage
- 0 “yes” → log only

## Why it works
- Prevents “everything becomes a project.”
- Keeps your ideation system **high throughput**.
- Creates a clean moment for deeper reasoning modes (Socratic / Adversarial / First Principles).
