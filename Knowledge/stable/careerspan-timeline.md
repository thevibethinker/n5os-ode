# Careerspan Timeline

## 2023: The ApplyAI Launch
Initial market entry with focus on story-to-resume and interview asset transformation, establishing foundational technology and early market traction. Founding by Vrijen Attawar and Logan Currie, driven by personal career change frustrations and lack of affordable coaching.

## Early 2024: Rebrand to Careerspan
Strategic rebrand from The ApplyAI to Careerspan to reflect expanded vision toward comprehensive career companion and dynamic "3D résumé" platform. $1M pre-seed funding; introduction of continuous profile features.

## 2025: B2B2C Integrations and Pilots
Implementation of B2B2C partnership integrations and pilot programs, with internal data demonstrating improved candidate-opportunity fit validation. Partnerships with Startery (enriched profiles for job applicants) and humanUPtions (university integrations).

## September 2025: GTM Pivot to Career Agent Wedge
Strategic go-to-market shift focusing on elite candidate supply model: free shortlists → pay-per-intro → subscription pipeline, with initial beachhead in Product Management roles at Series A/B startups. Driven by runway concerns; phases include seeding 20-30 PM candidates, engaging Series A/B employers, delivering value with guarantees.
