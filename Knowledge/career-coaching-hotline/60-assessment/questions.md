## Trigger Phrases

- "I don't know where to start"
- "Am I ready to apply for jobs?"
- "How do I know if I'm prepared?"
- "I've been applying but nothing's working"
- "What should I be doing right now?"
- "I feel stuck in my job search"

## Career Stage Diagnostic

This quick assessment identifies where you are in your job search readiness. Answer honestly — there's no judgment here, just clarity.

### Q1: Groundwork — Do you know your story?

**Can you describe three specific professional achievements with measurable results?**

- A) Yes, I have a documented bank of 10+ stories with metrics
- B) I can think of a few, but they're not written down
- C) I struggle to remember specific examples
- D) I haven't thought about this at all

### Q2: Materials — Are you tailored or generic?

**How customized are your resume and cover letter for each application?**

- A) I rewrite key sections for every role based on job requirements
- B) I swap out a few bullet points here and there
- C) I use the same resume for everything
- D) I haven't updated my resume in over a year

### Q3: Networking — Are you in the conversation?

**In the past month, how many meaningful professional conversations have you had?**

- A) 5+ conversations with people in my target field
- B) 2-4 conversations, mostly with existing contacts
- C) 1 or none — networking feels awkward
- D) I don't network; I just apply online

### Q4: Interview Readiness — Can you perform under pressure?

**Have you practiced answering behavioral questions out loud with another person?**

- A) Yes, multiple mock interviews with structured feedback
- B) I've talked through answers with a friend once or twice
- C) I practice in my head but haven't said answers aloud
- D) I wing it — preparation feels unnatural

## Scoring

**Count your answers:**

| Score | Level | What It Means |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| Mostly A's | **Ready** | You're prepared. Focus on volume and targeting. |
| Mostly B's | **Almost There** | Foundation exists but needs sharpening. Two weeks of focused work. |
| Mostly C's | **Building** | Significant gaps. Dedicate 4-6 weeks to preparation before applying heavily. |
| Mostly D's | **Starting** | You're not ready yet — and that's okay. Rushing now will waste opportunities. |

## What You Can Do Right Now

1. **If you scored C's or D's on Q1:** Block 30 minutes today to write down three work stories using the format: Challenge → Action → Result.

2. **If you scored low on Q3:** Message one former colleague this week. Not to ask for a job — just to reconnect.

3. **If Q4 is your weakness:** Say your answer to "Tell me about yourself" out loud right now. Hearing yourself matters more than thinking it through.
