## Using AI to Improve Your LinkedIn Profile

AI is a powerful tool for LinkedIn optimization — but only if you feed it the right inputs. You are the world authority on yourself. The AI just helps you articulate it.

## The Four-Step Prompting Approach

**Step 1: Load the Context.** Give the AI your current LinkedIn section, relevant resume bullets, and context about the scale of your work. Tell it to digest everything before proceeding.

**Step 2: Get a Critique.** Ask what's working and what isn't. What should you say? What should you cut? How will it read to a hiring manager?

**Step 3: Generate with Structure.** Have it create a new section using this framework:
- Who you helped and what you helped them with
- What was at stake (scale and consequences)
- Your impact with quantified results

**Step 4: Iterate.** Prompt and edit until it sounds like you. The AI drafts; you decide.

## What You Can Do Right Now

1. Copy your current LinkedIn work experience into an AI chat
2. Add your resume bullets and any context about scope or scale
3. Ask for a critique before asking for a rewrite
4. Use the who/stakes/impact structure for your revised section
