# The Four-Dimension Diagnostic

## Diagnosing Career Search Challenges

When a job search is stalling, the problem falls into one or more of four dimensions. Identifying the right dimension prevents wasting effort on the wrong solution.

## Dimension 1: Signal Strength

**Question:** Is your professional value clearly communicated?

- Can you articulate what you do and why it matters in 30 seconds?
- Does your resume demonstrate impact, not just responsibilities?
- Does your LinkedIn profile surface in relevant recruiter searches?
- Can you tell compelling stories about your accomplishments?

**If weak:** Return to self-reflection and materials. The issue is messaging, not effort.

## Dimension 2: System Fluency

**Question:** Do you understand how hiring actually works?

- Do you know how ATS filters applications?
- Do you understand recruiter incentives and workflows?
- Are you aware of the hidden job market and how to access it?
- Do you know what happens to your application after you click submit?

**If weak:** Education is the fix. Understanding the system changes how you interact with it.

## Dimension 3: Execution

**Question:** Are you putting in consistent, strategic effort?

- How many applications are you sending per week?
- How many networking conversations are you having?
- Are you following up systematically?
- Are you tracking your pipeline?

**If weak:** Build structure and accountability. Set weekly targets and track them.

## Dimension 4: Intelligence

**Question:** Are you targeting the right opportunities with the right information?

- Do you know which companies are hiring in your space?
- Do you understand compensation benchmarks for your target roles?
- Are you connected to people inside your target companies?
- Do you know what skills are in demand versus declining?

**If weak:** Invest in research and networking. Intelligence gaps lead to misdirected effort.
