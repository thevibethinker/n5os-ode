## What Careerspan Is

Careerspan is expert career coaching for white-collar professionals who are serious about landing the right role—not just any role.

Here's the reality: only 27% of job seekers secure a good position before they need one. Half of graduates end up underemployed. The average job posting gets 250 applications. Generic career services can't give you the honest, high-dosage support you need to stand out.

That's where Careerspan comes in.

## What We Do

Careerspan provides both **tactical** and **strategic** career development:

**Tactical (the nuts and bolts):**
- Resume review and optimization
- Cover letter development
- LinkedIn profile overhaul
- Interview preparation and mock sessions

**Strategic (the bigger picture):**
- Career positioning and narrative development
- Target role identification
- Job search strategy
- Negotiation guidance

This isn't a one-size-fits-all workshop. It's personalized, hands-on coaching that treats your career like the serious business it is.

## Why Careerspan Is Different

Most career services are "muzzled"—they can't tell you the hard truths because institutions don't want complaints. They give you generic advice and wish you luck.

Careerspan operates differently:

- **Honest feedback.** If your resume isn't working, I'll tell you why—specifically.
- **High-dosage support.** Real career transformation requires sustained, intensive coaching, not a 30-minute appointment.
- **Actual hiring experience.** The advice comes from someone who understands what hiring managers actually look for.

Research shows that hiring a skilled career professional can increase your chances of landing a job by 32%. That's not magic—that's what happens when someone helps you present your best self.

## What You Can Do Right Now

1. **Assess your materials honestly.** When's the last time your resume got results? If it's been quiet, something's not working.

2. **Consider whether you need personalized help.** If you've been applying without traction, generic advice isn't the answer.

3. **Ask about Careerspan coaching.** If you want hands-on, expert support to get unstuck, this is what we do.
