## Core Methodology (Verified)

### What We Do
AI-powered career coaching that helps candidates:
1. Reflect on experiences through guided Q&A
2. Identify strengths and accomplishments they often overlook
3. Translate insights into résumés, cover letters, interview responses
4. Build a "living portfolio" that grows over time

### Key Differentiators
1. **Candidate-first philosophy** — Pro-candidate vs. employer-first approach
2. **Story-based profiling** — Captures qualitative, narrative data (not just keywords)
3. **Continuous engagement** — "Career companion" relationship, not transactional
4. **Role decomposition** — Breaks roles into individual competencies, maps candidate stories to gaps

### Technical Approach
- **Foundation:** Built on GPT-4 (OpenAI)
- **Architecture:** Multi-agent system with specialized agents for input processing, evaluation, direction, extraction, quality control
- **AISS Framework:** Action-Impact-Scale-Skill — proprietary structure for resume bullets
- **Semantic Optimization:** Context-aware document optimization beyond keyword matching

### Methodology Origin
Built on 20+ years of professional coaching experience:
- Thousands of coaching sessions across all career stages
- Professional narrative expertise
- Structured coaching frameworks
- Evidence-based interventions for imposter syndrome, career transitions

### Build vs Partner Argument
**Verified claim:** The complexity of career coaching at scale is in the methodology, not just the AI. Role decomposition, competency extraction, and story-based assessment require deep expertise in both technology and career development.

**What NOT to claim:** Do not invent specific timelines (e.g., "18+ months to build") without source.

### Key Competitors (Positioning Against)
| Alternative | Our Differentiation |
|------------|---------------------|
| Job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed) | Pro-candidate vs. employer-first; match quality vs. volume |
| Assessments | Helping IN vs. screening OUT |
