## Core Product

### Virtual Recruiter Model
**What it is:** AI-mediated platform that builds richer candidate profiles for better matching

**Key differentiator:** "People and fit, not just keywords" [FROM THERESA MEETING]

**How it works:**
1. Candidates engage with AI coaching to build detailed profiles
2. System captures beyond-resume context (motivations, values, fit factors)
3. Warm introductions to employers based on genuine fit
4. Careerspan vouches for candidates (trust layer)

## Product Positioning

### Primary Use Cases
1. **Job seekers finding mission-aligned roles**
   - Target: Career changers, values-driven professionals
   - Pain point: Job boards focus on keywords, not fit
   - Solution: AI coaching builds richer picture

2. **Employers finding quality candidates**
   - Target: Startups, mission-driven orgs, hard-to-fill roles
   - Pain point: High volume, low signal; cultural fit hard to assess
   - Solution: Warm intros with Careerspan vouching

3. **Communities monetizing member career services**
   - Target: Micro-communities, professional networks
   - Pain point: Career support is valuable but hard to deliver at scale
   - Solution: White-label access as premium perk
