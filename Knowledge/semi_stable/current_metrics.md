## November 2024 Metrics Update

**Updated:** November 4, 2025  
**Source:** Strategic Intelligence Report (con_kSq36OtO8rmECYtW)  
**Confidence:** High

### Platform Engagement (Product Moat Evidence)

**Multi-session engagement:**
- **50%+ of users** return for multiple coaching sessions
- Industry context: Most career tools see <10% return rate
- **Significance:** Demonstrates sustained value delivery, not just curiosity-driven one-time use

**Day-30 Retention:**
- **20% D30 retention rate**
- Benchmark: 2x industry average for career tools (typically ~10%)
- **Significance:** Indicates product-market fit for coaching value proposition

**Session depth:**
- Multi-turn conversations (not just single-query interactions)
- Users engaging with follow-up coaching after initial sessions
- **Significance:** Captures longitudinal behavioral data (the data moat)

**See:** Knowledge/stable/company/overview.md (Three-Layer Moat section) for strategic implications

### Employer Pipeline (Network Moat Evidence)

**Current pipeline:**
- **~12 employers** actively in pipeline (as of Sep 2025)
- Mix of direct employer relationships and community partnerships
- **Significance:** Demonstrates demand-side validation

**Partnership traction:**
- Remotely Good partnership closed (Sep 2025)
- Revenue share model accepted without pushback
- White-label career services positioning resonates with communities

**See:** Knowledge/stakeholder_research/shrm_application_2024.md for partnership strategy

### Market Position Context

**Competitive benchmarks:**
- LinkedIn Learning: ~5% course completion rate
- Job boards (Indeed, ZipRecruiter): <5% return visit rate after initial search
- Career assessment tools: Typically one-and-done usage
- **Careerspan: 20% D30 retention = 4x job boards, 2x career tools**

**Data moat validation:**
- High engagement = sustained behavioral signal capture
- Competitors cannot replicate without similar engagement mechanics
- Longitudinal data advantage compounds over time

**See:** Knowledge/market_intelligence/competitive_landscape_2024.md for full competitive analysis

### Strategic Metrics to Track

**Product moat health:**
- [ ] Weekly active users (WAU)
- [ ] Session frequency (sessions per active user)
- [ ] Session depth (turns per conversation)
- [ ] Multi-session rate (% returning for 2+ sessions)
- [ ] D7, D30, D90 retention curves

**Data moat growth:**
- [ ] Total coaching conversations logged
- [ ] Average conversations per user
- [ ] Behavioral signal diversity (communication, learning agility, persistence)
- [ ] Longitudinal profile depth (users with 30+ day history)

**Network moat momentum:**
- [ ] Employer pipeline size and velocity
- [ ] Placement conversion rates
- [ ] Employer repeat hire rate
- [ ] Revenue per employer (LTV)
- [ ] Candidate→job matching hit rate

### Implications for Go-to-Market

**What these metrics enable:**

**For fundraising:**
- "20% D30 retention (2x industry average)" = product-market fit signal
- "50%+ multi-session engagement" = data moat viability proof
- "~12 employer pipeline" = demand-side validation

**For partnerships:**
- High engagement = valuable talent pipeline for partners
- Sustained usage = members actually use the benefit (not shelfware)
- Behavioral data = differentiated candidate profiles

**For employer sales:**
- Engaged candidates = higher quality, lower flake rate
- Multi-session users = demonstrated seriousness and self-awareness
- Longitudinal data = predictive signals competitors don't have
