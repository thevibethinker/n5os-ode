# Personalization: Make AI Know You

## The Breakthrough

Instead of re-explaining yourself every conversation, encode your preferences once. AI should know you before you start typing.

This is the difference between Level 1 (ephemeral) and Level 2 (persistent).

## Where to Set This Up

**ChatGPT:** Custom Instructions (settings menu)
**Claude:** Project files or system prompts  
**Zo Computer:** Bio, rules, and preferences
**Other platforms:** Look for "custom instructions" or "system prompts"

## What to Personalize

**Communication Style:**
- "I prefer bullet points over paragraphs"
- "Always ask clarifying questions for major work"
- "Be concise—I don't need elaborate explanations"

**Domain Context:**
- "I work in fintech"
- "I'm a non-technical founder"
- "I manage a team of 12"

**Quality Standards:**
- "If you're uncertain, ask rather than guess"
- "Never fabricate information"
- "Report honest progress, not false completion"

## Trigger Phrases Callers Use

- "I have to re-explain myself every time"
- "AI doesn't remember my preferences"
- "I want consistent results"
- "Can AI learn my style?"

## The Compound Effect

Each preference you set compounds with the others. Over time, AI develops a remarkably accurate model of how you work.

This isn't just convenience—it's productivity infrastructure.

## Next Steps

Pick one platform. Add three personalization rules. Notice how conversations immediately improve.
