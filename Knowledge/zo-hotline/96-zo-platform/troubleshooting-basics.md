If your Zo seems slow to start, it might be sleeping. On the free plan, your computer goes to sleep when not in use. Just wait for it to boot up.

If a scheduled task isn't working as expected, check the task's execution history in the Scheduled Tasks page. You can see what Zo did and refine the instructions.

If a site isn't loading, check the Services tab to make sure it's running. You can also ask Zo to diagnose the issue.

If an integration isn't working, try disconnecting and reconnecting it from Settings, then Integrations.

For any issue, you can reach out to the Zo team at support dot zocomputer dot com, email help at zocomputer dot com, or join the Discord community at discord dot gg slash zocomputer. The team also hosts regular office hours on Luma.
