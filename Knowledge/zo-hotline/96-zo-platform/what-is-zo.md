Zo Computer is a personal cloud computer powered by AI. Think of it as your own server in the cloud that's always on, always connected, and has a built-in AI assistant.

Unlike ChatGPT or Claude, which are just chat interfaces, Zo gives you a full computer. Your files, your AI, your code, and your hosting all live together in one place you control. It's like having a really smart assistant who also has their own office, filing cabinet, and workshop.

The tagline is "AI Companion for 24/7 Execution." That means Zo doesn't just answer questions. It can work while you sleep. It can send you text messages. It can check websites, send emails, build tools, and run automations, all without you being there.

Every user gets their own personal server with 100 gigabytes of storage, even on the free plan. You access Zo through a web app, a desktop app for Mac or Windows, or by texting and emailing your Zo directly.
