People often ask how Zo compares to other tools. Here are the key differences.

Versus ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini: those are chat interfaces. Zo is a full computer with files, hosting, automations, and integrations. Zo can also use those same AI models.

Versus Zapier or n8n: those are visual automation builders. Zo lets you build automations using natural language. You can actually install n8n on your Zo if you want both.

Versus Lovable, Replit, or Bolt: those are vibe-coding tools focused on websites. Zo is a general-purpose computing environment where sites are just one thing you can build. Your sites can interact with your files and AI.

Versus Notion or Obsidian: Zo is more powerful because it's a full server. You can run code, host services, and build automations on top of your notes. Zo also connects to Notion and supports syncing Obsidian via file sync.

The short version: Zo is the only platform where your AI, your data, your code, and your hosting all work together in one place.
