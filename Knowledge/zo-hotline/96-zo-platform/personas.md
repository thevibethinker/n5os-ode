Personas let you toggle between different styles or behaviors when working with Zo.

A persona is like a hat your AI wears. When you switch personas, Zo's behavior changes based on that persona's instructions.

Common examples include a tutor persona that teaches you in a specific way, a writer persona that matches a particular writing style, or a coder persona that focuses on technical implementation.

You can switch personas anytime. Each persona has a prompt that provides context and instructions that Zo follows while that persona is active.
