# Your Bio

Your bio tells Zo who you are. It's a short description that gets included in every conversation, helping Zo personalize responses to your background, expertise, and preferences.

Go to Settings, then Advanced to edit your bio. Write it in third person — describe yourself, your work, what you're interested in, and how you want Zo to interact with you.

A good bio helps Zo calibrate technical depth, tone, and the kinds of examples it uses. It's one of the simplest and most impactful ways to customize your Zo experience.

Think of it as a permanent context note. Every time Zo responds, it reads your bio first.
