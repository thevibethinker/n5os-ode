# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions people ask about Zo:

What is Zo? It's a personal AI computer — a cloud server with a full operating system, AI assistant, and all your tools connected in one place.

Is there a free plan? Yes. The free plan includes limited credits per month, enough to try things out.

What AI models does Zo use? Multiple models from Anthropic, OpenAI, and others. You can also bring your own API keys.

Can I use it on my phone? Yes, through the web app, SMS, or email. There's also a desktop app for Mac and Windows.

Is my data private? Yes. Each user gets their own isolated server. Zo doesn't train on your data.

How is it different from ChatGPT? Zo is a full computer, not just a chatbot. It can run code, manage files, host websites, connect to your apps, and automate tasks.

Where do I get help? Discord community at discord.gg/zocomputer, email help@zocomputer.com, or weekly office hours on Luma.
