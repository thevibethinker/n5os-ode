# Zo Gifts

You can gift Zo credits to friends, family, or even a random Zo user. Gifts require a paid plan.

Go to the Gifts tab in Billing. Choose an amount between five and one hundred dollars, add an optional message, then pick delivery: create a shareable code, send to a specific email, or surprise a random user. You can send anonymously.

To claim a gift, enter the code in the Gifts tab. Gift codes look like ZO-XX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. Credits get added to your account immediately.
