Zo connects to many apps and services out of the box. Here's the full list of built-in integrations.

Gmail: Zo can read, search, and send emails from your Gmail account. Your Zo also has its own email address at yourhandle at zo dot computer.

Google Calendar: Zo can view, create, and edit calendar events.

Google Drive: Zo can search, read, and upload files to your Drive.

Notion: Zo can search, read, and create documents in Notion.

Linear: Zo can search, read, and create issues in Linear for project management.

Airtable: Zo can search, read, and create records in Airtable.

Dropbox: Zo can search, read, and upload files to Dropbox.

Spotify: Zo can search and manage your Spotify music library.

Stripe: Zo can create products, payment links, and manage orders for selling things.

Each integration supports either Read Only or Read and Write permissions, and you can connect multiple accounts for each service. For example, both your personal and work Gmail.

If there's a service Zo doesn't integrate with natively, you can ask Zo to build a custom integration. Zo can write code and set up API connections for almost anything.
