Zo integrates with Stripe so you can sell products, services, or subscriptions directly.

Connect your Stripe account in the Sell tab. Once onboarded, you can create products with payment links that you share with customers.

Zo takes zero percent of your revenue. Only standard Stripe processing fees apply.

You can manage products and orders in Zo's interface or directly in the Stripe dashboard. Both stay in sync. You can also ask Zo to create products and manage sales through the chat.

This works especially well combined with Zo sites. You can build a storefront site and integrate payment links directly.
