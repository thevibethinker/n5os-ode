Your data lives on your own personal server. Zo uses a unique architecture where your files, AI memories, and internal services all run on your dedicated server.

Zo intentionally uses non-proprietary file formats and open-source technologies. Your data isn't locked into a custom format.

Server locations are generally in the USA and will be placed closer to your location in the future.

You can download your entire computer as a container image if you want a local backup.
