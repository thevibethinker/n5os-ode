Zo has desktop apps for Mac and Windows. They include all the same functionality as the web app, plus file syncing.

File syncing lets you choose any folder on your computer and keep it automatically in sync with your Zo workspace. This means files on your laptop and files on your Zo server stay up to date with each other.

This is useful for integrating with local apps. For example, you could sync your Obsidian notes folder to your Zo, giving your AI access to all your notes.

Download the Mac app from the Zo GitHub releases page or the Windows installer from the same location.
