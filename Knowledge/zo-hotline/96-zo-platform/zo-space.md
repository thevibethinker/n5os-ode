Zo Space is your personal domain at yourhandle dot zo dot space. It's a lightweight way to put things on the internet without setting up a full project.

You can build landing pages, one-off widgets and tools, API endpoints for webhooks, and small interactive apps.

Just ask Zo and it's live. For example, say "make my zo space home page a landing page with my name and links." Zo handles the code, deployment, and URL.

Space supports two types of routes. Pages are React components that can be interactive. APIs are backend endpoints that return data. Pages are private by default but can be made public. APIs are always public.

The difference between Sites and Space is scope. Sites are full projects with their own folders and build systems. Space is for quick, focused creations that don't need all that overhead.
