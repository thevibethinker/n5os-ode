Zo has four plans. Free, Basic, Pro, and Ultra.

The Free plan costs nothing. You get limited daily AI usage with free models only, 100 gigs of storage, and 1 hosted service. Your computer goes to sleep when not in use, so there may be a boot-up delay.

Basic is 18 dollars a month. You get full model selection, 10 dollars in monthly AI credits, always-on availability, 5 hosted services, custom domains, 4 CPU cores, and 32 gigs of RAM.

Pro is 64 dollars a month. You get 40 dollars in AI credits, 10 hosted services, 16 CPU cores, 128 gigs of RAM, and priority support.

Ultra is 200 dollars a month. You get 100 dollars in AI credits, 50 hosted services, 64 CPU cores, 512 gigs of RAM, and priority support.

AI credits are charged at cost, the same rates Zo pays the AI providers. You can top up credits anytime or let overage charge to your card. Paid users can also bring their own API keys from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Cerebras, or Groq.

Zo takes zero percent of any revenue you earn through Stripe selling. Only standard Stripe processing fees apply.
