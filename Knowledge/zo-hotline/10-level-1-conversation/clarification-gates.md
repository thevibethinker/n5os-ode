# Clarification Gates: Make AI Ask YOU Questions

## The Magic Phrase

"Ask me 5 clarifying questions before you respond."

This simple addition transforms any request. Instead of AI guessing what you want, it surfaces assumptions you didn't know you had.

## Why This Works

AI makes assumptions to fill gaps. Those assumptions are usually wrong for your specific situation. Clarification gates force AI to ask rather than assume.

## Examples That Transform

**Without Gate:** "Help me write better emails."
*Result: Generic email advice*

**With Gate:** "Help me write better emails. Ask me 5 clarifying questions first."
*Result: Questions about your audience, current challenges, email types, tone preferences, and biggest pain points*

## Trigger Phrases Callers Use

- "AI assumes too much"
- "It doesn't ask what I actually need"
- "The advice feels too generic"
- "I wish AI would probe deeper"

## Advanced Clarification Tactics

1. **Question Minimums:** "Don't answer until you've asked at least 3 questions"
2. **Specific Domains:** "Ask me about my audience, timeline, and constraints"
3. **Progressive Gates:** "Ask me one question, then based on my answer, ask two more"

## Implementation

Start every significant request with: "Before you answer, ask me clarifying questions about..."

The quality jump is immediate and dramatic.
