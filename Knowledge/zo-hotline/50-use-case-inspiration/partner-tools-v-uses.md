# Tools V Uses With Zo

V builds systems by connecting Zo to tools he actually uses day-to-day. These aren't hypothetical — these are running in production right now.

## AgentMail

Gives AI agents their own email inboxes. V uses AgentMail to let Zo send and receive email independently — the follow-up email you get after this call is sent through AgentMail. That's a live demo of what it does.

What it's good for: automated outreach, notification systems, email-based workflows where your AI needs its own address. Each inbox costs nothing. You create one with a single API call.

Website: agentmail.to

## Aviato

Contact enrichment — you give it a name or email, it gives you back professional background, company info, social profiles. V connects this to his meeting pipeline: after every meeting, Zo enriches the contact automatically and builds a relationship profile.

What it's good for: CRM enrichment, lead research, building intelligence about people you're meeting with. Pair it with a scheduled agent and you never have to manually look someone up again.

## Recall.ai

Meeting bot platform. V has Recall bots joining all his meetings — over 500 per year. The bots capture transcripts, and Zo processes them into action items, relationship data, and content drafts.

What it's good for: if you're in a lot of meetings and the information disappears afterward. Recall captures it, Zo makes it useful. Works with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams.

## Gamma

AI presentation builder. V uses Gamma to generate decks from meeting notes and briefs. Zo can take a meeting transcript and turn it into a polished presentation automatically.

What it's good for: anyone who spends too long making slides. Give it an outline or raw notes and it produces something shareable.

Website: gamma.app

## Short.io

Branded link management. V uses Short.io for tracking links across campaigns and content. Zo can create, manage, and track short links programmatically.

What it's good for: if you share a lot of links and want to know what gets clicked. Or if you want clean branded URLs for professional outreach.

## Exa

AI-powered web search built for agents. V uses Exa for research workflows — it returns semantically relevant results, not just keyword matches. Think of it as Google but designed for AI to use.

What it's good for: competitive research, market intelligence, finding information that's hard to Google. Pair it with a scheduled agent for daily monitoring.

## How V Connects These

The pattern is always the same: get an API key, add it to Zo as a secret, then describe what you want. "Every morning, check my calendar for today's meetings, look up each attendee on Aviato, and email me a one-page brief." That's a real workflow running on V's Zo right now.

Most of these tools have free tiers. You can start with one integration and build from there.
