# Meeting Intelligence: From Transcripts to Searchable Knowledge

## What It Does

Every meeting becomes searchable organizational memory. Transcripts get processed into structured intelligence: attendees, decisions, commitments, and strategic insights.

## The Build Journey

**The Need:** Drowning in meetings, notes everywhere, no way to remember commitments made three weeks ago

**The Intuition:** "I need a funnel. Transcripts come in, intelligence comes out, everything gets filed properly."

**The Stages:**
1. **CAPTURE** — Transcripts land in a designated folder
2. **ORGANIZE** — Create folders, track processing state  
3. **UNDERSTAND** — Extract structured intelligence blocks
4. **ARCHIVE** — File by week, make searchable forever

**The Build:** Six separate conversations over multiple days

## The Intelligence Blocks

**Meeting Context:** Who, when, what type of meeting
**Key Decisions:** What was decided and by whom
**Action Items:** Who committed to what by when
**Strategic Insights:** What this reveals about people/priorities
**Follow-up Needed:** What requires additional conversation

## Trigger Phrases This Solves

- "I can't remember what was decided in that meeting"
- "Who was supposed to follow up on this?"
- "I need to search my meeting history"
- "My meeting notes are scattered everywhere"

## Why Separate Conversations Worked

Context windows are finite. By building each component separately, every piece got full AI attention without losing track of details.

## The Broader Pattern

Any multi-step process can be broken into:
- Input handling (capture)
- Organization (staging)  
- Processing (analysis)
- Output (structured storage)

## Similar Applications

- Email intelligence (auto-categorize and extract action items)
- Document analysis (research papers → key insights)
- Customer feedback (support tickets → trend analysis)
- Financial data (transactions → spending patterns)

## The Key Learning

V didn't become a data engineer. V learned to think in stages and ask AI to build each stage separately.
