# Over-Engineering Day 1

## What it looks like
Caller wants to build complex pipelines, multi-agent systems, or elaborate workflows before they can get AI to give good answers to basic questions.

## Why it happens
Technical excitement overrides fundamentals. They see showcase demos and want the advanced stuff first. They think sophistication equals effectiveness.

## Fast fix
- Block Level 3 until Level 1 is solid
- Have them do 5 conversations with perfect clarification gates first
- Test: Can they get AI to stop assuming and start asking?

## One line to say to Zo
"Before building any pipelines, ask me 5 clarifying questions about what I actually need accomplished."
