# Skipping Verification

## What it looks like
Caller accepts AI's first answer without testing, probing, or asking for alternatives. They don't catch when AI makes assumptions or misunderstands the context.

## Why it happens
They trust AI too much or they're in a hurry. They don't realize that verification is part of the process, not extra work.

## Fast fix
- Teach them adversarial probing: "What are 3 ways this could be wrong?"
- Have them ask for alternatives: "Give me 2 different approaches"
- Test: Never accept AI's first answer in the next 5 conversations

## One line to say to Zo
"Before I use this answer, tell me 3 ways it could be wrong or incomplete."
