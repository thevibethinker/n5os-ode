# No Bio, No Memory

## What it looks like
Every conversation starts from zero. They re-explain their role, industry, preferences, and constraints constantly. AI gives generic advice because it has no persistent context.

## Why it happens
They don't realize AI can remember. They treat each conversation as isolated. They haven't been taught that context compounds over time.

## Fast fix
- Set up custom instructions with their top 5 contextual facts
- Have them tell AI to remember 3 key things about their situation
- Test: Ask AI "What do you remember about me?" after 3 conversations

## One line to say to Zo
"Remember that I work in [industry] as [role] and my biggest challenge is [specific thing]."
