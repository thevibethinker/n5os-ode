# Essential Career Development Statistics v4.0

**Source:** Internal research compilation, June 2025

---

## 1. Employment and Career Development

### School-to-Work Transition
- Only **27%** of seniors secure a good job before graduation (Strada)
- **50%** of college graduates find themselves underemployed after graduating
- **50%** of charter school alumni who graduated from college remain in jobs that don't pay a living wage (Bain)

### Job Search Reality
- Average job search takes **5 months**
- Only **35%** of job applicants are adequately prepared for job search
- Each corporate job posting receives an average of **250 applications**

### New Hire Failures
- **46%** of newly hired employees fail within 18 months (Leadership IQ)
- **89%** of failures are due to attitudinal reasons, not technical skills
- **60%** of workplace failures attributed to soft-skill gaps

### Entry-Level Crisis
- **35%** of entry-level postings required 3+ years experience (2023)
- **94%** of employers require prior work experience for entry-level tech jobs
- Entry-level jobs declined **14%** over the last decade

---

## 2. Resume Effectiveness

### The Brutal Funnel
- Only **2%** of resumes result in an interview
- Average: **50-100 resumes** must be submitted for one job
- Recruiters spend **6-7 seconds** on initial resume review
- Fewer than **1 in 10** resumes reach a hiring manager

### Tailoring Matters
- **63%** of recruiters expect resumes tailored to job description
- **83%** more likely to hire candidates with tailored resumes
- **55%** say biggest mistake is not tailoring resume

### Employer Dissatisfaction with Resumes
- **87%** of employers encounter problems with resumes as hiring tool
- **56%** struggle to tell if applicant has needed skills from resume alone
- **51%** worry resumes aren't accurate
- Resume factors (education, years of experience) have almost **no correlation (r ≈ 0.1)** with job performance

---

## 3. Career Services Gap

- University career centers average **5.42** FTE staff
- Each counselor serves **2,369 students** on average
- Only **24%** of students actively use career center services
- Career center users are **42%** more likely to perceive degree as worth the cost

### Graduate Employment Crisis
- Only **44%** of recent grads found jobs in their field within 6 months (2024) — down from **66%** in 2010
- **70%** of companies acknowledge need to overhaul hiring for early-career talent

---

## 4. Behavioral Insights

- **43%** of entry-level applicants refuse to apply without salary disclosure
- **52%** of professionals worry about economic uncertainty impact on employment
- Only **33%** of American workers feel engaged at work
- **58%** actively looking to make career change
- **49%** believe lack of good career advice negatively impacted their trajectory

### Career Coaching Gap
- Career coaching costs **$75-$250/hour** (avg $170)
- Only **1/3** of Fortune 500 companies provide executive coaching
- **62%** who received career coaching reported significant improvements
- Nearly half of millennials/Gen Z would use AI for career advice

---

## 5. AI in Hiring

### Adoption
- **82%** of hiring managers have used AI for hiring
- **65%** of CHROs plan to implement AI in recruiting by 2025
- **83%** of employers intend to use AI for initial resume reviews by 2025

### The Paradox
- **63%** of recruiters believe AI-using candidates are more qualified
- **40%** of HR say AI-generated content in applications is a dealbreaker
- **50%** of job seekers believe AI could improve hiring fairness
- **49%** believe AI recruiting tools are more biased than human hiring

---

## 6. Soft Skills Revolution

- **88.3%** of employers prioritize soft skills (up from 73.5% in 2022)
- **92%** consider soft skills equally or more important than technical skills
- MIT study: soft skills training led to **7.4% productivity boost** with **256% ROI**

### Cultural Fit
- **81%** believe cultural fit employees less likely to leave
- **85%** believe they perform better
- Employees with values alignment are **4× more likely** to be "fully engaged"

---

## 7. Skills-Based Hiring Revolution

### The Shift
- **81%** of organizations now use skills-based hiring practices (2024)
- **92%** believe it's more effective than resume screening
- **89%** say it's more predictive of job success
- **95%** agree it's the dominant trend for years ahead

### Degree Requirements Dropping
- **46%** of middle-skill jobs removed degree requirements (2017-2019)
- **44%** of employers say college degree not important for most roles (2025)
- **77%** would hire someone with missing skills and train them
- **1.4 million** additional jobs could open to non-degree holders in next 5 years

### The Gap
- **95%** agree skills-based hiring is future, but only **28%** of employees feel company emphasizes it
- **51%** of managers still instruct recruiters to only consider candidates meeting every requirement

---

## 8. Cost of Bad Hires

- Bad hire costs **$15,000-$17,000** on average
- Much higher at senior levels
- **27 million** "hidden workers" in U.S. eager to work but screened out

---

## Key Takeaways for Careerspan

1. **Resume system is broken** — employers know it (87% have problems)
2. **Soft skills matter most** — but aren't captured by current tools
3. **Skills-based hiring is inevitable** — but implementation lags
4. **Career guidance gap is massive** — only 24% use career services
5. **AI is double-edged** — helpful but trust issues on both sides
