# Vrijen's POV on the Future of Careerspan

**Strategic vision for transforming career development**

---

## 1. Overall Positioning & Vision

### Present vs. Future

| Timeframe | Identity |
|-----------|----------|
| **Present** | "Career development platform" |
| **Long-term** | "World's first career companion" — lifelong strategic guidance |

### Core Hypothesis

- Existing hiring systems rely on a superficial, "two-dimensional" resume approach
- They overlook candidate's personal values, soft skills, and mindset
- Soft skills and values are critical in determining successful hires
- Yet typical job platforms focus primarily on keywords and technical skills

### The Facebook Analogy

> "Facebook gave folks a reason to give up their data. We want to do the same for careers—give them the reason to invest real time into telling their story."

---

## 2. Problem with Existing Career Tools

### "End Product Orientation"
- Traditional tools focus on one-time outputs (resume, cover letter)
- Not ongoing professional development or deeper self-knowledge

### Resumes as Weak Proxy
- "Two-dimensional" — barely capture broader attributes
- Hiring dominated by keywords and technical screening
- Overlooks candidate's full potential

### Market Entrenchment
- Startups optimize existing resume-based funnel for B2B
- Instead of reimagining how personal data and candidacy are represented

---

## 3. Candidate-First Approach

### AI Coaching as Core Differentiator

> "So many people just have never had the opportunity to engage with someone that is just there to listen to them and ask good questions…"

- Conversational AI engages users to detail strengths, experiences, goals, values
- Helps people discover aspects of themselves they might not articulate
- Always-available, nonjudgmental AI makes sharing easier

### Bridging the "Hidden Curriculum"
- Those lacking built-in career guidance face unspoken disadvantages
- Careerspan empowers them to articulate professional identity effectively

---

## 4. Capturing Soft Skills and Values

### Approach
- **Prevalence Analysis:** Reviews conversations for recurring words/phrases tied to recognized skills/values
- **Not measuring mastery** — uncovers important traits; employers can assess proficiency later
- **Future integration:** Connect with standardized skill taxonomies (e.g., Lightcast)

---

## 5. Building a "3D Profile"

### The Vision

> "Our feeling is... we want to supplant that [the resume] with Careerspan as the data layer of the job market itself."

- **Invisible Resume:** Persistent, evolving career profile
- **Dynamic Data:** Platform accumulates deep record of personal growth over time
- **Industry Shift:** Richer candidate profiles → more holistic job matches

---

## 6. Partnership Strategy

### API Integration Over Building ATS
- Offer API to existing platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, etc.)
- Avoid reinventing the wheel
- Pioneer how candidate profiles are populated and assessed

### Avoiding B2B Status Quo

> "I think so many companies chase the enterprise dollar and end up further entrenching the same old system. We want a different path, candidate-first."

- Focus on candidates first → gain user trust and data
- Companies then seek out Careerspan's data pipeline

---

## 7. Economic Model & GTM

### User Attraction & Retention
- Demonstrate immediate value to individual job seekers
- Build critical mass of users
- Employers then motivated to integrate with platform's data

### 1-3 Year Horizon
- Evolve from "career development platform" to true "career companion"
- Disrupt existing resume paradigm
- Partnerships with HR tech systems central to scaling

---

## Key Quotes

   > "Our feeling is... we want to supplant that [the resume] with Careerspan as the data layer of the job market itself."

2. **On Soft Skills Gap:**
   > "We're actually extremely effective at evaluating hard skills... we're terrible at evaluating soft skills."

3. **On B2B vs. Candidate Focus:**
   > "I think so many companies chase the enterprise dollar and end up further entrenching the same old system. We want a different path, candidate-first."

---

## Strategic Summary

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| **Short-term** | User-friendly career development platform with AI coaching |
| **Core attraction** | Continuous AI conversations gathering nuanced data |
| **Long-term** | Full "career companion" with 3D profile feeding ATS via API |
| **Ultimate goal** | Reshape hiring with candidate-centric data layer replacing flat resume |
