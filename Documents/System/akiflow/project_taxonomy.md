# Akiflow Project Taxonomy (Final)
**For:** V (Careerspan CEO/Founder)  
**Version:** 2.0 | **Date:** 2025-10-22

---

## Current Structure (12 Projects)

### Personal/Life
1. **Personal** – Personal life, non-work
2. **LifeOps** – Life administration, household, errands
3. **Learning** – Technical skill development, courses, study

### Careerspan Business
4. **Careerspan** – Core company tasks, CEO duties, operations
5. **Operations** – Day-to-day company operations
6. **Product** – Product development, roadmap, features
7. **Growth** – Marketing, sales, business development
8. **Personnel** – Hiring, team management, HR
9. **Finance & Legal** – Financial admin, contracts, legal
10. **Careerspan Content** – Company blog, marketing content

### Professional/Network
11. **Networking** – Warm intros, relationship building, connections
12. **VA-ZO Content** – Content about/for Zo, AI tools, workflows

---

## Tag Standards

### By Type
- `meeting` – Meeting prep/follow-up
- `recap` – Post-meeting recaps/summaries
- `warm_intro` – Warm introduction tasks
- `recruiting` – Candidate pipeline, interviews
- `review` – Review/approval tasks
- `deep_work` – Focus time needed (30min+)
- `quick` – <10min tasks
- `waiting_on` – Blocked on someone else

### By Stage (Warm Intros)
- `draft` – Draft the intro
- `send` – Send the intro
- `follow_up` – Check if connected

### By Context
- `email` – Email-heavy task
- `call` – Phone/video call
- `async` – Can be done anytime
- `deadline` – Time-sensitive

---

## Priority Mapping
- **High:** Urgent, deadline-driven, CEO-critical
- **Normal:** Standard tasks, planned work
- **Low:** Nice-to-have, backlog

---

## Duration Estimates (Defaults)
- Warm intro draft: 15m
- Warm intro send: 5m
- Warm intro follow-up: 10m
- Meeting recap: 20-30m
- Candidate review: 30m
- Deep work: 60-90m
