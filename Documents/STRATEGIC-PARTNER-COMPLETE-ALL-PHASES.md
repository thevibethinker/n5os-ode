### ✅ Phase 2.4: Evolution Tracking

**`strategy-compounder`** - Quantitative strategic evolution analysis

**Scoring System:**
- **Consensus:** Recency-weighted theme frequency (0-1.0)
- **Divergence:** Conflict measurement within theme (0-1.0)
- **Trajectory:** Change in consensus over time (Δ)
- **Heat Zones:** Emerging/Solidifying/Locked-In/At-Risk
- **Cohesion Index:** Overall strategic alignment (0-1.0)
- **Baseline Diff:** Amplifies/Reinforces/Deviates/New

**Features:**
- Analyzes sessions quantitatively
- Compares to Knowledge/ hypotheses (baseline)
- Tracks strategic evolution over time
- Heat map visualization
- State persistence
- Configurable windows and decay

**Usage:**
```bash
strategy-compounder --period week
strategy-compounder --period month --baseline Knowledge/hypotheses/gtm_hypotheses.md
```
