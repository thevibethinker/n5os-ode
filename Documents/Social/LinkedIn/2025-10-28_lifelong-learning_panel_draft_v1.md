The best career advice in 2025? Never stop learning.

On Oct 28 at 1:00 PM ET, I’m joining Eric Stano (Magic EdTech) and Lisa Noble (Colby College) for a conversation on why learning—not the ladder—is the new career path.

We’ll cover how AI is reshaping work, how students and professionals can prepare for emerging roles, and why building skills with visible proof of capability matters more than titles. I’ll share my perspective from building Careerspan: practical ways to turn experience into evidence and narrative that compounds over time.
