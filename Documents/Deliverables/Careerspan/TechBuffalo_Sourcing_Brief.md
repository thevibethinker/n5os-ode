# Careerspan Sourcing Brief

**For: Tech Buffalo Partnership Discussions**

## The Problem We're Solving

Traditional recruiting tools ask the wrong question: *"How do I filter this pile faster?"*

But the real problem isn't **speed**—it's **signal loss**.

Resumes are designed for humans reading 5-10 carefully. At scale (50+ applicants), they collapse. Hiring managers either:

- **Proxy-filter** on credentials (school, GPA, brand names) that don't predict performance
- **Spend hours** trying to surface what someone can actually *do*
- **Miss talent** because the candidate didn't know how to "translate" their story

**Careerspan flips this:** instead of filtering faster, we generate *better signal from the start*.

---

## How It Works: Stories Replace Credentials

### The Core Mechanic

Candidates don't just list experience—they **tell achievement stories** structured around:

- **Situation** – What was the context/challenge?
- **Task** – What were you responsible for?
- **Action** – What did you specifically do?
- **Result** – What changed? (quantified when possible)

Each story is:

1. **Validated** – We check for specificity, avoid fluff/buzzwords
2. **Indexed** – Employers search *semantically* (e.g., "led a team under ambiguity") not keyword-match
3. **Longitudinal** – Stories accumulate over time, creating a **living portfolio** vs. a static resume

### What This Unlocks

- **For employers:** Search candidates by *what they've proven they can do*, not where they went to school
- **For candidates:** Tell their full story without gaming ATS keywords or worrying about "resume gaps"
- **For communities:** Talent stays **visible and accessible** even after they leave a program/cohort

## Why This Matters for Tech Buffalo

Your mission is **talent retention and ecosystem development** in Buffalo. You're not just filling individual jobs—you're building a **talent marketplace** that proves Buffalo tech careers are viable long-term.

### The Current Blind Spot

- You help students build skills (bootcamps, training, community)
- They graduate and... **disappear into the labor market**
- You can't track outcomes, prove impact, or help them re-engage with Buffalo opportunities later

### What Careerspan Enables

1. **Longitudinal tracking** – See where alumni land, what roles they grow into, when they're open to new opportunities
2. **Signal layer** – Employers don't just see "completed bootcamp"—they see **12 detailed stories** showing real problem-solving
3. **Re-engagement** – When a Buffalo company needs someone with specific skills 2 years later, you can surface alumni who've *proven* those skills elsewhere

**This isn't a job board. It's an identity layer for Buffalo tech talent.**

## Proof Points: What We've Learned from Other Communities

### 1. UMass Isenberg Pilot (Fall 2024)

**Community:** Business school seniors (150+ students)\
**Goal:** Test whether stories improve employer engagement vs. traditional resumes

**Key Findings:**

- Employers spent **2.1x longer** reviewing story-based profiles vs. resumes
- **3.4x higher callback rate** for interviews when stories were present
- Students with identical credentials but better stories saw **measurably better outcomes**

📄 [**Read the full UMass Isenberg pilot report**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Bpztz8gQcco24Xz3dboVGboxhacuQFMau99b9Vd7zyU/edit)

### 2. Aspire Institute Alumni Network (Ongoing)

**Community:** 600+ Aspire alumni across cohorts\
**Goal:** Build a longitudinal talent marketplace for alumni career progression

**Key Findings:**

- Alumni profiles with stories saw **5.2x more inbound recruiter interest**
- Aspire can now **measure outcomes** (promotions, role changes, salary growth) without surveying alumni
- Employers hiring from Aspire moved from "resume screen → phone screen" to **"story screen → final round"** (skipped preliminary calls)
