# Careerspan × TechBuffalo

## The Problem

Technical talent from Buffalo looks the same on paper as everyone else.

Résumés flatten people into keywords. LinkedIn profiles blur into sameness. The signal that makes someone *actually good* — how they think, what they've built, what they've overcome — gets lost.

For TechBuffalo's mission of retaining and placing WNY tech talent, this is the core bottleneck: **your people are better than their documents suggest**.

---

## What Careerspan Does

Careerspan is an AI career platform that builds a **talent graph** — a semantic profile of who someone actually is as a professional.

Instead of parsing résumés for keywords, we have structured conversations that surface:

- **Qualities & patterns** — not just "Python" but *how* they approach problems
- **Stories that demonstrate capability** — the projects, obstacles, and wins that don't fit a bullet point
- **Fit signals** — what environments they thrive in, what they're looking for next

The output: candidates who can articulate their value clearly, and a rich profile that employers can actually evaluate.

---

## How It Works

1. **Candidate signs up** → guided AI conversation (20-30 min)
2. **System extracts** → skills, qualities, stories, preferences
3. **Candidate gets** → polished positioning, interview prep, job-fit scoring
4. **You get** → talent profiles with real signal, not just keyword matches

No complex integration. Works alongside existing job boards and career services.

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Said Careerspan **strengthened their narratives** | **92%** |
| **Discovered new experiences** to highlight | **80%** |
| Confidence increase in interview settings | **+12.3%** |
| Story completion rate | **88%** |
