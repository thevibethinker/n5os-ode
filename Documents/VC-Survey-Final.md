## Scoring Guide (for manual review)

**HIGH QUAL (20-25 points):**
- Q1: 4-5 points
- Q3: $250+ (3+ points)
- Q6: 25-50% or 50%+ (3-4 points)
- Q8: Makes decision or strongly influences (4-5 points)
- Q9: $150+ (3+ points)

**MODERATE (12-19 points):**
- Q1: 2-3 points
- Some budget but lower
- Some pain but manageable
- Can influence but doesn't decide
- Q9: $50-$150

**WEAK (< 12 points):**
- Rarely have the problem
- Low/no budget
- No decision authority
- Low willingness to pay
