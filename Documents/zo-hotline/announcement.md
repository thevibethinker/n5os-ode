### The Framework in 30 Seconds

**Level 1: Conversation Engineering**  
Tactics within a single chat. Ephemeral — resets when you close the tab.

**Level 2: Environment Engineering**  
Persistent context that shapes ALL interactions. Your AI "knows you" before you type.

**Level 3: Pipeline Engineering**  
Living connections between AI and the world. Works when you're not there.

**The Meta-OS** emerges when all three levels work together — a personal operating system for knowledge work.
