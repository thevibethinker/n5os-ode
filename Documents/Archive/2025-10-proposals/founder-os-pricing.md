# Founder OS – Draft Pricing (Per Workspace)

Status: Draft
Audience: Solo founders, founders in companies <10, select VC teams

## Licensing Unit
- Per workspace (one logical environment per team/org instance)

## Core Setup (one-time)
- Solo founder: $3,000
- Team (<10): $5,000
- VC variant (investment-focused): $8,000
Includes: base workspace scaffolding, Zo Bridge MVP setup (Gmail, Drive, Notion), onboarding session, smoke tests.

## Add-Ons (one-time unless noted)
- Email Suite (drafting/triage/replies): $1,000 setup + $200/mo
- Voice Model (tone/style tuning): $1,250 setup (optional $100/mo retraining)
- CRM integration (n8n/native; scope-dependent): $1,500–$4,000
- Meeting Intelligence (auto-notes/actions/linking): $1,000 setup + $150/mo
- Reflection Pipeline Pro (hypotheses/insights): $1,250 setup + $150/mo

## Subscription: Monthly Capability Drops (per workspace)
- Solo founder: $200/mo
- Team (<10): $350/mo
- VC variant: $500/mo
Includes: monthly release, changelog, migration notes, update script, rollback plan, 30-min office hours.

## Custom Work
- Bespoke pipelines, data modeling, reporting: $200/hr or fixed bid per SOW

## Notes
- Prices are initial anchors; final quotes may vary by scope/complexity
- Nonprofit considerations currently out of scope
- Travel, third-party SaaS, or usage-based costs billed separately if applicable
