## Productivity Hypothesis

**Volume hasn't changed much, but composition has:**

Before tools:
- Manual follow-ups
- Ad-hoc meeting notes
- Mix of trivial + substantial

With tools (Superhuman + Zo):
- Automated digest generation
- Structured action extraction  
- Better triage of trivial emails

**Conclusion:** Tools may be improving *quality* and *organization* more than raw *volume*.
