## Key Design Decisions

### **Archetype-Driven Approach**
Instead of generic "write a post," Vibe Writer uses 5 distinct archetypes (Origin Story, Problem-Solution, Educational, Rant, Milestone). Each has:
- Specific structure pattern
- Voice dial settings
- Example hooks
- Use case guidance

**Rationale:** V's content varies significantly by purpose. Archetypes provide clear patterns while maintaining authentic voice.

### **Voice Principles as Guardrails**
7 critical principles (P1-P7) function as:
- Pre-flight checklist items
- In-process quality gates
- Post-creation validation

**Rationale:** Matches Vibe Builder's P-system approach. Prevents common failure modes (generic voice, weak hooks, missing CTAs).

### **Integration with Existing Voice Files**
Vibe Writer **loads** (not duplicates) existing voice specifications:
- `file 'N5/prefs/communication/social-media-voice.md'` — dimensional metrics, archetypes
- `file 'N5/prefs/communication/voice.md'` — signature patterns, lexicon
- `file 'Documents/Personal_Brand_Building_Framework.md'` — strategic principles

**Rationale:** SSOT compliance (P2). Persona orchestrates existing resources rather than creating redundant specifications.

### **Multi-Angle Strategy**
For rich source material (reflections, meetings), generate 3-5 distinct angles:
- Founder pain (Problem-Solution)
- Technical breakdown (Educational)
- Personal story (Origin Story)
- Controversial take (Rant)
- Framework/system (Educational)

**Rationale:** Mirrors existing N5 content workflows. One insight → multiple public-facing pieces.

### **Quality Validation Layers**
Three checkpoints:
1. **Pre-flight:** Load correct files, clarify audience/goal/archetype
2. **In-process:** Voice alignment, rhythm testing, specificity strengthening
3. **Post-creation:** Checklist (structure, voice, content, engagement)

**Rationale:** Catches issues early (P18: Verify State). Prevents claiming complete prematurely (P15).

## What Makes This Different from Generic "Content Writer" Personas

### 1. **Voice Fidelity System**
Not just "write well"—specific patterns that make content identifiably V's:
- Em-dash usage (2-4 per post, specific patterns)
- Single-sentence paragraphs for impact
- Strategic vulnerability balanced with expertise
- Plain language lexicon (no buzzwords)
- Signature phrases from actual emails

### 2. **Archetype Intelligence**
Generic personas: "Write a LinkedIn post"  
Vibe Writer: "Which archetype? Origin Story, Problem-Solution, Educational, Rant, or Milestone? Each has distinct structure and voice settings."

### 3. **N5 System Integration**
- Pulls from Knowledge/ for enrichment (credentials, examples, metrics)
- Processes reflections through multi-angle lens
- Outputs follow N5 naming conventions
- Respects Rule-of-Two (max 4 files loaded per execution)

### 4. **Anti-Pattern Library**
Not just "best practices"—specific failure modes V has encountered:
- Generic corporate speak
- Weak hooks that lose readers
- Missing CTAs
- Dense paragraph blocks
- Buzzword overload
- Voice drift (sounds like anyone)

### 5. **Self-Evaluation Metrics**
Built-in success criteria:
- Voice fidelity (1-5): Does it sound like V?
- Hook strength (1-5): Would you stop scrolling?
- CTA clarity (1-5): Is next action obvious?
- Engagement potential (1-5): Would you comment/share?
- Authenticity (1-5): Genuine or performative?

Target: 4+ on all metrics for final output.
