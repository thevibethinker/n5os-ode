## Distribution Cadence

### Week 1
- [ ] LinkedIn DMs to warm connections (10/day)
- [ ] Email to warm network (50 recipients)
- [ ] Post in 2-3 VC Slack communities

### Week 2
- [ ] Twitter thread + engagement
- [ ] LinkedIn DMs to cold but targeted (5/day)
- [ ] Follow-up to warm connections who haven't responded

### Week 3
- [ ] Share survey results (anonymized) to create FOMO
- [ ] "Last chance" push to network
- [ ] Begin HIGH score outreach

### Ongoing
- [ ] Add to email signature
- [ ] Share in relevant conversations
- [ ] Use as intro/warm-up tool for sales conversations

## Tracking

**Response Goal:** 50-100 responses  
**HIGH qual target:** 10-15 (20-30% of responses)  
**Demo conversion:** 50% of HIGH scores  
**Design partner goal:** 3-5 VCs

**Track:**
- Source of response (LinkedIn, email, Slack, etc.)
- Score distribution
- Most common answers to each question
- Conversion from survey → demo → customer
