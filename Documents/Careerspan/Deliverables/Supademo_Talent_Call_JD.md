# Talent Call: Join Supademo

---

## This isn't a job posting. It's an open call.

We're not hiring for a specific role right now. We're looking for **the right people** — and when we find them, we'll figure out where they fit.

If that sounds backwards, good. It's intentional.

Most companies post a role, then scramble to find someone who checks the boxes. We'd rather find great people first, then build around them.

---

## Who We Are

**Supademo** is the fastest and easiest way to create interactive product demos. We help B2B teams — sales, marketing, customer success — show their product without forcing users through signup walls or hour-long calls.

We're a small team (~10 people), fully remote, and profitable. We raised a Pre-Seed round, but we run like a bootstrap. No bloat. No busywork. Just shipping.

---

## What We're Looking For

We don't care where you went to school. We don't care how many years you've been doing something. We care about **how you think, how you work, and whether you'd make us better.**

Here's what matters to us:

### → You're radically honest

You say what you mean. You give feedback directly — not harshly, but clearly. You don't play politics, and you don't say things just to make people feel good. You'd rather have a hard conversation now than let a problem fester.

### → You're outcomes-obsessed

You measure yourself by what you ship, not how hard you tried. You know the difference between motion and progress. When someone asks "what did you accomplish this week?" you have a real answer.

### → You're adaptable

Plans change. Priorities shift. You don't spiral when the ground moves — you adjust and keep going. Low ego. High resilience.

### → You thrive without structure

We're remote and async. No one's going to check if you're online. No one's going to tell you exactly what to do every day. If you need hand-holding, this isn't your place. If you thrive with autonomy, you'll love it here.

### → You scale what works — you don't reinvent the wheel

We hire people to pour gas on fires that are already burning, not to rub sticks together hoping for a spark. If you get energy from building playbooks from zero with no validation, that's cool — but it's not what we need. We need people who can take something that's working and make it work 10x better.

### → You actually care

About the work. About the team. About doing something that matters. We're not a "just a job" kind of company. We're building something real, and we want people who feel that.

## How This Works

This is a **Talent Call**, powered by Careerspan.

1. **You apply** through the Careerspan platform
2. **You complete a structured self-reflection** — a series of prompts that help you articulate who you are, how you work, and what you're looking for
3. **Our system evaluates fit** based on the attributes above — not keywords, not credentials, but the substance of how you think
4. **If there's a match**, we reach out to start a conversation

No ghosting. No black hole. If you apply, you'll hear back — even if it's a "not right now."

And here's the thing: **the reflection process is valuable whether we hire you or not.** It's designed to help you get clearer on what you want and how to talk about yourself. You keep that.
