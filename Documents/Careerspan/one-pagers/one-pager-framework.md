# Careerspan Sourcing One-Pager Framework

## Purpose
Persuade a corp dev / BD contact to take a meeting. Not to close—just to open the door.

## Structure (5 Components)

| Section | Function | Length |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Hook** | Create urgency + relevance in <15 words | 1 sentence |
| **Problem** | Name THEIR problem (not ours) | 2-3 sentences |
| **Thesis** | Differentiated claim | 1 sentence |
| **Proof** | Evidence the claim is credible | 3-5 bullets |
| **Ask** | What you want them to do | 1 sentence |

## Two Document Types

### Type 1: General-Purpose
- **Audience:** Any talent acquisition platform (Handshake, Gem, Beamery, Greenhouse, etc.)
- **Use case:** Cold outreach, conference networking, warm intro asks
- **Hook formula:** [Inbound is broken] + [We have what you can't build]
- **Problem:** Generic—about the resume channel collapse, not company-specific
- **File:** `sourcing-one-pager-general.md`

### Type 2: Company-Targeted
- **Audience:** Specific company (e.g., Handshake)
- **Use case:** Targeted outreach via warm intro or direct contact
- **Hook formula:** [Your situation] + [What you're missing] + [What we bring]
- **Problem:** Company-specific—reference their strategy, gaps, metrics
- **File:** Example: `sourcing-one-pager-handshake.md`

## Voice Guidelines

**Directness:** 0.8+ (assertive, no hedging)

**Kill list:**
- "I wanted to reach out"
- "We believe"
- "We think"
- "If you have time"
- "Would love to"
- "Explore opportunities"

**Prefer:**
- Declarative statements
- Concrete metrics
- Specific names (partnerships, team)
- Interest-focused CTAs ("Worth a conversation?" not "Are you free?")

## Modular Components

### Problem Block (General)
> Inbound is broken. AI made resumes worthless—every candidate now produces the same polished artifact in seconds. The signal that used to differentiate high-agency candidates from everyone else is gone. Your recruiters hit an attentional speed limit. Application volume exploded; review hours didn't.

### Thesis Block (Consistent)
> Careerspan captures behavioral signal that resumes can't carry—through a coaching medium that candidates actually use.

### Proof Block (Modular)
Swap these bullets based on what resonates with the target:

**Community partnerships:**
- Peace Corps (7,000+ returnees/year)
- Emory University
- Columbia University
- humanUPtions

**Engagement metrics:**
- 4,000+ signups
- 1,500 fully activated users
- 35% complete 3+ stories
- 40+ minute average engagement

**Strategic validation:**
- Marvin Ventures (McKinsey alumni fund) sourcing partner

**Team credibility:**
- V: 10+ years career coaching
- Logan: Harvard Project on Workforce
- Ilse: CTO, validated matching approach

### Ask Block (Modular)

**General:** "30 minutes to show you how we surface the candidates your current sourcing misses."

**Targeted:** "A conversation about what [specific integration/outcome] looks like for [Company]."

## Adapting for New Targets

1. Research the company (funding, strategy, gaps, recent moves)
2. Write company-specific Problem block (3-4 sentences)
3. Connect Careerspan's value to THEIR strategic gaps
4. Customize the Ask to their specific opportunity
5. Use their language/metrics where possible

## Quality Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] First sentence = main point (no throat-clearing)
- [ ] Their problem, not our story, comes first
- [ ] No hedge words
- [ ] Active voice throughout
- [ ] Concrete metrics included
- [ ] One clear ask
- [ ] Scannable in 30 seconds
- [ ] Under 400 words
