# The $2B Opportunity Handshake Is Missing

## The situation

20M students pass through Handshake every year. You know their school, their major, their job preferences.

You don't know which ones are actually good.

Neither does anyone else. That's the problem—and the opportunity.

## The recruiting industry's dirty secret

The $200B recruiting industry exists because employers can't identify top talent at scale. Recruiters survive by maintaining relationships with the 1% of candidates everyone fights over. They track these people for years. They know who's actually exceptional vs. who just interviews well.

Right now, Handshake lets that value walk out the door. Students pass through, get jobs, and disappear. The recruiters maintain those relationships. You don't.

## The window

Handshake has something no one else has: distribution across 1,600 universities and 20M students. That's the hard part. You already did it.

But distribution without signal is a commodity. Other players are trying to crack early career recruiting. The window where you can lock in data supremacy is narrow.

## The play

Embed behavioral signal capture into your existing flow. Students already use Handshake for career development. Add a layer that captures how they think—not just what they've done.

What this unlocks:

**Identify the top 1% across your entire ecosystem.** Not by GPA. Not by school name. By actual behavioral markers that predict performance—agency, communication quality, follow-through, self-awareness.

**Own the longitudinal relationship.** Track top performers from freshman year through a decade into their careers. You become the system of record for elite talent—not the recruiter who happens to know them.

**Capture a slice of the $200B.** Premium placement fees. Recruiter-grade matching. Priority access for employers willing to pay for quality, not volume. Even 1% of the recruiting TAM is $2B.

## Why this works

The tech exists. Careerspan has 4,000 users doing 40+ minutes of structured self-reflection—producing behavioral profiles that predict performance better than any resume or assessment.

The distribution exists. You have it.

The only question is whether you plug them together before someone else figures this out.

## The ask

30 minutes to show you what data supremacy looks like in early career recruiting.

This isn't a feature add. It's how Handshake becomes the company that eats the recruiting industry.
