# The Signal Layer the Recruiting Industry Is Missing

## The $500B problem no one has solved

The recruiting industry exists because no one can identify elite talent before they're elite. Recruiters build relationships one by one. They track careers manually. They maintain surface area of attention that platforms can't replicate.

Why? Because every sourcing tool on the market sources from the same broken channel: resumes.

AI collapsed the resume's signal. The artifact that once proxied for effort, communication ability, and agency now costs nothing to produce. Anyone can generate a polished, tailored resume in seconds. The medium no longer carries information. Application volume exploded. Recruiters hit an attentional speed limit. The math became impossible.

LinkedIn is pay-to-play and commoditized—everyone has the same access to the same keyword-searchable profiles. Job boards attract active seekers, not the best candidates. Resume databases are shallow. And the most valuable talent populations—elite university alumni, Peace Corps returnees, professional communities—don't give open access.

Better parsing won't fix this. Smarter screening won't fix this. You're optimizing a broken channel.

## A new medium for better signal

Careerspan captures what no resume or assessment can: how someone actually thinks. 40+ minutes of structured self-reflection. Stories that reveal reasoning patterns, leadership signals, growth trajectory. Matching based on qualities—not keywords that get gamed.

The difference is the dynamic. Candidates perform for assessments. They reveal to coaches. Careerspan behaves like a coach—the candidate gets immediate value (clarity, better stories, better positioning). The interaction feels human and supportive. That inverted incentive structure produces authentic signal that traditional tools can't touch.

What this creates:

- **Profiles that reveal how people think and behave**, not just what they've done
- **Pre-selected candidates**—people who complete 40+ minutes of voluntary self-reflection are pre-filtered for high agency, follow-through, and self-awareness
- **Longitudinal data that compounds**—candidates come back, update their stories, demonstrate consistent engagement over years
- **Speed**—pre-profiled candidates can be surfaced in hours, not weeks
