## Survey Structure

**Title:** VC Productivity Survey

**Description (in form):**  
We're building N5OS—a personal OS that turns your knowledge into instant, queryable intelligence. This 4-minute survey helps us understand if we can solve your workflow challenges. If your answers reveal problems we solve, I'll show you what we're building.

**Total Questions:** 10 core + 4 contact fields = 14 question groups  
**Estimated Time:** 4 minutes  
**All questions required** except: Firm name, Early access checkbox

---

## Question Breakdown

### Q1: Context Retrieval Pain (MC)
How often can't you access relevant context quickly enough in conversations?
- Scoring: 1-5 points (Rarely to Constantly)

### Q2: Current Tool Spend (Checkboxes, Allow Other)
What productivity tools do you currently pay for?
- Reveals current stack, willingness to pay for tools

### Q3: Monthly Budget (MC)
Monthly spend per person on productivity tools?
- < $100 → $1,000+
- Direct budget qualification

### Q4: Memo Bottleneck (MC)
When writing memos, what takes longest?
- Identifies specific pain points we can solve

### Q5: Pattern Recognition Gap (Textarea, REQUIRED)
Would instant access to past patterns have changed a decision?
- Open-ended: reveals depth of need
- **Most qualitative insight**

### Q6: Time Arbitrage (MC)
% of week on delegable but context-dependent work?
- Quantifies time savings opportunity

### Q7: Feature Priority (MC)
Which feature would free up MOST time?
- Product roadmap validation
- Feature prioritization signal

### Q8: Decision Authority (MC) 
Role in tool adoption decisions?
- **Critical buying authority qualifier**

### Q9: Willingness to Pay (MC) - THE MONEY QUESTION
What's an AI assistant worth per month?
- $0 → $500+
- **Direct pricing signal**

### Q10: Adoption Trigger (MC)
What would make you adopt a new system?
- Reveals psychological buying triggers

## Lead Scoring Framework

### HIGH QUALIFICATION (20-25 points)
- Q1: Frequently/Constantly (4-5 pts)
- Q3: $250+/month (3+ pts)
- Q6: 25-50% or 50%+ (3-4 pts)
- Q8: Makes decision or strongly influences (4-5 pts)
- Q9: $150+/month (3+ pts)

**Action:** Immediate personal outreach with demo

### MODERATE (12-19 points)
- Some pain, some budget, some influence
- Q9: $50-$150 range

**Action:** Nurture sequence, wait for product readiness

### WEAK (< 12 points)
- Low pain, no budget, no authority, won't pay

**Action:** Newsletter only

## Strategic Design Elements

### Qualification-First Approach
✅ Every question surfaces buyability signals  
✅ Makes respondents aware of their problems  
✅ Sets up "I have this solution" follow-up  
✅ No wasted questions—all serve dual purpose (insight + qualification)

### Pain Amplification
- Q1: Makes them acknowledge frequency
- Q4 & Q6: Forces them to quantify impact
- Q5: Makes them reflect on missed opportunities

### Buying Intent Validation
- Q8: Authority
- Q9: Willingness to pay
- Q3: Current budget allocation
- Q10: Adoption psychology
