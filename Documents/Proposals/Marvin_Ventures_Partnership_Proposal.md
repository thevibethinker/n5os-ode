## The Problem Every Founder Faces

Early-stage hiring is broken:

- **Can't afford traditional recruiters** ($30-50K per hire, 20-25% of first-year salary)
- **Can't afford full ATS platforms** (Greenhouse, Ashby: $500-1K/month + onboarding)
- **Can't afford to spend 40 hours/week reviewing resumes**
- **Can't find quality talent fast enough** (average time-to-hire: 36-42 days)

Result: Founders either overpay for recruitment or drown in operational hiring burden.

---

## What Careerspan Offers

We built an AI-powered talent platform that delivers interview-ready candidates without the operational burden of traditional recruiting or ATS platforms.

### **Two Ways to Find Your Next Hire:**

#### **1. Magic Link — Bring Your Own Candidates**

Post your role publicly, Careerspan handles the rest.

**How it works:**

- Share your custom Magic Link anywhere (job boards, social media, email)
- Candidates apply through our platform
- You record a short voice message with your unstated preferences—culture fit, grit, qualitative signals that don't belong in a job description
- We filter candidates using both explicit criteria and your qualitative preferences
- You receive interview-ready matches delivered to your inbox daily
- Raw candidate data is preserved if you want to reference it, but you never have to

**Why founders use it:**

- No resume screening
- No drowning in unqualified applicants
- No configuration or table management
- Just qualified candidates, daily

**Stats:**

- We filter out 98% of candidates
- Candidates we pass through have \~40% screening-to-interview conversion (vs. industry average \~10%)
- 1 in 5 Careerspan candidates makes it to final round (vs. industry average \~1 in 20)

---

#### **2. Pool Access — Search Our Pre-Vetted Talent Community**

Skip public posting entirely. Search our existing talent pool.

**How it works:**

- Search Careerspan's existing community (3,000+ users, 1,000+ hours of coaching data, rich candidate profiles)
- Our platform surfaces best matches based on your criteria
- Choose **one-time scan** (single search) or **ongoing monitoring** (continuous alerts as new matches join)
- No public job posting required

**Why founders use it:**

- Perfect for confidential or stealth roles
- Pre-filtered, pre-vetted talent
- Get a head start on hiring before posting publicly
- Our goal: eventually, you never need to look beyond the Careerspan pool
