## V's Opening (10 min)

### Beat 1: Survey Insights (2 min)
> "75% scratching the surface. Half overwhelmed. But you're using LOTS of tools.
> The problem isn't knowledge — it's coherence. Tactics but no mental model."

### Beat 2: Core Thesis (90 sec)
> "Magic is when the gap between intent and outcome collapses.
> Your job is to learn how to collapse that gap. That's context engineering."

### Beat 3: Three Levels Framework (2 min)
> "Level 1: Conversation. Ephemeral.
> Level 2: Environment. Persistent.
> Level 3: Pipeline. Living.
> Master all three → Meta-OS emerges. A gestalt — greater than the sum of its parts."

### Beat 4: Level 1 Tactics (90 sec)
- Delay the draft (build context first)
- Clarification gates (AI asks YOU questions)
- Adversarial probing (stress-test the output)
- Threshold rubrics (control WHEN AI collapses to answer)

### Beat 5: Level 2 Tactics (2 min)
- Personalization instructions (custom instructions, rules, CLAUDE.md)
- Memory & preferences (compound over time)
- Tool access (what can AI reach?)
- Cognitive guardrails (encode YOUR standards)
- **Caveat:** Signal-to-noise matters

### Beat 6: Level 3 Tactics (2 min)
- Import from the wild (GitHub, prompt libraries, templates)
- Bring data in (webhooks, APIs, scheduled syncs)
- Let it act (scheduled tasks, automated workflows)
- **Principle:** Meta-OS is an accretion of adapted building blocks

### Bridge (30 sec)
> "Logan shows Level 2 in action. Ben shows the full environment. I'll come back to show you what these levels look like fully built out."

### The Pattern (30 sec)
> "The only method you need:
> 1. I have a need
> 2. I ask: 'What are the blocks?'
> 3. Build each block, one conversation at a time
> 4. Connect the blocks"

### Use Case 1: Flight Search (90 sec)
