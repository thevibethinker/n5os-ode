## Phase 2: The Reply Strategy (Ongoing)

### 2.1 Second-Order Reply Technique

**Don't:** Reply directly to mega-influencers (too noisy, you'll be buried)

**Do:** Find the **top-voted reply** on their thread and reply to THAT person

**Example flow:**
1. @naval posts about hiring
2. Top reply from @somefounder gets 500 likes asking about "implementation"
3. You reply to @somefounder: "The real risk isn't the AI — it's the vibes-based hiring process that can't measure output. At Careerspan we solved this by [specific insight]..."

**Why:** You draft off their momentum in a less crowded sub-conversation.

### 2.2 The "Search & Rescue" Reply

Use X Search for phrases like:
- "hiring is broken"
- "how to hire engineers"
- "interviewing sucks"
- "recruiting AI"

Reply to people with <500 followers asking these questions with **genuine, specific help**.

**Why:** High-intent followers. 100 grateful followers > 1,000 passive ones. They engage, share, and boost your algo score.

### 2.3 Volume Guidelines

| Phase | Daily Replies | Original Posts |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| 0-1K followers | 30-50 | 1-2 |
| 1K-5K followers | 15-20 (high-quality) | 2-3 |
| 5K+ | 5-10 (peer networking) | 3-5 |

**The 3:1 Ratio:** For every 1 original post, send 3 replies to larger accounts.

## Phase 3: Content Strategy

### 3.1 The 1-3-1 Thread Format

```
[1 HOOK - pattern interrupt]
[3 BULLETS - pain points or insights]
[1 CONTRARIAN SOLUTION - your unique take]
```

Keep threads under 6 posts. "Crisp-form" beats "long-form" in 2026.

**Example:**
```
The most successful founders I know STOPPED doing structured interviews in 2026.

Here's what they do instead:

• Pain point 1
• Pain point 2  
• Pain point 3

The contrarian solution: [Your Careerspan insight]
```

### 3.2 Content Types by Reach

| Format | Reach Multiplier | Best For |
|--------|------------------|----------|
| Threads | 3x single tweets | Deep insights, stories |
| Native video (60s) | 2-3x text | Face-to-camera tips |
| Screenshots of insights | 2x | Cross-posting LinkedIn gold |
| Single tweets | 1x baseline | Quick takes, engagement |
| External links | 0.3x (penalized) | AVOID unless necessary |

### 3.3 The "Curation Bridge"

Screenshot your best LinkedIn posts or Careerspan artifacts. Post on X with 2-sentence hot take.

**Why:** X penalizes external links. Visual curation lets you bridge depth without losing reach.

### 3.4 Ghost-Writing for Grok

Structure posts with **clear definitions** of emerging terms:
- "What is Agentic Hiring?"
- "The Library of Alexandria for your career"

**Why:** Grok cites recent posts that define terms. You become the authority.

## Phase 4: Network Leverage

### 4.1 The "Anti-Pod" DM Group

**Don't:** Use automated engagement pods (X detects and shadowbans)

**Do:** Form a DM group of 5-8 founder-peers (non-competitors in HR tech)

**Rules:**
- Share your 1 best post of the day
- Everyone gives genuine engagement in first 10 minutes
- Manual only, no automation
- Relevant bios (keyword: "Founder", "HR") signal industry relevance to algo

### 4.2 Getting Retweets from Bigger Accounts

**The Quote-Tweet Bait:**
Post something that bigger accounts can "yes, and" or "actually, here's the nuance"

**The Direct Ask (use sparingly):**
DM a power mutual: "Hey, wrote something on [topic you discussed]. Would love your take if it resonates."

**The Tag Strategy:**
When posting about a topic someone is known for, tag them naturally: "This builds on what @LorimerVentures said about..."

### 4.3 Twitter Spaces as Growth Lever

**Co-hosting > Hosting alone**

- Find a power mutual doing Spaces on hiring/careers
- Offer to co-host on a specific sub-topic
- Cross-pollinate audiences

**Why:** Their audience sees you as peer, not random.

## Phase 6: Mistakes That Kill Growth

### 6.1 Shadowban Triggers
- [ ] Link spam in replies
- [ ] Automated follow/unfollow
- [ ] Identical content posted repeatedly
- [ ] Engagement pods with bot-like behavior
- [ ] Aggressive following (>100/day)

### 6.2 Perception Killers
- [ ] "I actually wrote a thread about this, check it out!" (me-monster)
- [ ] "LFG" / "💯" / "This" (filtered as low-quality)
- [ ] Replying without adding value
- [ ] Messy profile when reply goes viral (they click, they bounce)

### 6.3 Strategic Mistakes
- [ ] Posting into the void without engagement anchors
- [ ] Chasing follower count over engagement rate
- [ ] Inconsistent posting (algo deprioritizes dormant accounts)
