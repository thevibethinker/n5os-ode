# STYLE DIRECTIVE FOR GAMMA

**Color Palette:**
- Primary background: Deep navy (#0D2149) or gradient navy-to-black
- Text: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Soft blue (#4A90D9) — use sparingly for emphasis
- Secondary accent: Light gray (#B8C5D6) for subtle elements

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, uppercase or sentence case, MAX 6 words
- Body: Clean sans-serif, generous line spacing
- NO italic unless for emphasis

**Layout Rules:**
- One core idea per slide
- Left-aligned text (not centered) for readability
- Generous whitespace — don't fill every corner
- Consistent margins throughout

**Image Style:**
- Abstract, geometric shapes
- Dark backgrounds with light elements
- NO stock photos of people
- Prefer: gradients, nodes/connections, building blocks, layers
