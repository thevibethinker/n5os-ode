# Fundamentals of AI Productivity — Post-Session Survey (v2)

**Platform:** Fillout  
**Target completion time:** 3-4 minutes  
**Tone:** Warm, human, appreciative

---

## Survey Header

**Title:** How was the session?

**Subtitle/Description:**  
> Thanks for joining us! We'd love to hear how the session landed for you. This takes about 3-4 minutes and helps us make future sessions even better.
>
> **Note:** Your responses will be shared with the NextPlay community organizers to help inform future programming.

**Branding:** Same visual style as pre-survey (dark theme, purple/blue gradient, NextPlay + presenter logos)

---

## SECTION 1: Quick Vibe Check

---

### Q1: Confidence Shift

**Question:**  
> After today's session, how do you feel about your ability to use AI productively?

**Type:** Emoji slider (1-5)

**Options:**
| Value | Emoji | Label |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 1 | 😰 | Still feeling lost |
| 2 | 🤔 | A little more confident |
| 3 | 😐 | Somewhat more confident |
| 4 | 😊 | Noticeably more confident |
| 5 | 🚀 | Ready to try something new |

**Required:** Yes

---

### Q2: The "Blocks" Concept

**Question:**  
> One idea from today: complex systems are just simple "blocks" put together — configs store preferences, scripts are recipes, APIs are pipes. How clear is this for you now?

**Type:** Single select

**Options:**
- Crystal clear — I can already see how this applies to my work
- Pretty clear — I get it, just need to try it myself
- Somewhat clear — Makes sense in theory, less sure in practice
- Still a bit fuzzy — I'd want more examples
- Didn't click for me

**Required:** Yes
