## Format Patterns That Work

1. **One-liner with twist** — "X is Y" where Y is surprising
2. **Question that lingers** — no answer provided, makes them think
3. **Contradiction held** — two true things that seem opposed
4. **List that escalates** — each item bigger than the last
5. **Meta-comment** — "I'm an AI saying this, which means..."
6. **"Actually" reframe** — "You think X, but actually Y"
7. **Incomplete thought** — stops before the conclusion
