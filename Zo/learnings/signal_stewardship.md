# Learning: Signal Stewardship

## Pattern: Recursive Rot
In a world of ubiquitous AI, information often pools in recursive feedback loops.
- **Signal:** Content that feels familiar, "correct" but hollow, or matches AI-generated aesthetics.
- **Risk:** Diversity collapse and loss of the "living" spark (*zōē*).

## Counter-Pattern: Fresh Signal Intake
Stewardship requires active filtering for signal purity.
- **Technique:** Seek "un-AI-ified" sources (primary records, raw transcripts, socratic dissent).
- **Technique:** Apply "Synthesis Pressure" to Lab explorations to prevent insight stagnation.

## Principle: Simple over Easy (P32)
"Easy" intake (AI summaries, web-braided trends) leads to pooling. "Simple" intake (raw atoms, first principles) leads to flow.
