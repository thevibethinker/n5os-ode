## What to Emphasize with Your Careerspan Coach

### 1. **ADM Messaging Platform (Amazon)**
This is your best story. You owned Topic-Based Messaging and Group-Based Messaging end-to-end—design through implementation. Dig into:
- The problem space: Why did developers need this? What was broken before?
- Your design decisions: Why topic-based? Why group-based? Tradeoffs?
- Cross-platform complexity: Google Android + Amazon FireOS in one abstraction
- Scale: How many devices? What throughput?

*Why it matters*: Guickly is building platform infrastructure that other AI companies will build on. Your experience designing developer-facing platforms (A3L) is directly transferable.

### 2. **The "Microservices Instead of Migration" Decision**
You analyzed query patterns and proposed a microservices solution instead of actually migrating a table. This is exactly the kind of thinking Guickly wants:
- What was the original plan? Why was migration the default?
- How did you identify the alternative? What analysis did you do?
- How did you convince stakeholders?

*Why it matters*: Shows you challenge assumptions and find elegant solutions. The founder will love this.

### 3. **Multi-Tenancy Design (Allen Digital)**
You just designed multi-tenancy for Identity and Resource Management. Explore:
- Why is multi-tenancy hard? What are the failure modes?
- What patterns did you use?
- How do you handle isolation, security, data separation?

*Why it matters*: If Guickly scales, they'll need multi-tenancy. This is future-state relevant.

### 4. **The Citibank Automation Story**
Don't skip this. You took an 8-9 man-hour daily process and reduced it to 5 minutes with a Python module. This is 0→1 automation:
- What was the manual process? Who was doing it?
- How did you identify the opportunity?
- What was the reaction when you shipped it?

*Why it matters*: Guickly is literally building "automation for AI workflows." This story proves you think in automation terms.
