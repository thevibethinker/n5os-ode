## What This Is

These are **pre-coaching briefs** for four candidates being submitted for the Guickly Founding Engineer role. Each guide tells the candidate which aspects of their professional experience to emphasize when working with the Careerspan AI career coach.

## Why These Exist

Guickly's founder has a reputation for being exacting. He uses a detailed screening form that filters for specific traits:
- 0→1 building experience (not just feature work)
- Comfort with ambiguity ("There is no Spec Doc. There is a problem and there is you.")
- High intensity, in-office, timezone flexibility
- Hands-on coding (80%+), not management
- Genuine enthusiasm for early-stage chaos

These guides help candidates understand what stories and experiences to surface in their Careerspan sessions so they can present themselves in the most relevant light for THIS specific opportunity.

## Guide Structure

Each 1-page guide contains:
1. **Narrative Arc** — The candidate's positioning in one sentence
2. **What to Emphasize** — 4 stories/experiences to dig into with the Careerspan AI
3. **What to De-Emphasize** — Experiences that might hurt or distract
4. **The Question They'll Face** — The obvious concern and how to address it
5. **Form Response Notes** — Specific guidance for the Guickly screening form

## Next Steps

These guides are delivered to candidates before their Careerspan AI coaching session. The AI coach will then help them unpack the recommended stories, identify supporting anecdotes, and prepare for the screening form and interviews.
