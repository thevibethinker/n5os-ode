## Strategic Context

Alex emphasized the short-term value of offering a managed recruiting service to bootstrap revenue and accelerate supply growth. The long-term vision remains a data-first career platform; the proposed short-term tactical product (managed recruiting + embedded community sourcing) aligns with the product's need to capture longitudinal, high-signal candidate data.

Key positioning insight: Lead with outcomes ("4 high-quality candidates"), not a long feature list. Use scarcity/trust (only send a small curated set) to build reputation rather than huge candidate lists.
