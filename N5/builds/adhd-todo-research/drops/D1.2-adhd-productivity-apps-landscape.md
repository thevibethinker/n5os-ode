# D1.2: ADHD Productivity Apps — Landscape Analysis

## Objective
Survey the landscape of productivity apps specifically designed for or popular with ADHD users. Understand what exists, what works, what fails, and why.

## Research Questions
1. What are the top ADHD-focused productivity tools? (Focusmate, Goblin Tools, Llama Life, etc.)
2. What features do ADHD users praise vs. criticize in productivity apps?
3. What's the difference between "ADHD-friendly" design vs. neurotypical assumptions?
4. What do user reviews and ADHD community discussions say about these tools?
5. Are there any tools that implement "external accountability" or "blocking" mechanisms?

## Apps to Research
- Focusmate (body doubling)
- Goblin Tools (task breakdown)
- Llama Life (time boxing)
- Structured / Tiimo (visual scheduling)
- Beeminder / StickK (commitment devices)
- Freedom / Cold Turkey (blocking)
- Forest (gamification)
- Habitica (RPG-style gamification)
- Any others discovered during research

## Sources to Prioritize
- r/ADHD subreddit discussions
- ADHD Twitter/X community
- Product Hunt reviews
- ADDitude Magazine tool recommendations
- App Store / Play Store reviews filtered for ADHD mentions

## Deliverable
A competitive analysis with:
1. **Executive Summary** — Top 3-5 insights for our system
2. **App-by-App Analysis** — What each does, strengths, weaknesses
3. **Pattern Analysis** — What works across apps, what fails
4. **Gaps** — What's missing in the market that we should address
5. **Feature Inspiration** — Specific features to steal/adapt
