### 6. Launch Decision

Two options:

**Option A: Deploy on V's Zo (va.zo.computer)**
- Pros: Immediate availability, easier monitoring
- Cons: Security surface area (even read-only adds exposure)

**Option B: Export to Zoeputer**
- Pros: Complete isolation, purpose-built
- Cons: Requires Zoeputer setup, separate monitoring

V decides based on:
- Comfort with security model
- Zoeputer readiness
- Timeline
