## Design Brief

**Who is V (Vrijen Attawar)?**
- Founder of Careerspan (career coaching + recruiting tech)
- Decade of experience as career coach
- ~4 years in tech/entrepreneurship
- Non-technical but pushing boundaries

**What should the landing page convey?**
- Professional but distinctive (not generic corporate)
- Credibility and expertise
- A sense of someone who builds and ships
- Easy way to contact or connect

**Sections to include:**
1. Hero with name/title and brief value prop
2. About section (brief bio)
3. What I do / Current focus (Careerspan)
4. Ways to connect (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, email)
5. Minimal footer

**Aesthetic direction:**
- Dark mode preferred (like NYSKI)
- Modern, clean typography
- Subtle animations/interactions
- Mobile-first responsive
