## Synthesis Guidelines

When writing `analysis.md`:
1. Lead with **key findings** (3-5 bullet executive summary)
2. Ground in **quantitative data** (cite specific numbers from D1.2)
3. Integrate **Level Upper insights** explicitly (call them out: "🔮 Novel perspective: ...")
4. Include **actionable recommendations** based on synthesis
5. End with **questions for further exploration**
