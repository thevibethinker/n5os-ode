## The "Untapped Talent" Argument

Frame it as: **"The most untapped talent source is the one you already have."**

Supporting points:
- Your managers aren't talent spotters—they're managers
- Managers don't have time or training to deeply understand each employee's aspirations
- Performance reviews capture what someone *did*, not what they *want* or *could do*
- You need: (1) an objective lens beyond the manager's view, (2) the employee's own reflection on their career

## Coaching Captures What Reviews Miss

From the PDF V shared—coaching conversations reveal:

- Gap between stated ambitions and follow-through
- Pattern recognition across career decisions

This is intelligence that performance reviews and skills taxonomies never capture.
