## Your Positioning Angle: Internal Mobility

**The thesis:** Careerspan is uniquely suited to be an internal talent marketplace / career development layer for enterprises.

**Why this matters to acquirers:**
- Internal mobility is THE hot topic (quiet quitting, retention crisis, skills-based hiring)
- Gloat, Eightfold, Workday are all building internal marketplace features
- But nobody has the SIGNAL CAPTURE layer—the qualitative data about what employees actually want and can do

**The Careerspan advantage:**
- Story-based profiling reveals career aspirations, hidden skills, growth trajectory
- Coaching conversations surface what performance reviews miss
- Employees who complete profiles demonstrate self-awareness, initiative (signal itself)
- Platform already proven to get users to do deep reflection (40 min avg)

**Target acquirers for this angle:**
- Gloat (Series D, $90M) — internal talent marketplace
- Eightfold.ai (Series E, $410M) — AI talent intelligence
- Workday — already acquired HiredScore, building career hub
- Lattice — performance + career
