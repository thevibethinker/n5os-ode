## Template Structure

### voice-lessons.md Template

```markdown
---
created: 2026-01-19
description: Voice lessons for personalized writing style
---

# Voice Lessons

Your AI learns from your writing edits over time. This file stores lessons about your preferred style.

## How It Works

1. When you edit AI-generated text, the system can learn from the changes
2. Lessons are categorized by content type (email, social-post, etc.)
3. retrieve_voice_lessons.py queries lessons for a given content type

## Lesson Format

Each lesson has:
- **content_types**: Which content types this applies to
- **pattern**: What to do (preferred) or avoid (anti-pattern)
- **example**: Concrete before/after demonstration
- **rationale**: Why this matters for your voice

## Your Lessons

<!-- Add your voice lessons below -->

**Pattern:** PREFER: Direct opening without pleasantries

**Rationale:** Respects reader's time, gets to the point
```
