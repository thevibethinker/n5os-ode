# D9: Hotline-as-Intake — SMS/Call Conversion Funnel

## Objective

Replace the traditional application form with a phone/SMS-based intake funnel. Instead of filling out fields, prospective members call or text a dedicated number to start their FounderMaxxing journey. This is both the qualifying conversation AND the intake mechanism.

## Why This Is Better Than a Form

1. **Lower friction** — Founders are busy. Texting "I'm interested" is 100x easier than filling out 8 form fields.
2. **Higher signal** — A live conversation reveals more about fit than any form. Tone, urgency, specificity of questions all signal quality.
3. **Re-engagement built in** — Once someone texts, we have a channel. Reminder texts, follow-ups, session previews — all organic, not email-drip.
4. **Differentiator** — Nobody does intake by phone. This IS the FounderMaxxing experience: personal, human-in-the-loop, AI-augmented.
5. **Lazy convenience** — Founders can do it from their couch, in an Uber, between meetings. Come back to it whenever.

## Architecture

### Intake Flow
1. Visitor sees CTA on landing page: "Call or text to see if you qualify"
2. They call/text the intake number
3. Zo answers — loaded with FounderMaxxing context, V's voice, qualifying criteria
4. Zo has a natural conversation that covers:
   - What they're building
   - Current AI usage / frustration
   - What they're hoping to get out of FMXG
   - Whether they meet baseline criteria (founder/operator, genuine AI interest)
5. Zo generates a structured intake summary → sent to V for review
6. V approves/rejects → system sends response to applicant
7. If approved → payment link + onboarding

### Technical Components
- **Dedicated phone number** (separate from Vibe Thinker Hotline)
- **Intake webhook** — routes to Zo with FMXG intake system prompt
- **System prompt** includes:
  - FounderMaxxing value prop and qualifying criteria
  - V's conversational style and philosophy
  - Structured data extraction (name, company, stage, AI usage, goals)
  - Graceful handling of unqualified leads (redirect to free content, newsletter, etc.)
- **Intake summary → V** — via SMS or email, one-tap approve/reject
- **Applicant response** — automated text with next steps (payment link, onboarding calendar)
- **SQLite or Airtable** — structured storage of intake conversations + outcomes

### Integration with Landing Page (D5)
- Primary CTA: "Text or call [number] to see if you're a fit"
- Secondary CTA: "Ready now? Apply directly" (keeps form path as fallback)
- The hotline number should be visually prominent — not buried in a footer

### Integration with Member Hotline (D8)
- Same telephony infrastructure, different number/context
- Intake hotline = pre-purchase qualifying
- Member hotline = post-purchase support
- Could eventually merge into one system that recognizes member vs prospect

## Scope Control
- V1: Text-only intake (simpler, Zo handles well)
- V2: Voice intake (requires more telephony work)
- V1 is sufficient for launch
