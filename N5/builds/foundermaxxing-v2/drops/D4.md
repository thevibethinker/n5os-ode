# D4: Venn Diagram + Visual Assets

## Objective

Generate the key visual assets for the FounderMaxxing landing page and marketing materials.

## Asset 1: The N-of-1 Venn Diagram

V's unique positioning as a 3-circle Venn intersection:
1. **Decade of career coaching** — deep people/talent instinct
2. **4 years building a tech company** — founder operational reality
3. **Consumer AI power user** — not just ChatGPT, full agentic stack (Zo Computer)

The intersection is the story: V isn't an AI researcher, a course seller, or a tech bro. He's a career coach who became a founder who became an AI-native builder. That combination doesn't exist elsewhere.

### Design Requirements
- Dark theme (black/near-black background) — matches brand
- Clean, modern, NOT clip-art
- Subtle color differentiation for each circle (warm tones preferred — avoid default blue)
- Text labels inside or adjacent to circles
- Center intersection should be visually prominent — this is V's positioning
- Center label: something like "FounderMaxxing" or "The N-of-1"
- Professional enough for LinkedIn/X sharing
- Generate in high-res

### Upload
Upload to zo.space assets at `/images/foundermaxxing-venn.png`

## Asset 2: Social Share Card (OG Image)

For link previews when sharing the landing page:
- 1200x630px (OG standard)

- Upload to `/images/foundermaxxing-og.png`
