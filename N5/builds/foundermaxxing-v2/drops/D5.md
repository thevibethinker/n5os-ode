## Page Architecture

The page tells a STORY. Not a feature list. Not a SaaS template.

### Narrative Flow (refined by D2 findings)

**Beat 1: The Problem (Visceral)**
AI is moving fast. You feel it. You're a founder — you don't have time to take courses or read newsletters. But you know that if you don't figure this out, someone who did will eat your lunch.

**Beat 2: Why You Can't Figure This Out Alone**
The tools change every week. The real unlock isn't learning tools — it's building heuristics. A meta OS for evaluating, adopting, and integrating AI into how you actually work.

**Beat 3: V's Credibility (Show, Don't Tell)**
- Talks to founders at Enrich (CTOs, technical leaders) and Not Another CEO about AI
- Zero lines of code written — everything built through AI
- GitHub profile as proof (link or screenshot)
- "The best non-technical coder most people have met"

**Beat 4: What This Actually Is**
Weekly live builds. Not lectures. Not courses. You show up, we build something real. Three phases per session. You leave with something that works.

**Beat 5: The Zoputer Reveal**
This is co-led by an AI. Zoputer is V's Zo — it knows the curriculum, reviews your work between sessions, and is available 24/7 for co-building. It picks up what sessions put down.

**Beat 6: "World's first agentic AI productivity training for founders"**
Not a claim made lightly. This isn't "how to use ChatGPT." This is building real systems with real AI infrastructure. The training itself runs on the same infrastructure you'll learn to build.

**Beat 7: Pricing + Application**
- Founding 15: $100/mo (first 15, locked forever)
- Standard: $300/mo
- Zo-to-Zo standalone: $150/mo
- Application form — filtered for loyalty, engagement, genuine passion

## Design Requirements

### Aesthetic: Dark Editorial
- Near-black background (#0a0a0a or similar)
- Warm accent color — NOT blue. Think amber, copper, or warm white.
- One serif font for headlines (or a distinctive sans-serif, not Inter/Geist)
- Body text left-aligned, proper line-height
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Asymmetric layouts where it makes sense

### Anti-Slop (from frontend-design skill)
- NO gradient hero
- NO 3-column equal card grid
- NO "Hero → Features → Pricing → CTA" template
- NO emojis as design elements
- NO centered-everything
- NO generic blue

### Mobile
- Must work beautifully on mobile
- Touch targets 44px+
- Form must be easy to fill on phone
