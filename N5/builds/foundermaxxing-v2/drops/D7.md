For V to send to founders he knows personally. Variants:

### 2. LinkedIn Post (1-2 variants)
Announcing FounderMaxxing publicly. Tone: confident, not salesy. The post itself should demonstrate V's AI fluency.

### 3. X/Twitter Thread (1 variant)
For V's X presence. Shorter, punchier. May reference the "zero lines of code" angle or the GitHub proof.

### 4. Email to V's Network (1 variant)
Longer-form for email blast. Can include more detail about the format, Zoputer, the meta OS concept.

### 5. Substack Teaser (if Observer tier launches)
Short piece that gives a taste of FounderMaxxing thinking. Free content that drives paid conversions.

- Finance-crossover metaphors (portfolio, returns, compounding, optionality)
- Direct address ("you")
- Intellectual density — say more with less
- NO exclamation points
- NO emojis as decoration

- Raw energy where appropriate (V said "rockstar" energy, not polished corporate)
