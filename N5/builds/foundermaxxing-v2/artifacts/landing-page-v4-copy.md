## SECTION 4: OBJECTION HANDLING

### Section Header

The reasons you won't do this are the reasons you should.

### The Objections

**"I don't have time."**\
We agree—that's why we've started The Vibe Pill community. It's for founders who want to level up their AI proficiency at a consistent rate without having to coordinate all that learning themselves. 

You don't even need to attend the sessions if you can't make it—after each session, the post-game summary will be sent directly to your Zo. 

**"My current setup works."**\
Works isn't a competitive advantage. You're optimized for what was considered productive six months ago but too comfortable to make a change—until it's too late. The founders who are going to eat your lunch are here and they already think in AI systems. Do you? 

**"I can figure this out myself."**\
No doubt—I certainly did.

But no person is an island or possesses a monopoly on good ideas. 

So consider how much faster you'd go learning from V's experiences, having ready access to an on-demand AI co-builder, and benefitting from conversations with other like-minded founders. 

**"I'm already good at AI."**\
Sure, but forever? The landscape resets every quarter. The question that matters is whether you're good at the patterns underneath, or just at using the tools that just evolved.

## COPY PRINCIPLES APPLIED

1. **No hedge words.** No "might," "could," "help," "we think," "see if you qualify." Every claim is stated as fact.
2. **Founder register.** Short sentences. Direct address. Respect for the reader's time and intelligence.
3. **The page is proof.** The quality of the design and writing IS the argument. If the page is mid, the pitch is dead.
4. **Curse once, in the right spot.** "I got pretty fucking good at it" — V's voice, V's moment, maximum impact.
5. **Fewer words, harder.** Every section could be read in under 10 seconds. The bento cards are scannable. The objections are one-paragraph kills.
6. **No car salesman energy.** "Reach out" not "see if you qualify." "Apply now" not "subscribe." "A conversation" not "a screening call."
7. **"on AI" dropped from hero.** The line hits harder without the qualifier — the reader fills in the blank themselves, which makes it land deeper. "Falling behind" is universal. It haunts.
8. **Dual gallery = dual proof angles.** Technical builders validate the expertise ("he knows his shit"). Non-technical builders validate the empathy ("he can actually teach it"). Together they answer every objection a founder has before they scroll past.
