## Objective

Develop **R08 (Venture Idea)** — the block that extracts business model sketches, market size signals, and non-Careerspan business ideas.

## R08 Domain: Business/Venture Thinking (Non-Careerspan)

### What R08 Sees
- Business ideas outside Careerspan scope
- Business model sketches
- Market size signals
- Competitive moat ideas
- Resource requirement estimates
- "Someone should build this" thoughts

### What R08 Ignores
- Careerspan features → R05
- Market analysis for Careerspan → R04
- Strategic Careerspan decisions → R03

## Analysis Dimensions for R08

1. **Idea Type:**
   - Full business concept
   - Business model innovation
   - Market opportunity spotted
   - Problem worth solving

2. **Market Size Signal:**
   - Large (obvious big market)
   - Medium (niche but viable)
   - Unknown (needs research)

3. **Why Not Careerspan:**
   - Different market
   - Different skillset needed
   - Conflicts with core
   - Just different

4. **Resource Intensity:**
   - Bootstrappable
   - Needs funding
   - Needs team

5. **V's Unfair Advantage:**
   - Strong (domain expertise)
   - Moderate (adjacent knowledge)
   - Weak (just an idea)
