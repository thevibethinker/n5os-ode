## Objective

Develop **R09 (Content Idea)** — the block that extracts content thesis, audience, format, and publishing opportunities.

## R09 Domain: Content/Publishing Ideas

### What R09 Sees
- Blog post ideas
- Social media content angles
- Newsletter topics
- Speaking/podcast opportunities
- "People need to hear this" moments
- Teaching opportunities
- Thought leadership angles

### What R09 Ignores
- The actual learning → R02
- Market analysis → R04
- Synthesis frameworks → R06

## Analysis Dimensions for R09

1. **Content Type:**
   - Long-form (blog, article)
   - Short-form (social, thread)
   - Interactive (talk, podcast)
   - Educational (course, guide)

2. **Target Audience:**
   - Founders/entrepreneurs
   - Career professionals
   - Recruiters/HR
   - Tech community
   - General

3. **Uniqueness:**
   - Novel take
   - Contrarian view
   - Personal experience
   - Synthesis of known

4. **Timeliness:**
   - Time-sensitive (news hook)
   - Evergreen
   - Seasonal

5. **Effort to Produce:**
   - Quick (< 1 hour)
   - Medium (hours)
   - Substantial (days)
