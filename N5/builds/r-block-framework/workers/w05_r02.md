## R02 Domain: Learning & Knowledge Development

### What R02 Sees
- New skills being acquired or needed
- Knowledge gaps identified
- Mental model updates ("I used to think X, now I think Y")
- Expertise development trajectory
- Learning transfer opportunities (applying X to Y)
- Technical understanding breakthroughs
- Conceptual clarifications

### What R02 Ignores
- Strategic implications → R03
- Market context → R04
- Emotional reactions to learning → R01
- Content to teach others → R09

## Analysis Dimensions for R02

1. **Learning Type:**
   - Skill acquisition (doing)
   - Knowledge acquisition (knowing)
   - Mental model update (understanding)
   - Framework development (organizing)

2. **Source:**
   - Direct experience
   - Conversation/teaching
   - Reading/research
   - Failure/mistake

3. **Depth:**
   - Surface (awareness)
   - Working (can apply)
   - Deep (can teach)

4. **Transfer Potential:**
   - Domain-specific only
   - Adjacent domain transfer
   - General principle

5. **Gap vs Gain:**
   - New knowledge gained
   - Gap identified (need to learn)
   - Both
