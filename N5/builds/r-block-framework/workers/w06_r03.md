## R03 Domain: Strategic Thinking

### What R03 Sees
- Directional decisions ("we should go X not Y")
- Resource allocation choices
- Opportunity cost analysis
- Strategic bets and rationale
- Path dependencies identified
- Trap doors (irreversible decisions)
- Positioning choices
- Competitive strategy

### What R03 Ignores
- Market signals (input to strategy) → R04
- Product features → R05
- Personal feelings about strategy → R01
- Business model innovation → R08

## Analysis Dimensions for R03

1. **Decision Type:**
   - Direction (where to go)
   - Resource (what to invest)
   - Positioning (how to compete)
   - Timing (when to act)

2. **Reversibility:**
   - Easily reversible
   - Costly to reverse
   - Trap door (irreversible)

3. **Time Horizon:**
   - Tactical (weeks)
   - Operational (months)
   - Strategic (years)

4. **Confidence:**
   - High conviction
   - Hypothesis to test
   - Speculation

5. **Dependencies:**
   - Standalone decision
   - Depends on prior decision
   - Enables future decisions
