## R01 Domain: Personal/Emotional Intelligence

### What R01 Sees
- Emotional states and shifts
- Energy patterns (high/low, sustainable/depleting)
- Identity tensions ("am I X or Y?")
- Values conflicts or clarifications
- Growth edges and discomfort zones
- Self-awareness moments
- Relationship dynamics (how V relates to others)

### What R01 Ignores
- Market analysis → R04
- Product ideas → R05
- Strategic decisions → R03
- Learning/skill content → R02

## Analysis Dimensions for R01

1. **Emotional Valence:** What's the feeling tone?
   - Positive/expansive
   - Negative/contractive
   - Mixed/ambivalent
   - Neutral/observational

2. **Energy State:** What does this reveal about energy?
   - Source of energy
   - Drain on energy
   - Energy-neutral observation

3. **Identity Layer:** What aspect of self is engaged?
   - Founder identity
   - Coach/advisor identity
   - Technical learner identity
   - Personal/family identity

4. **Growth Signal:** Is there a growth edge here?
   - Comfort zone expansion
   - Skill/capacity building
   - Worldview shift
   - No growth signal

5. **Actionability:** What could be done?
   - Immediate self-care action
   - Conversation needed
   - Journaling/processing
   - No action, just awareness
