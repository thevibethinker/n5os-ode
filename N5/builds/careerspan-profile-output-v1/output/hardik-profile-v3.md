# What The Meeting Will Reveal

*These questions can't be answered from a resume. 30 minutes will tell you.*

### 1. Is the motivation real?

He's in a senior ML role at Keysight working with Fortune 500 clients. Why FlowFuse? Why low-code AI? Why now?

**Ask:** "You're building ML platforms for Apple and Samsung. What specifically excites you about Node-RED and low-code AI?"

### 2. Does he fit your operating model?

His entire career is enterprise (AmEx, Keysight, Flipkart) — sync-heavy, process-heavy, defined roles. FlowFuse is async-first, low-structure, "disagree and commit."

**Ask:** "Walk me through how you'd approach a week where no one tells you what to do and your manager is in a different timezone."

### 3. What does he want to become?

He's scaled a team from 0 to 20 and mentored interns. Does he want to stay IC or move to management? FlowFuse may not have AI team lead opportunities for years.

**Ask:** "In 3 years, do you see yourself going deep as a principal engineer, or leading an AI team?"

### 4. Does he know how he fails?

Smart people fail in predictable ways. Self-awareness here is a strong signal.

**Ask:** "Tell me about a project that failed or underdelivered. What was your role in that failure?"
