## The Anti-Resume Framework

The output format we designed (see `file 'N5/builds/careerspan-profile-output-v1/output/ANTI-RESUME-FRAMEWORK-v1.md'`):

### Panel Structure
1. **The Thesis** — 80/20 panel. Who they are, what you get, the trade-off. 4-5 sentences max.
2. **Hard Skills: Bar Assessment** — JD requirements mapped to Cleared/Gap/Probe with evidence
3. **What Makes Them Different** — 2-4 genuine differentiators with proof points
4. **The Holes** — What the resume CAN'T tell you. Known unknowns + interview priorities
5. **Reasons to Say Yes / Reasons to Say No** — Decision support for the hiring manager

### Key Principles
- Trade-offs build credibility (no perfect candidates)
- Specificity over generality (evidence for every claim)
- 80/20 rule (Panel 1 must stand alone)
- Employer-centric framing (what's in it for THEM)
