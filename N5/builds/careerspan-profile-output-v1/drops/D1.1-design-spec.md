# D1.1 — Careerspan design capture

## Color palette
| Role | Description | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary surface | Crisp canvas for the page and cards | `#FEFEFE` |
| Primary text / emphasis | Almost-black for headings and body copy | `#111111` |
| Soft neutral background | Warm layers for section separators and translucent cards | `#F7F7F7` / `#F3F3F1` |
| Brand gradient | Radiant magenta/purple glow used in hero, overlays, and badge glows | `#991F6E` → `#D511FD` → `#EDD5E5` |
| Action accent | Warm orange for secondary CTAs, badges, and highlight strokes | `#FE6A2E` |
| Interaction accent | Electric blue for score rings, link underlines, and hover states | `#405DE5` with supporting `#1B2BB8` |
| Supportive tones | Mint / forest hues for success chips and micro-illustrations | `#CCE8E0`, `#156949` |
| Subdued text | Medium gray used for captions, footer links, and metadata | `#989897`

## Typography
- **Hero & section headers**: Inter Display or Inter Bold (700). Headline sizes range from ~48px (hero) to 32px for smaller section titles with a compact 1.1–1.2 line height that keeps statements punchy.
- **Body copy**: Inter 400 at ~18px with ~1.5 line height for readability. Primary text stays at `#111111`; supporting copy can drop to `#494949`/`#616161` where necessary.
- **Microcaps & stats**: DM Mono 500 (or Source Code Pro 600 in badge artefacts) at 12–14px for score cards, stats, and label badges. Keep letter spacing tight (0.02em) so the mono treatment aligns with the high-tech tone.
- **Buttons / CTA text**: Inter 600 at ~16px, optionally uppercased (e.g., “START LANDING SMARTER MATCHES”) with generous tracking to convey confidence.

## Spacing + layout rhythm
- Sections sit on generous vertical cushions (roughly 96–160px between major blocks, ensuring the hero feels detached from the nav).
- Internal card padding ranges between 32px and 40px; multi-column rows maintain 48px gutters so cards breathe side by side.
- CTA rows, badges, and icon groups are separated by 24px gaps, keeping compact clusters readable even within dense modules.

## Visual style summary
The homepage layers a luminous magenta-purple glow over a crisp white canvas, blending cinematic gradients with softly rounded cards and gentle drop shadows. Bold Display headings contrast with approachable Inter body copy while orange and blue accents salsa through CTAs and score rings, preventing the calm palette from feeling flat. Recurring panels (hero with high-level score, benefit cards, testimonial grid, guided steps) are orchestrated with steady whitespace so the storytelling feels linear yet premium.

## Recurring UI patterns
1. **Hero panel**: Centered headline, gradient background lights, and a large card displaying the career score, CTA pills, and meta badges. Rounded corners (~24px) and floating iconography reinforce the premium feel.
2. **Benefit / value cards**: Three- to four-column tiles that share a soft background fill, subtle shadow, and icon + copy pairs. Buttons respond with blue outlines and light shadows.
3. **Step-by-step CTA**: Numbered vertical stack with 32px spacing and small descriptive copy, often accompanied by minimalist illustrations.
4. **Testimonials**: Grid of quote cards with generous padding, oversized quote text, and grey subtext for names/roles.
5. **Footer**: A row of gray links with underline-on-hover, anchored by the Careerspan logo to reinforce branding.

## Gamma styling hints
- **Hero gradient**: Use Gamma’s `surface.gradient` token to represent the purple-magenta-orange glow (`#991F6E` → `#D511FD` → `#EDD5E5`). Layer it behind white cards (surface: `#FEFEFE`) with soft shadows to simulate the floating hero card.
- **Buttons and CTAs**: Map Gamma’s `primary.fill` to `#405DE5`, `primary.text` to `#FEFEFE`, and keep `primary.cornerRadius` at `24px`. Secondary buttons should use `#FE6A2E` with `#111111` text and the same radius to maintain visual continuity.
- **Cards & panels**: Assign Gamma’s `surface` to `#FEFEFE`, `surface.lowlight` to `#F7F7F7`, and `surface.border` to `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`. Stick to 32px padding internally and 24px spacing between stacked elements to mimic the site’s rhythm before Gamma renders the layout.
- **Accent chips & badges**: Route success chips to `#CCE8E0` / `#156949` and use DM Mono/Source Code Mono for their copy to preserve the tech-meets-human tone.
- **Typography tokens**: Set `heading.fontFamily` to Inter Display, `body.fontFamily` to Inter, and `mono.fontFamily` to DM Mono. Keep heading weights at 700, body at 400, and microcaps at 500 with 0.02em letter-spacing so Gamma outputs match the source’s tension between polished and accessible.
