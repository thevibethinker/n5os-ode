# D2.0: Remarkable Scan (Pre-Rubric)

## Objective

BEFORE applying the evaluation rubrics, scan the candidate data for what's genuinely surprising, unusual, or rare.

## Purpose

This prevents the rubric from constraining what we notice. Some of the most compelling candidate signals don't fit neatly into JD requirements.

## Instructions

1. Read ALL candidate data files
2. Ask yourself: "What's surprising here? What don't I see often?"
3. Look for:
   - Unusual career transitions
   - Rare skill combinations
   - Unexpected achievements
   - Non-obvious strengths
   - Interesting contradictions
   - Things that would make someone say "huh, that's different"

4. Do NOT filter through the JD lens yet — just observe
