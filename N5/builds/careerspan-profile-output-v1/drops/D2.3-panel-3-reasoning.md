2. **Team scaling from 0 to 20 members**
   - **Reason:** Leadership experience is valuable but not uncommon. What makes this notable is that it happened within the context of founding-to-scale platform ownership, which is already captured in Differentiator 2.

3. **Mentoring 4 SDE interns**
   - **Reason:** Mentoring is table stakes for senior engineers. While it demonstrates leadership, it doesn't differentiate H. from other qualified candidates.

4. **Hackathon wins (Top-5 India Innovation, 3rd GrowthHack)**
   - **Reason:** External recognition is valuable but not exceptional enough on its own. These achievements support the elite foundation but don't stand alone as differentiators.

5. **ML model achieving 35% productivity improvement**
   - **Reason:** Strong business impact, but this is a single project outcome. The broader pattern of proven ROI across multiple contexts (AmEx + Keysight) is more compelling.

6. **IIT Delhi degree**
   - **Reason:** While prestigious, an IIT degree alone doesn't differentiate—thousands of engineers graduate from IITs annually. What's rare is the **combination** of elite academics with founding-level platform experience and validated production ROI.

## Selection Rationale

These three differentiators were chosen because they represent **rare combinations** rather than individual strengths:

1. **Elite academics + business impact** is unusual—most top-tier engineers excel at theory OR practice, not both at this level
2. **Founding-to-scale platform experience** is genuinely rare—most engineers join existing platforms; H. built one from scratch and scaled it to enterprise customers
3. **Full-stack AI delivery** across LLM + traditional ML + UX is uncommon—most AI engineers specialize narrowly

The selection prioritized **breadth of impact** (platform-level ownership, measurable ROI) over **depth in specific technologies** (which are captured in Panel 2 hard skills). These differentiators answer the question: "What makes H. memorable beyond the list of technologies they know?"
