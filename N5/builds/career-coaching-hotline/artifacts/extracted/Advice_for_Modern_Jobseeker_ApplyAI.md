Youʼre extending your job hunt each time you …

1.   Jump straight into applying for jobs without doing enough groundwork

2.   Prioritize applying to “moreˮ jobs over applying more effectively

3.   Spend time redesigning materials that donʼt need to be designed (donʼt
     submit Canva resumes or PDFʼs to application systems)

4.   Submit the same or similar materials everywhere - regardless of role or
     company

5.   Are reluctant to network, particularly through cold outreach

6.   Donʼt prepare for interviews until he or she is invited

7.   Donʼt approach job hunting systematically and with attention to detail

Youʼre shortening your job hunt each time you …


1.   Apply to the right jobs and present themselves in the right way

2.   Create ATS and reader friendly materials tailored to the role and
     company

3.   Set yourself apart through their knowledge of the company or the role

4.   Improve your chances of getting picked for interviews by networking

5.   Prepare for a variety of interview questions and situations

6.   Put in consistent and systematic effort
