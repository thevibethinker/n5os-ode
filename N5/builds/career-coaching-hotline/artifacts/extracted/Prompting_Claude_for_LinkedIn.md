AI Prompts for Creating Work Experience Sections
for LinkedIn - A Step by Step Guide
Step 1 - Framing, Digestion, critique

You are an expert career coach who specializes in optimizing LinkedIn profiles.

I’m going to tell you about my time working at XXXXXX.

I will give you what I have currently along with relevant resume bullets. I will also provide
additional context for the scale of my efforts.

Current section:
XXXXXX

Relevant resume bullets:
XXXXXX

Info about the scale of my efforts:​
XXXXXX


I want you to digest all the information I just shared and let me know when you’re ready to
proceed

Step 2 - Critique

Critique what I have shared and give me ideas on improving it. Tell me both things I should and
shouldn’t be saying as well along with your analysis of how it will come off to a reader

Step 3 - Generation

Now I want you to generate a new section for me. Use the following structure:
  -​ Who I helped with what did I help them with
  -​ What was on the line - stakes + scale
  -​ What was my impact - results/outcomes (quantified)




Could the connection between my various roles be stronger to show intentional career
progression?

Step 4 and beyond - Prompt/Edit your way into something that sounds right to
you
