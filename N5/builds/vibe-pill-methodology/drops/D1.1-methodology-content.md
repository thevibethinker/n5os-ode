# D1.1 — Write Full Methodology Page Content

## Objective

Write the complete content for The Vibe Pill's methodology page. This is the intellectual backbone of the product — it needs to articulate WHY this approach works and what makes it different from every AI course, bootcamp, and tutorial out there.

## Context

The Vibe Pill is V's (Vrijen Attawar's) invite-only community for non-technical founders preparing for a post-technical era. Key positioning:

- NOT a course. Not a bootcamp. Not tutorials.
- It teaches the "Meta OS" — architectural thinking, design patterns, and technical primitives that transfer when tools change every 6 months
- The core framework is **The Troika**: Builder Mindset + Zo Platform + Community. These three compound together.
- Sessions are 45 min, three phases: "How I Built This" (10 min demo), "Let's Build" (20 min group), "Your Turn" (15 min apply)
- The "trojan horse" — productivity is the entry point, AI fluency is the real product

## Current Page Structure

The methodology page (`/methodology` on va.zo.space) currently has:
- § 01 — THE META OS (hero section)
- § 02 — WHY NOT TUTORIALS (dark section, 2-col)
- § 03 — SESSION FORMAT (3 phase cards)
- § 04 — THE TROJAN HORSE
- § 05 — EXAMPLE BUILDS (6 build cards)

## Voice Guidelines

- V's voice is direct, confident, slightly irreverent. Not corporate. Not guru.
- Use concrete examples, not abstractions
- "We build systems" not "we empower learners"
- Technical enough to be credible, accessible enough for non-technical founders
- The reader should feel like this person has actually done what they're talking about
- Avoid: buzzwords, hype language, "unlock your potential" type nonsense

## Key Phrases V Uses

- "Post-technical era"
- "You describe, Zo executes"
- "The tools change every six months"
- "Productivity is the trojan horse for AI literacy"
- "Design patterns that transfer"
- "Built entirely through conversation"
- "Compound interest on knowledge"
