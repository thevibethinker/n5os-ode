# D1.2 — Generate Troika Infographic

## Objective

Create a visual diagram/infographic showing "The Troika" — V's three-part framework for The Vibe Pill:

1. **Builder Mindset** — Architectural thinking, design patterns, the Meta OS
2. **Zo Platform** — The AI computer that executes what you describe; Zøren as the AI persona
3. **Community** — Peer learning, shared builds, compound knowledge

These three elements aren't just listed — they COMPOUND together. The visual should communicate that interaction/synergy.

## Design Constraints

- **Style**: Blueprint aesthetic. The site uses a warm off-white canvas (#F8F6F2) with monospace annotations, thin rule lines, and a blue accent (#2B5BA0).
- **Approach**: Use the `generate_d2_diagram` tool to create a clean architectural diagram. D2 is ideal for this — it creates precise, technical-looking diagrams that fit the blueprint aesthetic perfectly.
- **NOT wanted**: Generic SaaS infographic, colorful marketing graphic, or cheesy clipart. This should look like something an architect would sketch.
- **Elements to show**: The three pillars AND how they connect/compound. Could be a triangle, a Venn diagram, three interlocking gears, or a circuit-like diagram. The key is showing INTERACTION not just listing.

## Data Points to Visualize

### Builder Mindset
- Design patterns that transfer
- Architectural thinking
- Technical primitives
- "The Meta OS"

### Zo Platform  
- AI computer
- "You describe, Zo executes"
- Zøren — your AI persona

### The Compounding Effect (center/connection)
- Each element makes the other two more powerful
- Builder Mindset + Zo = you build faster
- Builder Mindset + Community = you learn patterns from each other
- Zo + Community = shared systems, collective intelligence
