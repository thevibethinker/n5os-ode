## Goals

1. **Graph fills available space** — From left sidebar edge to right panel edge
2. **Proper dimension calculation:**

4. **Responsive** — Works on resize and different screen sizes
5. **Mobile** — On mobile, graph should fill full width (sidebars collapse)
