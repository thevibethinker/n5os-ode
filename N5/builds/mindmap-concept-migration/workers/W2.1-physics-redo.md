# Worker Brief: Physics Redo

## Context

Previous attempt at heterogeneous graph migration failed — concepts clustered in top-left corner instead of distributing as hubs. Site was reverted. This worker's SOLE job is to get the physics right.

## The Problem

We added concept nodes (type: "concept") that connect to thought nodes (type: "thought"). The goal:
- **Concepts should act as gravitational hubs** — spread across the canvas
- **Thoughts should orbit their connected concepts** — clustering around relevant concepts
- **Cross-domain bridges visible** — concepts connecting multiple domains should be spatially between those domain clusters

What happened instead: All concepts clustered together in one corner, with thoughts streaming away in diagonal lines.
