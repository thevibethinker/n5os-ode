# Drop Brief: Routing, Titles, and Move-Ready Rules

**Mission:** Define improved meeting/reflection routing, dynamic block selection behavior, title rules, and the post-processing move-ready state.

**Output:** `N5/builds/meeting-system-recovery-redesign/artifacts/routing-and-titling-rules.md`

## Context

V wants historical items brought into the fold and normalized, while future items receive better routing and generation behavior. The redesign must improve:
- meeting vs reflection identification
- block/generation setup going forward
- title generation quality
- explicit signal that a processed meeting is ready to move/archive

These rules must support downstream knowledge quality as well.
