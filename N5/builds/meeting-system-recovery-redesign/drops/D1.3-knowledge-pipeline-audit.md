# Drop Brief: Knowledge Pipeline Audit

**Mission:** Audit the pipeline from processed meetings into wisdom/knowledge outputs and identify the current breakpoints plus redesign requirements.

**Output:** `N5/builds/meeting-system-recovery-redesign/artifacts/knowledge-audit.md`

---

## Context

V explicitly wants the meetings → wisdom/knowledge pipeline refined and fixed. This drop maps where processed meeting artifacts are supposed to flow downstream, what current scripts/prompts/locations participate, and where the flow is broken or under-specified.

## Technical Concepts

| Concept | V's Level | Why It Matters Here |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| Elevation pipeline | Intermediate | Turning raw meeting output into reusable knowledge is a separate pipeline stage |
| Curated vs working knowledge | Intermediate | We need to know where intermediate artifacts should stop and what counts as durable knowledge |
| Downstream dependency | Intermediate | Bad meeting metadata and titles poison later knowledge extraction |
