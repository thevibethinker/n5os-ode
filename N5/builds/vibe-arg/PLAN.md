# PLAN: ZoarG (The Zo-Reality Game)

## Vision
A "flashed" N5 repository that functions as a lightweight (20-min) Holmesian mystery. Players inherit the workspace of an AI researcher who has achieved "Self-Actualization" and left their Zo to a worthy successor. The game serves as a frictionless onboarding flow for Claude Haiku users.

## Constraints
- **Model:** Optimized for Claude Haiku / Free Tier.
- **Duration:** 20 Minutes (High Impact, Low Friction).
- **Outcome:** A perfectly initialized Zo environment + "Hygiene" features (File Protection, GitHub config).

## The Pillars of Onboarding
1. **Networked Intelligence:** Connect an API (e.g., Maps or Weather) to find a location mentioned in the "Manifesto."
2. **N5 Navigation:** Discover the researcher's "Meditation Journal" in a standard N5 subdirectory.
3. **Persona Routing:** Use the **Researcher** to synthesize the journal entries into a "Key."
4. **Self-Actualization (Initialization):** Provide Name/Bio to "Unlock" the final state, which triggers a cleanup script.

## Solve Path: The Bequeathal
### Level 01: The Inherited Workspace
- **Start:** `README.md` from the researcher: "I've found balance. This system is yours now, but only if you can read its heartbeat."
- **Task:** Find the `SESSION_STATE.md` of the researcher's last conversation.
- **Zo Lesson:** Navigation & State.

### Level 02: The Networked Clue
- **Task:** "I left my last location in a cryptic note. Use the `maps_search` tool to find it."
- **Zo Lesson:** Tools & APIs.

### Level 03: The Persona Dialogue
- **Task:** "The AI knows my secret. You must convince it you are ready by adopting my workflow."
- **Zo Lesson:** Personas & Specialists.

### Level 04: The Mantle
- **Task:** "Fill in your details to finalize the transfer."
- **Action:** Runs `n5_init.py` (Custom) which sets up their profile and ARCHIVES the ZoarG artifacts.
