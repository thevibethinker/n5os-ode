## Key Query Changes

```sql
-- OLD: Find person by email
SELECT * FROM individuals WHERE email = ?
-- or
SELECT * FROM profiles WHERE email = ?

-- NEW: Unified query
SELECT * FROM people WHERE email = ?

-- NEW: Get person with their deals
SELECT 
    p.*,
    GROUP_CONCAT(d.company || ' (' || dr.role || ')') as deal_involvement
FROM people p
LEFT JOIN deal_roles dr ON p.id = dr.person_id
LEFT JOIN deals d ON dr.deal_id = d.id
WHERE p.email = ?
GROUP BY p.id
```
