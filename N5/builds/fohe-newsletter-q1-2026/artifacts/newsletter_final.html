New Leadership Energy

February opened with Oleksandra's chapter-wide welcome for Vrijen as Co-Lead. The Slack channel lit up with heart emojis, gratitude posts, and excitement about his cross-sector experience. December's Holiday Fun event is still paying dividends. Members turned one great night into ongoing coffee chats, job leads, and welcome messages. 2026 Momentum Building
