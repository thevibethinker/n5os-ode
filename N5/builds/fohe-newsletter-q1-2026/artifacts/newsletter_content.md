## Survey Insights: What 59 Members Told Us

**The big number:** 66% want more panels. AI Adoption in Education dominated with 41 mentions—clearly the hottest topic of 2026.

**Three key takeaways:**

1. **Work-life integration matters.** The #1 request is daytime events and virtual options to balance busy schedules. Message received.

2. **You want deeper connections.** 17 people signed up as Neighborhood Ambassadors vs. 7 Event Greeters. You're not just looking for networking—you want ongoing relationships.

3. **Geographic spread is real.** We're not just Manhattan-centric anymore. 34% of you are in Brooklyn, NJ, and outer boroughs. FOHE NYC truly means tri-state.

**Community voice:** *"I'm a new member, but am grateful for the network access more than anything, particularly being a first time founder in the Edtech space."* This captures exactly why we're here—supporting each other through the hard parts.

**What this means for 2026:** More panels (you asked, we listened), better scheduling flexibility, and programming that serves our full geographic footprint. Plus hackathons—multiple people specifically requested them.
