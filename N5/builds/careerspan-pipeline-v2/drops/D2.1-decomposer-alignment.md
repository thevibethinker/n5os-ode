## Intelligence Brief Structure (from Product Overview)

### Overall Output
- **Score:** e.g., 72/100
- **Bottom Line:** Single sentence verdict (e.g., "Strong senior-level contributor...")
- **Category Breakdowns:** Background, Uniqueness, Responsibilities, Hard Skills, Soft Skills

### Per-Skill Assessment
For each responsibility/skill, Careerspan provides:
- Importance (1-10)
- Level Required (Fundamental Awareness → Expert)
- Why Needed (context)
- Assessment Criteria (for each level: NA, Fundamental, Novice, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert)
- "Our Take" — the actual assessment narrative

### Signal Strength
- Story-Verified (✓✓) — backed by first-hand stories
- Resume-Only (✓) — claimed but not story-verified
- Inferred (~) — deduced from context

### Interview Prep
- Tailored questions based on gaps
- AI reasoning for each question
