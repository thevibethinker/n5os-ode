### 2. Section Completeness

Confirm all sections are present:
- ☐ Hero/Verdict with confidence score
- ☐ "What's Clear" vs "What's Not" summary
- ☐ 6 "What You're Getting" evidence cards
- ☐ 4 "What You Shouldn't Expect" gap cards
- ☐ Risk Profile table (4 rows)
- ☐ 5 Meeting Questions
- ☐ Decision Matrix (6 scenarios)

### 3. Naming/Phrasing

- ☐ Uses "Recommendation Confidence: 89%" (not "Score: 89")
- ☐ Uses "What You Shouldn't Expect" (not "What You're Not Getting")
- ☐ Candidate referred to as "H." (not full name)

### 4. No Hallucinations

Flag any content that appears in the webpage but NOT in the source:
- New statistics not in v5
- Claims about technologies not mentioned
- Overstated or understated assessments

### 5. Visual Quality

- ☐ Professional appearance
- ☐ Readable on desktop
- ☐ Data tables render correctly
- ☐ No broken sections or layout issues
