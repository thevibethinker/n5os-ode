# W4: Voice Variant System

## Objective
Define the 4 voice variants with prompt templates and examples.

## Output Files
- `Projects/x-thought-leader/config/voice_variants.yaml`
- `Projects/x-thought-leader/config/voice_examples.md`

## The 4 Variants

| Variant | Tone | Risk |
|---------|------|------|
| SUPPORTIVE | Warm, builds on their point | Low |
| CHALLENGING | Curious, questions assumptions | Medium |
| SPICY | Bold, contrarian, provocative | High |
| COMEDIC | Witty, playful, absurdist | Medium |

## Each Variant Needs
- Description and characteristics
- Constraints (what to avoid)
- Temperature setting
- Example starters
- 3-5 example tweets

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] All 4 variants fully defined
- [ ] Each feels distinct but authentically V
- [ ] Examples reference real positions
