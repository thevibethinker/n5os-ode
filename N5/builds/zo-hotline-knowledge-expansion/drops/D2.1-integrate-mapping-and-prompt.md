1) the webhook's `explainConcept` concept map, and

### 1) Expand `conceptFiles` mapping
In `Skills/zo-hotline/scripts/hotline-webhook.ts`:

### 3) Update system prompt "What You Advise On"

- Keep it short and hotline-friendly.

### 4) Sanity checks (run)
- Verify every `conceptFiles` entry points to an existing path under `Knowledge/zo-hotline/`.
