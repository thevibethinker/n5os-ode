**Perspective:** Speaking as an experienced IT professional navigating modern hiring (job seeker)

### Key Insights
1. ATS opacity remains a major barrier for experienced candidates transitioning careers.
   - Implication: Candidates with legacy resumes are systematically disadvantaged unless coached on keywords and format.
   - Strategic value: Careerspan's product positioning (resume + career brain + niche scraping) addresses a real, persistent pain.

2. Network and niche job boards matter more than general boards in the current market.
   - Implication: Direct sourcing and network-enabled introductions will yield better outcomes than broad applications.
   - Strategic value: Prioritize tooling and workflows that facilitate warmed, personalized outreach and scraper tuning for specific roles.

3. AI is most valuable as a thought partner (augmenting candidate drafting and outreach) rather than a blind autopilot.
   - Implication: Users benefit when AI is framed as collaborator (e.g., iterate on story, create targeted blurbs) instead of fully automated resume generation.
   - Strategic value: Emphasize features that let candidates craft authentic narratives while leveraging AI for structure and distribution.
