**Perspective:** Founder / advisor in talent/operations

### Key Insights
1. Employers increasingly value front-loaded, high-signal candidate signals (async interviews, curated pools) over credentials alone — opportunity to price for signal quality.
   - Implication: Position Careerspan as a high-signal pipeline and charge a premium for quality applicants.

2. There is a gap between ATS-driven volume approaches and curated, community-driven talent pools; embedding in trusted communities creates defensibility.
   - Implication: Prioritize formalizing partnerships with communities (contracts, exclusive pipelines).

3. Screening modality matters: async/video-first approaches can compress hiring cycles and improve interview quality; candidate resistance to video interviews is a barrier but coaching can bridge that gap.
   - Implication: Combine coaching (to improve candidate signal) with async screening to maximize conversion and perceived value.

4. Pricing framing matters: early-stage buyers expect lower-cost options; framing product as a high-signal filter justifies mid-to-high pricing tiers.
