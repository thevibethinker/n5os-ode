## 📊 Process Decisions

### 7. Focus on "Apply Correctly" Not "Easy Apply"
**Decision**: Both founders explicitly rejected "easy apply" approach in favor of "apply correctly" - quality over quantity.

**Who Decided**: Vrijen Attawar & Emily Nelson de Velasco (mutual)

**Rationale**:
- Easy apply creates spam and wastes everyone's time
- Recruiters are drowning in bad applications
- Candidates aren't helped by applying to 100 jobs with generic resumes
- "Be good, not look good" - actually fill gaps vs fabricate credentials

**Impact**:
- Product design focuses on depth of candidate understanding
- Coaching-first approach to gather rich candidate data
- Slower application process but higher quality matches
- Differentiation from incumbent career tech products (Teal, etc.)
