## Feature/Product Ideas Discussed

### 1. Community-Specific Job Distribution System

**Description:** Careerspan scans entire candidate database weekly, identifies top-rated matches for specific jobs, and distributes privately to community members (not spam to everyone). Community owners can position this as premium perk.

**Rationale:** Solves B2C monetization challenge for community-focused job boards. Provides differentiation from generic job boards (curated, quality-filtered). Reduces noise for job seekers (only see relevant opportunities). Creates revenue share model for community owners without requiring technical investment.

**Source:** Vrijen: "What we're already doing with communities is splitting the revenue we get from employers with the community owner... we do weekly scans of our whole community. We give you a system for distributing privately to your community and only to the highest rated candidates as well. So it doesn't just spam everyone. And we can call that a perk of your premium plan."

**Confidence:** High - Already implemented with other community partners (Happily Employed mentioned), proven model, lightweight implementation confirmed

### 2. AI Coaching Integration for Job Board Users

**Description:** Embed Careerspan's coaching tools (resume optimization, cover letter generation, interview prep, AI voice agent) into Remotely Good's platform as white-label or co-branded offering. Replaces need for Theresa to build these features from scratch.

**Rationale:** Theresa already building similar features but with limited resources/technical capability. Careerspan's tools are "so developed and so far along" (her words). Partnership accelerates her product roadmap by months/years. Allows her to focus on community growth and fundraising instead of technical debt. Creates lock-in for candidates (data captured through coaching flows).

**Source:** Vrijen: "Would love to have some parallel discussions around how could Careerspan tech fit into your product, maybe save you a little bit of development." Theresa: "So much of what Careerspan built and is working on is what I hope to at least try to integrate."

**Confidence:** High - Explicit interest from Theresa, clear product gap identified, technical feasibility confirmed (already offering similar tools standalone)

### 3. Data Layer Strategy for Micro-Communities

**Description:** Position Careerspan as underlying data infrastructure ("data mules") that captures human insights through coaching, then powers multiple micro-community products. Each community maintains its own brand/UX but leverages shared data layer for matching intelligence.

**Rationale:** Avoids building Goliath platform that "gobbles up everything." Enables rich ecosystem of specialized micro-products. Data portability philosophy aligns with candidate preferences (data follows them across communities). Creates network effects without centralization. Allows Careerspan to scale horizontally (many small communities) vs. vertically (one large platform).

**Source:** Vrijen: "We see our place as being just the data mules, effectively, right? We want to get really good at the capturing of human data through coaching conversations and use that to power ideally other products."

**Confidence:** High - Core strategic positioning for Careerspan, validated by Theresa's enthusiasm ("dream partnership"), differentiated from LinkedIn/Indeed model

### 6. ATS-Friendly Resume Builder (Beta Feature)

**Description:** Full AI resume builder that creates ATS-tracker-friendly resumes. Currently in beta as part of Theresa's $30/3-month premium tier. She's "hoping to build out features over next season" to justify the price point.

**Rationale:** ATS compatibility is major pain point for job seekers. Vrijen explicitly critiqued ATS systems as "dead" because LLMs can game them, but resume formatting/compatibility still matters for getting through filters. Potential replacement with Careerspan's resume tools.

**Source:** Theresa: "I'm starting at basically new and there's some features that I'm hoping I can build out in the next over this next season that will make it so that I feel comfortable charging $30 a month for it... at this tier you can basically do a full AI resume builder where you can make an ATS tracker friendly resume."

**Confidence:** Medium-High - Feature exists but incomplete/beta quality. Theresa acknowledges it needs more work to justify pricing. Direct replacement candidate for Careerspan partnership, but wasn't explicitly discussed as such. Vrijen's ATS critique suggests he might recommend different approach (coaching-based insights vs. keyword optimization).
