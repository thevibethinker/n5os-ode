# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

**5 Strategic messaging blurbs tailored to Emory pilot**

1. Value prop (30s): "Careerspan helps alumni find high-signal opportunities by combining curated employer relationships with targeted, geo-focused matchmaking — we surface the best fits so alumni and employers save time and hire with confidence."

2. Technical credibility: "We verify role fit by multi-step vetting and adaptive filtering so alumni see fewer, higher-quality matches — reducing noise and improving conversion."

3. Mission alignment: "This partnership elevates Emory's commitment to lifelong career support by turning alumni networks into curated pathways to meaningful roles."

4. Event blurb (reverse fair): "Join a curated reverse career fair — vetted founders and hiring managers meet top Emory talent in a focused, high-signal environment."

5. Employer pitch: "Partner with Emory and Careerspan to access pre-vetted alumni talent for critical hires — high-conversion, lower screening cost, and stronger alumni engagement."
