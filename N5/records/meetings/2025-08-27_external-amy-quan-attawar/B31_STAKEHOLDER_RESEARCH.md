**Perspective:** Speaking as a community/events organizer (Amy)

### Key Insights
1. Sponsor-enabled events materially lower per-event cost and attract higher-quality founder attendees.
   - Implication: Prioritize sponsor outreach early to reduce budget and improve attendee mix.
   - Strategic value: Sponsors (banks, corporates, education orgs) also bring credibility and potential speaker pipelines.

2. Curated invite lists and date certainty are primary levers to secure founder attendance.
   - Implication: Provide clear dates quickly; founders are more likely to commit with confirmed timing.
   - Strategic value: Invest effort in pre-event curation (shortlist + RSVP confirmations) over mass promotion.

3. Multiple venue/partner types are viable (corporate sponsor venues, General Assembly classroom spaces, beverage co-sponsors).
   - Implication: Maintain a tiered backup plan (sponsor venue → GA classroom → paid venue) to avoid delays.
   - Strategic value: Flexibility in venue selection reduces risk and enables faster execution
