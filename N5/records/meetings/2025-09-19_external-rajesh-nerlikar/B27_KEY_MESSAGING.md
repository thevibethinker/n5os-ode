# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

---
**Tailored messaging blurbs & talking points (for follow-up, intros, and short pitches)**

1. Value prop (30s): "Careerspan helps communities convert engaged members into hired talent by combining AI-driven candidate fit scoring with a community-first distribution model—employers only pay for candidates they accept into the pipeline."

2. Candidate-agent blurb: "We act as an ongoing career agent—weekly check-ins and personalized coaching—so candidates stay ready and employers get higher-quality, ready-to-interview talent."

3. Voice-enabled GTM line: "Busy hiring managers can submit a 1–2 minute voice memo describing the role; we parse it and deliver top-fit candidates quickly—no long intake meetings required."

4. Startup GTM focus: "We prioritize scale-ups and high-urgency hiring teams where faster, higher-quality hiring moves the needle—not Fortune 500 at initial stages."

5. Pricing positioning: "Our model reduces upfront friction: a low-touch trial (first 1–2 candidate intros free) demonstrates quality before committing to placement fees."

6. Advisory outreach opener: "Rajesh—thanks again for the product guidance. We'd love to formalize a short advisory engagement (biweekly or monthly) to help shape our product roadmap and employer pilot strategy."

**Usage contexts:** follow-up email, intro blurb, short pitch to community partners, product briefing with potential advisors.
