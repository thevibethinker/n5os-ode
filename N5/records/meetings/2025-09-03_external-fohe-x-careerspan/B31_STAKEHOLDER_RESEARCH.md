# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

**Perspective:** FOHI leadership speaking as a community operator (Jake, Logan, Ray)

### Insight 1 — Community distribution is a differentiator
- Evidence: FOHI references 5,000+ members and recent title sponsorship freeing capacity (04:47–05:44).  
- Why it matters: Access to a curated pool of professionals accelerates employer outreach and creates a stronger signal-to-noise ratio than public listings.  
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ● ○ (4/5)

### Insight 2 — Passive candidate activation is realistic with low-friction prompts
- Evidence: Logan describes returning users and the idea of prompting story submissions to surface talent (15:35–16:20).  
- Why it matters: Passive candidates expand the available talent pool and improve match quality for employers.  
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ○ ○ (3/5)

### Insight 3 — Employer demand is moving toward vetted, high-quality shortlists
- Evidence: Logan: employers want speed to application and vetted candidates; public listings declining (09:54–10:31).  
- Why it matters: There is commercial upside for community-driven curated candidate delivery if quality and speed are proven.  
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ● ○ (4/5)
