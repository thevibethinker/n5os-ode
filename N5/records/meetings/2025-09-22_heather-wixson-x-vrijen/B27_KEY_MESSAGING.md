## KEY_MESSAGING

**Usage context:** Follow-up emails, founder introductions, partner conversations

1. Value prop (30s): "Careerspan helps communities like Landedly surface qualified, ready-to-interview candidates directly to hiring managers — reducing screening time and improving placement outcomes."

2. Mission alignment: "We partner with community-driven coaching organizations to provide tools that turn coaching outcomes into real placements — rewarding coaches and participants alike."

3. For coach adoption: "Give your coaches a single dashboard to track candidate progress, identify gaps, and tailor coaching so more members get placed."

4. For partner pitch to employers: "Candidates sourced through Careerspan arrive with curated stories and fit signals, giving hiring managers confidence and reducing time-to-hire."

5. Objection handling: "If you’re worried about quality, we can run a small pilot with your cohort; we’ll only surface candidates who meet agreed criteria."

What Resonated: Cohort management, retention after onboarding, and warm introductions were the strongest themes — frame messaging around operational support and placement pathways.
