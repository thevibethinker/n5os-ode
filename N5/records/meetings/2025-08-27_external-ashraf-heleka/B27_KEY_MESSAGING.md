# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

**Purpose:** Strategic messaging tailored to what resonated in the meeting (usage labels included)

1. Value prop (For follow-up emails): "Careerspan helps hiring teams quickly surface pre-vetted candidates from niche product communities by combining coaching-driven signals with practical hiring filters."
   - Usage: Follow-up / intro emails

2. Technical credibility (When asked about method): "We quantify direct experience vs. transferable skills so hiring teams know what percentage of role-specific experience a candidate truly holds."
   - Usage: Product conversations

3. Mission alignment (Networking): "We help communities monetize member success while giving members ongoing career development."
   - Usage: Community partnerships

4. Specific fit (Pitch to Reforge/Product School): "We can offer members free access and a revenue-share model for hires, delivering higher-quality candidate pipelines to your partners."
   - Usage: Partnership outreach

5. Objection handling (Cost/Access): "We recommend starting with a small paid test to validate candidate quality before committing to larger programs—this minimizes risk and provides real ROI data."
   - Usage: Negotiations with community owners
