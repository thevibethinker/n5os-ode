**Perspective:** Speaking as early-stage founder (Atis) transitioning markets from mortgages → legal; hiring constraints common to early startups.

### Key Insights
1. Community access is gatekept — premium communities (Reforge) require paid entry or relationships; backdoor Slack participation can be effective.
   - Implication: Paid acquisition into targeted communities may be a viable, fast channel for sourcing PM candidates.
   - Strategic value: Prioritize investment in Reforge trial to validate candidate quality quickly.
2. Product School is commercially oriented and may accept paid partnerships; job channels exist there.
   - Implication: Lower-friction channel vs. Reforge; useful for volume and cross-posting.
3. Influencer-led distribution (Lenny, Shreyas, Elena) can amplify reach but likely requires relationship-building or paid arrangements.
   - Implication: Combine paid community access + influencer outreach as parallel tactics.
