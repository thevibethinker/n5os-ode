### Insight 2: Job seeker churn is endemic to job board industry - platforms struggle to retain users who platform-hop

**Evidence:** "Since we're in the job board industry, most of the partners already work with each other. It's a little incestuous. [...] It's the industry that a lot of the users just bounce from one platform to the next one."

**Why it matters:** Explains why retention metrics (2-3% monthly reactivation) and engagement depth (20 min+ coaching) are valuable differentiators. Job boards are paying for fresh traffic because existing users commoditize quickly across platforms.
