# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

---

**Perspective:** Internal product & operations team reflecting on outreach and candidate experience.

### Key Insights
1. **Demo-first outreach increases credibility**
   - Evidence: Team repeatedly emphasized using a candidate-facing demo for partner and community conversations.
   - Why it matters: Demonstrable product reduces friction for community partners and increases conversion likelihood.
   - Signal strength: ● ● ● ● ○

2. **Magic link tracking is a data blindspot**
   - Evidence: Discussion that magic-link applicants are invisible until they "click to apply" and Ilsa's manual workaround.
   - Why it matters: Without tracking, it's hard to measure channel effectiveness and attribute signups.
   - Signal strength: ● ● ● ○ ○

3. **Targeted community messaging matters more than broad blasts**
   - Evidence: Team noted timing and community selection (technical communities vs general) affected signups.
   - Why it matters: Better targeting and concise messaging likely yield higher activation than large, untargeted sends.
   - Signal strength: ● ● ● ○ ○
