### Resolved Tactical Debates

**Issue:** ATS replacement vs. ATS integration approach

**Options Considered:**
- **Option A:** Disrupt the ATS entirely (replace existing systems)
- **Option B:** Integration approach ("Filter Plus" that feeds into existing ATS)

**Resolution:** Integration approach (Option B)

**Rationale:** 
- Cannot disrupt workflow and data history components of ATS in short timeline
- Integration reduces friction - companies won't adopt if it requires replacing core systems
- Verification/filtering is the unique value-add, not workflow management
- "Filter Plus" positioning acknowledges ATS limitations while complementing rather than competing

---

**Issue:** Who feels the pain most acutely in current hiring system?

**Options Considered:**
- Job seekers
- Hiring managers  
- Community leaders
- ATS buyers (HR departments)

**Resolution:** Hiring managers + job seekers are primary pain points

**Rationale:**
- Hiring managers bear consequences of bad hires and waste time on fake/bullshit candidates
- Job seekers (specifically top candidates) face noisy systems and lack of recognition
- Community leaders and HR departments are more insulated from direct pain
- Product strategy should target those feeling acute pain for faster adoption

---

**Issue:** Raise VC round vs. pursue sale

**Options Considered:**
- **Raise pre-seed:** Extend runway, maintain control, continue building
- **Raise seed:** Match competitors (A16Z-backed Jack, other recent raises)
- **Pursue sale:** Exit strategy, team transition, maximize current value

**Resolution:** Pursue sale as primary strategy, VC raise as backup

**Rationale:**
- Limited runway (3 months) creates urgency
- Don't have demonstrable revenue/traction needed for strong fundraising position
- Sale allows ethical team treatment with 3-month transition
- Recent competitor raises (Jack/Jill) prove market interest but also increase pressure
- Can still pivot to raise if sale generates unexpected traction
- Lean operation (V + Logan + limited Ilse) is sustainable for sale timeline
