# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

**Perspective:** Internal team observations about partner (Superposition) and candidate funnel

### Insight 1 — Employer-side recruiting vendors can be a near-term job feed
- Evidence: Superposition raised ~$2M pre-seed and is already generating revenue (Vrijen).
- Why it matters: Partnering with employer-side recruiting platforms is a pragmatic channel to source job postings and revenue share opportunities.
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ● ○

### Insight 2 — Semi-manual onboarding is an acceptable MVP path
- Evidence: Team discussed using Slack channel/manual uploads and the existing voice-agent onboarding to ingest jobs.
- Why it matters: Immediate integration can be lightweight; focus on rapid ingestion and conversion rather than full automation.
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ○ ○

### Insight 3 — Candidate acquisition via Typeform shows early funnel promise
- Evidence: Logan reported 9–10 promising submissions and referrals from existing networks.
- Why it matters: There is tangible supply-side momentum that can be converted with simple resume capture and white-glove onboarding.
- Signal strength: ● ● ● ○ ○

---
**Actionable recommendation:** Prioritize a minimal conversion tracking implementation (Typeform → resume capture → manual ingest) to measure LTV and conversion before investing in automation.
