# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

**Perspective:** Speaking as an event organizer / connector between investors and startups

### Key Insights

1. **Events as a measurable funding channel**
- Observation: Caleb reports ~60% funding rate within 6–8 months for participants.  
- Implication: Events can accelerate investor introductions and compress fundraising timelines.  
- Strategic value: Prioritize quick-prep investor narratives and leverage event media assets to maximize follow-up conversion.

2. **Sponsorships underwrite founder costs**
- Observation: Sponsors (Insperity, JP Morgan, law firms) contribute material support (flight vouchers, partial fees).  
- Implication: Sponsorships make participation attainable for early-stage teams; organizers mediate sponsor relationships.  
- Strategic value: Devise sponsorship outreach (shared value proposition) or consider revenue-share to reduce founder fees.

3. **Media + lead lists multiply event ROI**
- Observation: Attendees receive a one-minute professional video, photos, and a lead list post-event.  
- Implication: These assets extend the event's marketing and fundraising impact beyond the night itself.  
- Strategic value: Prepare pitch collateral optimized for 60–90 second video and post-event outreach to leads.
