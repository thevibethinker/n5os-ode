## Memorable Quotes

1. **"I don't want to start talking to you because once I start talking to you're never going to leave me alone ever again."**

3. **"If I see percentage fit scores, I'm like, an algorithm did this. It might just be bullshit, but if I see like, hey, these are really clear fits. Hey, here are some hidden gems. Don't discount them. Give them a second look. Then I really, I'm a lot more engaged."**

## Salient Questions

1. **"Would this approach be more effective for jobs that just got posted because the hiring manager is really engaged, or is it better for jobs that got posted a month ago because the hiring manager's burnt out and wants an easy solution?"**  
   **Why it matters:** Timing of outreach could significantly impact conversion rates. Fresh postings = high attention but less desperation. Old postings = fatigue but urgency.  
   **Who asked:** Alex (timestamp ~44:00)  
   **Action hint:** Run A/B test tracking response rates by job posting age (0-1 week, 1-4 weeks, 1+ month). Segment outreach accordingly.
