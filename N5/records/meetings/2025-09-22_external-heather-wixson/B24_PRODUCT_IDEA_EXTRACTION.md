**Product ideas / features mentioned**

1. Vibe Check -> job-description fit scoring
   - Description: Compares candidate profile to job description and surfaces fit/gaps
   - Rationale: Helps coaches and candidates prioritize applications and tailor narratives
   - Source: Vrijen (product demo context)
   - Confidence: High

2. Cohort management / activity tracking for coaches
   - Description: Track resume tailoring, job applications, candidate progress within a cohort
   - Rationale: Addresses Heather's need to 'see where they are' and manage clients
   - Source: Heather (operational pain with current platform)

3. Company tie-ups for direct-sourcing (Warm Jobs / hiring manager routing)
   - Description: Surface jobs within first-degree network and route qualified candidates directly to hiring managers
   - Rationale: Increases placement probability and avoids ATS
   - Source: Vrijen (mentions Warm Jobs partnership)
   - Confidence: Medium
