User-research notes (candidate & hiring manager friction)

Findings from conversation:
- Magic link drop-off: Users hit signup pages with unclear role context; need an interstitial that previews the role and explains value.
- Hiring managers want a lightweight shortlist and to keep control of referrals; they distrust overloaded HR teams.
- Non-tech hiring managers are often pragmatic and will adopt tooling that reduces their friction.

Suggested quick user-research experiments:
- A/B test two interstitial designs for magic links (short JD vs JD+value props).
- Phone-check interviews with 5 hiring managers in target ICP to validate pilot offer and pricing sensitivity.
