Risks (identified) and recommended mitigations

1. Runway pressure (limited cash) — Mitigation: prioritize revenue pilots, hire BD contractors on equity + small cash, offer paid pilot options.
2. Execution gap (follow-through on sales/process) — Mitigation: clear scorecards, one-pager scripts, dedicated BD resource.
3. Product friction (magic link → signup leakage) — Mitigation: implement JD interstitial, measure funnel, quick UX fixes prioritized.
4. Over-reliance on external communities — Mitigation: focus on hiring-manager ICP for immediate revenue; parallel community work but not primary.
5. Misalignment in success metrics — Mitigation: set explicit pilot KPIs before kickoff (e.g., qualified candidates in X days).
