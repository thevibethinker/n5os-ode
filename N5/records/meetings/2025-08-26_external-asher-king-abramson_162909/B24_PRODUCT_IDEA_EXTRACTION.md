## PRODUCT_IDEA_EXTRACTION

1. Hiring-manager detection Chrome extension (feature discussed) — Confidence: High
   - Description: Surface hiring manager & hiring team members on job postings; enable quick searches & intros
   - Rationale: Improves quality of candidate outreach; valuable to recruiters and power users

2. Recruiter BD features (search & intro workflow) — Confidence: Medium
   - Description: Tools to let recruiters find jobs in network and get intros to hiring managers
   - Rationale: Alternate revenue stream via recruiter usage

3. GTM-engineer/product role focus features (verticalization) — Confidence: Medium
   - Description: Tailored job distribution and messaging for product/GTM roles
   - Rationale: Product managers more likely to pay; narrows TAM to higher-LTV segment

4. Community/privileged-access flows for curated candidate lists — Confidence: Medium
   - Description: One-pagers and privileged distribution channels to communities
   - Rationale: Low-friction pilot channel to validate employer demand
