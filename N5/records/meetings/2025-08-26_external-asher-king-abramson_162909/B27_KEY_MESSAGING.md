## KEY_MESSAGING

**Usage context:** follow-up emails / community blurbs / partner outreach

1. Value prop (30s): "Careerspan delivers a short list of well-curated candidates for product and GTM roles—ready for warm intros—so hiring teams can evaluate quality without wading through hundreds of resumes."

2. Technical credibility: "We pair human curation with practical tools (candidate packaging + job distribution) so employers see vetted fits quickly."

3. Mission alignment: "We help talented professionals find roles that fit their career trajectory, and we help employers find candidates who actually show up and perform."

4. Recruiter angle: "If you source candidates, we can provide curated lists that make outreach more effective—then you collect bounties or placement fees as usual."

5. Differentiation: "Unlike generic job boards, our approach prioritizes curated, intro-first distribution—it's about quality and connection, not volume."

6. CTA (for community pitch): "Reply if you'd like a short list of vetted candidates to review, or we'll happily provide a 15-minute intro call to discuss fit."
