### Major Strategic Decisions

#### 1. **Dual-Brand Business Model: Careerspan + V&L LLC**
- **Decision**: Run two parallel businesses under umbrella structure
  - **Careerspan**: B2C platform for passive job seekers (asset to sell)
  - **V&L LLC**: Services company (V & Logan personal revenue)
- **Rationale**: Keep Careerspan "clean" for acquisition while generating personal income through services
- **Key Quote**: "We want to plumpen up career span with lots of users...but we also want to capture some of those career span users to our own owned emails"
- **Implementation**: Separate LLCs, possibly Dubai-based for V&L to optimize taxes (Feb 2026)

#### 2. **Target: $25k/Month Sustainability by End of Year**
- **Decision**: Achieve $25k monthly run rate through combination of:
  - Elite placement services (Careerspan Select): 10 placements @ $2k each = $20k
  - Custom AI implementations for executives: $5k+ per client
  - Lenza job distribution partnership revenue
- **Timeline**: 2 months to hit first $25k, then maintain monthly
- **Key Constraint**: Zero payback pressure from Rab/Dad (no forced timeline)

#### 3. **Team Wind-Down Strategy**
- **Danny & Rockwell**: 
  - Pay through end of year
  - Place them via Careerspan Select (first case studies)
  - Use as proof point for platform
- **Ilsa**:
  - Offer part-time retention (Jan-Feb 2026)
  - Focus on cost reduction & maintenance mode
  - Possible equity partnership if she commits long-term
- **Rationale**: "The most responsible thing...we can't take the pressure of having to pay you guys while...building out this product"

#### 4. **Sell to Jack & Jill Within 6-12 Months**
- **Decision**: Position Careerspan as U.S. acquisition target for UK competitor Jack & Jill
- **Exit Logic**: "All we need is for Jack and Jill to succeed and for us to survive as they succeed...when someone's looking to get into that market, where are they going to buy? We'll come with a clean cap table, no employees, self-funded with a brand"
- **Success Criteria**: Match their user numbers (~100k users by Jan 1) with better data quality

#### 5. **Pivot to Passive Job Seeker Focus (Kill Reactive Job Seeker Messaging)**
- **Decision**: "We don't prioritize reactive job Seekers...fuck the reactive people, let's focus on passive people"
- **Rationale**: Aligns with brand messaging, higher quality candidates, better fit for slow-burn growth
- **Product Implication**: Message to "planners" and "people who give a shit" vs. desperate job seekers

#### 6. **Launch "Careerspan Select" - Elite Placement Service**
- **Decision**: High-touch placement service for top 10-20 candidates
- **Model**: 
  - Hand-pick best candidates from platform (high story count + target role match)
  - Career coaching + placement hustle by V & Logan
  - $2k fee per placement (split with candidate or paid by hiring manager)
  - Candidate exclusivity contract (can't go around Careerspan to employer)
- **First Targets**: Danny, Rockwell, + 8-18 more from existing user base

#### 7. **Build Two Personal Brands**
- **V's Brand**: "AI & productivity for the elite" - custom Zo implementations for executives
- **Logan's Brand**: "AI & careers for the everyman" - content, community, scale play
- **Time Allocation**: 50/50 split between Careerspan and personal brands initially, trending toward 30/70 over time
- **Synergy**: Elite clients hire from Careerspan network; Careerspan users become V&L customers

#### 8. **Employer-Side Product: Dead Simple Job Submission**
- **Decision**: Build minimal employer interface
  - Submit job via voice memo or text
  - Careerspan anonymizes and matches to candidates
  - Weekly email digest of matched candidates (also anonymous)
  - Direct intro only after mutual interest
- **Target**: 20-30 hiring managers from V&L networks
- **Key Innovation**: "Bypass HR" - direct to hiring manager, passive pipeline building

#### 9. **Messaging Pivot: "For the Planners"**
- **Decision**: Brand Careerspan for proactive, thoughtful, type-A (but not desperate) professionals
- **Key Quote**: "Career span is for people that like care...people that like give a shit about being better"
- **Anti-positioning**: Not for people "slaving over LinkedIn" - for people building passive career infrastructure
