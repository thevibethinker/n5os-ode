## 🔑 Key Insights

### What FutureFit Cares About
- **Scale**: 200K users/year through org partners (workforce development, community colleges, nonprofits)
- **UX coherence**: Past integrations created user complexity; needs embedded solutions, not external redirects
- **Employer-side data scarcity**: Struggles with capturing "intangible elements" employers want (values, soft skills, culture fit)
- **Proof over promise**: Hamoon explicitly asked "what is the strongest part of what you have built?"—vision is not enough

### What Resonated About Careerspan
- **Qualitative profiling depth**: 100+ data points (biographical + values/mindset/soft skills) via conversational AI
- **Hiring manager conversation tech**: 5-8 min AI-driven requirement elicitation (vs. parsing JDs)
- **High-signal communities**: Exclusive access to Harvard alumni, VP+ groups (no fake candidates)
- **"Internet of careers" vision**: Becoming the data layer for jobs/hiring market

### Partnership Models on the Table
1. **Embedded point solution**: FutureFit users click tool → experience Careerspan feature (e.g., "vibe check") → data returns to FutureFit
2. **Data layer API**: FutureFit sends candidate inputs → Careerspan returns structured profiles
