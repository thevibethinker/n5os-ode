One-pager: Careerspan referral-management pilot

Problem
Hiring managers are overwhelmed by low-signal inbound and an HR team that can't reliably triage referrals.

Solution
A lightweight pilot: Careerspan provides a magic-link JD interstitial + referral intake and delivers a ranked shortlist with referral signal—no heavy product integration required.

Scope
- Roles: 2 (example: Data-focused PM, Data Scientist)
- Timeline: 2–4 weeks
- Deliverables: JD interstitial, 1 kickoff, weekly shortlists, pilot report

Success metrics
- 8–12 qualified candidates delivered
- 1 hire or LOI to continue within 60 days

Next steps
- Confirm HR approver and JD drafts. Kickoff within 7 days of approval.
