## Landidly Company Background

**Business Model:**
- Concierge job search service: AI technology + human coaching
- Personalized Job Search Program with dedicated concierges
- $0 until hired (BNPL model: $110-195/month after placement, reimbursed during search)
- 3-week risk-free trial

**Services:**
- Weekly 1-on-1 concierge sessions (90 min)
- Targeted networking outreach on behalf of job seekers
- Resume and LinkedIn optimization
- High-volume job applications (automated + personalized)
- Interview coaching and offer negotiation support

**Target Market:**
- Professionals with 3+ years experience
- Clear career goals, specific role targets
- Salary expectations $60K+ USD
- U.S. job market only

**Traction & Data:**
- **146,340 job applications** sent for clients in 2024 (per Weston's LinkedIn post)
- **7.3% positive response rate** for top 50 companies
- **0.6% response rate** for companies outside top 50
- **70% of placements** start with network connection, not applications

**Value Proposition:**
- "10x your job search in 1/10th the time"
- Full-service delegation (job seeker focuses on interviews, Landidly handles applications/networking)
- Strategic vs. high-volume spray-and-pray
