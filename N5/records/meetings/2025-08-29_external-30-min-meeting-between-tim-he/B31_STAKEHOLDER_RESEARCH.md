**Perspective:** Speaking as a community/platform operator (Twill)

### Key insights
1. Community-first sourcing is valued but fatigues members if roles are repetitive. Implication: Employers need fresh, high-signal job opportunities or member incentives to sustain engagement.
2. Founders/operators prefer predictable commercial models (subscription) over ad-hoc per-placement fees; simplifies internal buy-in. Strategic value: Position Careerspan as an MRR partner rather than a one-off vendor.
3. Distribution/brand trust matters: Twill prioritizes member referral integrity and may avoid public attribution; Careerspan should offer anonymized shortlists and compliance assurances.
4. Conversion signals matter more than raw volume: high open rates (80–90%) and solid CTRs (12–16%) indicate quality engagement—prioritize demonstrating conversion not just reach.

**Implication for Careerspan go-to-market:** Lead with a small pilot that demonstrates conversion rate and clear ROI; package as 'subscription + sample scans' to lower procurement friction.
