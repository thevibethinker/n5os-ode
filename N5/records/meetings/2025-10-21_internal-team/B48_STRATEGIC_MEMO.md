- If no overlap in founder desires → graceful dissolution may be more ethical than forced march

**Step 4: Pick ONE Primary Model (Not Four)**
- Options: Magic links, Free distribution, White-glove recruiting, VC subscription
- Criteria: (1) Fastest to revenue OR user growth, (2) Least founder effort, (3) Technically feasible with constraints
- Secondary bets optional ONLY if don't distract from primary

**Step 5: Define Product Scope with Ilse**
- Not "can you build this?" but "what CAN you build given timeline/team/cost constraints?"
- Let Ilse define art of the possible, THEN decide if that's sufficient for acquisition value
- If Ilse says "not possible" → believe her, adjust strategy

**Step 6: Commit or Dissolve**
- If aligned → execute with full commitment
- If misaligned → discuss equity buyouts, graceful wind-down, or role changes
- Middle path (pretend alignment while harboring doubt) = worst outcome for everyone
