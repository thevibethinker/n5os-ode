# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

**Usage:** Follow-ups, pitch to employers, objection handling

1) Value prop (30s): "Careerspan surfaces high-signal candidates from trusted communities and delivers curated shortlists to hiring teams—faster than recruiters, higher quality than boards."

2) Technical credibility blurb: "Our approach pairs coaching-derived candidate artifacts with community provenance—so you see how a candidate thinks, not just what their resume says."

3) Mission alignment narrative: "We help underused talent pools connect to meaningful roles, reducing time-to-hire while preserving candidate dignity."

4) Specific fit (for employers): "We deliver a shortlist of vetted candidates within 12 hours and you only pay for the ones you pursue."

5) Objection handling (pricing): "We position pricing against recruiter fees—for a fraction of recruiter cost, you get higher signal and faster shortlists; initial pilot pricing can be tailored per employer."

(Each blurb tailored to what resonated: async screening + community provenance)
