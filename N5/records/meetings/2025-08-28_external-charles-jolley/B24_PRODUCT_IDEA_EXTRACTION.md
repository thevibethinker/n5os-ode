# B24_PRODUCT_IDEA_EXTRACTION

### Product / Feature ideas mentioned

1. Async interview artifacts (short video responses to 3 questions) as a screening signal
   - Rationale: Reduces time-to-insight for employers; filters for engagement and fit
   - Source: Charles (described Wisdom Partners' async interview practice)
   - Confidence: High

2. Candidate coaching as a differentiator — convert coaching outputs into standardized candidate profiles (the "new resume")
   - Rationale: Coaches surface higher-quality presentation and better signals for employers
   - Source: Vrijen
   - Confidence: High

3. Secondary screen product that helps employers winnow candidates in a pro-candidate way
   - Rationale: Adds value between job boards and recruiters
   - Source: Vrijen
   - Confidence: Medium
