# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

---
**Perspective:** Founder / advisor (Wisdom Partners) speaking from hiring and founder support experience

### Key Insights
1. Market appetite for new hiring modalities: Employers are increasingly open to novel screening formats (async interviews, curated shortlists) as traditional networks and ATS pipelines underperform.
   - Implication: A product that combines candidate coaching with concise screening artifacts can command premium pricing vs. job boards.

2. Two-sided pricing gap exists: job boards (low price, high volume) vs. recruiters (high price, low volume) — there is a viable middle market for high-signal curated candidates.
   - Implication: Position Careerspan as the high-signal middle ground with explicit pricing tested in market.

3. Screening signal > credentials: Employers are open to de-emphasizing credentials in favor of evidence of fit via async artifacts and curated community sourcing.
   - Strategic value: Emphasize coaching-derived artifacts and community provenance as core differentiators.
