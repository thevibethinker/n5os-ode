# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

**Perspective:** Internal product/ops team discussion

### Key Insights

1. Embedded-apply flow reduces friction while preserving referral feel — potential lightweight acquisition channel for specific roles.
   - Implication: Can A/B test embedded-apply vs. external job board placements for conversion lift.
   - Strategic value: Low-cost funnel with higher referral quality.

2. Small-dollar purchase anomalies (resume licenses) can surface non-standard user segments (students/parents) and inform pricing/licensing approaches.
   - Implication: Consider purchase patterns when designing license bundles and free upgrade policies.
   - Strategic value: Learn customer segments and adjust defaults/flags to reduce support load.

3. Quick internal experimentation is feasible (Ilse can set flags), enabling rapid iteration — advantage for product discovery.
   - Implication: Formalize lightweight experiment cadence and ownership for quick hypothesis tests.
   - Strategic value: Faster learnings, lower cost of failure
