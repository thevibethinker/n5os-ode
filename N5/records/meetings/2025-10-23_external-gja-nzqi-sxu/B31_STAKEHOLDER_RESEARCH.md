#### 3. Solo features insufficient - users need complete interconnected solutions

**Evidence**: Vrijen: "Workflows get powerful when the workflow that transforms my meetings into notes syncs with my workflow that extracts salient insights from my notes and adds it to a compounding understanding of who I am"; David: "It has to go from a feature to a solution"

**Why it matters**: Template marketplace strategy must provide *end-to-end solutions* (meeting → notes → insights → email) not just individual workflow templates. Users are willing to tinker/customize but need solution-level starting points, not feature-level building blocks.
