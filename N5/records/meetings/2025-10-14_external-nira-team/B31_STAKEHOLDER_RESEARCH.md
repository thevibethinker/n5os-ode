### 3. "Tool → Operating System" Is the New B2B SaaS Wedge Strategy

**Evidence:** "In order to get the right to become your operating system, we have to be a tool first that earns our trust... That's where we start earning the rights to become the operating system."

**Why it matters:** Articulates a GTM strategy for platform businesses that avoids the cold-start problem. This is the inverse of LinkedIn's "be everything to everyone" approach.
