# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

---
**Perspective:** Speaking as a founder of a two-sided marketplace (B2C + B2B)

### Key insights

1. Market problem framing: employers have "too much noise" (applicant flood) and need higher-signal candidate channels — strategic value: Careerspan's curated communities can position as signal-filtering distribution.

2. Demand driver: retention and quality of hire (Laura framed purchaser value as hires who stay 18+ months) — strategic implication: emphasize retention and long-term fit in sales messaging.

3. Distribution advantage: access to engaged communities (Slack/Discord/newsletter audiences) is a defensible channel vs. generic job boards — tactical: highlight community provenance of candidates in outreach.

4. Product positioning: potential niche around "AI-ready" or "AI-forward" candidates — opportunity to differentiate via a filter/label that signals capacity to work with AI.
