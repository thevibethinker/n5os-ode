# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

---
**Usage:** Follow-up emails / Intro pitches / Objection handling

1. Value prop (30s): "We source high-signal candidates from curated professional communities to deliver hires who actually fit and stay — not just another resume dump."

2. Technical credibility blurb: "We combine community provenance with rapid curated shortlists to surface candidates that match role context and cultural fit."

3. Mission alignment: "We help companies cut through applicant noise so hiring teams can make confident, long-term hires."

4. Specific fit (for early-stage employers): "For early-growth product roles, we surface candidates who are active adopters and fast learners — ideal for AI-forward teams." 

5. Differentiator: "We prioritize retention and provenance — our community-sourced approach reduces false positives from keyword-matched applicants."

**What resonated:** 'Signal from noise' framing and rapid curated delivery (5+ candidates) — use both in outreach.
