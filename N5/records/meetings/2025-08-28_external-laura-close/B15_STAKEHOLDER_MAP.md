# B15_STAKEHOLDER_MAP

---
**Key players referenced in the call**

- Job Seekers: End users of the B2C product — interest: access to roles; role in system: supply
- Community Founders: Owners/moderators of engaged communities (Slack/Discord/newsletters) — interest: member value; role: distribution partners
- Employers / Hiring Managers: Buyers of the B2B service — interest: quality hires and retention; role: purchaser/decision-maker

**Dynamics:** Communities provide high-signal candidate pools; team must translate that provenance into buyer value (retention/quality) to convert employers.

**Recommendation:** Prioritize mapping specific buyer titles (hiring manager, TA lead) and decision criteria in next sprint.
