# Career insights (coaching & market signals)

1. Freelance/contract market is accelerating — high-caliber talent exploring fractional work.
2. Employers will increasingly look for on-demand, vetted product talent; positioning matters.
3. Communities are effective supply funnels for early-stage talent sourcing efforts.
4. Offering curated, community-specific pilots (free trial) reduces friction for adoption.
5. Founders value IRL connections and curated cohorts — these can be leveraged to build trust and product advocates.
