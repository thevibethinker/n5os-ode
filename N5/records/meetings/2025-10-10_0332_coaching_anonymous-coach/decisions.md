# Decisions — Meeting summary

   - Rationale: Symptoms of burnout are reducing output and harming company performance; short rest is highest-impact intervention.
   - Impact: Temporarily pause non-critical tasks; assign urgent handoffs.

2. Prioritize a meditation-style retreat (silent/structured) as the leading reset option
   - Rationale: Coach reports strong, lasting benefits from 10-day retreats; faster behavioral reframing than a typical vacation.
   - Impact: Schedule planning, integration checklist, and mental bandwidth adjustments on return.

   - Rationale: Leverage relationships and founder network to surface partnerships, sponsorships, and product feedback.
   - Impact: Short-term event planning and sponsor outreach; potential supply-side pipeline for product.

4. Use community pilot (free access) to validate product-market fit for product role sourcing
   - Rationale: Low-friction acquisition; test conversion and data pipelines with engaged communities.
   - Impact: Build evidence for broader GTM and revenue experiments.

5. Pursue warm introductions (Shivam, DLN founder network) to support event curation and sponsorship
   - Rationale: Introductions accelerate venue, sponsorship, and speaker access.
   - Impact: Faster event delivery and higher-quality attendee list.

   - Rationale: User cannot travel overseas; domestic alternatives are practical and timely.
   - Impact: Shortlist US-based retreats and nature-based resets.
