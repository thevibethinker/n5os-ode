## Key Insights Provided

### Pain Points (Hiring Manager Perspective)

1. **Recruiter fatigue**: Tired of relationship-building attempts before seeing value
2. **Time scarcity**: Don't have bandwidth for exploratory calls with every recruiter
3. **Trust issues**: Assume recruiters will be persistent/annoying once engaged
4. **Value-first expectation**: Want to see candidates immediately to evaluate fit

### What Works (Alex's Preferences)

1. **Lead with candidates**: Show qualified profiles upfront
2. **Low-friction engagement**: No obligation, easy to evaluate
3. **Self-service approach**: Let hiring manager explore on their own timeline
4. **Clear value exchange**: "Here are candidates, interested in paying?"
