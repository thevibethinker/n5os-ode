# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

1. Value prop (30s): "Careerspan connects product communities to vetted candidates—our outreach pairs founder-sourced opportunities with highly relevant product talent, reducing hiring friction."
2. Technical credibility: "We surface candidates proven in product roles and match them to hiring managers using behavioral and portfolio signals—no generic lists."
3. Mission alignment: "We help communities monetize by placing members while giving members career outcomes they value."
4. Objection handler (pricing): "We begin with a short pilot to demonstrate ROI—if conversions justify scale, we expand engagement."
5. For outreach (founder-sourced): "Quick note: saw your recent post about [deal]; we help place product managers from top product communities—can I introduce a candidate?"

Usage contexts: follow-up email, outreach subject lines, founder-to-founder intro.
