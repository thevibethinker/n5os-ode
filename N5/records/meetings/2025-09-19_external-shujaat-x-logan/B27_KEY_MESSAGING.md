# B27_KEY_MESSAGING

---

Five short messaging blurbs tailored to product managers and community champions (use contexts noted).

1. For follow-up to a PM champion (usage: invite/pilot ask):  
"Thanks for the chat — we’re piloting a product-manager cohort focused on signal-first hiring and practical skill-building. It’s a short pilot (4 weeks) designed to surface top candidates who demonstrate product instincts and communication skills. Would you be willing to recommend 3–5 PMs to join a trial cohort?"

2. Elevator pitch (30s):  
"Careerspan helps product teams find high-signal candidates faster by combining community validation with practical, skills-focused assessments — reducing wasted interviews and improving hire quality."

3. For advisor outreach (usage: clarify expectations):  
"We’re exploring advisory partnerships that include defined deliverables (intro volume, strategic sessions, pilot support). We’ll share tiered options so you can pick the level that matches your bandwidth and impact goals."

4. For community evangelists (usage: social copy):  
"We’re running a short pilot to test signal-first hiring for product managers — if you know PMs who consistently deliver, we’d love to invite them to a curated cohort to get early access and influence evaluation criteria."

5. Objection-handling blurb (usage: investor/partner conversations):  
"We prioritize pragmatic, research-backed candidate development rather than abstract credentials — our pilots use community validation and focused evidence to surface the right people faster."
