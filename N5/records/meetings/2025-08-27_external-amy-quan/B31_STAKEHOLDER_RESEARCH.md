**Perspective:** Community events organizer / partnerships (NYC)

### Key Insights

1. Sponsors reduce direct financial and operational burden and help attract higher-quality founders (implication: prioritize sponsor conversations early; strategic value: lowers acquisition cost for attendees).

2. Community partners (General Assembly, beverage brands) provide flexible venue formats (classroom/workshop vs. larger event); choose format to match goals (knowledge exchange vs. larger showcase).

3. Curated founder attendance matters more than raw headcount for this type of event — curation increases perceived value for sponsors and panelists (use targeted invites rather than broad promotion).

**Strategic value:** Design event format and sponsor ask around curated founder invite lists; position Careerspan as curator/facilitator to justify sponsor investment.
