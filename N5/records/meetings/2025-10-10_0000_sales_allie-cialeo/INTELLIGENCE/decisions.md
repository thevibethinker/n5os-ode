# Decisions Made: Greenlite Partnership
**Date**: 2025-10-10

## Strategic Decisions

### 1. Agreement to Proceed with Pilot Partnership

**Decision**: Move forward with low-risk pilot partnership between Greenlite and Careerspan for tech recruiting

**Rationale**: 
- Low effort (15 min/week review time)
- Low risk (pay-on-success, $300 per warm intro only)
- High upside (pre-vetted candidates from quality communities)
- Addresses immediate pain: 22 open roles, 1 recruiter, need better pre-filtering

**Decided By**: Allie Cialeo (Greenlite) + Vrijen Attawar (Careerspan)

**Impact**: High - establishes new recruiting channel that could scale to replace or supplement agencies

**Quote**: _"I'd be happy to explore and see what happens. I mean, there. I don't see this as a loss of anything."_ — Allie

---

### 2. Focus on 4 Tech Roles Initially

**Decision**: Pilot will focus on 4 specific tech roles through end of year:
1. Site Reliability Engineer
2. ML Engineer (senior/staff/principal level)
3. 1-2 Full Stack Engineers

**Rationale**: 
- Most urgent hiring needs
- Through end-of-year headcount already approved
- Allows focused testing of matching quality
- Other roles (sales, operations) already being handled

**Decided By**: Allie Cialeo

**Impact**: Medium - constrains scope to prove concept before expansion

---

### 3. Stealth Company Name in JD Distribution

**Decision**: Keep Greenlite name confidential in job distribution through Careerspan's Magic Link system

**Rationale**: 
- Prevent candidates from directly applying and flooding ATS
- Allie: _"I feel like if you screen them out, they'll still probably try to apply to greenlight otherwise"_
- Maintains control over candidate flow
- Ensures all candidates go through Careerspan's pre-vetting

**Decided By**: Allie Cialeo

**Impact**: Medium - affects distribution strategy and candidate experience

## Tactical Decisions

### 4. Weekly Candidate Delivery Cadence

**Decision**: Careerspan will deliver candidate packets weekly

**Rationale**: 
- Balances freshness with manageability
- 15 minute weekly review time is sustainable
- Allows for iterative feedback loop
- Aligns with Careerspan's 200-400/week onboarding rate

**Decided By**: Mutual agreement (Vrijen + Allie)

**Impact**: Low-Medium - sets operational rhythm

---

### 5. Pay-on-Success Pricing Model ($300 per intro)

**Decision**: $300 per warm introduction to candidates Greenlite wants to meet (no upfront fees)

**Rationale**: 
- Careerspan is "collecting proof points" and needs to prove value
- Low financial risk for Greenlite
- Only pay for candidates that pass Allie/Ed's initial review
- Incentivizes Careerspan to send high-quality matches only

**Decided By**: Vrijen (offered) + Allie (accepted)

**Impact**: Low - establishes clear, low-risk commercial terms

---

### 6. Include Paul (Product Lead) When Product Roles Open

**Rationale**: 
- No product roles through end of 2025
- Paul interested in passive talent pipeline ("always happy to me compelling people and sort of like...keeping them on the bench")

**Decided By**: Allie + Paul (Greenlite)

## Process Decisions

### 7. Ed (Head of Engineering) to Provide Hiring Preferences

**Decision**: Ed will provide subjective context about what he looks for beyond what's in the JD

**Rationale**: 
- Ed currently screens all agency candidates himself
- Allie is still learning his preferences
- Subjective context improves semantic matching quality
- Reduces time Ed spends reviewing weak matches

**Decided By**: Allie + Vrijen

**Impact**: High - critical input for matching algorithm quality

**Note**: Ed was not on the call but Allie committed to getting this from him

---

### 8. White-Glove Follow-Up for Promising Candidates

**Decision**: If Greenlite is interested in a candidate but wants more info on X/Y/Z, Careerspan will handle getting candidate to update profile and submit incremental analysis

**Rationale**: 
- Reduces burden on Allie/Ed
- Maintains candidate experience through single point of contact
- Allows Careerspan to fill in gaps before formal introduction

**Decided By**: Vrijen (offered) + Allie (accepted)

**Impact**: Medium - improves candidate quality and reduces Greenlite's sourcing burden
