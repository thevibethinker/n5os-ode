## Careerspan Value Proposition

### Core Offering
- **Decentralized talent network** drawing from high-quality professional communities (Lenny's, ex-McKinsey, Stripe alumni, etc.)
- **Semantic matching system** (not keyword-based) that decomposes JDs and matches against candidate stories/backgrounds
- **Coaching-led approach**: Candidates build detailed profiles through coaching tool, giving employers deep context
- **Pre-filtering**: Careerspan handles initial vetting, only sends candidates they believe are strong matches

### Differentiation
- **Depth over breadth**: Candidates spend significant time building profiles ("month filling out Workday profile"), but employers scan in seconds
- **Gap identification**: System tells candidates about gaps in their story and gives them time to fill those gaps before matching
- **Community focus**: Targeting micro-communities (100-300 person groups) vs oversaturated large communities
- **Iterative improvement**: Takes feedback from employers to refine matching algorithm over time

## Proposed Pilot Structure

### What Careerspan Will Do
1. Take 4 tech role JDs from Greenlite
2. Accept subjective context from Ed (Head of Engineering) beyond what's in JD
3. Scan 200-400 weekly candidate influx from product/engineering communities
4. Send weekly candidate packets with:
   - Names
   - Analysis performed
   - Resumes
5. Distribute JDs via "Magic Link" in micro-communities (stealth company name)
6. Refine algorithm based on Greenlite's feedback ("these were lacking X")

### What Greenlite Commits
- **Time**: 15 minutes per week to review candidate packets
- **Feedback**: Tell Careerspan what's working/not working
- **Payment**: $300 per candidate they want to be introduced to (pay-on-success)
- **JDs**: Provide 4 tech role descriptions + subjective context

### Risk Mitigation
- **Low effort**: 15 min/week manageable alongside other priorities
- **Low cost**: Only pay for candidates they actually want
- **Stealth approach**: Prevents rejected candidates from applying directly
- **No exclusivity**: Can continue using agency partners in parallel
