### FOUNDER_PROFILE_SUMMARY

- Company: Recruiting/Talent platform focused on quality sourcing and candidate storytelling (video profiles, consolidated portfolio/profile for fast recruiter digestion).
- Product: Helps recruiters source and vet real candidates faster; helps candidates present their story and work in one place; goal is to land interviews efficiently.
- Motivation: Build a practical, time-saving workflow for recruiters while giving candidates a better way to stand out.
- Challenges: Avoiding spammy mass-apply tactics; ensuring quality and trust in candidate pools.
- Quote: “It’s basically a cheap way for [recruiters] to buy back time and guarantees that what they're spending their time on isn't going to be throwaway work.”
