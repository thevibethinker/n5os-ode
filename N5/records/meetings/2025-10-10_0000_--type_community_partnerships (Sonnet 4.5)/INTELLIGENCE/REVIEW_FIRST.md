## 📋 Careerspan Context Shared

### Company Overview
- 2.5 years old; started as career coaching, pivoted to B2B matching platform
- Raised $1M pre-seed from family office in India
- Paused bridge round to focus on GTM; plan to resume fundraising in ~3 months
- Team: 2 non-technical co-founders (Vrijen in Brooklyn, co-founder in Boston) + CTO

### Current Strategy
- **Candidate roles:** Engineering, product, growth (high-value, qualitative evaluation needs)
- **Company size:** Talent teams of ≤3 people (we're "virtual in-house recruiter")
- **Geography:** Boston and New York (founder presence)
- **Business model:** Bootstrapping toward per-placement via scan fees or warm intro fees first

### Unique Value Props Highlighted
- **Semantic matching:** Decomposes roles into composite qualities, matches on meaning not keywords
- **Traceability:** "This person has this trait because this situation where they did this thing that exhibited that quality"
- **Multilingual:** Accepts 50 languages, outputs English
- **Community model:** Free coaching to talent communities, charge employers for access
- **Equity mechanism:** Can incentivize employers to scan undervalued talent communities first (free scans)

### Concerns Acknowledged
- Risk of perpetuating elitism through "high signal community" focus
- Growth tactics vs. values tension
- Need to build trust in matching system before employers pay per-placement
