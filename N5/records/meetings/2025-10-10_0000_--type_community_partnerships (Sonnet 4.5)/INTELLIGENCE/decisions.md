## Tactical Decisions

### 4. Target Market Niching Down
- **Decision**: Careerspan is actively evaluating approaches to niche down on both candidate and company sides
- **Who Decided**: Careerspan team
- **Details**:
  - **Candidate side**: Focusing on engineer, product, and growth roles (high-value, qualitative, harder to evaluate systematically)
  - **Geographic focus**: Boston and New York (founder presence)
  - **Company side**: Companies with talent teams of 3 or under
- **Rationale**: 
  - These roles command higher placement fees
  - Product and growth roles lack good evaluation systems
  - Engineer roles offer volume
  - Small talent teams have urgent hiring needs but lack resources
- **Quote**: "On the candidate side we're thinking, you know, engineer, product growth... On the company side we're basically down to either companies where you have a talent team of three or under"
- **Impact**: Provides clearer go-to-market strategy and positioning
- **Category**: Market Segmentation

### 5. Monetization Approach: Scan-Based + Intro Fees Initially
- **Decision**: Bootstrap toward per-placement model by first charging for database scans or warm intros
- **Who Decided**: Careerspan team
- **Rationale**: 
  - Lower barrier to entry than placement fees
  - Demonstrates value before bigger commitment
  - Provides revenue while building trust in matching system
- **Quote**: "The way we're hoping to sort of bootstrap our way there is to first charge for either a scan of our database and charge a fixed amount for that or provide the scan for free and essentially charge for warm intros"
- **Impact**: Creates clear revenue pathway while building toward more lucrative per-placement model
- **Category**: Pricing Strategy

### 6. Incentivize Scanning of Underserved Communities
- **Decision**: Plan to offer free or discounted scanning for high-signal but underserved talent communities
- **Who Decided**: Vrijen Attawar / Careerspan team
- **Rationale**: 
  - Addresses concern about system devolving into "sheer elitism"
  - Aligns with mission of fair hiring
  - Growth tactic distinct from core values
- **Quote**: "Bring in communities where there's still what I would consider and you would consider really high signal talent and essentially incentivize companies to scan those places first... we won't charge you anything to scan these folks"
- **Impact**: Maintains values alignment while scaling; creates differentiation from traditional recruiting
- **Category**: Social Impact Strategy
