# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

---

**Perspective:** Internal product/GT M experimentation (Careerspan team)

### Insight 1 — Hiring-manager outreach is noisy (Signal Strength: ●●●○○)
- Evidence: Logan notes hiring managers are inundated when a role posts; low expected immediate responsiveness.
- Why it matters: Cold outreach to posted roles may yield low conversion; consider TA contacts or founders at smaller companies.
- Strategic value: Prioritize channels with clearer ownership (TA teams, founders with active hiring mandates).

### Insight 2 — Positioning matters: "secret combination of communities" > generic 'best communities' (Signal Strength: ●●●●○)
- Evidence: Discussion emphasized that claiming "best communities" is hand-wavy; differentiation comes from curated mix.
- Why it matters: Messaging should highlight proprietary sourcing process and refined curation, not broad claims.
- Strategic value: Use concrete examples (recent placements) and named micro-communities to increase credibility.

### Insight 3 — Two complementary monetization levers (Signal Strength: ●●●○○)
- Evidence: Team proposed warm-intro per-role charges and higher-priced scout-for-pay guarantees.
- Why it matters: Multiple price points allow rapid testing of willingness-to-pay across ICP segments (founders vs TA teams).
- Strategic value: Run segmented experiments with distinct offers and metrics.

**Actionable:** Use these insights to design targets for A/B tests, messaging, and channel selection.
