## Strategic Context

Careerspan is testing monetization approaches that convert the community and curated pipeline into near-term revenue. Two complementary offers were proposed: immediate warm-intro outreach (low-friction, per-intro charge) and a higher-priced scout/guarantee product for founders and TA teams. Emphasis on clarity of ask ("5 candidates, 10 minutes to review") and unique positioning: "secret combination of communities" rather than generic "best communities".
