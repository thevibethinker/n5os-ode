**Perspective:** Speaking as a founder / operator in talent/TA market

### Key Insights

1. Buyer profile clarity: Companies ~250+ headcount surfaced as more likely buyers for TA-oriented value (Laura noted this explicitly).
   - Implication: Targeting mid-market TA leaders (director-level) may improve early B2B traction.
   - Strategic value: Prioritize outreach to organizations where TA leaders are evaluated on time-to-fill and retention metrics.

2. Product-market fit signals: B2C traction is strong (high engagement/open rates), but B2B value must be framed around specific hiring outcomes (time-to-fill, conversion, retention).
   - Implication: Build sales narratives and pilot metrics tied to these outcomes.

3. Distribution via communities and grassroots recruiter outreach is a viable GTM lever (memes / community campaigns can reach recruiters and TA practitioners).
   - Implication: Consider community-first distribution and recruiter-focused marketing experiments as low-cost acquisition channels.

4. Differentiation opportunity: Positioning around "AI-ready" or "AI-forward" candidate pools was discussed as a defensible feature that can both narrow audience and answer buyer pain.
   - Implication: Investigate simple signals/filters to surface AI-capable candidates in product and GTM messaging.
