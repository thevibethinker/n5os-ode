# B31_STAKEHOLDER_RESEARCH

---
**Perspective:** Speaking as product/partnership representative for a spreadsheet-first analytics vendor

### Key Insights

1. Product focus is historically on financial modeling and advanced spreadsheet use cases — integration for qualitative job-lead ingestion is non-standard but of interest.
   - Implication: Equals values novel customers who push new use cases; successful pilots can inform product roadmaps.

2. Implementation/pricing constraints on lower tiers limit guided integrations; lightweight trials are their primary path to evaluate fit.
   - Implication: Requesting small pilot access is the correct tactical approach; expect limits on customization.

3. Data connectors strategy uses A5tran (or similar) for ingestion; there is appetite for agentic AI analyst features but plumbing matters.
   - Strategic value: If Careerspan demonstrates reliable ingestion and value, integration could move from exploratory to paid with clear ROI messaging.
