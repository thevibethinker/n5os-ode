## Critical Contradictions & Unresolved Tensions

### Contradiction 1: Verification vs. Passive Hiring

**Session 1 Position**: Careerspan's value is **verification technology**
- Story-based consistency checking
- Targets hiring managers with fraud anxiety
- B2B SaaS or acquisition by verification companies (Checkr, Tofu)

**Sessions 2 & 3 Position**: Careerspan's value is **passive candidate network**
- Career agent for planners
- Targets job seekers who want autopilot career management
- B2C platform for acquisition by Jack & Jill

**Impact**: These are fundamentally different products with different buyers
- Verification → Hiring manager pain, B2B sales, compliance moat
- Passive hiring → Candidate experience, network effects, brand moat

**Resolution Status**: ❌ **UNRESOLVED** - Session 3 appears to abandon verification completely, but Session 1 logic was sound

---

### Contradiction 2: 3-Month vs. 12-Month Timeline

**Session 1**: Urgent 3-month sale deadline

- "End of year target"

**Impact**: Changes team communication urgency and resource allocation
- 3 months → Sprint mentality, minimal new builds, focus on proof points
- 12 months → Sustainable pace, personal brand investment, user growth priority

**Resolution Status**: ⚠️ **PARTIALLY RESOLVED** - Appears to be 12-month timeline but Dec 31 team wind-down creates artificial 3-month checkpoint

### Contradiction 3: B2B vs. B2C Focus

**Session 1**: Explicitly B2B

### Contradiction 4: Manual Services vs. Platform Scale

**Session 1**: Platform/tech focus
