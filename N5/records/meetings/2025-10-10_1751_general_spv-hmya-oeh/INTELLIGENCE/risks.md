# Risks identified

1. IP / third-party carve-outs (Ilsa conversational tech) — may scuttle sale or reduce valuation. Mitigation: legal buy-in negotiation and market valuation.
2. Team attrition if communications are poor or runway unclear. Mitigation: guaranteed payout plan and transparent timeline.
3. Dirty data room/financial records prolonging diligence. Mitigation: prioritize finance cleanup immediately.
4. Over-promise distribution scale without engagement evidence. Mitigation: run small pilots and publish clear metrics.
5. Raising institutional capital now (bad money) could complicate sale math. Mitigation: prefer bridge angels.
6. Buyer timing mismatch — deep-pocket buyers may not be actively buying. Mitigation: widen buyer list and include strategic roll-up targets.
