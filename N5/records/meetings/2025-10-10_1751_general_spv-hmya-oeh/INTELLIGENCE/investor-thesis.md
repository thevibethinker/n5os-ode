# Investor / buyer thesis (top-line)

Thesis summary: Careerspan is a tech-enabled distribution asset — unique candidate data + community trust — that can be sold as either (A) an embedded HR-tech asset (API/SDK for ATS/Workday-like buyers) or (B) a distribution play (acquirable audience for recruiting/distribution buyers).

Why it matters to buyers:
- HR/ATS buyers: immediate product integration to improve hiring signal quality and candidate matching.
- Distribution buyers: instant, trusted access to a candidate pool and community channels.

Proof required:
- Clean IP and contracts
- Demo-ready tech milestones (Ilsa features)
- 3 community pilot signups and basic engagement metrics

Suggested ask: position as acquisition (asset + data) with target price band $5M–$10M depending on proven tech + distribution.
