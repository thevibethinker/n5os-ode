# Careerspan Timeline

## 2023: The ApplyAI Launch
Initial market entry with focus on story-to-resume and interview asset transformation, establishing foundational technology and early market traction.

## Early 2024: Rebrand to Careerspan
Strategic rebrand from The ApplyAI to Careerspan to reflect expanded vision toward comprehensive career companion and dynamic "3D résumé" platform.

## 2025: B2B2C Integrations and Pilots
Implementation of B2B2C partnership integrations and pilot programs, with internal data demonstrating improved candidate-opportunity fit validation.

## September 2025: GTM Pivot to Career Agent Wedge
Strategic go-to-market shift focusing on elite candidate supply model: free shortlists → pay-per-intro → subscription pipeline, with initial beachhead in Product Management roles at Series A/B startups.
