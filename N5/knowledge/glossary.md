# Glossary

## AI-mediated career companion
Careerspan's core product offering: an AI-powered platform that guides users through coaching-style conversations to capture comprehensive professional profiles (skills, values, mindset, stories) for improved opportunity matching.

## Career Agent wedge
Careerspan's employer go-to-market strategy: free pre-vetted candidate shortlists → pay-per-intro model → subscription for always-replenished curated pipeline, initially focused on Product roles.

## Truths → Lenses → Interpretations → Decisions pipeline
Careerspan's data processing framework: captures source-backed facts/events/stories, applies reference frames for context, generates reasoned judgments with confidence scores, and answers "fit" queries with traceability.

Aliases: T-L-I-D pipeline

## AISS semantic optimization
Action-Impact-Scale-Skill framework for resume and document rewriting that improves clarity and ATS alignment through grounded, evidence-based optimization without gaming algorithms.

Aliases: AISS framework

## 3D résumé
Careerspan's dynamic profile concept: comprehensive representation of a person's professional capabilities beyond traditional 2D resume format, incorporating skills, values, mindset, and narrative stories.

## B2B2C integrations
Business-to-business-to-consumer model where Careerspan embeds reflective interview capabilities into partner platforms and workflows.

## Coach-first design
Product philosophy prioritizing authentic, Socratic coaching conversations over algorithmic optimization, informed by founders' decade of coaching/education experience.
