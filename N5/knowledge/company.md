# Careerspan Company Information

## Overview
Careerspan is an AI-mediated career companion building a richer "human data layer" for hiring. The platform uses guided conversations to capture a three-dimensional profile of a person (skills, values, mindset, stories) and then applies that data to improve resumes, interviews, and—most importantly—opportunity matching. Founded in 2023 by Vrijen Attawar and Logan Currie; rebranded from "The ApplyAI" in early 2024 to reflect a whole-career focus.

## History
- **2023**: Launch of The ApplyAI; early traction helping job seekers transform story → resume/cover letter/interview assets
- **Early 2024**: Rebrand to Careerspan; vision expands from "application help" to a continuous career companion and dynamic profile ("3D résumé")
- **2025**: B2B2C integrations and pilots (e.g., embedding reflective interviews into partner flows); internal data shows improved candidate-opportunity fit
- **September 2025**: GTM pivot to a "Career Agent" wedge for employers: seed elite supply → deliver free shortlists → pay-per-intro → subscription for an always-replenished pipeline (initial focus: Product Management roles at Series A/B startups)

## Strategy
**Summary**: Build and own the qualitative talent data layer; use it to 1) help candidates present truthfully and powerfully, and 2) help employers hire faster and smarter through curated, high-signal supply.

### Strategic Pillars
1. **AI-mediated coaching → proprietary data**: Deep stories, values, meta-skills; not just keywords
2. **Opportunity matching**: Prioritizes mindset, values, and role-relevant evidence over superficial resume filters
3. **Career Agent wedge (employer GTM)**: Free shortlist → pay-per-intro → subscription; beachhead in Product roles for Series A/B teams
4. **Community-led supply & integrations**: Universities, alumni groups, talent communities to enrich candidate profiles and distribution without building a monolithic ATS

## Product & Technology
**Guided conversations (Socratic coaching)**: Capture primary-source stories, encode them into structured facts, sentiments, and narratives.

**Truths → Lenses → Interpretations → Decisions**:
- Truths = source-backed facts/events/stories
- Lenses = frames of reference (Source/Target role)
- Interpretations = reasoned judgments w/ confidence
- Decisions API answers "fit" queries with traceability and reuse of sub-decisions

**AISS semantic optimization**: Resume/doc rewriting grounded in Action–Impact–Scale–Skill; improves clarity and ATS alignment without "gaming."

**Technological foundation**: Multi-agent, quality-controlled middle layer atop frontier LLMs: input processors, evaluation agents, directors, extractors, and QC—all implementing structured coaching frameworks with multi-stage verification to minimize hallucinations.

## Employer Offer (Career Agent Wedge Motion)
- **Free shortlist**: Pre-vetted Product candidates (evidence-rich profiles)
- **Pay-per-intro**: When a company wants to meet someone
- **Subscription upsell**: "Always-on" curated pipeline; guarantee structure to de-risk

## Candidate Offer
Always-available AI coach that helps people surface genuine strengths, craft truthful narratives, and prepare role-aligned materials—reducing the storytelling gap and bias of traditional interviews.

## Operating Principles
- **Candidate-first & authenticity over spin**: Coach-style data capture; verifiable sources
- **Evidence > optics**: Richer inputs over fancier sorting
- **Bias reduction & context-aware evaluation**: Via Lenses and transparent Interpretations

## Mission & Philosophy
Candidate-first, evidence-first. Careerspan's bet: the hiring crisis is an informational problem (resume-centric, shallow signals), not a sorting-algorithm problem. Rich qualitative data gathered through coaching-style conversations is the missing input to better matching; the company aims to become the data layer the market consults when talent meets opportunity.

## Market Rationale
- ~2% of resumes lead to interviews; recruiters spend 6–7s on a first scan
- 46% of new hires fail within 18 months—89% for attitudinal/culture reasons
- Employers report 87% dissatisfaction with resumes as a hiring tool; weak correlation with performance
- ATS/AI usage expanding while entry-level roles shrink and experience demands rise

## Key Beliefs
- The resume is a lossy compression of a human; better inputs beat fancier filters
- Interviews over-reward rehearsed storytelling; collect primary-source truth instead
- Structured, transparent reasoning (Truths/Lenses/Interpretations/Decisions) wins employer trust and scales matching ethically
