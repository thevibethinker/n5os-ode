# B13: Partnerships & Collaboration

## Objective

Document partnership discussions, collaboration opportunities, alliance strategy, and relationship-building opportunities.

## What to Capture

**Partnership Opportunities:**
- Potential partners identified
- Collaboration models discussed
- Strategic fit and value
- Win-win scenarios

**Partnership Terms:**
- Proposed structure
- Value exchange
- Responsibilities
- Success metrics

**Integration Points:**
- How organizations would work together
- Resource sharing
- Joint initiatives
- Technical integrations

**Relationship Building:**
- Trust-building steps
- Next steps to advance partnership
- Decision-makers involved
- Timeline for partnership development

**Risks & Concerns:**
- Potential issues or conflicts
- Competitive concerns
- Resource requirements
- Commitment levels

## Output Format

### Partnership: [Partner Name]

**Strategic Rationale:**
[Why this partnership makes sense]

**Value Proposition:**
- **For Us:** [What we gain]
- **For Them:** [What they gain]

**Collaboration Model:**
[How we'd work together]

**Key Terms Discussed:**
- [Term or condition]
- [Term or condition]

**Integration Requirements:**
- [Technical, operational, or business integration needed]

**Next Steps:**
1. [Action item]
2. [Action item]

**Decision Makers:**
- Our side: [Names]
- Their side: [Names]

**Timeline:**
[Expected progression]

**Risks:**
- [Risk or concern]
- [Mitigation approach]

## Generation Guidelines

1. Listen for collaboration language:
   - Partnership, alliance, collaboration
   - Working together, joint effort
   - Strategic relationship
   - Win-win, mutual benefit

2. Capture value exchange:
   - What does each party get?
   - How does this serve strategic goals?
   - What's the business case?

3. Note structure:
   - How would it work operationally?
   - What resources required?
   - What governance or process?

4. Identify next steps:
   - What needs to happen?
   - Who needs to decide?
   - What's the timeline?

5. Surface concerns:
   - What could go wrong?
   - What are the risks?
   - What's needed to de-risk?

## When to Generate

Generate B13 for meetings involving:
- Partnership exploration or negotiation
- Strategic alliance discussions
- Collaboration opportunities
- Joint venture planning
- Ecosystem or network building

Skip for meetings not focused on partnerships.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Strategic rationale clear
- [ ] Value proposition for both parties
- [ ] Collaboration model described
- [ ] Key terms and conditions noted
- [ ] Integration requirements captured
- [ ] Next steps and timeline defined
- [ ] Risks and concerns identified
- [ ] Decision-makers identified
