# B08: Stakeholder Intelligence & Profiling

## Objective

Extract intelligence about people, organizations, and stakeholders mentioned in the meeting. Build profiles useful for CRM, relationship management, and strategic engagement.

## What to Capture

**About Individuals:**
- Role, title, responsibilities
- Background, experience, expertise
- Motivations and priorities
- Communication style and preferences
- Decision-making authority
- Relationships to others
- Interests and concerns
- Personal context relevant to business

**About Organizations:**
- Structure and reporting lines
- Decision-making process
- Priorities and initiatives
- Challenges and pain points
- Culture and values
- Budget and resources
- Timeline and urgency

**Network & Relationships:**
- Who knows whom
- Influence pathways
- Coalition patterns
- Gatekeepers and champions
- Potential connections to leverage

**Strategic Intel:**
- What matters to this stakeholder
- How to engage effectively
- What they need from us
- Potential objections or concerns
- Best path to influence/collaborate

## Output Format

Organize by person/organization:

### [Person Name - Role/Company]

**Background:**
- [Title, responsibilities, tenure]
- [Relevant experience or expertise]

**Priorities & Motivations:**
- [What they care about]
- [Current focus areas]

**Decision Authority:**
- [What they can approve/influence]
- [Who they report to or influence]

**Engagement Strategy:**
- [How to work with them effectively]
- [Communication preferences]
- [Key concerns to address]

**Network:**
- [Relationships to others mentioned]
- [Potential warm intro paths]

**Follow-Up:**
- [Next steps with this stakeholder]
- [Information they need]

## Generation Guidelines

1. Listen for profile data:
   - "She's the VP of..."
   - "He's been there for..."
   - "They really care about..."
   - "The way they work is..."
   - "You should know that..."

2. Capture motivations:
   - What drives this person?
   - What problems are they solving?
   - What success looks like for them?

3. Map relationships:
   - Who do they work with?
   - Who influences them?
   - Who do they influence?

4. Strategic thinking:
   - How should we engage?
   - What matters to them?
   - Where are opportunities?

5. Document evidence:
   - Base profiles on what was actually said
   - Note source of information
   - Flag assumptions vs. facts

## When to Generate

Generate B08 for meetings involving:
- New stakeholders or relationships
- Partnership/sales discussions
- Political or organizational navigation
- Key decision-makers
- Network mapping opportunities

Skip for purely technical or operational meetings with established teams.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Profiles based on meeting content (not speculation)
- [ ] Strategic engagement insights included
- [ ] Network/relationship connections mapped
- [ ] Decision authority and influence noted
- [ ] Actionable for follow-up and relationship management
- [ ] Names and titles accurate
- [ ] Appropriate sensitivity (this is private intelligence)
