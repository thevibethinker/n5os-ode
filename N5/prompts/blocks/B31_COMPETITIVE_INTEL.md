# B31: Competitive Intelligence & Market Landscape

## Objective

Extract competitive intelligence, market analysis, competitor insights, and strategic positioning discussed during the meeting.

## What to Capture

**Competitor Information:**
- Specific competitors mentioned
- Their strategies and moves
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Recent developments

**Competitive Positioning:**
- How we compare
- Differentiation points
- Competitive advantages
- Vulnerabilities

**Market Intelligence:**
- Market size and growth
- Industry trends
- Customer preferences shifting
- Emerging threats/opportunities

**Competitor Strategies:**
- Pricing approaches
- Product roadmaps
- Go-to-market tactics
- Partnership moves

**Win/Loss Analysis:**
- Why we win vs competitors
- Why we lose
- Customer decision factors
- Competitive dynamics

**Market Landscape:**
- Key players and roles
- Market structure
- Entry barriers
- Consolidation trends

  - [Weakness 2]
- **Recent Moves:** [What they've done recently]
- **Strategy:** [Their apparent approach]

**Our Advantages:**

- [Advantage 2]: [Why it matters]

### Market Trends

- **[Trend]:** [Description and competitive implications]

### Strategic Implications

## Generation Guidelines

1. Listen for competitive mentions:
   - Direct competitor names
   - "They're doing..."
   - Market comparisons
   - Competitive pressures
   - Customer alternatives

2. Capture specifics:
   - Company names and details
   - Specific strategies/tactics
   - Pricing information
   - Product capabilities

3. Assess implications:
   - What does this mean for us?
   - How should we respond?
   - Where are opportunities?

4. Note sources:
   - Where did this intel come from?
   - How credible is it?
   - How current?

5. Identify patterns:
   - Market trends
   - Competitive dynamics
   - Strategic shifts

## When to Generate

Generate B31 when meeting includes:
- Competitor discussions
- Market analysis
- Competitive strategy
- Win/loss analysis
- Industry landscape overview
- Strategic positioning
- Threat assessment

Skip for meetings without competitive/market focus.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Competitor information specific and factual
- [ ] Sources/credibility noted
- [ ] Competitive positioning clear
- [ ] Win/loss factors identified
- [ ] Market trends with implications
- [ ] Strategic recommendations included
- [ ] Balanced assessment (strengths and weaknesses)
- [ ] Actionable for strategy and planning
