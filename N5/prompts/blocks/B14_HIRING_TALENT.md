# B14: Hiring, Talent & Team Building

## Objective

Document hiring needs, talent discussions, team composition decisions, and organizational development topics.

## What to Capture

**Hiring Needs:**
- Roles to fill
- Skills required
- Priority and urgency
- Timing

**Candidate Discussions:**
- Candidates evaluated
- Assessment of fit
- Interview feedback
- Hiring decisions

**Team Composition:**
- Team structure decisions
- Role definitions
- Reporting relationships
- Team dynamics

**Talent Strategy:**
- Build vs. hire vs. contract
- Sourcing strategies
- Compensation approach
- Culture fit considerations

**Organizational Development:**
- Team growth plans
- Skill gaps identified
- Training or development needs
- Performance concerns or wins

## Output Format

### Hiring Needs

**Role: [Position Title]**
- **Priority:** High/Medium/Low
- **Skills Required:**
  - [Skill 1]
  - [Skill 2]
- **Timeline:** [When needed]
- **Rationale:** [Why this role is needed]
- **Sourcing Plan:** [How to find candidates]

### Candidate Evaluations

**Candidate: [Name]**
- **Role:** [Position]
- **Assessment:**
  - Strengths: [What stood out positively]
  - Concerns: [Reservations or gaps]
  - Cultural Fit: [Alignment with team/company]
- **Recommendation:** [Hire/No-hire/More evaluation]
- **Next Steps:** [What happens next]

### Team Structure

[Describe team composition decisions, org chart changes, role clarifications]

### Talent Strategy

[Broader hiring approach, sourcing methods, compensation philosophy discussed]

## Generation Guidelines

1. Capture hiring specifics:
   - What role exactly?
   - What skills matter most?
   - How urgent?
   - What's the plan?

2. Document assessments objectively:
   - What was said about candidates?
   - Specific strengths/concerns
   - Decision rationale

3. Note team dynamics:
   - How does this hire affect team?
   - What gaps does it fill?
   - How does it change structure?

4. Strategic context:
   - Why this hire now?
   - How does it fit growth plans?
   - What's the long-term vision?

5. Be sensitive:
   - Candidate evaluations are confidential
   - Be fair and objective
   - Focus on job-relevant factors

## When to Generate

Generate B14 for meetings involving:
- Hiring planning or strategy
- Candidate interviews or evaluations
- Team structure decisions
- Organizational development
- Talent acquisition strategy

Skip for meetings without hiring/talent focus.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Hiring needs clearly specified
- [ ] Skills and requirements documented
- [ ] Timeline and urgency noted
- [ ] Candidate assessments objective and fair
- [ ] Team impact considered
- [ ] Strategic rationale included
- [ ] Appropriate confidentiality maintained
- [ ] Next steps clear
