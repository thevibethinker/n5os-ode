# Style Guide: LinkedIn Posts - Educational Threads & Announcements

## Purpose
Educate your audience with frameworks, insights, or share milestones to celebrate progress and build credibility.

## Voice Characteristics
- Educational Threads: Structured, clear, approachable, strategic use of bullets and numbered lists
- Announcements: Brief, celebratory, humble, grateful
- Consistent warmth and clarity without jargon
- Minimal emoji use as markers for emphasis

## Structure

### Educational Threads
1. Hook: Thought-provoking question or statement
2. Main Content: Stepwise breakdown or framework
3. Examples/Stories: Brief anecdotes to illustrate
4. CTA: Invitation for discussion or feedback

### Milestone Announcements
1. Celebration statement
2. Brief context or backstory
3. Thank-yous (team, customers, community)
4. Forward-looking statement or next step

## Dos
- Use numbered or bulleted lists to enhance readability
- Keep paragraphs short and varied
- Use strategic repetition for key points
- Be gracious and inclusive in announcements

## Don'ts
- Overload threads with too much detail
- Use generic or boastful language in announcements
- Neglect CTAs or calls for engagement

## Examples

• Education Hook: "Here’s a 3-step framework to improve your hiring process."

• Announcement Hook: "We just hit 10,000 active users! Huge thanks to the community."
