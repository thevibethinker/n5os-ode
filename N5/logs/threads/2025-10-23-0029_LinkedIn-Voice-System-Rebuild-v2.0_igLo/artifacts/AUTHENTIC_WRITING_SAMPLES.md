## PATTERNS OBSERVED (Authentic V Voice)

### Opening Style:
- "Hey [Name]—" or "Hi [Name]," (casual but professional)
- "Ayyy" for very close contacts
- Direct acknowledgment of context
- Often mentions recent conversations

### Body Language:
- Uses em-dashes frequently for asides/emphasis
- Mix of flowing paragraphs and structured lists
- Occasional italics for emphasis (*Quick hits*)
- Natural contractions (I'm, we're, it'd)
- Specific details that show care

### Tone Markers:
- "Man," as interjection for excitement
- "so, so much" for genuine gratitude
- "No pressure though" after requests
- "Figured I'd..." for casual suggestion
- Personal touches: "(and literally less in debt)!"

### Closing:
- Always "Best," or "Warmly," never just "Thanks,"
- Full signature block
- Natural follow-up references

### What's NOT Present:
- ❌ Emoji usage in professional emails
- ❌ Single-sentence paragraphs for effect
- ❌ Performative vulnerability
- ❌ LinkedIn-style hooks
- ❌ Excessive line breaks
