## What RPI Actually Means
RPI = (Actual Emails / Expected Emails) × 100

It's a PERCENTAGE, not an absolute score:
- 150%+ = Invincible (sending 1.5x expected)
- 125-149% = Top Performance
- 100-124% = Meeting Expectations (FIRST TEAM starts here)
- 75-99% = Catch Up Needed
- <75% = Behind Schedule (you're here at 23.55%)
