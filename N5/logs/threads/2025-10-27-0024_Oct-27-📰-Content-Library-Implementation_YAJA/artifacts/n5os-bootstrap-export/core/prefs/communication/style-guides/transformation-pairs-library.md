# Transformation Pairs Library (Seed)

Purpose: Seed examples for few-shot voice conditioning.

Format per pair:
- Context Type:
- Style-Free (neutral rewrite):
- V-Voice (authentic):
- Notes:

---

1) Context Type: Intro email (warm intro)
Style-Free:
"Connecting Jabari with Ben Guo (Zo). Reason: alignment between AI Collective and Zo. Notable facts: Ben early at Venmo, ~10yrs at Stripe, Zo = promptable cloud computer. My POV: use it daily; built personal OS on it."
V-Voice:
"Hi Jabari — … one of the founders of Zo Computer. … I’ve built my entire personal operating system on it — 250k+ lines of code … It’s essentially a computer in the cloud you can prompt and set to work on your behalf."
Notes: Anchors — concrete pedigree, lived-use, vivid descriptor, one clear ask.

2) Context Type: Follow-up after event
Style-Free:
"Apology for delay due to family emergencies. Provide update. Request: sponsor if possible; otherwise, confirm attendance."
V-Voice:
"Apologies for going quiet … back-to-back family emergencies … Thankfully, things have settled, and I’m now fully focused on making the most of this week’s event. … I realize it’s likely too late to secure sponsorships … If not, I’ll aim to make it back on track …"
Notes: Human context first, then agenda; specific constraints; polite but direct.

3) Context Type: LinkedIn — thought leadership (seed)
Style-Free:
"Observation about early hires: underinvested but critical. Claim: wasted spend elsewhere. Suggest: invest in first 5 hires."
V-Voice:
"Founder math is wild … $8K/month on tools … $25K on a booth … $50K on fractional … But the first five people? ‘Can we just post for free?’ … Your first five hires are your foundation."
Notes: Make it visual; money figures; cadence in lists; land the principle.

4) Context Type: LinkedIn — origin story (seed)
Style-Free:
"Personal struggle: context switching → missed follow-ups → self-doubt. Comparison with other founders. N5 OS helped reclaim agency."
V-Voice:
"I spent 10 years giving career advice. Then I became a founder and forgot how to follow my own. … I was drowning … N5 OS made it boring to do the right thing … I trust myself again."
Notes: Lead with identity reversal; 2–3 lived specifics; resolution without bravado.
