## Provenance Tracking

Every entry captures:
- **Conversation ID** (where it was generated)
- **Thread ID** (specific conversation within workspace)
- **Script Path** (if generated by script)
- **Pipeline/Run ID** (if part of multi-step process)
- **Parent Output ID** (if derived from another output)
- **Timestamp** (when created)
- **Content Hash** (for files, detect changes)
