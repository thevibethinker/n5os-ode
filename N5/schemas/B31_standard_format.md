# B31 Standard Format (v2.0)

**Effective Date:** 2025-10-30  
**Purpose:** Structured format for programmatic extraction into GTM intelligence database

---

## Template

```markdown
# B31: Stakeholder Research

**Meeting Date**: YYYY-MM-DD  
**Stakeholder**: [Name] - [Role/Company]  
**Research Focus**: [One-line summary]

---

## Insight 1: [Title] ●●●●○

**Category**: [Category Name]  
**Signal Strength**: [1-5, shown as ●●●●○]

**Evidence**:
[Direct quotes, observed behaviors, specific examples]

**Why It Matters**:
[Strategic implications for Careerspan]

**Source Credibility**: [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW]  
**Confidence Level**: [HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW - reasoning]

---

## Insight 2: [Title] ●●●○○

[Same structure repeated]
```

---

## Required Fields (Programmatic Extraction)

Each insight MUST have:
- **Title** (after "## Insight N:")
- **Signal Strength** (1-5 filled circles: ●●●●● down to ●○○○○)
- **Category** (one of: GTM Strategy, Market Pain Points, Product Insights, Competitive Landscape, etc.)
- **Evidence** (under `**Evidence**:`)
- **Why It Matters** (under `**Why It Matters**:`)
- **Confidence Level** (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW with reasoning)

---

## Category Taxonomy

**GTM & Distribution**
**Recruiting Agency Pain Points**
**Hiring Manager Pain Points**
**Job Seeker Pain Points**
**Founder Pain Points**
**Consultant Pain Points**
**Product Strategy & Features**
**Competitive Landscape**
**Market Dynamics**
**Pricing & Business Model**
**Fundraising & Investor Relations**

---

## Signal Strength Guide

- **●●●●●** (5/5): Multiple confirmations, high-stakes decision-driving
- **●●●●○** (4/5): Strong evidence, actionable, some validation
- **●●●○○** (3/5): Solid observation, worth tracking
- **●●○○○** (2/5): Interesting signal, needs more data
- **●○○○○** (1/5): Weak signal, speculative

---

## Example (Correct Format)

```markdown
## Insight 1: Recruiting agencies desperate for quality sourcing ●●●●●

**Category**: Recruiting Agency Pain Points  
**Signal Strength**: 5

**Evidence**:
"LinkedIn and job boards are completely saturated. Inbound applicant quality is terrible. We're out of ideas for how to get to talent." - Jeff Sipe mentioned this is universal across his agency network (Superposition, LaunchSam, Matchbox).

**Why It Matters**:
This validates agency channel as high-urgency GTM. Agencies aren't just interested—they're desperate. "Refined gold not ore" positioning directly addresses their #1 pain point.

**Source Credibility**: HIGH (industry veteran with network of 20+ agencies)  
**Confidence Level**: HIGH - Corroborated by multiple agencies expressing interest unprompted
```
