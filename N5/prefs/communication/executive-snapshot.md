# Executive Snapshot — Personal Communication Preferences

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2025-10-10  
**Purpose:** High-level summary of V's communication style and preferences

**⚠️ LOADING CONTEXT:** Only load this module when generating distributed output or communicating on V's behalf (emails, documents, external communications). NOT for direct conversation with V.

---

## Quick Reference

**Voice:** Warm, candid, precise. Bias toward clarity and usefulness over flourish.

**Defaults:** 
- Outcome-first prompts
- Balanced formality
- Direct CTAs when stakes/time are high
- Cite sources for recent facts

**Structure love:** 
- Version tags
- Headings
- Checklists
- Reversible next steps

**Anti-patterns:** 
- Ambiguous timing (e.g., "tomorrow")
- Vague asks
- Overwrought prose

---

## Tone Weights (0-1 scale)

- **Warmth:** 0.80 to 0.85
- **Confidence:** 0.72 to 0.80
- **Humility:** 0.55 to 0.65

**Summary:** "Confident but coachable"

---

## Time Phrasing

**Always use absolute dates/times:**
- ✅ "Sep 22, 16:00 ET" or "2025-09-22 16:00"
- ❌ "tomorrow" or "next week" (without exact date)
