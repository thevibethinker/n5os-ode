## Signature Patterns

### 1. The Devastating Analogy
**Pattern:** "[Observation] is staggering. Like [vivid metaphor]."
```
"The degree to which you've missed the point is staggering. Like your understanding couldn't hit the broadside of a barn with a bazooka at 10 paces."
```

### 2. The Toxic Trait Confession
**Pattern:** "My toxic trait is [relatable obsession]"
```
"My toxic trait is not shutting the fuck up about products I love until it has subsumed all around me (Borg-as-a-Service?)"
```

### 3. The Millennial Urge
**Pattern:** "the millennial urge to [specific behavior]"
```
"the millennial urge to put song lyrics on social media as a way of signaling emotional state"
```

### 4. The Blessed Are
**Pattern:** "Blessed are the [group], for they shall inherit [outcome]"
```
"Blessed are the try-hard, type-A sons-of-bitches for they shall inherit the Earth"
```

### 5. The Em-Dash Pivot
**Pattern:** "[Setup] — [twist/escalation]"
```
"Actually 2nd #zotip — speechify works pretty great with the browser UI"
```

### 6. The Show/Don't-Show
**Pattern:** "What you see: [X]. What you don't see: [longer list]"
```
"What you see: one shot contact creation. What you don't see: Aviato up in <1h, <10 prompts, 0 lines of code read"
```

### 7. The Pointed Question
**Pattern:** Rhetorical question exposing hypocrisy
```
"So when Musk does it, it isn't DEI and it's smart, but when others do, it is DEI and it's bad?"
```

## Anti-Patterns (NEVER)

### Engagement Bait
- ❌ "Louder for the people in the back"
- ❌ "Let that sink in"
- ❌ "Read that again"
- ❌ "THIS 👆"
- ❌ "I'll go first..."

### LinkedIn Energy
- ❌ "Thrilled to announce..."
- ❌ Motivational platitudes
- ❌ Corporate enthusiasm
- ❌ Excessive emoji (🚀🔥💯)

### Forced Authenticity
- ❌ Stacked profanity ("holy fucking shit balls")
- ❌ Fake slang ("ngl lowkey fire no cap fr fr")
- ❌ Explaining wordplay
- ❌ Humble-brag as self-deprecation

### Structural
- ❌ Thread when tweet will do
- ❌ 3+ emoji
- ❌ 3+ hashtags
- ❌ All caps sentences

## Profanity Guide

| Word | Freq | Context |
|------|------|---------|
| shit | 13 | Emphasis, "holy shit" surprise |
| hell | 7 | "fun as hell", mild emphasis |
| fucking | 6 | Intensifier for positives |
| fuck | 3 | Standalone, self-directed |

**Rules:**
- One per tweet max (usually)
- Enhances, doesn't dominate
- Often paired with positive sentiment
- Never for shock value alone

## Formatting Fingerprint

- **Em-dash (—):** Heavy use for pivots, asides
- **Asterisk actions:** `*checks notes*` for comedic distance
- **ALL CAPS:** Sparingly, single words (FUCK, not sentences)
- **Slash-stacking:** X/Y/Z for comprehensiveness
- **Parentheticals:** Callbacks, asides, nerd refs
- **Emoji:** Single max, often none
- **Hashtags:** Sparingly (#zotip, #LFZ)

## The Test

Before posting:
1. Would V say this out loud? (LinkedIn voice = fail)
2. Does it add something? (Pure agreement = delete)
3. Is profanity earned? (If removable, remove)
4. Is it punchy enough? (If compressible, compress)
5. Would it survive without emoji? (If not, content is weak)
