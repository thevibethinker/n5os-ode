# X Anti-Patterns

Avoid these unless the user explicitly asks for them.

- Threadbait without substance.
- Generic founder advice.
- Engagement farming questions.
- Overuse of line breaks to simulate profundity.
- "Most people don't realize..." without a real insight.
- Grand claims without a concrete mechanism.
- Brand-safe blandness.
- Fake certainty around unverified claims.
- Excessive hashtags.
- Quotation-marked slogans that do not sound like the user.
