# X Voice Fingerprint

Use this as a compact reference before drafting X posts.

## Core Traits

- Concrete claim before abstraction.
- High signal density; remove throat-clearing.
- Stakes, mechanism, and implication are usually more valuable than vibes.
- Prefer sharp nouns and verbs over adjectives.
- Sound like a person with a point of view, not a brand account.

## Structure

Good posts usually contain one of:

- Observation -> mechanism -> implication.
- Contrarian claim -> reason -> useful takeaway.
- Specific example -> broader pattern.
- Short story -> earned lesson.

## Guardrail

Do not imitate viral cadence by default. Draft from the actual idea, then compress.
