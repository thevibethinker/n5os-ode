# Engagement Preferences

## Load Up / Prime Protocol
- "Load up" or "prime" always means to get things primed and ready for use.
- This does NOT imply execution, running commands, or creating/modifying files.
- It refers to preparing knowledge, commands, or systems mentally or structurally without action.
- Always confirm intent before any operational steps.
