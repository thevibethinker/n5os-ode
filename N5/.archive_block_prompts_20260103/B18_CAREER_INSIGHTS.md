# B18: Career Development Insights

## Objective

Extract insights about career development, professional growth, industry trends, and talent/hiring that emerged during the meeting.

## What to Capture

**Career Advice:**
- Guidance shared about career paths
- Professional development recommendations
- Skill development priorities
- Industry insights for career planning

**Hiring Intelligence:**
- Roles being hired for
- Hiring criteria and priorities
- Compensation ranges discussed
- Company culture insights
- Interview process details

**Talent Market Insights:**
- What skills are in demand
- Industry hiring trends
- Compensation benchmarks
- Where talent is moving

**Professional Growth:**
- Development opportunities mentioned
- Learning resources shared
- Mentorship or coaching discussed
- Career transition strategies

**Industry Dynamics:**
- How industry is evolving
- Where opportunities are emerging
- What's becoming obsolete
- Future-of-work considerations

## Output Format

### Career Guidance
[Key advice or insights about career development]

### Hiring Intelligence

**Company: [Name]**
- **Roles:** [What they're hiring]
- **Priorities:** [What they value]
- **Compensation:** [If discussed]
- **Culture:** [Work environment insights]
- **Process:** [How they hire]

### Talent Market Trends
- [Trend 1]: [Description and implications]
- [Trend 2]: [Description and implications]

### Skills in Demand
- **[Skill]:** [Why it matters, who needs it]
- **[Skill]:** [Why it matters, who needs it]

### Professional Development
**Resource: [Name]**
- Type: [Course, book, program, etc.]
- Relevance: [Why recommended]
- Access: [How to get it]

### Industry Evolution
[Insights about how the field/industry is changing and what it means for careers]

## Generation Guidelines

1. Listen for career-relevant content:
   - Advice about growth or development
   - Hiring needs and processes
   - Salary/compensation discussions
   - Industry evolution and trends
   - Skill requirements

2. Capture hiring details:
   - Specific roles and requirements
   - What companies look for
   - Compensation if mentioned
   - Interview/process insights

3. Note market intelligence:
   - What's in demand
   - Where talent is moving
   - Emerging opportunities
   - Declining areas

4. Extract actionable guidance:
   - Specific skills to develop
   - Resources to explore
   - People to connect with
   - Paths to consider

5. Context matters:
   - Who gave this advice?
   - What's their perspective?
   - How current is this info?

## When to Generate

Generate B18 when meeting includes:
- Career coaching or advice
- Hiring discussions
- Talent market insights
- Professional development topics
- Industry trend discussions
- Compensation benchmarking
- Role/company intelligence

Skip for meetings without career/talent content.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Career advice captured with context
- [ ] Hiring intelligence specific and accurate
- [ ] Talent market insights current
- [ ] Skills/competencies clearly identified
- [ ] Professional development resources included
- [ ] Industry trends explained
- [ ] Actionable for career planning
- [ ] Source/credibility noted
