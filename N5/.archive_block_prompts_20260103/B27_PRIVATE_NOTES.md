# B27: Private Notes for Vrijen

## Objective

Capture sensitive insights, strategic thinking, personal observations, and private context that should NOT be shared externally. This is Vrijen's private intelligence layer.

## What to Capture

**Strategic Observations:**
- Patterns or insights not obvious to others
- Red flags or concerns
- Opportunities to leverage
- Competitive intelligence
- Power dynamics observed

**Personal Assessment:**
- Gut reactions and intuitions
- Trust calibration
- Capability assessments
- Reliability predictions
- Personal compatibility thoughts

**Tactical Intelligence:**
- Negotiation leverage points
- Information asymmetries
- Timing considerations
- Unspoken agendas detected
- Political maneuvering observed

**Sensitive Context:**
- Things said "off the record"
- Confidential information shared
- Personal situations affecting business
- Relationship tensions
- Internal politics

**Reminders & Flags:**
- Things to watch for in future
- Follow-up to do privately
- Relationships to nurture/avoid
- Sensitivities to remember

## Output Format

Free-form notes organized by category. Use clear headers. Be direct and honest.

### Strategic Assessment
[High-level read on situation]

### People & Relationships
[Private observations about individuals]

### Risks & Red Flags
[Concerns or warning signs]

### Opportunities
[Leverage points or strategic openings]

### Tactical Notes
[Things to do/remember privately]

### Gut Check
[Intuitive reactions worth noting]

## Generation Guidelines

1. This is PRIVATE:
   - Write honestly and directly
   - Include sensitive observations
   - Flag things that feel "off"
   - Note political dynamics

2. Strategic value:
   - What does Vrijen need to know?
   - What should inform next moves?
   - What patterns are emerging?

3. Actionable insights:
   - What should be done differently?
   - What opportunities exist?
   - What risks need management?

4. Context for future:
   - What will matter later?
   - What should be remembered?
   - What explains behavior/decisions?

5. Trust your gut:
   - Intuitions are valuable
   - Note what feels significant
   - Flag inconsistencies or concerns

## When to Generate

Generate B27 for meetings involving:
- High-stakes negotiations
- New or uncertain relationships
- Political complexity
- Sensitive topics
- Strategic decision-making
- Anyone Vrijen needs private intel about

Skip for routine operational meetings unless something noteworthy emerges.

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing, check:
- [ ] Includes insights not captured elsewhere
- [ ] Honest and direct (this is private)
- [ ] Strategic value for Vrijen
- [ ] Sensitive information handled appropriately
- [ ] Actionable recommendations included
- [ ] Future-oriented (what to watch/do)
- [ ] **NEVER shared externally or in public docs**
